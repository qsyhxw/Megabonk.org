üîì How to Unlock Scythe

The Scythe requires completing a multi-step process involving the Graveyard map and defeating the boss Big Bob in the Crypt.

‚öîÔ∏è Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Play Graveyard Map: Start a run on the Graveyard map, which contains the Crypt area.
  2. Collect 4 Crypt Keys: Search the Graveyard for 4 Crypt Keys scattered throughout the map.
  3. Enter the Crypt: With all 4 keys, locate and open the Crypt Door to access Big Bob's arena.
  4. Defeat Big Bob: During the fight, stand near lanterns to deal damage to Big Bob. Avoid his one-shot attack!
  5. Purchase Scythe: After winning, the Scythe appears in the Unlocks menu under "Weapons" for purchase.
‚ö†Ô∏è Big Bob Boss Tip

Big Bob has a powerful attack that can one-shot kill you. Watch for wind-up animations and keep moving. Only deal damage while positioned near the lanterns in the arena!

Sweeping Arc Attacks

The Scythe swings in a wide arc that can hit multiple enemies in front of you. This makes it exceptionally effective in corridors and against grouped enemies.

Upgrade Priorities

  • Damage: Primary stat for improving clear speed
  • Attack Speed: More frequent swings = more crowd control
  • Crit Chance/Damage: Scales well with wide-hitting attacks
  • Area: Increases the arc width for even more enemy coverage

Best Characters for Scythe

  • Close-range fighters: Characters comfortable with melee range benefit most
  • Lifesteal builds: Hitting multiple enemies per swing means more healing
  • Speed builds: Fast movement helps position for optimal arc coverage
