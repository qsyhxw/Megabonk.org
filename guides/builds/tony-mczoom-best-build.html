Lightning Staff or Revolver?

Take Lightning Staff for pack control; choose Revolver for elites/bosses. Pick based on drops and lobby.

When to add Aegis?

Do I need movement items if I'm confident?

You can skip one, but Tony converts speed into safety and uptime. At least one speed item is recommended.
