🔓 How to Unlock Dragonfire

📜Requirement

Kill 4,000 enemies with fire damage.

Fast Progress Tips

  1. Use Firestaff and Flamewalker to apply consistent fire damage.
  2. Once available, add Dragon’s Breath for sweeping lanes.
  3. Prefer dense maps for faster kill counts; kite mobs through burning paths.

🧪 Mechanics

What the effect does

  • Each of your hits has a 15% chance to spawn a burst of fire.
  • That fire applies burn (damage over time) to enemies caught inside.
  • Picking multiple Dragonfires increases overall proc frequency (more rolls).
ℹ️Note on “global burn” wording

Evidence supports that the Dragonfire proc itself applies burn. There’s no confirmed official note that it globally makes all fire damage sources apply burn by default.

❓ FAQ

How do I unlock Dragonfire?
Kill 4,000 enemies with fire damage. Progress persists across runs.
Does Dragonfire stack?
Yes. Picking multiple Dragonfires increases the overall frequency of burn-spawning procs.
