Attack Patterns

‚ö°
Chain Lightning
INSTANT KILL

Pattern: Lightning jumps between all enemies and the player in sequence

Damage: 600-800 per hit (usually fatal)

Range: Entire screen - nowhere is safe

Frequency: Every 6-8 seconds

Counter: This attack cannot be dodged by positioning. Only lightning resistance or immunity can save you.

‚ö° Lightning Resistance Required

Without significant lightning resistance (75%+), this boss is nearly impossible. Plan your build around electrical defense or accept that this fight will end quickly.

Thunder Storm
EXTREME THREAT

Pattern: Multiple lightning strikes hit random locations across battlefield

Damage: 400-500 per strike

Count: 8-12 simultaneous strikes

Frequency: Every 10-12 seconds

Counter: Rapid movement and prayer. Some strikes can be avoided, but the coverage is massive.

‚ö°
Lightning Dash
HIGH THREAT

Pattern: Boss teleports instantly to player location with electrical explosion

Damage: 500-600 in medium radius

Speed: Instantaneous - no warning

Frequency: Every 15-20 seconds

Counter: Constant movement is your only defense. Never stay in one place for more than 2-3 seconds.

Phase Guide

1
Storm Gathering (100-70% HP)

Focus on Chain Lightning and occasional Thunder Storms. This is your chance to establish rhythm and test your lightning resistance. If you can't survive this phase, you won't survive the fight.

2
Electrical Chaos (70-30% HP)

Lightning Dash joins the rotation. All attacks become more frequent. Thunder Storms now have 12-15 strikes instead of 8-12. Maximum mobility and resistance are both essential.

3
Tempest Fury (30-0% HP)

All attacks overlap. Chain Lightning every 4-5 seconds, constant Thunder Storms, frequent Lightning Dashes. Only perfectly optimized builds with maximum defenses can survive this phase.
