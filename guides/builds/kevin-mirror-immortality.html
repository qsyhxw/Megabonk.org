üí• INVULNERABILITY FRAME CHAINING

The Real Mechanism (Not Regeneration!):

1. Kevin has 25% chance to self-damage on every attack

2. Every self-damage proc triggers Mirror's invulnerability window

3. High attack speed means constant Kevin procs

4. Constant Kevin procs = overlapping invulnerability frames

5. Overlapping i-frames = permanent immunity to damage

Result: With sufficient attack speed, you can stand motionless in the Final Swarm completely surrounded by enemies while taking zero damage. Your kill count skyrockets while you literally do nothing.
