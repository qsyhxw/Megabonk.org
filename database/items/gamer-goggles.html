Gamer Goggles

⭐ RARITY: Rare (community)
Effect: Increases damage the lower your HP.
Unlock
Defeat a Stage Boss with <10% HP
Item Type
Item (Offense scaler)
Best For
Low-HP risk builds

Overview

Gamer Goggles is a damage-scaling item that shines when you’re on the brink: as your health drops, your damage ramps up. If you’re cataloging or comparing items, browse the full Megabonk Item Database for context and counters.

How to Unlock

  1. Fight any Stage Boss down to low HP.
  2. Ensure your own HP is kept above zero but below 10% at the moment you deliver the finishing blow.

Tip: Higher maximum HP gives you a wider safety buffer for the 10% threshold.

Strategy & Build Hooks

Because damage scales at low health, prioritize survivability tools that help you hover safely at low HP, and finishing power to close boss fights without healing above the threshold.

Note: The above are playstyle suggestions based on the item’s effect; they are not patch-locked mechanics.
