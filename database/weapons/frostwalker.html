üîì How to Unlock Frostwalker

Your goal is straightforward: equip Ice Cube and freeze 1,000 enemies. Progress is cumulative across runs.

  1. Enable & find Ice Cube
    Since Ice Cube is Legendary and in the default item pool, prioritize loot to increase your odds (open every chest you can; visit shops when available).
  2. Play for density
    High-enemy-density moments (later waves, swarms) generate more on-hit checks, turning the 20% freeze chance into steady unlock progress.
  3. Use multi-hit weapons
    Pair Ice Cube with multi-target or rapid-tick weapons in your loadout to trigger more Ice procs while you work toward Frostwalker.
  4. Track your progress
    Use the interactive counter below as an external tracker. The game tracks this internally; the counter helps with planning.
  5. Claim & purchase
    After reaching 1,000 freezes, Frostwalker becomes available; unlocking it earns 2 Silver and it can be purchased with Silver.
