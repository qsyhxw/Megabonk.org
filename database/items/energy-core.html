🔓 How to Unlock Energy Core

  1. Progress through the game until you can challenge a Final Boss.
  2. Defeat a Final Boss twice (across runs). This permanently adds Energy Core to your item pool.

Source consensus (guides & community): unlock requires two Final Boss kills. See references below.

🔗 Best Uses & Synergies

Energy Core shines in ability-intensive loadouts where shorter cooldowns and dependable energy sustain translate directly into DPS or control uptime. Consider pairing with tomes and weapons that scale with frequent ability casts.
