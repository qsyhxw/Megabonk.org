🎯 Build Overview

This is a late‑game ceiling control shell: make Black Holes larger and more frequent so waves remain glued in place. You then funnel grouped enemies into a Dexecutioner execute or Katana multi‑hit cone while Aura keeps you safe. Use Bananarang or Lightning Staff as your cleanup slot. Tome order: Size (mandatory) ▶ Quantity ▶ Cooldown ▶ Luck/XP/Damage.

Playstyle

Macro control • grouping • execute cleanup

Difficulty

Medium — slow early, explosive mid‑late

Power Spike

Mid‑late once Size + Quantity are stacked

Best For

High scores, timer pushes, dense maps

⚔️ Core & Supporting Weapons

Keep to 3–4 total weapons to avoid upgrade dilution

Black Hole

CORE

Your entire plan. Size is the highest‑value stat — it directly increases pull radius and control time. Quantity + Cooldown multiply uptime.

Dexecutioner (or Katana)

FINISHER

Pick Dex to convert grouped packs via executes; choose Katana if you prefer multi‑hit melee cones to chew through the pile.

Aura

BODYGUARD

Hands‑free damage near your feet so stragglers don’t chip you while you herd.

Bananarang (or Lightning Staff)

CLEANUP

Orbiting cleanup that scales with Size, or swap to Lightning Staff if you want chain stuns to keep edges tidy.

📈 Upgrade Path Timeline

Minute‑by‑minute; high‑rarity rolls can preempt

0–3 min

Stabilize

Open with Size the moment it appears. Keep weapons to 2–3 (Black Hole + finisher) to focus upgrades. If fragile, take Aura early.

3–6 min

Go Online

Add Quantity ranks and a cleanup slot (Bananarang or Lightning Staff). Maintain small weapon count.

6–10 min

Continuous Control

Begin Cooldown and pick up Battery/Time Bracelet. Your map should feel ‘sticky’ now.

10–15 min

Accelerate

Start Luck/XP to speed high‑rarity rolls and leveling; upgrade your finisher (Dex/Katana) to capitalize.

15+ min

Close Out

Cap Size/Quantity/CDR and add Damage last for thresholds. Hold lanes and eat grouped packs for score/time pushes.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

Ignoring Size early

It’s the largest power spike for this archetype — don’t delay it.

Too many weapons

Dilutes upgrades and delays your control spike; stick to 3–4.

Taking Damage before control

Dead DPS does no DPS — solve grouping and uptime first.

No pickup radius

Without Sucky Magnet you stall tomes and slow your scaling.

🧠 Why This Works

Black Hole has one of the best Size scalings in the game: larger radius drastically increases control time and enemy density, which elevates any finisher you pair with. Guides and community runs repeatedly place it at the center of high‑score and time‑push strategies. Combining Black Hole with Dexecutioner (execute checks) or Katana (multi‑hit cones) is a mainstream, proven approach.
