‚öôÔ∏è Core Mechanics

Gravitational Pull System

Black Hole fires projectiles that create gravitational fields, pulling enemies toward a central point and clustering them for devastating AoE follow-up attacks. Enemies are grouped but NOT stunned - they continue attacking if you position poorly!

üí° Strategic Depth: Predictive placement matters more than reactive firing. Shoot where enemies WILL BE, not where they are. Think defensively - Black Holes create safe zones where enemies can't reach you.

Four Upgrade Paths

üìà Damage

Increases direct damage from 4 base. Low priority since crowd control is the main value.

‚è±Ô∏è Duration

CRITICAL! Base 2s requires tome investment. Keeps enemies grouped longer for combo execution.

üî¢ Projectile Count

Multiple Black Hole zones = screen-wide control. Essential for late-game swarms.

üìê Size

MANDATORY! "Size Tome is what makes this build function" - Martyr_Dom. Difference between 3 enemies vs 30.

üìú Version 1.0.12 Balance Changes (October 8, 2025)

‚úÖ Buffs

  • +20% Base Size: Compensatory buff addressing community complaints about ineffective pull radius
  • Duration Bug Fixes: Projectiles no longer stop prematurely. Duration Tome now scales consistently
  • Performance Optimization: "Significantly less FPS drop when combined with Dexecutioner" - no more slideshow gameplay

‚ùå Nerfs

  • Proc Coefficient: 0.9 ‚Üí 0.7 (30% reduction in item synergy effectiveness)
  • Hitbox Bug Fix: Eliminated map-wide coverage. Previously affected enemies across ENTIRE map regardless of projectile location
‚ö†Ô∏è Developer Quote: "Fixed a bug where the Black Hole hitboxes were like 10x the size of the actual projectile, basically covering the entire map lol." This "bug fix" was actually the largest effective nerf - Black Hole lost its unintentional god-tier status.

Net Result

Black Hole dropped from A-tier/low S-tier to solid B-tier with A-tier potential in specific builds. No longer a universal power pick but remains a respected specialist tool for crowd control enabler strategies.

üéì Advanced Strategies

üéØ Positioning Tactics

  • Think Defensively: Black Holes create safe zones where enemies can't reach you. Use them as shields!
  • Predictive Placement: Shoot where enemies WILL BE, not where they are. Lead your shots!
  • Corner/Wall Positioning: Limits enemy approach angles and concentrates Black Holes
  • Circular Movement: While kiting, place Black Holes in enemy pursuit paths
  • Choke Point Control: Excels at narrow passages where Black Hole forces clustering

‚è±Ô∏è Timing Strategies

  • Early Game (0-10min): Focus mobility between 7s cooldown shots. Black Hole alone won't carry damage
  • Mid Game (10-30min): Coordinate Black Hole shots with secondary weapon high-damage windows
  • Late Game (30min+): Maintain constant battlefield coverage through high Projectile Count

üëë Boss Fight Strategies

‚ö†Ô∏è CRITICAL BOSS WARNING: Final Boss deletes all weapons except your HIGHEST-LEVELED weapon. If Black Hole is highest level, you face final boss with only 4 base damage!

Solution: Keep a high-damage weapon (Dexecutioner, Katana, Revolver) as highest level throughout run!
  • Single Target Weakness: Low base damage (4) means secondary weapons must carry boss DPS
  • Add Control: Black Hole excels at controlling boss-spawned minions. Group adds for AoE clear
  • Positioning Over Damage: Use Black Hole to create safe zones during boss special attacks
