Combat Achievement Strategy

Most combat achievements require dedicated farming runs. Don't try to complete them all at once. Many can be combined (e.g., weapon kill counts while farming for character unlocks). The rarest achievement, Cactus (0.56%), requires specific builds and patience. Average completion time is 35-40 hours.

Cactus

Kill 100 cactus enemies with Thorns damage

0.56% Unlocked - RAREST
⚠️ Extreme Difficulty Warning
This is the RAREST achievement in the entire combat category (0.56%). Requires very specific build, location, and patience. Not recommended for casual completion.
🎯 Required Setup
  • Character: Athena (gains +2 Thorns per level)
  • Weapon: Aegis (provides thorns on block)
  • Location: Desert stages only (where cactus enemies spawn)
  • Strategy: Let cactus enemies attack you while Aegis blocks damage
  • Items: Stack all thorns-related items you can find
💡 Expert Strategy
You must first unlock Athena (Thorns Tome Level 9). Then unlock Aegis (block 500 damage as Sir Oofie). Play as Athena on Desert stages. Find areas with high cactus density. Stand still and let them attack you - Aegis will block and Thorns will reflect damage back to kill them. This takes multiple dedicated runs.
🌵 Required Cactus Build
🛡️ Aegis weapon (MUST HAVE - thorns on block)
⚡ Athena character (MUST HAVE - +2 thorns per level)
📚 Thorns Tome (max level for bonus damage)
💚 Health items (survive longer while tanking)
🦾 Armor items (reduce damage taken)

⚔️ Combat Achievement Mastery Tips

🎯 Combine Objectives
Many combat achievements can be worked on simultaneously. Farm goblin kills for Ogre while also working on Sword kills for Brass Knuckles and Dexecutioner.
📊 Track Your Progress
Use the kill counters on this page to manually track your progress between runs. Steam doesn't show partial progress for most kill count achievements.
🔄 Specialize Runs
Don't try to work on all combat achievements at once. Dedicate entire runs to specific goals. One run for Sword kills, another for thorns, etc.
⏰ Save Cactus for Last
The Cactus achievement (0.56%) requires extreme dedication. You need Athena unlocked, Aegis unlocked, and Desert stage access. Tackle this one last.
🔓 Unlock Priority
Unlock Banish (40 quests) and Toggler (buy 40 items) first. These meta-upgrades let you remove bad items from pools and optimize your builds for achievement runs.
🛡️ Walking Apocalypse Build
The Aura + Mirror item + Kevin item combo creates near-invincibility. Kevin is an item (not character) that works with Mirror for immortality. Master this for survival achievements.
