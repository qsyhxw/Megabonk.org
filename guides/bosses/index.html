💡 Universal Boss Strategies

🎯 Build for Your Starting Weapon

Tier 3 bosses disable secondary weapons. Focus all upgrades on your starting weapon for late-game viability.

🛡️ Survivability > Damage

Use Defense Tome, Shield Tome, or Health/Armor Tomes. You can't DPS if you're dead.

⚡ The Holy Trinity

XP Tome + Curse Tome + Luck Tome is the meta combo for all boss fights. Increases power scaling exponentially.

🎲 Boss Curse Shrines

Spawns extra bosses but gives extra reward chests. Use strategically for achievements like "Speedboi".

💨 Speed is Life

Movement Speed helps you dodge attacks, destroy pylons (Bark Vader), and collect XP faster.

🔄 Don't Go Wide

Don't level multiple weapons equally. Deep investment in one weapon beats spreading upgrades thin.
