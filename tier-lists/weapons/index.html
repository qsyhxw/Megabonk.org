📊 How We Rank Weapons

Rankings are based on community consensus from competitive leaderboard data, multiple tier list sources (IGN, GameRant, Destructoid, Reddit), and analysis of build synergies. Weapon effectiveness varies by character and build — use this as a starting point, not gospel.

S

S-Tier — Meta Dominance

Best-in-class weapons for competitive play. These define the current meta and appear in nearly every leaderboard run.

Aura
Area Damage
Emits a constant 360° damage field around the player. No aiming required — just walk into enemies.
🔓 Unlock: Don't take any damage for 2 consecutive minutes during a run
Best with: Sir Oofie Size builds AFK setups
Aegis
Defense + Offense
Shield weapon that blocks incoming damage while dealing damage to attackers. Unrivaled balance of offense and defense.
🔓 Unlock: Block 500 damage with Armor as Sir Oofie
Best with: Sir Oofie Thorns builds Tank setups
Dexecutioner
Melee Piercing
Close-range piercing stab that scales with Size, Crit Chance, and Damage. Executes damaged targets instantly.
🔓 Unlock: Kill 12,500 enemies using the Sword
Best with: Bandit Crit builds Attack Speed
Flamewalker
Trail AoEDEFAULT
Leaves a trail of fire behind you. With cooldown reduction and attack speed, becomes a moving inferno that melts everything.
🟢 Default: Available from the start
Best with: Cooldown builds Speed builds AoE setups
Katana
Melee Single-Target
Targets the nearest enemy with fast, devastating slashes. Becomes incredibly strong with increased Attack Speed and Projectiles.
🔓 Unlock: 5% chance from breaking a Tumbleweed on Desert Stage 1
Best with: Attack Speed Evasion builds Projectile stacking
Dice
RNG Multiplier
High base crit chance with RNG multipliers. Lucky rolls permanently increase crit chance, creating snowball potential.
🔓 Unlock: Get the Luck Tome to Level 12
Best with: Dicehead Luck builds Crit stacking
Blood Magic
Lifesteal Burst
Targets a specific enemy within range, dealing solid instant damage. Converts health into devastating area damage in long runs.
🔓 Unlock: Get the Bloody Tome to Level 12
Best with: Vlad HP stacking Lifesteal builds

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best weapon in Megabonk?
Aura, Aegis, and Dexecutioner are consistently ranked as the top S-tier weapons across multiple tier lists and community sources. Aura is particularly dominant for its passive 360° damage field that requires no aiming.
How many weapons are in Megabonk?
Megabonk has 29 weapons total. 7 are available by default (Flamewalker, Sword, Lightning Staff, Firestaff, Chunkers, Bone, and Bow), while the remaining 22 require specific unlock conditions.
How do you unlock the Katana in Megabonk?
The Katana has a 5% chance to drop from breaking a Tumbleweed on Desert Stage 1. Since it's RNG-based, it may take multiple attempts. It's one of the strongest S-tier melee weapons once unlocked.
What is the easiest S-tier weapon to get?
Flamewalker is available from the very start and sits in S-tier. For unlockable weapons, Blood Magic (Bloody Tome Level 12) and Dice (Luck Tome Level 12) are relatively easy to unlock during normal gameplay.
Were any weapons changed in the latest patch?
In v1.0.64 (January 2026), Bone and Shotgun both received knockback buffs and had their knockback upgrade options replaced with Crit Chance/Damage upgrades, making them more offensively viable.

📰 Recent Weapon Balance Changes
