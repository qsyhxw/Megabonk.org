Overview

The Cooldown Tome boosts your character's Attack Speed, a global stat that reduces the time between attacks for all weapons. This makes projectile weapons fire faster and melee weapons swing more rapidly, directly increasing your damage output and on-hit effect triggers.

According to v1.0.7 community documentation, Tome upgrades stack additively with each level providing the same percentage increase. Different rarity tiers apply multipliers (Common baseline, with Uncommon at 1.2×, Rare at 1.4×, Epic at 1.6×, and Legendary at 2.0×) to the base +7.5% per level value.

Version Note: Data based on v1.0.7 (September-October 2025) community guides and technical documentation. Official patch notes may supersede this information.

How Attack Speed Works

Attack Speed is a character-level stat that affects all equipped weapons, reducing their attack intervals universally. This makes it one of the most versatile offensive upgrades in the game.

  • Universal Application: Affects every weapon you use during a run
  • Additive Scaling: Each level adds the same percentage increase (not multiplicative or exponential)
  • Rarity Matters: Higher rarity Cooldown Tomes provide larger boosts per level
  • Immediate Impact: Attack speed changes are noticeable from the first upgrade

Synergy Guide

🩸 Lifesteal Synergy
Lifesteal in Megabonk triggers per hit (with >100% lifesteal giving additional chances). More attacks per second means more healing triggers, making this combination extremely powerful for sustain.
💥 Damage Tome
Complements attack speed by increasing per-hit damage. Together they provide both higher attack frequency and harder-hitting attacks for superior DPS.
🎯 Precision Tome
Higher attack speed means more chances to trigger critical hits. Great synergy for crit-focused builds that benefit from frequent attacks.
🔢 Quantity Tome
More projectiles per shot combined with faster shooting creates screen-filling bullet patterns. Excellent for projectile-heavy builds.
💨 Agility Tome
Movement speed pairs well with attack speed for kiting playstyles, allowing you to maintain offense while repositioning constantly.
🎲 On-Hit Items
Items with on-hit effects (like Kevin, Mirror, status applications) benefit greatly from increased attack frequency since they trigger more often.

Strategy Guide

  • Early Priority: Often recommended as an early Tome investment due to immediate DPS improvements
  • Universal Value: Works effectively with virtually every weapon and playstyle
  • On-Hit Builds: Essential for builds relying on on-hit effects like Kevin+Mirror combinations
  • Healing Synergy: Significantly increases healing rate when combined with lifesteal items
  • Build Foundation: Core component of popular community builds like "Speed Demon" and "Bullet Hell"

Important Considerations

  • Balance Defense: High attack speed without sustain or defensive stats may leave you vulnerable
  • Resource Management: Faster attacking may require more careful positioning and resource awareness
  • Complement with Survivability: Consider pairing with defensive Tomes like Health, Armor, or Evasion for balanced progression
  • Movement Speed Balance: Pair with Agility to maintain kiting capability
