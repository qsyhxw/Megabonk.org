Megabonk Best Tomes Tier List (2026 Guide)

Tomes form the foundation of your power scaling in Megabonk. Picking the right combination of passives drastically impacts whether you survive the final waves or get overwhelmed. In our updated Megabonk Tomes Tier List, we rank every tome based on versatility, exponential scaling, and late-game impact. We also provide the exact unlock conditions for hidden tomes. (Based on version 1.0.64)

Tomes Tier List Rankings

S

S-Tier (Must-Picks & Global Scaling)

Cooldown Tome

The undisputed king of scaling. Reducing your attack cooldowns means faster wave clears, more procs for on-hit effects, and overwhelming DPS.

Default Unlock

Quantity Tome

Fires additional projectiles or multiplies attack instances. It directly doubles or triples your damage output for almost every weapon in the game.

Unlock: Fire 5,000 Projectiles

Damage Tome

A pure, raw damage boost. Fundamental for ensuring your weapons can keep up with the scaling health pools of late-game elites and bosses.

Default Unlock

XP Tome

Accelerates experience gain, allowing you to hit weapon power spikes much earlier. Essential for snowballing hard before minute 15.

Default Unlock

Luck Tome

Boosts the chance of rare item drops and high-tier weapon upgrades. Critical for pushing high scores and perfecting your loadout.

Default Unlock

HP Tome

Significantly increases your maximum health pool. The best flat-value defensive buffer to prevent fatal one-shots.

Default Unlock
A

A-Tier (Strong & Very Flexible)

Bloody Tome

Provides life steal on hit. The premier sustain tool that keeps you alive through intense chip damage in long runs.

Unlock: Kill 5,000 Enemies

Precision Tome

Increases critical strike chance. Becomes heavily potent when combined with high fire-rate weapons and overcrit mechanics.

Default Unlock

Agility Tome

Boosts movement speed. Crucial for outmaneuvering boss patterns, dodging projectiles, and escaping encirclements.

Default Unlock

Size Tome

Massively expands AoE indicators, projectiles, and explosions. Phenomenal for crowd-clearing weapons.

Default Unlock

Armor Tome

Flat damage reduction. Pairs exceptionally well with HP and regeneration to make your character a walking fortress.

Unlock: Take 5,000 Damage in one run
B

B-Tier (Situational & Mid-Tier)

Regen Tome

Provides passive healing over time. Less reliable than Bloody Tome for aggressive play, but an okay substitute.

Shield Tome

Generates a buffer shield that stops damage. Breaking it requires avoiding damage for a short duration to let it recharge. Good for slower builds.

Golden Tome

A farming tome that boosts coin drops. Great for account progression but provides zero combat value mid-run.

C/D

C/D-Tier (Niche or Farming Focused)

These tomes should generally be avoided unless you are doing a specific challenge or farming for unlocks.

  • Attraction Tome: Increases pickup radius. Gets outclassed by passive pet items.
  • Silver Tome: Yields more silver currency. Pointless once the talent tree is finished.
  • Duration Tome: Modifies weapon lifespan. Very few weapons actually benefit effectively from this in late game.
  • Thorns Tome: Returns damage upon getting hit. Requires you to take damage, which makes it weak on high difficulty.
  • Chaos Tome: Random stat changes are too unpredictable to base a run on.
  • Cursed Tome: Actively buffs enemies to increase challenge. Only for hardcore masochists.

How to Unlock Hidden Tomes

While many tomes are granted to you natively at the start of the game, Megabonk hides some of its most powerful options behind cumulative gameplay challenges. Here is a cheat sheet to unlock them all:

Tome Unlock Requirement
Quantity Tome Fire a cumulative total of 5,000 projectiles across all runs.
Bloody Tome Kill 5,000 enemies total across your account.
Armor Tome Take 5,000 points of damage over the course of a single run.
Attraction Tome Collect 5,000 XP Orbs total.
Duration Tome Deal 50,000 total damage.
Chaos Tome Reroll level-up weapon/tome offers 100 times.
Thorns Tome Block 250 attacks using the Aegis weapon.
Cursed Tome Survive for exactly 15 minutes in a single run.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best tomes in Megabonk?

The absolute best S-Tier tomes are the Cooldown Tome, Quantity Tome, Damage Tome, XP Tome, HP Tome, and Luck Tome. These scale exponentially and fit in almost every character build by multiplying your damage output and survivability.

How do I unlock the Quantity Tome?

You can unlock the Quantity Tome by firing a total of 5,000 projectiles. This carries over between runs, so playing characters with fast-firing multi-projectile weapons will unlock it extremely quickly.

How do you unlock the Bloody Tome (Lifesteal)?

The Bloody Tome is unlocked globally for your account once you accumulate a total of 5,000 enemy kills throughout your gameplay.
