⚔️ Core Weapons & Items

The three pillars of the Walking Apocalypse build

💪
Aura
Primary Weapon

Unlock: Survive 2 minutes without taking damage

Surrounds you with a damage-dealing aura that grows with your character size. The foundation of this build. Scales incredibly well and hits all enemies around you automatically.

✓ Prioritize size-increasing items to maximize Aura range
✓ Damage scales per level, making it stronger throughout the run
🪞
Mirror
Force Multiplier

Unlock: Default unlock

Creates a duplicate of yourself that mirrors your movements and attacks. Essentially doubles your Aura coverage and damage output. Game-changing item for AFK builds.

✓ Mirror clone inherits all your weapons and stats
✓ Position strategically to cover more ground
👤
Kevin
Immortality Core

Unlock: Found in game

THE most broken item in Megabonk. Grants near-immortality through insane regeneration. Combined with Mirror, you become virtually unkillable. Absolutely essential for true AFK gameplay.

✓ Kevin + Mirror = Two sources of immortality
✓ Allows you to stand in boss attacks without worry

📚 Recommended Tomes

Maximize your power with these tome selections

📖
XP Tome
Priority #1

The foundation of every meta build. Faster leveling means more stat bonuses, stronger weapons, and quicker power spikes. Always take this first.

📕
Curse Tome
High Risk, High Reward

Increases enemy spawn rate and difficulty but grants massive stat bonuses. With Mirror + Kevin immortality, the downside is negligible while the benefits are huge.

📘
Luck Tome
Holy Trinity Completion

Better item drops and increased crit chance. Combined with XP and Curse tomes, forms the "Holy Trinity" that every meta build uses.

🎮 Gameplay Strategy

Phase 1: Early Game (0-10 minutes)

Foundation Building

Priority Goals:

  • Unlock Aura if you haven't already (2 min no damage)
  • Pick up XP Tome as soon as possible
  • Focus on survivability items and size increases
  • Avoid risky plays - stay safe to build your foundation
💡 Move in circles to maximize Aura damage coverage
💡 Don't worry about damage yet - focus on not dying
Phase 2: Mid Game (10-20 minutes)

Power Spike

Key Actions:

  • Obtain Mirror - this is your biggest power spike
  • Add Curse Tome to increase spawn rate
  • Get Kevin for immortality if available
  • Level up Aura to maximum damage
💡 Once you have Mirror, you can handle 2x enemies easily
💡 Position mirror clone to cover a different area
Phase 3: Late Game (20+ minutes)

True AFK Mode

Optimization:

  • You should have: Aura + Mirror + Kevin + 3 Tomes
  • Add Luck Tome for the Holy Trinity
  • Stack size and damage modifiers
  • Literally AFK - the build plays itself
💡 You can leave your keyboard at this point
💡 Stand near boss spawn points to kill them instantly
