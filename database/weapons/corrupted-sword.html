⚙️ Corrupted Sword Mechanics
Understanding dark power
⚔️ Corruption Strike
Each sword strike inflicts corruption damage over time. Corrupted enemies take escalating damage and spread corruption to nearby targets.
Corruption Duration: 8-12 seconds
Damage Type: Escalating DOT
Spread Range: Medium radius around target
🌑 Shadow Wave
Unleashes waves of dark energy that pierce through enemies. Wave size and damage increase based on corruption stacks active on the battlefield.
Wave Pattern: Expanding dark energy
Pierce: Unlimited enemy penetration
Scaling: Based on active corruption stacks
💀 Soul Harvest
When corrupted enemies die, harvests their souls to temporarily boost weapon power. More corrupted enemies = stronger soul harvest effects.
Soul Duration: 15-20 seconds
Damage Boost: Stacking per soul harvested
Max Stacks: No limit (theoretically)
👑 Sir Chadwell Synergy
Perfect synergy with Sir Chadwell's corruption mastery. Character abilities enhance sword effectiveness and provide additional dark powers.
Character Unlock: Requires Forest Tier 3
Synergy Rating: Perfect (100%)
Dark Powers: Enhanced corruption effects

🧠 Advanced Corrupted Sword Strategies
Master the dark arts
💀 Corruption Timing
Don't kill corrupted enemies immediately. Let corruption spread to maximize the number of affected targets before triggering soul harvest.
⚔️ Soul Harvest Windows
Time soul harvests during high enemy density. Multiple corrupted deaths in quick succession create exponential power scaling.
🌑 Shadow Wave Chaining
Use shadow waves when corruption stacks are highest. More corrupted enemies = stronger waves that can clear entire screens.
👑 Endgame Scaling
Corrupted Sword becomes exponentially stronger in late game. Focus on survival until corruption mechanics reach critical mass.
⚡ Risk Management
High-power corruption builds are glass cannons. Invest in survivability items to survive long enough for scaling to kick in.
🎯 Target Prioritization
Corrupt high-health enemies first, then spread to groups. Elite corrupted enemies provide better soul harvest rewards.
