🎮 Gameplay Strategy

Phase 1: Early Game (0-10 minutes)

Building Speed Foundation

Priority Goals:

  • Unlock Calcium if you haven't (kill 1,000 skeletons)
  • Get Speed Boots ASAP - this is your #1 priority
  • Pick up XP Tome for faster leveling
  • Focus on NOT getting hit - preserve your speed stacks
  • Practice kiting patterns in circles
💡 Keep moving constantly - your speed builds passively
💡 Early game is weak - play defensive until you get speed items
Phase 2: Mid Game (10-20 minutes)

Speed Ramping

Key Actions:

  • Stack multiple Speed Boots if possible (2-3 is ideal)
  • Add Lightning Staff for crowd clear while kiting
  • Get Dash ability for safety and mobility
  • Speed Tome is mandatory at this point
  • Your damage should be ramping up significantly now
💡 You should be noticeably faster than enemies now
💡 Master dash timing - it's your lifeline
Phase 3: Late Game (20+ minutes)

Maximum Overdrive

Optimization:

  • Speed should be 300%+ at this point
  • You're moving so fast enemies can barely touch you
  • Damage output is insane due to speed conversion
  • Circle strafe everything - bosses included
  • Haste Potion for emergency situations only
💡 Control can be difficult - reduce mouse sensitivity if needed
💡 One hit = 50% speed loss = massive damage drop

💡 Advanced Tips & Tricks

🎯
Perfect Kiting

Move in wide circles around enemy groups. Your speed lets you outrun everything while your weapons do the damage. Never stop moving!

🛡️
Dash Management

Save dashes for unavoidable attacks or boss abilities. The i-frames are crucial for maintaining your speed multiplier. Don't waste them!

⚡
Speed Recovery

If you take damage, immediately retreat and kite enemies at maximum distance. Your speed will rebuild over ~10 seconds of safe movement.
