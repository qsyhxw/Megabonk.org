🏜️ DESERT STAGE 1 ONLY
Find and break tumbleweeds on Desert Stage 1. Each has a 5% chance to drop Katana. Once it drops, purchase with Silver in Unlocks/Shop.
1) Enter the Desert map and select Stage 1 (first area of any Desert Tier).
2) Approach rolling tumbleweeds and interact to break them.
3) If you continue to Stage 2, the tumbleweed drop table changes (e.g., Shotgun).
4) After Katana drops once, buy it with Silver to add permanently.

💡 Note: No item or stat modifies the 5% drop chance; it's pure RNG.

What it does & how it scales
🎯 Targeting & Damage
Katana auto-targets the closest enemy and has 11 base damage. It shines in single-target DPS, especially with Crit-focused upgrades.
Targeting: Closest enemy
Base DMG: 11
Type: Melee slashes (primarily single-target)
📚 Upgrade Pool
Rolls among Damage, Crit Chance, Crit Damage, Projectile Count, Size. Quantity/Size increase coverage and multi-hits; Damage/Crit scale burst.
Best tomes: Cooldown, Quantity, Damage, Crit Chance/DMG, then Size

Ninja starts with Katana, gains +0.5% Evasion/level, and can execute enemies on successful evades—synergizing with aggressive, mobile play.
Playstyle: Bait attacks → evade → counter
