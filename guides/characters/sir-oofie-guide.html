Character Overview

Master the art of defensive combat with Sir Oofie's armor scaling

üåü Passive Ability: Armor Per Level

Sir Oofie gains 1% Armor per level, making him incredibly tanky:

  • Level 10: +10% Armor (early game survivability boost)
  • Level 20: +20% Armor (mid-game tankiness)
  • Level 50: +50% Armor (significant damage reduction)
  • Level 100: +100% Armor value (approximately 57% damage reduction due to hyperbolic scaling)

This passive synergizes perfectly with the Armor Tome and defensive items, making Sir Oofie the easiest character to build for new players.

  • Consistent Performance: Reliable for loot farming and character unlocks

  • Basic Playstyle: Less exciting than characters with unique mechanics

Sir Oofie Progression Guide

From novice knight to reliable tank

1

Early Levels (1-10)

Priority: Survive early waves and establish baseline defense

  • Upgrade Sword to level 3-4 for consistent damage output
  • Take any Armor or Max Health items when offered
  • Learn sword combat timing and effective range
  • Stay mobile - don't get surrounded by enemies
  • Your armor passive is still weak - play cautiously until level 10+
2

Mid Levels (10-30)

Priority: Armor passive becomes noticeable, add tomes

  • 10-30% armor bonus starts making significant difference
  • Add Armor Tome for multiplicative armor stacking
  • Consider adding Thorns Tome for passive damage
  • Prioritize defensive items to synergize with passive
  • Can play more aggressively due to increased survivability
  • Begin working toward character unlock goals
3

Advanced Levels (30-60)

Priority: True tank playstyle with 30-60% armor bonus

  • 30-60% armor from passive plus tomes/items = 70-80% damage reduction
  • Can now tank in the middle of large enemy groups safely
  • Add offensive weapons like Axe or damage-boosting tomes
  • Master positioning to maximize sword and area damage
  • Focus on completing character unlock requirements
4

Master Levels (60-100+)

Priority: Peak tankiness with 60-100%+ armor bonus

  • 60-100%+ armor makes you extremely difficult to kill
  • Can facetank boss attacks and massive swarms
  • Focus on optimizing damage output while maintaining defense
  • Excellent for farming late-game character unlocks
  • Note: Other characters may perform better in endgame competitive content

‚öîÔ∏è Sword Combat Tips

Master these techniques for effective sword combat:

  • Optimal Range: Stay at sword's maximum reach to hit without taking unnecessary damage
  • Area Damage: Use sword's wide arc to hit multiple enemies per swing
  • Crowd Management: Position yourself to maximize hits on grouped enemies
  • Boss Strategy: Circle bosses to stay at their sides, avoiding frontal attacks
  • Aggressive Tanking: Use your high armor to position aggressively for better DPS

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes

Avoid these pitfalls when playing Sir Oofie:

  • Ignoring Armor Items: Your passive multiplies armor effectiveness - always prioritize armor
  • Playing Too Safe Early: You're tankier than you think by level 20+
  • Pure Defense: You still need damage to clear waves - don't neglect offensive upgrades
  • Standing Still: Even tanks need to move and position properly
  • Using Only Sword: Add Aegis, Axe, or tomes for more versatile builds

  • Weapons: Sword, Aegis, Axe, Bone, Katana, Revolver, etc. (29 total)
