üîì How to Unlock Snek

Snek is hidden in a secret room within the Crypt area of the Graveyard map. Unlike most items, you don't need to complete a challenge‚Äîjust find and reach the secret.

üêç Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Enter the Crypt: Access the Crypt area in the Graveyard map. You'll need 4 Crypt Keys.
  2. Find the Secret Room: Look for a room with a "high-low" layout‚Äîplatforms in the middle with a significant drop. Check for a room with a huge vertical drop-off.
  3. Activate the Pressure Plate: On the lower platform, locate the pressure plate. Step on it and listen for the activation sound.
  4. Jump into the Lava: After activating the plate, a hidden passage opens in the lava. Jump in at the correct spot and follow the tunnel.
  5. Collect Snek: Interact with the snake at the end of the tunnel. It goes directly into your inventory!
üí° Pro Tip

The snake's location often mirrors the layout. Check the minimap‚ÄîSnek is usually on the side with the high treasure chest. Characters with low HP (like Clank at 50 HP) may struggle in the lava, so use a tankier character for easier unlocking.

‚öôÔ∏è Mechanics Deep Dive

Poison Stack Application

Each time you hit an enemy, Snek applies 4 poison stacks. These stacks deal damage over time and can be amplified by other poison-related effects.

Synergy with Lifesteal

At 100% lifesteal, each poison tick heals you for 1 HP. With 4 stacks ticking simultaneously on multiple enemies, you can achieve absurd sustain levels, making you nearly unkillable in crowded situations.

Best Characters for Snek

  • Amog: Native poison synergy with "Plague" passive causes poisoned enemies to explode and spread poison on death.
  • Steve / Big Bob: Starting a run with Snek as an extra legendary gives massive early advantage.
  • Any lifesteal character: Combine with lifesteal items for incredible sustain.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do you unlock Snek in Megabonk?
Find the secret room in the Crypt area. Look for a high-low room with platforms, step on the pressure plate on the lower level, then jump into the lava to enter the hidden tunnel where Snek awaits.
What does Snek do?
Snek applies 4 stacks of poison to enemies every time you hit them, creating massive damage-over-time potential.
Is Snek worth getting?
Absolutely! Snek is considered S-tier for poison builds and remains useful in almost any build due to the strong DoT damage and lifesteal synergy.
Can I start a run with Snek?
Once unlocked, Snek can appear in the item pool during runs. Some players "bridge" the Snek discovery into a run with Steve or Big Bob for an early legendary advantage.
