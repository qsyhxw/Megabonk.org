Frequently Asked Questions

Is Campfire unlocked from the start?

Yes. Campfire is available by default.

Does Campfire heal while moving?

No. Its effect only applies while you are standing still.

Can Campfire ever harm me?

Community reports indicate that if your health regen is negative, staying still may drain HP instead of healing. This behavior is not officially confirmed; use with caution.
