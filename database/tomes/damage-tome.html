The Damage Tome is one of the most straightforward but powerful Tomes in Megabonk. It boosts the base damage of your attacks, making every hit stronger. Since it multiplies with other effects, it's a core part of nearly every offensive build and provides consistent scaling throughout your entire run.

Overview

  • Stat: Damage
  • Base upgrade per level: +0.08x (+8% per level)
  • How to unlock: Default (available at start)
  • Max level: 99

Damage is the most fundamental offensive stat. The Damage Tome directly increases the damage dealt by all attacks, providing universal benefit across melee, ranged, and AoE weapons. It's essential for scaling your power as runs progress.

Effects

  • Directly increases the damage dealt by all attacks
  • Works with melee, ranged, and AoE weapons universally
  • Provides strong scaling when combined with critical chance or attack speed
  • Multiplies with other damage modifiers for exponential growth
  • At max level (99), provides nearly 800% base damage increase

This Tome is available from the start of the game. No special condition is needed.

Synergies

Precision Tome
Critical hits deal more when base damage is higher. Essential synergy for crit-focused builds.
Cooldown Tome
Faster attacks combined with more damage = massive DPS. The ultimate offensive combo.
Quantity Tome
More projectiles each benefit from the damage boost. Multiplies your total damage output.
Size Tome
Larger hitboxes plus more damage make AoE clear stronger. Perfect for crowd control builds.
Knockback Tome
Higher damage makes knockback more effective, creating space and controlling enemy positioning.
Duration Tome
Longer-lasting damage-over-time effects benefit from higher base damage values.

Strategy Guide

  • A must-have for most offensive builds - Core DPS foundation
  • Best to invest early for snowballing damage as runs go longer
  • Combine with defense Tomes if you're struggling to survive while scaling damage
  • Works universally with every weapon type - never a wasted investment
  • Scales exponentially with other damage multipliers like crits and attack speed
  • Essential for meta builds and leaderboard runs

Why Damage Tome is Essential

  • Considered one of the strongest "always useful" Tomes
  • High synergy with almost every offensive Tome in the game
  • Simple, straightforward damage boost with no complex mechanics
  • Scales consistently from early to late game
  • Core component of virtually all competitive character builds
  • Provides immediate, noticeable power increase at all levels

Pro Tips

  • Prioritize Damage Tome early to maximize scaling throughout your run
  • Pairs with Luck Tome to increase legendary item drops that boost damage further
  • Balance damage investment with survivability - raw damage won't help if you can't stay alive
  • Each level provides 8% more damage - stacks multiplicatively with itself
  • Part of the "Holy Trinity" build when combined with Luck and XP Tomes
