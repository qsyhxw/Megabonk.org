Tactical Glasses

How Tactical Glasses Works

Tactical Glasses adds a conditional damage bonus that only applies to “healthy” targets. The +20% modifier is checked when the enemy’s current HP is above 90%, rewarding strong openers and precision shots.

  • Conditional bonus: +20% damage, only while the target’s HP > 90%.
  • Unlock quest: defeat a Miniboss within 25 seconds from engagement.

Sources: DualShockers, Destructoid, GameRant, ProGameGuides (see source list below).

Practical Uses & Clean Synergies

This item shines when you can front-load damage to start fights. Consider pairing with high burst or execute-style weapons and builds for reliable first-hit pressure.

Sniper Rifle

High opening shot damage to capitalize on the >90% HP window.

Dexecutioner

Execute-centric melee that appreciates early chunking.

Build: Execute Melee

Hero Sword

Fast openers help clear elites before they scale.

Unlock Guide

  1. Queue into a run and locate the next Miniboss arena/event.
  2. Burn down the Miniboss within 25 seconds to complete the quest.
