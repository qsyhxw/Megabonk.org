Step-by-Step Build Guide

1

Early Game (Levels 1-5)

Focus on Lightning Bolt levels 1-3. Take any Movement Speed or Mana Regen items. Avoid other weapons for now - you want to concentrate your upgrades.

2

Mid Game Setup (Levels 6-10)

Add Thunder Cloud levels 1-4. Start looking for Lightning Damage and Chain Range items. Continue leveling Lightning Bolt to 5-6.

3

Evolution Time (Levels 11-15)

Max Lightning Bolt to level 6, then max Thunder Cloud to level 4. This triggers the Storm Lord evolution - your power spike moment!

4

Support Weapons (Levels 16-20)

Add Static Field for area control. Consider Wind Blade for mobility or Energy Ball for finishing enemies. Focus on items that boost your lightning damage.

5

Late Game Dominance (Level 20+)

You should be clearing entire screens. Focus on survival items like Health Regen and Movement Speed. Look for rare items that boost spell effects.
