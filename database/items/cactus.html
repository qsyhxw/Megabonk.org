🧪 How Cactus Works

Cactus turns taking damage into offense: whenever you’re hit, it emits spikes whose damage scales with your current Thorns value. This naturally rewards close-range, defensive play.

Mechanics

  • Triggers on incoming hits to you (contact/projectiles).
  • Spike damage increases with your Thorns (from tomes/items).
  • Best on builds that can safely take chip damage while retaliating.

🔓 How to Unlock Cactus

  1. Run the Desert (Tier 1) map where cactus enemies spawn.
  2. Ensure you have Thorns Tome online; let Thorns deal the killing blow.
  3. Repeat until you accumulate 100 cactus kills by Thorns in total.

Player-tested routes include resetting if cacti don’t appear early and stacking defensive stats to survive while Thorns does the job.
