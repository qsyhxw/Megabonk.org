🔓 How to Unlock Ogre (Corrected Process)

Ogre requires dedication but offers incredible scaling once obtained. Here's the complete, accurate unlock process:

⚠️ Important: The unlock process has THREE separate requirements, not two. You need to unlock the Axe weapon first before you can purchase Ogre.

📋 Complete Unlock Requirements

  1. Kill 2,000 enemies with the Sword weapon to unlock the Axe weapon
  2. Kill 15,000 Goblins across all your runs (cumulative counter)
  3. Purchase Ogre from the Unlocks tab using Silver currency

💡 Efficient Unlock Strategy

Work on both requirements simultaneously! Use a Sword-wielding character to farm Goblins in Forest Tier 1. This way you'll progress toward both the 2,000 Sword kills AND the 15,000 Goblin kills at the same time.

Farm Forest Tier 1 exclusively for goblins. Use AOE characters and weapons to kill goblins quickly. Don't worry about completing runs - focus purely on goblin kills per hour. Reset runs when goblin density drops.

🎯 Advanced Strategies

📈 Understanding Warrior Passive Scaling

The Warrior passive's power curve:

  • Level 10: +10% damage (110% total)
  • Level 25: +25% damage (125% total)
  • Level 50: +50% damage (150% total) - Major power spike
  • Level 100: +100% damage (200% total) - Double damage
  • Level 150+: +150%+ damage (250%+ total) - God mode

💡 Multiplicative Scaling

The Warrior passive is MULTIPLICATIVE with other damage bonuses. A +50% damage item at level 50 gives: Base × 1.5 (items) × 1.5 (passive) = 225% total damage!

⚔️ Axe Weapon Mechanics

  • Projectile Type: Throws spinning axes in an arc
  • AOE Damage: Hits multiple enemies per throw
  • Range: Medium-range weapon (70 base range)
  • Unlock Cost: 2,000 enemy kills with Sword weapon

🎮 General Tips

  • Be patient early game - Ogre is weak before level 20
  • Focus on surviving until level 50 when scaling becomes powerful
  • Prioritize damage multipliers over flat damage increases
  • Use positioning to maximize Axe's AOE potential
  • Don't neglect some survivability - dead Ogres deal no damage
