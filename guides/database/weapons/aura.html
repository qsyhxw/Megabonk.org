üîì How to Unlock Aura

Aura requires one of the more challenging unlock conditions: surviving 2 consecutive minutes without taking any damage. Here's the proven strategy:

1
Choose the Right Character
Sir Oofie is ideal due to his armor blessing. Fox with luck can also work for better item RNG.
2
Start Conservative
Focus on movement speed and ranged weapons. Avoid risky positioning and enemy dense areas.
3
Use Safe Weapons
Lightning Staff, Bow, or Revolver work well. Avoid melee weapons that require close positioning.
4
Priority Items
Movement speed, armor, damage reduction, and health regeneration. Stay mobile and defensive.
5
Timer Management
The 2 minutes must be consecutive. If you take damage, the timer resets. Play extremely safe during the attempt.

üí° Pro Unlock Strategy

Use Forest Tier 1 for the unlock attempt. Enemy spawn rate is predictable, and there's plenty of room to kite. Avoid Desert maps due to unpredictable enemy spawns and environmental hazards.

üéØ Advanced Aura Strategies

üîÑ Multi-Aura Stacking

The most powerful Aura builds use multiple Aura weapons:

  • 2-3 Auras: Balanced approach with good coverage
  • 4-5 Auras: Maximum power, screen-wide destruction
  • 6+ Auras: Overkill territory, but incredibly satisfying

üéÆ Positioning Mastery

Even with Aura's passive nature, positioning matters:

  • Center Control: Stay in areas with maximum enemy spawns
  • Corner Avoidance: Don't get trapped against walls
  • Boss Positioning: Keep moving during boss fights
  • Swarm Management: Position for maximum AoE overlap

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes

  • Neglecting Size: Aura without size scaling is underwhelming
  • Standing Still: Even with Aura, you need to move and position
  • Ignoring Other Weapons: Aura alone isn't enough for high-level play
  • Poor Tome Priority: Size > Damage > Cooldown > Everything else

üèÜ Mastery Goal
