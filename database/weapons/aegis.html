üîì How to Unlock Aegis

Aegis requires blocking exactly 500 damage with Armor while playing as Sir Oofie. This is much easier than it sounds with the right approach:

1
Play as Sir Oofie
Sir Oofie gains +1% armor per level, making him ideal for this challenge. His sword is also decent for early game.
2
Focus on Armor Items
Prioritize any armor-giving items in your runs. Even small amounts add up over time.
3
Let Enemies Hit You
Don't be overly defensive - you need to take hits for armor to block damage. Just don't let your HP drop too low.
4
Track Your Progress
The game tracks blocked damage across all runs. It typically takes 2-3 dedicated runs to reach 500.
5
Purchase After Unlock
Once you've blocked 500 damage, return to the main menu and purchase Aegis for 1 Silver.

üí° Pro Unlock Strategy

Use Forest Tier 1 where enemies deal consistent but manageable damage. Avoid Desert maps during unlock attempts as the damage can be more unpredictable and spike-heavy.

üîß Unlock Tips & Troubleshooting

‚ùì Common Unlock Issues

Q: I'm not blocking enough damage, what's wrong?
A: Make sure you're playing as Sir Oofie specifically. Other characters don't count for this unlock. Also ensure you have some armor items equipped.
Q: How do I know how much damage I've blocked?
A: Unfortunately, the game doesn't show a counter. You'll get a notification when you hit 500. Usually takes 2-3 focused runs.
Q: Does armor from items count or just Sir Oofie's passive?
A: All armor counts! Items like armor pickups, defensive tomes, and Sir Oofie's passive all contribute to blocked damage.
Q: Can I lose progress if I die?
A: No! Blocked damage progress is persistent across all runs and deaths. Each bit of damage blocked counts toward the 500 total.
