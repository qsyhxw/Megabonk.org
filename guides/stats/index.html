üõ°Ô∏è Player Stats

Player stats determine your character's survivability and defensive capabilities. These stats can be increased through items, tomes, and character abilities.

‚ù§Ô∏è

Max HP

The maximum amount of HP your character has. This is your health pool that determines how much damage you can take before dying.

How to Increase: Use Oats, Demonic Blood, or the HP Tome to boost your maximum health.
üíö

HP Regen

Health passively regenerated per minute. This provides constant healing throughout your run without requiring any action.

Best Source: The Regen Tome significantly increases your passive health regeneration, making it excellent for tanky builds.
üíñ

Overheal

Allows you to have extra health equal to a percentage of your normal HP. This temporary health layer provides additional survivability.

Key Item: Chonkplate is the primary source of overheal, making it valuable for extending your effective health pool.
üõ°Ô∏è

Shield

A protective barrier that absorbs damage before it affects your health. Shields recharge after 5 seconds of not taking damage.

Pro Tip: Even a small shield (5 points) can prevent one-shot kills since a single damage instance cannot penetrate both shield and HP. Use the Shield Tome to increase this stat.
üî∞

Armor

Provides percentage-based damage reduction. Armor increases are additive with diminishing returns, meaning each additional point of armor becomes less effective.

Important: It's impossible to reach 100% armor and negate all damage due to diminishing returns. Balance armor with other defensive stats for optimal survivability.
How to Get: Use the Armor Tome or defensive items to increase this stat.
üí®

Evasion

A percentage chance to completely avoid incoming damage. Like armor, evasion has diminishing returns and cannot reach 100%.

Character Synergy: Ninja excels with evasion, as his passive ability allows him to execute enemies after successfully dodging their attacks. Build with Evasion Tome for maximum effect.
ü©∏

Lifesteal

Percentage chance to steal 1 HP from enemies per attack. When lifesteal exceeds 100%, you gain a chance to steal 2 HP per attack instead of 1.

Build Synergy: Lifesteal pairs excellently with high attack speed weapons. Works great with Revolver or Sword builds.
üåπ

Thorns

Reflects damage back to enemies when they hit you. This offensive defensive stat is particularly effective in close-range, tanky builds.

Best Use: Combine thorns with high armor and shield stats for a "reflect tank" build. Use Thorns Tome and let enemies hurt themselves. Works exceptionally well with Aura builds.

‚öîÔ∏è Weapon Stats

Weapon stats control your offensive capabilities and damage output. These are the core stats for creating devastating builds that can clear waves of enemies efficiently.

‚ö°

Damage

Your overall weapon damage, represented as a multiplier (e.g., "2.0x" = 200% base damage, "15.0x" = 1500% base damage). This is the foundation of all offensive builds.

Priority Stat: Damage is the most important offensive stat. Scale it with Damage Tome and damage-boosting items. Essential for all weapons.
üí•

Crit Chance

The probability your attacks become Critical Hits, applying your Crit Damage multiplier. Exceeding 100% Crit Chance enables Overcrits, which apply Crit Damage twice!

Overcrit Power: Stack Crit Chance beyond 100% using Precision Tome to achieve devastating Overcrit damage. Pairs perfectly with high Crit Damage builds.
üí¢

Crit Damage

The extra damage multiplier applied on Critical Hits. For example, with 10x Damage and 5x Crit Damage, your crits deal 50x your base damage.

Math Example: Damage 10.0x √ó Crit Damage 5.0x = 50.0x damage on crits. Always maximize both Crit Chance and Crit Damage together for exponential damage scaling.
‚ö°

Attack Speed

Controls how fast your weapons attack, shown as a percentage (e.g., 150% = 1.5x faster). Reduces delay between individual attacks and multi-projectile volleys.

Weapon Specific: For Aura, attack speed increases damage tick rate. For Revolver, it dramatically increases bullets per second. Use Cooldown Tome to boost this stat.
üéØ

Projectile Count

Increases how many projectiles are fired at once or during one attack cycle. Affects rocks from Chunkers, shields from Aegis, and flames from Flamewalker.

Maximum Coverage: Increase with Quantity Tome for massive screen coverage. Essential for clearing large enemy waves efficiently.
üîÑ

Projectile Bounces

The number of times a projectile bounces from one enemy to another. This stat is weapon-specific and cannot be increased via tomes or items.

Weapon Exclusive: Only available on specific weapons like Revolver, Bone, Wireless Dagger, and Lightning Staff as level-up upgrades.

üéØ Miscellaneous Stats

These stats affect various aspects of gameplay including movement, progression, and special mechanics. Optimize these for complete build mastery.

üìè

Size

The size of your projectiles or Aura, shown as a multiplier (e.g., "2.0x" = 200% base size). Larger projectiles hit more enemies simultaneously.

AOE Power: Increase with Size Tome for better enemy coverage. Especially powerful for Axe, Tornado, and Black Hole builds.
üöÄ

Projectile Speed

How fast projectiles move through the air, as a multiplier. For orbital weapons like Chunkers, this affects rotation speed around the player.

Increase Speed: Use Projectile Speed Tome to make projectiles travel faster, improving hit rate against distant or mobile enemies.
‚è±Ô∏è

Duration

Determines how long weapons and effects remain active before disappearing. Relevant for Axe, Flamewalker, Frostwalker, Space Noodle, and more. Also extends elemental status effects (Freeze, Burn, Poison).

Status Effect Power: Boost with Duration Tome to maximize the effectiveness of elemental weapons and DOT builds. Essential for Dragon's Breath burn builds.
üëë

Damage to Elites

Increases damage dealt specifically to Elite enemies and stage bosses, shown as a multiplier. This multiplies your damage against these tougher foes.

Boss Killer: Essential for boss fights. Stack this stat to trivialize elite encounters and speed through difficult stages. All stage bosses count as elites.
üí´

Knockback

How far enemies are pushed back when hit, as a multiplier. Higher knockback creates more distance between you and enemies.

Safety Tool: Increase with Knockback Tome for better enemy control. Helps keep melee enemies at bay, especially useful for ranged weapon builds.
üèÉ

Movement Speed

How fast your character moves, shown as a multiplier. Higher movement speed improves positioning and kiting capabilities.

Survivability: Use Agility Tome or Speed Boi item for better mobility. Essential for dodging attacks and collecting XP gems efficiently.
ü¶ò

Extra Jumps

Number of mid-air jumps available before touching the ground. For example, 3 extra jumps = initial jump + 3 air jumps = 4 total jumps.

Aerial Control: More jumps provide better vertical mobility and escape options. Helps avoid ground-based attacks and access hard-to-reach XP gems.
‚¨ÜÔ∏è

Jump Height

Determines how high your character jumps. Affects both initial jump and extra jumps, improving vertical navigation.

üçÄ

Luck

Affects the rarity of items from all sources, shown as a percentage increase. Higher luck = better upgrades more often.

Important Note: Luck does NOT affect item proc chance or crit chance‚Äîonly the quality of item drops and upgrade choices.
Farm Better: Increase with Luck Tome to get more Legendary and Mythic items. Some characters like Fox have innate luck bonuses.
‚ö†Ô∏è

Difficulty

Affects enemy quantity, HP, damage, and speed. Does NOT increase XP drops directly, but more enemies = more XP potential.

High Risk, High Reward: Use Cursed Tome to increase difficulty for faster leveling through increased enemy spawns. Only recommended for experienced players with strong builds.
üß≤

Pickup Range

Determines how far away you can collect XP gems. Does NOT affect powerup drops pickup range, which is fixed.

Quality of Life: Use Attraction Tome or Sucky Magnet for automatic XP collection. Lets you focus on combat while passively leveling.
‚≠ê

XP Gain

The amount of XP gained per XP shard, as a multiplier. Higher values mean each gem gives more XP for faster leveling.

Capped at 10x: XP Gain is capped at 10x maximum (1000% XP per gem). Beyond this, additional XP Gain has no effect.
Fast Leveling: Boost with XP Tome to reach level 99 faster. Stack until you hit the 10x cap for maximum efficiency.
ü™ô

Gold Gain

Multiplier applied to gold obtained per kill and broken jar. Higher values dramatically increase your in-run gold income.

Farm Gold: Use Golden Tome or Golden Ring to maximize gold collection for more shop purchases and item rerolls.
üíø

Silver Gain

Multiplier applied to permanent currency (Silver) earned from the run. This affects your post-run progression rewards.

Permanent Progression: Increase with Silver Tome for faster unlock progression. More silver = more characters, weapons, and items unlocked.
üëπ

Elite Spawn Increase

Increases the number of elite enemies that spawn, as a multiplier. More elites = more challenge but also better loot opportunities.

High Risk: Only increase if you have strong "Damage to Elites" stat and solid defensive stats. Elites hit much harder than regular enemies.
‚ö°

Powerup Multiplier

Increases both the magnitude and duration of powerup drops (like damage boost, speed boost, invincibility) as a multiplier.

Maximize Buffs: Powerups become significantly stronger and last longer. Great for builds that rely on temporary power spikes to clear tough waves.
üéÅ

Powerup Drop Chance

The chance enemies drop random powerups on death, as a multiplier. This ALSO increases the chance for chest drops.

Double Benefit: Higher powerup drop chance means more buffs AND more chests. Excellent for consistent power boosts throughout your run. More chests = more items = stronger builds!

üõ°Ô∏è Defensive Build Priority

2. Shield ‚Üí Prevents one-shots, recharges quickly.
3. Armor OR Evasion ‚Üí Choose one, stack it (diminishing returns).
4. HP Regen + Lifesteal ‚Üí Sustain in long fights.

Tanky builds work best with Aura or Chunkers. Add Thorns Tome for reflect damage.

‚öñÔ∏è Balanced Build Priority

1. Damage + Max HP ‚Üí Equal investment.
2. Crit Chance + Shield ‚Üí Offense + safety.
3. Attack Speed + Armor ‚Üí DPS + mitigation.
4. Projectile Count + Movement Speed ‚Üí Coverage + mobility.

Balanced builds are versatile and work with most weapons and characters. Great for beginners!
