What Feathers Does

Browse all items first if you’re planning a mobility-focused run. Feathers is a movement item that increases jump height and mid-air distance, helping you traverse high platforms, reposition above swarms, and grab airborne pickups more safely. (Effect confirmed by independent guides.) :contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}

Best Uses & Synergies

Playstyle Tips

  • Use the extra height to dodge boss telegraphs vertically and to cross gaps faster.
  • Pair with characters who already excel in the air (e.g., Monke) for greater map control.

Recommended Synergies

Monke (high jumps & wall-climb) Tornado (air control & kiting) Kite builds (keep enemies below you)

About Scarf (Airborne Damage)

Why Feathers + Scarf? Feathers gets you airborne more easily and for longer; Scarf increases damage while airborne (buffed in v1.0.17 from +33% → +50%). :contentReference[oaicite:13]{index=13}
