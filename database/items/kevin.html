üß™ How Kevin Works

Kevin is one of the most unique items in Megabonk. Instead of directly buffing your character, it creates a controlled self-damage loop that can be weaponized with the right build.

Mechanics Breakdown

  • Self-Damage Trigger: Approximately 25% chance per hit to damage yourself
  • Cannot Kill: Kevin will never deliver a killing blow - it stops at 1 HP
  • Bypasses Shields: Deals damage directly to your health pool, ignoring shield layers
  • Triggers On-Hit Effects: Each self-punch counts as being "hit," triggering items like Electric Plug and Thunder Mitts
  • Scales with Attack Speed: More hits = more Kevin procs = more on-hit triggers
üî¨ Technical Detail

Kevin's self-damage is pre-mitigation, meaning it calculates before armor reduction. However, the amount is minimal and designed to keep you hovering at low HP without outright killing you.

Why Would You Use This?

The key insight: Many powerful items trigger when YOU get hit, not when you hit enemies. Kevin lets you proc these effects on demand, turning defensive items into offensive weapons.

‚úì Advantages

  • Enables on-hit synergies with normally defensive items
  • Cannot kill you - provides a safety floor at 1 HP
  • Excellent with lifesteal/regen builds
  • Creates unique build archetypes impossible without it
  • Bypassing shields means you can stay at low HP intentionally

‚úó Disadvantages

  • Constantly keeps you at dangerously low HP
  • Requires strong sustain to avoid one-shot deaths
  • Useless without synergy items
  • Makes shields nearly worthless
  • High skill floor - easy to die if not prepared

üîì How to Unlock Kevin

Unlock Requirement

Obtain 3 Leeching Crystal items in a single run.

  1. Level Regen Tome to Level 8
    Leeching Crystal only appears in your item pool once Regen Tome reaches Level 8. This is a prerequisite.
  2. Start a New Run with High Sustain
    Play a character with good regen or lifesteal (like Megachad or Athena).
  3. Hunt for Leeching Crystals
    Open every chest. Leeching Crystal is rare but appears more frequently once Regen Tome is leveled.
  4. Collect All Three in One Run
    You must get all 3 Leeching Crystals in the same playthrough. Progress resets if you die or complete the run.
  5. Kevin Unlocks Permanently
    Once unlocked, Kevin joins your item pool and can appear in all future runs.
üí° Pro Tip: Fast Unlock Strategy

Disable non-essential items in your pool to increase Leeching Crystal spawn rates. Play quick Tier 1 runs and reset early if you don't see crystals by Stage 2. The Forest map is recommended for fast cycling.

What is Leeching Crystal?

Effect: +50 Max HP, -50% HP Regen

Leeching Crystal trades regen for raw HP. It's a double-edged sword that synergizes with max HP scaling items like Beefy Ring (+20% damage per 100 Max HP).

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can Kevin kill you?
No. Kevin can bring you to 1 HP, but it will never deliver the killing blow. However, this means you're extremely vulnerable to any enemy damage while at low HP.
Q: Why can't I find Leeching Crystal?
Leeching Crystal only appears after Regen Tome reaches Level 8. If your Regen Tome isn't high enough level, the crystal won't spawn in your item pool. Focus on leveling Regen Tome first.
Q: Do Kevin procs ignore shields?
Yes. Kevin's self-damage bypasses all shield layers and deals damage directly to your health pool. This is confirmed by extensive community testing on Reddit and Steam forums.
Q: Is Kevin good for beginners?
No. Kevin is one of the hardest items to use effectively. It requires specific synergy items, strong sustain, and game knowledge to avoid dying. New players should unlock it for completion but avoid using it until comfortable with the game.
Q: What's the best character for Kevin?
Megachad is the best Kevin character due to his high base HP and sustain capabilities. Athena and Calcium are also good choices. Avoid glass cannon characters like Ninja.
Q: Does Kevin work with all on-hit items?
Kevin triggers items that proc when YOU get hit, not when you hit enemies. Items like Mirror, Electric Plug, Thunder Mitts, and Spiky Shield all work. Items that require you to hit enemies (like crit effects) do not synergize.
Q: Can I stack multiple Kevins?
No. You can only have one Kevin per run. However, you can stack multiple on-hit trigger items (Mirror, Plug, etc.) to maximize the value from each Kevin proc.
Q: Is the "Kevin Immortality Build" still viable?
The original immortality exploit was patched in v1.0.12+. However, Kevin + Mirror builds remain viable - they're just no longer game-breaking. See our updated Kevin Build Guide for current strategies.
