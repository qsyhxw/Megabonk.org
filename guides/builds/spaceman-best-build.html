🌌 CONTROL-FOCUSED BUILD

Spaceman Gravity Control Build

Black Hole grouping with Aura & Lightning Staff for dependable crowd control.

Weapons Database • Tomes Index • Items Index
Primary Style: Gravity crowd control
Learning Curve: Positioning focused
Patch Context: Balanced since late-game updates

⚔️ Core Weapons Setup

Reliable trio for grouping → AOE conversion → chain finishing.

Black Hole

Aura

Lightning Staff

Black Hole

CORE

Creates singularities that pull enemies together—the build’s control engine. Use it to stack foes, then finish with Aura and chains. Placement and rhythm matter more in recent patches; drop holes where they intercept the largest streams.

Balance note: Post-1.0.12 difficulty changes mean less free wins—Black Hole remains strong, but good placement is expected.

Aura

AOE

A persistent circle of close-range AOE. Converts grouped mobs into safe clears while you reposition. Scale with Quantity/Cooldown to keep enemies inside damage for longer.

Lightning Staff

CHAIN

Fires bolts that chain between clustered targets. Naturally spikes once Black Hole is online; chains jump through condensed packs and clean stragglers.

📚 Essential Tomes Priority

Level momentum first, then scale projectile count and uptime. See: Tomes Index

XP Tome

LEVELING

Speeds up early access to key upgrades. As of v1.0.17 the bonus is ~7% per stack. One early stack is highly impactful; add more if drops allow and pacing feels good.

Quantity Tome

PROJECTILES

+Projectile count—more Black Holes, Aura pulses and lightning chains. Dramatically improves map coverage and control density.

Cooldown Tome

UPTIME

Lower cooldowns = higher cast frequency. Feels like attack speed for most weapons and keeps pulls/auras rolling.

Projectile Speed Tome

ALTERNATIVE

Increases projectile travel speed—use if you prefer faster holes/bolts over pure cooldown reductions.

Damage Tome

LATE GAME

Add once your control package is online to multiply clears on dense packs.

Cursed Tome

OPTIONAL

Increases difficulty (more/stronger/faster enemies). It does not directly boost your damage—take only when your control & defense are stable.

🎯 Recommended Items

Keep pulls frequent, add safety, and scale damage. See: Items Index

Battery

ATTACK SPEED

Boosts attack rate—great synergy with Black Hole and Aura for steadier control.

Mirror

DEFENSE

Reflects incoming damage and grants brief invulnerability on trigger—excellent safety net while grouping packs.

Demonic Soul

SCALING

On-kill stacking damage up to +100%. No health cost—simply rewards continuous clears.

Clover

CONSISTENCY

Improves luck for better rolls and rewards—helps you find key tomes/items earlier.

Credit Card (Green)

LOOT LUCK

Chest bonus that also grants a small +Luck. Use with Clover if you want maximum drop consistency.

Power Gloves

ON-HIT

Chance to create large explosions on hit. Not an attack-speed item—use as damage/clear utility.

Thunder Mitts

ON-HIT

Triggers lightning strikes on hit on a cooldown—extra coverage for grouped packs (utility, not speed).

Time Bracelet

XP

Grants bonus XP gain. Helps reach your key breakpoints faster; complements XP Tome early.

⏱️ Suggested Progression

Adjust to your drops and map pressure; numbers are guidance, not hard rules.

Early

Secure Control & Levels

Pick up XP Tome early to accelerate unlocks, then lock in Black Hole as your first weapon. Add either Quantity or Cooldown next based on drops.

Mid

Stabilize the Loop

Add Aura for reliable AOE. Begin stacking Quantity and Cooldown to keep holes and pulses flowing. Grab Battery for steadier output and Mirror for safety.

Late

Scale & Close

Pick up Lightning Staff to chain through condensed packs. Add Damage Tome and Demonic Soul for finishing power. Consider Cursed Tome only if your control and defense are comfortable.

⚠️ Common Pitfalls

❌ Mislabeling items

Power Gloves and Thunder Mitts are on-hit effects, not attack speed. Keep Battery for speed needs.

❌ Treating Cursed Tome as damage

It raises difficulty, not your damage. Add it only when your control & survivability are stable.

❌ Forgetting defense

Mirror dramatically increases run consistency. Don’t drop all defenses for greed.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Was Black Hole nerfed? Is it still worth using?

End-game difficulty increased in later patches, so placement matters more. Black Hole remains a top control pick and synergizes perfectly with Aura and chain weapons.

How many weapons should I run?

The core trio (Black Hole, Aura, Lightning Staff) covers control, AOE, and finish. You can flex a defensive or utility option if your mode/rolls allow—just keep tomes scaling in mind.

What’s a safe early plan?

Pick XP Tome early, secure Black Hole, then weave in Quantity/Cooldown. Add Mirror when pressure spikes; take Cursed Tome later if you want more challenge and drops.

Pro Tip: Drop Black Holes where lanes converge—let mobs walk into your Aura instead of chasing. Quantity increases coverage, Cooldown smooths uptime, and Lightning Staff mops up condensed packs.
Version notes: As of v1.0.17, XP Tome provides ~7% XP per stack. Late-game difficulty was increased in earlier updates, so control placement and survivability matter more than before.
