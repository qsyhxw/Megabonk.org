🔓 How to Unlock Blood Magic
Verified unlock path using real game data
Required Steps
Blood Magic is unlocked by progressing the Bloody Tome Tome. Once you upgrade Bloody Tome to Level 12 in a single run, Blood Magic becomes available to purchase in Unlocks (if your version requires a purchase).
1) Unlock Bloody Tome (commonly: kill 12,500 enemies).
2) During a run, level Bloody Tome to Lv.12.
3) After the run, open Unlocks and buy Blood Magic with Silver (if prompted).
Note about Vlad & Desert T2: Vlad (who uses Blood Magic) is typically unlocked by clearing Desert Tier 2, then purchasing him/weapon in Unlocks. This is a character unlock, not the Blood Magic weapon unlock itself.
⚙️ Blood Magic Mechanics
What it actually does in-game
🩸 On-Kill: Max HP Growth
Every enemy kill has a 5% chance to grant +1 permanent Max HP, enabling long-run survivability and scaling.
• Type: Area-of-effect pulses
• Benefit: Long-term Max HP stacking on kills
• Works best in dense waves and long stages
💉 Lifesteal Clarification
Blood Magic itself does not heal by damage percentage. Lifesteal primarily comes from the Bloody Tome (chance to heal 1 HP per hit; above 100% lifesteal adds a chance to double-heal).
• Source: Bloody Tome (Tome, not weapon)
• Heal Unit: +1 HP on hit by chance
• Synergy: More hits → more healing chances
📈 Stat Scaling
As an AoE weapon, Blood Magic scales excellently with Size (coverage), Cooldown (frequency), Damage (clear), and Quantity (more pulses/projectiles).
• Priorities: Size → Cooldown/Damage → Quantity
• Result: Faster clears → more kill rolls → more +Max HP procs
