Undead Boss (Calcium's Dad) Strategy Guide

Everything you need to know about Megabonk's hidden secret boss: how to spawn him, his mechanical weaknesses, and the drops he provides (Graveyard map unlock).

The Undead Boss (officially known in the lore as Calcium's Dad) is one of the most elaborate secret encounters in Megabonk. Unlike standard waves or elites, he does not appear on a timer. You must follow a specific set of steps involving the Desert Tier 2 map and the character Calcium to summon him.

Defeating this boss is the only way to unlock the highly coveted Graveyard map.

🪦 How to Spawn the Undead Boss

⚠️ CRITICAL REQUIREMENT: You must play as the character Calcium to trigger this event. If you play as Megachad, Roberto, or anyone else, the graves will simply not spawn. You also must be on the Desert Tier 2 map.
1

Select the Correct Loadout

Start a new run. Pick Calcium as your character and select the Desert Tier 2 map. Since this is going to be a tough boss fight, prioritize long-range damage or high burst setups from the start.

2

Locate and Disturb the 4 Graves

Once you load in, keep an eye on your minimap. You will see markers indicating the locations of coffin-style graves scattered across the desert. Navigate to them and interact with/disturb them. There are 4 graves in total.

3

Defeat the Four Colored Ghosts

Each time you disturb a grave, a colored Ghost will spawn (White, Black, Purple, and Red). You must defeat each ghost. Make sure your build is scaling well enough to handle these mini-bosses while you navigate the map.

4

Interact with the Golden Skeleton Statue

Once all 4 ghosts are defeated, a final marker will appear on your map, leading you to a tall golden skeleton statue. Approach it and interact with it. Calcium's Dad (The Undead Boss) will immediately spawn. Be ready.

⚔️ Boss Mechanics & Weaknesses

Health Pool

Calcium's Dad has a massive health pool (estimated around 35,400 HP on base settings). Burning him down requires sustained, high-tier single-target DPS.

Primary Attacks

He utilizes a combination of standard desert boss mechanics: causing large rocks to suddenly rise beneath you, and tracking you with a highly lethal red laser beam.

Strategy Guide: Exploiting Weaknesses

The Undead Boss thrives on locking you down and bursting you. Here is how you counter him:

  • Mobility is your greatest weapon: Fortunately, Calcium is one of the fastest characters in Megabonk. Do not rely on tanking hits (like you would with Megachad). Use your naturally high movement speed and Agility Tomes to constantly kite in circles, especially to dodge the red laser tracking.
  • Avoid Greed Shrines: It is highly recommended to steer clear of Greed Shrines during the summoning process and fight. Activating them can flood the screen with unnecessary elite enemies, making it exponentially harder to weave between the boss's rocks and lasers.
  • Focus on Attack Speed: Cooldown Tomes and Attack Speed are vital. Because you will be running for most of the fight, your damage windows need to be impactful. Let automatic/tracking weapons chip away at his health.

💎 Undead Boss Drops (Map Unlock)

When the Undead Boss finally falls, he drops a special treasure chest. Opening this chest is the culmination of the questline.

🗺️ Graveyard Map Unlocked: Upon defeating Calcium's Dad, you will permanently unlock the Graveyard Map for the character select screen.

Good to know: Megabonk is forgiving with this unlock. If you defeat the boss and unlock the notification, but then die to a random wave of enemies three seconds later, you keep the unlock. You do not need to extract or survive to the end of the run to keep the Graveyard map.

FAQ

Q: Why can't I see the graves on the minimap?

A: You are either not playing as the character Calcium, or you are not on the Desert Tier 2 map. Both conditions must be met for the grave puzzle to activate.

Q: What happens if I die after killing the boss? Do I lose the drops?

A: No. Once you defeat Calcium's Dad, the Graveyard map drops and unlocks securely. Even if you wipe immediately after the kill, the map will be sitting on your menu the next time you hit "Play".

Q: Is Calcium's Dad the hardest boss in Megabonk?

A: He is certainly one of the strongest due to his heavy health scaling and high-damage laser mechanic, but with Calcium's high-speed kiting, he is very manageable once you learn the attack pattern.
