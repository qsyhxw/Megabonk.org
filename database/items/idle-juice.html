🔓 How to Unlock Idle Juice

  1. Pick a safe lane or chokepoint. Your goal is to stay still while enemies come to you.
  2. Use auto/area damage weapons to kill without moving (see Aura & Frostwalker below).
  3. Reach 500 kills while stationary. Once achieved, Idle Juice enters your future item pool.

Verified: unlock = 500 stationary kills; effect cap = +100% (see Sources).

🔗 Best Synergies & Usage

Idle Juice shines when you can confidently hold your ground. The following pairings help you maintain uptime on the buff:

Aura

Always-on proximity damage so you can keep stacking damage while rooted.

Frostwalker

Slow/freeze helps control crowds around you, improving stand-still safety.

Campfire

Regeneration aura supports longer stationary windows and sustained fights.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock Idle Juice?
Kill 500 enemies while standing still. Afterward, Idle Juice can appear in your runs.
What exactly does Idle Juice do?
While stationary, your damage ramps up and caps at +100%. Move-friendly builds get less value; hold-position builds get more.
