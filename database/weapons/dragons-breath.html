Dragon's Breath

A-tier (Destructoid) / B-tier (Twinfinite & PGG)

Movement-directed flame stream that applies fire damage over time while you move.

🎯 Weapon Type

Flame Stream (movement-directed)
Fires in the direction you move and burns enemies along the path.

🔓 Unlock Requirement

Kill 1,000 Wisps as Fox on Desert
Then purchase in Unlocks with Silver (price as shown in-game).

⚔️ Damage Profile

Direct Fire + DoT (Burn)
Maintain contact for maximum DPS; movement control is key.

📊 Upgrade Paths (3)

Damage • Duration • Size
No built-in upgrade nodes for Crit, Projectile Count/Speed, or Bounces.

🐉 Weapon Overview

Dragon's Breath is a straightforward AoE weapon: a continuous flame stream that follows your movement direction. Your positioning and route through enemy packs determine how much damage you deal—sweeping crowds and “cutting across” their approach yields the best results. Stopping movement effectively halts your output.

💡 Key Mechanic: Movement-Based Targeting

The stream always points in your current movement direction. Keep moving to keep burning; sweep through mobs and herd them into the flame to maintain DoT ticks.

Core Properties

  • Pattern: Continuous stream that tracks your movement
  • Range: Short-to-medium cone from your character
  • Scaling: Simple, reliable increases from Damage and useful coverage/duration boosts from Size/Duration
  • Limitations: No crit/quantity/shot speed upgrades; output depends on your pathing

✅ Excels At

  • Clearing dense groups via controlled sweeps
  • Sustained damage on bosses while circle-kiting
  • High-mobility “hit & herd” playstyles

⚠️ Keep In Mind

  • Requires constant movement (not for idle/AFK builds)
  • No built-in crit/quantity scaling compared to meta carries
  • Damage heavily depends on positioning discipline

🔓 How to Unlock Dragon's Breath

Step-by-Step

  1. Pick Fox — default character with a Luck-scaling passive.
  2. Select Desert — any tier works; Tier 2–3 see Wisps more consistently.
  3. Kill 1,000 Wisps — progress accumulates across runs.
  4. Purchase — open Main Menu → Unlocks → Weapons and buy it with Silver.

🌀 Faster Wisp Farming

Use the Swarm challenge to increase enemy spawns, and restart Desert runs until Wisps appear early. AoE weapons like Aura or Lightning Staff help secure fast kills.

📈 Upgrades & Tome Synergy

Available Upgrade Stats

🔥 Damage (Priority)

  • Boosts both direct hit and ongoing burn DPS
  • Most consistent power gain

⏱️ Duration

  • Extends flame persistence and DoT uptime
  • v1.0.12 introduced a max duration cap; still valuable, just not abusable

📏 Size

  • Wider cone → hits more targets per sweep
  • Useful for wave clear and crowd control lanes

Recommended Tomes

  • Damage Tome — best raw scaling for both hit & burn
  • Duration Tome — complements sustained contact (now capped by patch)
  • Size Tome — improves coverage for swarm clears
  • Agility Tome — faster movement = easier herding and safer kiting
  • XP/Luck Tomes — generic economy/quality boosts depending on build goals

💡 Practical Note

Keep enemies in contact with the flames to continuously refresh the burn damage. Circle-kiting bosses lets you dodge while maintaining DPS.

🎮 Builds & Play Patterns

Movement-Based Kiting

🔥 Flame Sweeper

Idea: Mobility-first sweeping through waves.

  • Weapons: Dragon's Breath • Aura • Lightning Staff • Aegis
  • Tomes: Damage → Duration → Agility → XP
  • Items (examples): Turbo Socks, Turbo Skates, Gym Sauce, Overpowered Lamp
  • How to play: Curve around packs; cut across their approach paths.

Boss Focus

💀 Sustained Burn

Idea: Longer contact windows, safe circles.

  • Focus: Damage & Duration; add survivability if needed
  • Items (examples): Big Bonk, Spicy Meatball, Holy Book
  • Technique: Tight circles to refresh DoT while dodging.

Swarm Clearing

🌊 Wave Annihilation

Idea: Max coverage for mobbing phases.

  • Weapons: Dragon's Breath • Flamewalker • Aura • Bone
  • Tomes: Damage → Size → Duration → Quantity
  • Items (examples): Moldy Cheese, Overpowered Lamp, Battery

Combat Tips

✅ Core Tactics

  • Circular Kiting: keep contact while dodging
  • Perpendicular Sweeps: cross the enemy flow to maximize hits
  • Use Obstacles: funnel packs into narrow lanes
  • Keep Moving: stopping = no stream/no damage

💡 Balance Snapshot

Community lists generally place Dragon’s Breath in the A–B range: reliable and easy to use, but without meta-defining crit/quantity scaling.

🎯 Community Tiering

  • Pro Game Guides: B-tier — solid as a secondary pick.

❓ FAQ

Do I need to finish the run for Wisp kills to count?

Answer: No — progress accumulates across runs.

Which Desert tier is best for farming?

Answer: Tier 2–3 spawn Wisps more consistently; using the Swarm challenge further accelerates spawns.

Does the burn “stack”?

Answer: Treat it as a damage-over-time that refreshes with contact. Keep enemies in the flame to sustain DPS.

Is Dragon’s Breath a primary carry?

Answer: It can carry early/mid content, but most top-end metas lean on crit/quantity-centric weapons. Use Dragon’s Breath for reliable coverage alongside a meta DPS.
