🏆 Character Overview

Megachad is the ultimate defensive powerhouse in Megabonk, specializing in damage mitigation and crowd control. With his signature Aura weapon and unique damage-stopping abilities, Megachad can create safe zones in the battlefield and provide unparalleled team support in chaotic situations.

🔓 Unlock Requirements

  • Get the Damage Tome to Level 7 - Requires multiple successful runs
  • Purchase Aura Weapon - Available after character unlock
  • Estimated Time: 15-25 hours of gameplay
  • Difficulty: Advanced (requires tome progression knowledge)

⚔️ Weapon & Abilities

🔆 Aura

Megachad's signature defensive weapon that creates protective fields around him.

🎁 RNG Blessing: Damage Immunity

Effect: Stops damage and stuns enemies within range

Cooldown Reduction: -0.2 seconds per level

Maximum Level: Level 50 (10-second cooldown reduction)

🎯 Unlock Strategy Guide

Phase 1: Damage Tome Progression (Levels 1-4)

Early Tome Leveling Strategy

  • Focus on damage-dealing characters like Fox or Ninja for faster clears

  • Aim for 15+ minute survival runs to maximize tome experience
  • Use constructs that complement high-damage weapons

Phase 2: Advanced Tome Progression (Levels 5-7)

Efficient Farming Techniques

  • Target 25+ minute runs for maximum tome experience gain
  • Use Calcium's speed-scaling builds for sustained farming
  • Focus on boss elimination for bonus tome experience
  • Combine offensive tomes for synergistic power spikes
