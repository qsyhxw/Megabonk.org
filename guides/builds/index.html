🔥 The Holy Trinity - Universal Meta Strategy

The foundation of every god-tier run in Megabonk. This strategy creates an exponential growth engine that outscales the game's difficulty curve. Master this, and you'll dominate any character build.

📚

XP Tome

More XP = faster leveling = more upgrade choices. The acceleration engine of your build.

Priority: HIGHEST
😈

Curse Tome

Makes game harder BUT dramatically increases drop quality. Risk-reward perfection.

Priority: HIGH
🍀

Luck Tome

Ensures every level-up gives legendary/epic upgrades. Multiplies the power of XP Tome.

Priority: HIGH
💡 Pro Tip: Always prioritize these three tomes first in ANY build. They create a feedback loop where leveling up → better upgrades → faster clearing → more XP → more levels → even better upgrades. This is the secret to 100K+ kill runs.

🛡️ Kevin + Mirror Immortality

Best Character: Any (Universal)

The most broken combo in Megabonk right now. Kevin damages you (can't kill), Mirror gives invulnerability when damaged. With enough attack speed, you become literally immortal. This is how leaderboard runs are done.

⚔️ Weapons (High DPS Required)

  • Aura - Constant damage
  • Lightning Staff - Chain damage
  • Revolver - Fast hits
  • Any high-DPS weapon

📚 Tomes

  • Cooldown Tome - MANDATORY! More attack speed
  • Damage Tome - Kill faster
  • Holy Trinity - XP + Curse + Luck

🎁 Key Items (BOTH REQUIRED)

  • Kevin - Damages you (can't kill you)
  • Mirror - Invulnerability on damage
  • Battery - More attack speed
  • Any attack speed items - Stack as much as possible
🔥 How It Works: Kevin damages you constantly (scaled by attack speed). Mirror triggers on each damage tick, giving you brief invulnerability. With high enough attack speed, the invulnerability windows overlap, making you completely immortal. You literally cannot die while this combo is active!

💡 Pro Tips & Advanced Strategies

🎯

Stat Priority

Always prioritize: Luck → Movement Speed → Attack Speed → Damage. Luck affects all future upgrades, making it the highest-value long-term investment.

⏰

Early vs Late Game

First 5 minutes: Focus on XP and leveling. Minutes 5-15: Build your weapon loadout. 15+ minutes: Stack damage and survive. Plan your build timeline!

🎲

Embrace the Curse

Don't fear Curse Tome! Making the game harder dramatically increases drop quality. The Holy Trinity (XP + Curse + Luck) is meta for a reason.

🔄

Reroll Strategy

Visit shrines and vendors often. With high Luck, rerolls can give you game-breaking legendary items. Don't hoard currency - spend it on rerolls!

⚡

Attack Speed Scaling

Most builds become exponentially better with attack speed. It affects Kevin+Mirror combo, lifesteal healing, and on-hit effect procs. Never skip attack speed!

🏆

Leaderboard Strategy

For top scores: Always use Holy Trinity, focus on XP early, don't die before minute 20, and abuse Kevin+Mirror if you find them. Kill count matters more than time survived!
