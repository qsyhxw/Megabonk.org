🎯 Build Overview

Bandit starts with Dexecutioner and gains +1% Attack Speed per level, enabling high on-hit uptime and fast execute thresholds. Choose Execute/Melee when you can weave into packs; pick Black Hole Size when you prefer control and screen-wide clumps. Both paths can greed XP + Luck early if safe to accelerate scaling.

Playstyle

Execute dives (Build 1) or zone & clump (Build 2)

Difficulty

Medium — early safety picks are key

Power Spike

Mid game after Size & core tomes

Best For

Elites/boss melts (B1) or wave dominance (B2)

📈 Upgrade Path Timeline

Use this minute-by-minute plan and choose the branch you’re on

0–3 Minutes

Stabilize & Farm

Grab XP/Luck early if safe; otherwise take Size for your chosen core (Katana or Black Hole). Add one support weapon (Flamewalker or Dice) for consistency.

3–6 Minutes

Online

Exec: Size → Cooldown → Damage；slot Joe’s Dagger/OP Lamp asap。BH: Size → Quantity → Cooldown/Duration；stack on-hit synergies。

6–10 Minutes

Scaling

Roll for higher-tier tome ranks. Keep total weapons at 3–4 as a general recommendation to avoid upgrade dilution; expand only when your core is capped.

10–15 Minutes

Spike

Exec build melts elites/bosses once thresholds are met. BH build fills the screen — feed kills into Dice if taken.

15+ Minutes

Victory Lap

Cap remaining ranks; for BH add Damage late, for Exec add safety/QoL if lobby is spiky.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌ Too many weapons

As a rule of thumb keep 3–4 until your core is capped; over-picking dilutes upgrades and delays spikes.

❌ Ignoring Size/Quantity

Exec wants Size for safe connects; BH literally scales with Size × Quantity.

❌ Misusing Cursed Doll

It’s fine for waves, but it does not deal % max-HP to bosses — don’t rely on it for boss phases.

❓ FAQ

When should I choose Exec vs. Black Hole?

Pick Exec on open maps or if you’re confident in melee weaving. Pick Black Hole when waves are dense or you want safer control with on-hit scaling.

Is Cooldown always mandatory on Bandit?

It’s widely recommended because more casts mean more procs；但有部分攻略认为 Bandit 的等级攻速让 Cooldown 相对收益下降。实际取舍看节奏与掉落。

Best support if I already have Black Hole?

Dice for bursts；Flamewalker或Lightning Staff 做稳定清线与眩晕控制；别把武器格塞满太早。
