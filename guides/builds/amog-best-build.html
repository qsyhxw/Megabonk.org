📚 Essential Tomes Priority (Corrected)

Community-favorite early curve and how each tome actually works. Browse all tomes: Tomes Index.

Early Curve: XP → Luck → Cursed

RUSH

XP Tome speeds leveling. Luck Tome improves odds of higher-tier offers (level-ups, shrines, etc.). Cursed Tome increases enemy difficulty (quantity/HP/speed/strength)—it is not a direct damage multiplier.

Bloody Tome (Lifesteal)

SUSTAIN

Adds lifesteal on hit (at 100% chance you reliably heal 1 per hit; above 100% can heal more). Stack until sustain feels comfortable for poison play.

Cooldown Tome

FASTER STACKS

Increases attack speed/frequency so Poison Flask applies stacks faster—smoothest DPS gain once sustain is online.

Damage Tome

MULTIPLIER

Global damage increase that benefits most weapons. Generally picked after Bloody + Cooldown to convert uptime into kills.

Quantity / Size (Situational)

OPTIONAL

Quantity adds more projectiles; Size enlarges areas—both can help hit more targets when clustered by Black Hole.

⏱️ Minute-by-Minute Progression (Guideline)

A safe curve that respects how tomes/items actually work.

0–3 min

Foundation

Grab XP → Luck → Cursed to accelerate offers and scale difficulty for better rewards. Take Poison Flask as soon as it appears.

3–7 min

Control Online

Add Black Hole for grouping. Start stacking Bloody Tome (Lifesteal) until sustain feels stable; pick up Battery if offered.

7–12 min

Clear Speed

Take Cooldown to apply poison faster and add Damage to convert uptime into kills. Slot Flamewalker as the third weapon for constant coverage.

12–18 min

Scaling

Consider Demonic Soul for per-kill damage scaling; Mirror as a safety layer. Keep stacking Bloody/Cooldown/Damage as offers allow.

18+ min

Polish

Round out situational picks (Quantity/Size). Maintain grouping discipline—drop Black Hole, then spread poison through the packed wave.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best weapon for Amog?

Poison Flask anchors the build. Use Black Hole to group enemies so flasks tag many targets, and add Flamewalker for constant area damage. Browse: Weapon Database.

Which tomes should I prioritize?

A common start is XP → Luck → Cursed (note: Cursed raises enemy difficulty). Then stack Bloody Tome (Lifesteal) for sustain, followed by Cooldown and Damage. All tomes: Tomes Index.

What items pair well with this build?

Moldy Cheese (extra poison-on-hit), Battery (attack speed), Mirror (reflect + brief invulnerability), Demonic Soul (damage per kill), and Chonkplate (overheal + lifesteal). See Items Index.

Tip: Drop Black Hole first to pull waves together, then pepper the pack with Poison Flask. Prioritize Bloody Tome (Lifesteal) until sustain is consistent, then invest in Cooldown and Damage. Items like Moldy Cheese, Battery, and Mirror make the loop smoother and safer.
