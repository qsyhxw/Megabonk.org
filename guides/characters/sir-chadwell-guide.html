üîì How to Unlock Sir Chadwell

Unlock Requirement: Complete Forest Tier 3

Sir Chadwell is one of the most challenging characters to unlock. Forest Tier 3 is elite-level content that requires mastery of game mechanics, optimal builds, and excellent execution.

üèÜ Elite Challenge Overview

Forest Tier 3 is significantly harder than Tier 1 or 2. Expect:

  • 2-3x enemy HP compared to Tier 1
  • Significantly higher enemy damage - mistakes are punishing
  • More dense waves - crowd control matters
  • Tougher boss - requires strong build and mechanics
  • Longer run duration - stamina and focus required

Step 1: Master the Basics

Before attempting Forest Tier 3, ensure you have:

  • Completed Forest Tier 1 and 2 multiple times
  • Understanding of optimal builds for your chosen character
  • Mastery of positioning and dodging
  • Knowledge of Forest enemy types and patterns
  • 10+ successful Tier 2 completions across different biomes

Step 2: Choose Your Character

Recommended characters for Forest Tier 3:

  • Dicehead - Infinite crit scaling handles high HP enemies
  • Fox - Reliable, balanced, good survivability
  • MegaChad - Tankiness helps survive mistakes
  • Bandit - Kevin + Mirror cheese if you're experienced
  • Vlad - Lifesteal sustain for long fights

Step 3: Optimize Your Build

For Forest Tier 3 success, prioritize:

  • Damage Scaling: High DPS to kill tanky enemies
  • Survivability: Max HP, Lifesteal, or Damage Reduction
  • Crowd Control: Freeze, slow, or knockback useful
  • Shop Optimization: Buy S-tier items aggressively
  • Balance: Don't neglect defense OR offense

Step 4: Execute the Run

During the Tier 3 attempt:

  • Play patiently - don't rush or get greedy
  • Respect every enemy - they hit hard
  • Dodge boss attacks - memorize patterns
  • Use shops wisely - buy what you need, not want
  • Stay focused - one mistake can end the run
‚ö†Ô∏è Boss Warning: Forest Tier 3 boss is significantly tougher. Save strong items, maintain high HP before boss, and know boss attack patterns. This is where most runs fail.

Step 5: Unlock Sir Chadwell!

Upon defeating the Forest Tier 3 boss, you'll receive notification that Sir Chadwell is unlocked. Congratulations - you've proven your mastery and earned this elite character!

üí° Persistence is Key: Forest Tier 3 may take 3-10+ attempts. Each failed run teaches you something. Learn enemy patterns, optimize builds, improve positioning. When you finally complete it and unlock Sir Chadwell, the achievement feels earned. Don't give up!

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is Sir Chadwell good for beginners?

Absolutely not. Sir Chadwell is locked behind Forest Tier 3, one of the hardest challenges in the game. You must be an experienced player to even unlock him. He's designed as a reward for skilled players, not a starting character. Begin with Fox or MegaChad instead.

How hard is Forest Tier 3 really?

Very hard. Expect to fail multiple times. Enemies have 2-3x more HP and deal much more damage than Tier 1. The boss is a significant challenge requiring strong builds and good mechanics. Most players need 5-10 hours of practice before completing Tier 3 successfully.

Is Sir Chadwell worth the unlock effort?

Yes, if you're an experienced player seeking powerful characters. Sir Chadwell has excellent damage (30% weapon bonus + 15% Elite Prowess) and good survivability (10% damage reduction). He's a well-rounded elite character. However, he's not dramatically stronger than S-tier characters - the unlock is more about the achievement.

Best character to unlock Sir Chadwell with?

Dicehead or Fox are top choices. Dicehead's infinite crit scaling handles high HP enemies well. Fox is reliable and balanced. Avoid high-risk characters - you need consistency for Tier 3, not coin flips.

Can I play Sir Chadwell without Corrupted Sword?

You can, but you lose 30% damage bonus which is enormous. Other weapons make him significantly weaker. Always prioritize buying Corrupted Sword first shop - it's essential to his power level.

How does Elite Prowess work exactly?

Elite Prowess increases ALL damage sources by 15%. This includes: weapon damage, on-hit effects (Kevin, Poison), explosions, DOTs, everything. It's a multiplicative 15% boost to your total damage output, making it extremely valuable when stacking multiple damage sources.

Is 10% damage reduction significant?

Yes! 10% reduction means you effectively have 11% more HP. If you stack more damage reduction items to reach 20-30% total, you become very tanky. This is one of the few defensive passives in the game - use it.

Best build for Sir Chadwell?

Corrupted Blade Master (crit build) has the highest damage ceiling. Stack Crit Chance to 40%+, Crit Damage to 200%+, then add general damage scaling. Critical corruption procs become your main damage source. Very powerful once completed.

Why is Sir Chadwell only A-tier?

He's strong but not specialized. S-tier characters (Dicehead, Bandit) have unique mechanics that enable specific strategies. Sir Chadwell is a "generalist elite" - high damage, good defense, but no unique gimmicks. He's consistently strong across all content but doesn't excel at anything specific.

Late game strategy?

With Corrupted Sword + full crit build + Elite Prowess, you're dealing 2-3x base damage with critical corruption procs. Add the 10% damage reduction and you're both a tank and a damage dealer. Play confidently but smartly - you've earned this power through skill. Dominate content that once challenged you.
