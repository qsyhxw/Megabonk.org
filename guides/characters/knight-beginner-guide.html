This blessing synergizes perfectly with armor items, making Sir Oofie the ultimate tank character.

Sir Oofie Progression Guide

From novice knight to legendary defender

1

Priority: Establish basic combat and survive early waves

  • Upgrade Sword to level 3-4 for consistent damage
  • Take any armor or health items when offered
  • Learn sword combat range and timing
  • Stay close to enemies but avoid getting surrounded
  • Your armor blessing is still building - play cautiously
2

Mid Levels (10-20)

Priority: Armor blessing becomes noticeable, add secondary weapons

  • 10% armor bonus starts making a difference
  • Add a second weapon for variety (Thorns Tome recommended)
  • Prioritize armor and health items to stack with blessing
  • Begin playing more aggressively due to increased survivability
  • Work toward first character unlock goals
3

Advanced Levels (20-30)

Priority: Become a tank powerhouse with 20-30% armor bonus

  • Armor blessing (20-30%) makes you significantly tankier
  • Can now survive in the middle of large enemy groups
  • Focus on damage items to complement your survivability
  • Master positioning to maximize sword effectiveness
  • Begin tackling higher difficulty content
4

Master Levels (30+)

Priority: Near-invulnerability with 30%+ armor bonus

  • 30%+ armor bonus makes you nearly invulnerable
  • Can tank boss attacks and massive enemy swarms
  • Focus purely on damage optimization
  • Master advanced positioning and crowd control
  • Help unlock other characters by completing their requirements
