🧪 How Chonkplate Works

Chonkplate converts any healing you receive while at full HP into an Overheal buffer up to 75% of your Max HP. The built-in +20% Lifesteal feeds that buffer continuously once your damage output ramps up.

Advantages

  • Massive effective HP via Overheal during swarms
  • Lifesteal scales with DPS for snowball sustain
  • Pairs naturally with Max-HP stacking strategies

Disadvantages

  • Requires reliable healing sources to stay topped off
  • Weak in low-DPS or low-pickup scenarios
  • Competes with other Legendary slots

🔓 How to Unlock Chonkplate

Requirement: Reach 500 Max HP in a run. After you meet this condition once, Chonkplate can drop in future runs.

Practical ways to hit 500 Max HP

Leeching Crystal

⭐⭐⭐⭐ Good

Run Health-oriented tomes early to raise your baseline Max HP.

💡 Fast unlock route

Prioritize Leeching Crystal rolls and pick up Demonic Blood whenever available; supplement with the Health Tome.
