🎯 Weapon Overview

Bone throws a projectile that bounces between nearby enemies, letting a single throw hit multiple targets. It’s Calcium’s starting weapon and fits his Speed Demon playstyle—moving faster over time and converting that speed into damage, as long as you avoid taking hits.

🌟 Why Bone Works

  • Bouncing Clear: Natural multi-target coverage against clustered waves.
  • Safety: Ranged throws keep distance while enemies get chain-hit.
  • Speed Synergy: Calcium’s speed->damage scaling amplifies Bone’s output if you keep your stacks.

⚔️ Mechanics & Upgrade Priority

Core Mechanics

  • 🎯 Bounces: On hit, Bone searches for a nearby target and continues. More Projectile Bounces = longer chains.
  • ➕ Projectile Count: More bones thrown per attack dramatically increases total hits.
  • 💨 Attack/Move Speed: Faster attacks and movement (with correct items) multiply clear speed.
  • 📏 Projectile Speed: Helps bones reach the next target sooner, improving chain consistency.

💡 Practical Tips

  • Fight in density: Bounces thrive on grouped mobs; in sparse fights, consider a single-target sidearm.
  • Don’t get hit (Calcium): Taking damage guts your speed stacks and lowers damage output.
  • Keep moving: Circular kiting keeps enemies packed and you safe.

🏗️ Best Bone Builds

💨 Calcium Speed Demon (Bone Core)
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Character: Calcium — speed increases over time; damage scales with speed.

Tomes

  • Agility (Movement Speed) → fuels passive
  • Quantity (Projectile Count) → more bones
  • Damage / Precision (optional later)

Key Items

  • Turbo Skates — converts Move Speed to Attack Speed (cap ~+40%/stack)
  • Turbo Socks — +15% Move Speed
  • Golden Sneakers — economy from moving (pairs with speed)
  • Feathers — higher jumps / mobility comfort
  • Anvil — extra stat line on weapon upgrades

Play pattern: stay untouched, keep speed stacking, let bounces clear screens.

🔄 Bounce Duo (Bone + Lightning Staff)
⭐⭐⭐⭐

Idea: Double down on chaining damage. Lightning Staff chains between targets while Bone bounces—together they erase packs reliably.

Focus

  • Quantity & Bounces across both weapons
  • Cooldown/Attack Speed to tighten uptime
  • Size (for Lightning chain consistency)

Great wave clear, swap to single-target option for bosses if needed.

🎯 Hybrid Clear + Boss (Bone + Revolver)
⭐⭐⭐⭐

Why: Bone handles crowds, Revolver supplies focused DPS and scales well with crit.

Build Notes

  • Quantity / Bounces for both weapons
  • Crit stats primarily for Revolver
  • Keep mobility so Bone stays online

✅ Strengths & Weaknesses

Strengths

  • Natural multi-target coverage via bounce

  • Safe ranged play, good kiting

Weaknesses

  • Needs density—weak on isolated targets
  • Boss DPS usually requires a partner weapon
  • Requires Count/Bounce investment to shine

🆚 Comparisons

Bone vs. Lightning Staff

Lightning Staff: Chains between targets for very reliable AoE; benefits from Size and uptime stats.

Bone: Higher projectile counts possible and great with speed scaling; needs density to shine.

Bone vs. Wireless Dagger

Wireless Dagger: Smart-targeting projectiles with bounce; can scale with Duration in some setups.

Bone: Simpler chain logic, strong early crowd control when Count/Bounce roll in.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bone only good on Calcium?

No. It’s best on Calcium, but still usable on others (e.g., Bandit for attack speed). For bosses, add a single-target weapon.

How many bounces should I aim for?

Get several Bounce upgrades by mid-game and keep stacking Quantity. More chains + more projectiles = exponential hits.

What items are must-haves in speed builds?

Turbo Skates (speed → attack speed) and Turbo Socks (+move speed). Pick Golden Sneakers if you want economy while moving.
