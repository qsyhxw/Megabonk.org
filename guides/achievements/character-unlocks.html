💰 Silver Farming Strategies

Most characters require purchasing weapons, which costs silver. Here are the most efficient farming methods:

🏃 Speed Farming (Best Overall)
⚡ Efficiency: ★★★★★ | 800-1200 silver/run
  • Focus on killing enemies fast with AOE builds
  • Use Aura or Flamewalker for auto-damage
  • Stack Luck Tome for better silver drops
  • Forest Tier 1 has best enemy density
  • Complete run in 10-15 minutes
⏱️ Final Swarm Farming
💀 Efficiency: ★★★★☆ | 1000-1500 silver/run
  • Build for survival and extend Final Swarm time
  • Use Walking Apocalypse build (Aura + Mirror + Kevin)
  • Silver bonus increases the longer you survive
  • Requires good defensive build
  • Higher risk but higher reward
🎲 Luck Build Farming
🍀 Efficiency: ★★★★☆ | 900-1400 silver/run
  • Use Fox character (+1% Luck per level)
  • Stack Luck Tome, Lucky Charm, and luck items
  • Better quality drops = more silver per enemy
  • Combine with One Lucky Fox build
  • Consistent and reliable method
🏆 Boss Rush Strategy
👑 Efficiency: ★★★☆☆ | 600-900 silver/run
  • Rush to boss as fast as possible
  • Boss kills give large silver bonuses
  • Complete multiple tiers if possible
  • Use high DPS builds for speed
  • Less consistent but faster runs

🎯 Recommended Unlock Order

Follow this order for optimal progression and to unlock the most powerful characters first:

1
🤖 CL4NK (Forest Tier 1)
First major unlock. Crit-focused playstyle opens new strategies. Revolver is easy to unlock (7,500 kills). Essential for crit-based achievements.
2
💪 Megachad (Damage Tome 7 + Aura)
Best for AFK/tank builds. Aura weapon is S-tier. Makes Final Swarm survival easy. Required for Walking Apocalypse meta build.
3
💀 Calcium (1,000 Skeleton Kills)
Unlocks naturally while playing Forest. Speed-based gameplay is fun. Good for farming achievements. Bone weapon has unique mechanics.
4
🥷 Ninja (Desert Tier 1 + Katana)
S-tier character with execution mechanic. Katana is one of the best weapons. Opens Desert progression. High skill ceiling, high reward.
5
🐵 Monke (Find in Forest)
Fun secret character with wall climbing. Bananarang is unique weapon. Easy to unlock if you explore. Good for mobility-focused builds.
6
⚡ Athena (Thorns Tome 9 + Aegis)
Required for Cactus achievement (rarest in game). Aegis is A-tier defensive weapon. Thorns mechanic enables unique builds. Tanky playstyle.
7
👹 Ogre (15,000 Goblin Kills)
Long grind but S-tier character. +1% damage per level scales infinitely. Axe weapon is powerful AOE. Save for late game when farming other achievements.
8+
🌟 Remaining Characters
Unlock as you complete challenges, tiers, and find hidden bosses. Bush, Bandit, and other tier-based unlocks will come naturally during progression.

💡 Character Unlock Pro Tips

🎯 Combine Objectives
Work on multiple unlock requirements simultaneously. For example, farm goblins for Ogre while also working on weapon kill achievements.
💰 Farm Silver Early
Most characters require purchasing weapons. Build up a silver reserve early so you can unlock characters immediately when you meet requirements.
📚 Prioritize Tomes
Upgrading tomes unlocks characters AND improves your builds. Focus on XP Tome, Damage Tome, and Thorns Tome for character unlocks.
🗺️ Explore Thoroughly
Hidden characters like Monke and Bush require exploration. Take time to check unusual areas, suspicious objects, and hidden paths.
⏰ Don't Rush Tier 3
Some characters unlock from Tier 2 and Tier 3 completion. Focus on unlocking easier characters first before attempting harder tiers.
🎮 Try Each Character
Each character has unique playstyle. Test them after unlocking to find your favorite. Some achievements require specific characters.
