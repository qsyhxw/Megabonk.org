Core Mechanics

How Lifesteal Works

  • On-Hit Healing: Each time you hit an enemy, there's a chance to recover 1 HP
  • 100% Threshold: At ‚â•100% lifesteal, you are guaranteed 1 HP per hit
  • Overflow Mechanic: Lifesteal beyond 100% provides additional proc chances
    • 200% lifesteal ‚âà guaranteed 1 HP + high chance for +1 HP (‚âà2 HP/hit)
    • 300%+ ‚âà near-guaranteed 2 HP per hit
    • Community reports suggest overflow works as separate proc rolls
  • All Damage Sources: Triggers on melee, projectiles, AoE, DoT ticks, and other damage types (subject to proc coefficients)
  • Healing Cap: Cannot overheal beyond max HP; excess healing is wasted (community consensus)

How to Unlock

Unlock Requirement

  • Condition: Kill 12,500 enemies (cumulative across all runs)
  • Track Progress: View in the in-game Quests menu (not the Stats page)
  • Cumulative Progress: Progress carries over between runs unless quest explicitly states "in a single run"
  • Version Note: Unlock count confirmed as 12,500 in multiple October 2025 sources (earlier community data may list 12,000)

Strategy Guide

  • Prioritize on melee and high-tick-rate builds - Weapons that hit frequently benefit most (view all weapons)
  • Rush to 100% lifesteal (Level ~10) - This is the guaranteed healing breakpoint; prioritize reaching it
  • Combine with area or duration effects to blanket crowds and sustain through chip damage
  • Don't neglect mitigation‚Äîlifesteal shines when you can safely stay in range to keep hitting
  • Extremely powerful in boss fights with adds (many targets = constant healing)
  • Essential for aggressive facetank playstyles (explore builds)

Pro Tips

  • The 100% threshold (Level ~10) is the most important breakpoint - prioritize reaching it early
  • At high levels, lifesteal becomes very consistent - potentially near-guaranteed 2 HP per hit
  • Actual sustain depends heavily on hit frequency and enemy density
  • Works best with weapons that have many small hits rather than few large hits (compare weapons)
  • Combines exceptionally well with AoE and duration effects
  • Can enable "immortal" builds when combined with high attack speed and mitigation (view builds)
  • Proc Coefficients: Some multi-hit abilities may have reduced lifesteal trigger rates (introduced in September 2025 patches)

üí° Pro Tip: Pair Bloody Tome with Cooldown Tome and fast-hitting weapons like Lightning Staff or Dragon's Breath for maximum healing output.
