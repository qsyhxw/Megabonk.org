üéØ Fox Strategy Guide

Early Game (0-10 minutes)

üí° Early Game Priorities
  1. Upgrade Firestaff first: Reach Level 4 to 6 before adding additional weapons
  2. Take XP Tome immediately: Accelerates Luck buildup (+7% per level, v1.0.12+)
  3. Acquire Luck Tome next: Compounds with passive for exponential scaling
  4. Collect all XP crystals: Every level now provides +2% Luck (v1.0.17+)
  5. Save gold: Avoid spending until Level 15 to 20 when Luck is higher
  6. Practice positioning: Medium to long range is optimal for survivability
  7. Find Clover as soon as possible: +7.5% Luck, highest priority item
üçÄ At Level 10 (~25% total Luck, v1.0.17+)

You will begin noticing improved upgrade options. Focus on reaching Level 20 for significant improvement in item quality and drop rates.

Mid Game (10-20 minutes)

üí° Mid Game Strategy
  1. Add 2 to 3 more weapons: Lightning Staff, Aura, or Bow work particularly well
  2. Take Quantity Tome: Provides significant projectile and coverage boosts for ranged weapons
  3. Acquire Credit Card (Green): Provides +2% Luck per chest opened, stacking permanently
  4. Begin spending gold: At 25%+ Luck, shop quality may improve (effect unverified)
  5. Visit shrines: Permanent stat boosts compound over the run
  6. Stack damage items: Joe's Dagger, Big Bonk, Demonic Soul
  7. Consider Cursed Tome: More enemies equals more XP equals faster leveling
üçÄ At Level 30 (~65% total Luck, v1.0.17+)

Rare items become common. Epic and Legendary upgrades appear frequently. You should feel noticeably stronger than other characters at this progression point.

Late Game (20+ minutes)

üí° Late Game Optimization
  1. Target 100%+ total Luck: Level 50 baseline goal for premium rewards
  2. Maximize weapon upgrades: Push priority weapons to highest available rarity
  3. Stack survivability items: Chonkplate, Holy Book, Mirror are essential
  4. Utilize Shrine of Succ: XP-absorbing shrine for rapid leveling
  5. Open every chest: High Luck makes all loot valuable
  6. Prepare for Final Swarm: Endless waves spawn after missing the teleporter at 20 minutes
  7. Acquire Sucky Magnet: Game-changing pickup radius increase (unlock by completing all Charge Shrines in one map)
üçÄ At 100%+ Luck (Level 50+, v1.0.17+)

Legendary drops dominate. Every upgrade feels impactful. At 140%+ Luck with optimized builds, the game becomes substantially different with near-constant premium loot.

üìö Tomes & Items Explained

What are Tomes?

Tomes are passive stat upgrades chosen during level-ups. They provide permanent bonuses for the run and stack to high levels. You start with 2 Tome slots. Additional Tome slots become available in the Shop after completing approximately 60 Quests (current live requirement as of October 2025). Developers have indicated this requirement may be reduced in future patches. Once unlocked in the Shop, purchase slots with Silver.

Essential Tomes for Fox:

  • XP Tome: Provides +7% XP gain per level (adjusted from +9% in v1.0.12). Accelerates leveling equals faster Luck buildup. Highest priority.
  • Luck Tome: Provides +7% Luck per level. Stacks multiplicatively with Fox's passive. Second priority.
  • Quantity Tome: Provides significant projectile and coverage boosts for most ranged weapons. Game-changing screen coverage improvement.
  • Damage Tome: Multiplicative damage boost for all weapons.
  • Cooldown Tome: Reduces all weapon cooldowns for increased attack frequency.
  • Size Tome: Increases area-of-effect radius for Firestaff explosions, Aura, Lightning chains.
  • Cursed Tome: Spawns more enemies for increased XP gain. Use in Holy Trinity build.

Essential Items for Fox:

  • Clover (Common, Green): +7.5% Luck. Highest priority item. Acquire immediately.
  • Credit Card (Green) (Common, Green): +2% Luck per chest opened, permanently. Scales infinitely.
  • Sucky Magnet (Legendary, Yellow): Massive pickup range increase. S-tier game-changer. Unlock by completing all Charge Shrines in a single map.
  • Chonkplate (Legendary, Yellow): +20% Lifesteal with ability to Overheal up to 75% maximum HP. S-tier defense. Unlock by reaching 500 Maximum HP.
  • Joe's Dagger (Legendary, Yellow): 1% execute chance, +1% damage per execute permanently. S-tier scaling. Unlock with 10,000 kills using Dexecutioner.
  • Dragonfire (Legendary, Yellow): 15% burn proc. Best fire item for Firestaff builds.
  • Spicy Meatballs: Fire damage boost. Essential for fire builds.
  • Big Bonk: Damage multiplier. Stacks with multiple copies. S-tier.
  • Holy Book (Legendary, Yellow): +100 Maximum HP plus 50 HP Regeneration with damaging aura effect. Unlock by defeating the first Boss without taking damage.
  • Anvil (Legendary, Yellow): Weapon upgrades gain +1 additional stat line. Synergizes perfectly with Fox's Luck for better upgrade quality.

üìä Fox Tier Ranking & Meta Position

Fox is rated B-tier to C-tier in competitive Megabonk rankings, with variation across different community tier lists. While excellent for beginners, Fox is outclassed by specialized characters in endgame optimization.

üéØ Why B-C Tier?
  • Indirect scaling: Luck does not provide immediate combat power like critical hit or damage passives
  • Outclassed specialists: CL4NK, Dicehead, Robinette dominate competitive play
  • Firestaff is B-tier: Reliable but not meta-defining
  • Power peaks late: Needs 20+ levels to feel Luck advantage
  • Not speedrun viable: Other characters clear content faster
üí° Why Still Play Fox?
  • Best beginner character: Forgiving due to improved loot quality; teaches game mechanics
  • Immediately available: No unlock grind required
  • Fun loot farming: High Luck makes every run feel rewarding
  • Completes all content: B-tier does not mean ineffective, just not optimal
  • Unique playstyle: Only character with passive Luck scaling
  • Great for learning: Forces understanding of item quality and synergies

S-tier characters that outperform Fox: CL4NK (critical scaling), Dicehead (critical-focused), Robinette (Bow plus gold scaling), Calcium (speed scaling), Monke (survivability).

Community consensus: Fox is a serviceable starter character but an outclassed specialist. Fox serves as training wheels for new players and a fun loot-farming character for casual play, but is not competitive for leaderboards or speedruns.

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå Spending Gold Too Early

Problem: Purchasing items when Luck is low (Level 1 to 15) may waste gold on mediocre loot.
Solution: Save gold until Level 20+ (approximately 45%+ Luck with v1.0.17 passive) when shop quality may improve (shop quality effect unverified).

‚ùå Neglecting XP Tome

Problem: Slow leveling equals slow Luck buildup equals wasted passive potential.
Solution: Take XP Tome immediately. Every level matters for Fox's power curve. Current value is +7% XP per level (v1.0.12+).

‚ùå Not Stacking Luck Items

Problem: Skipping Clover, Credit Card, or Luck Tome wastes Fox's unique advantage.
Solution: Prioritize luck-stacking items. They multiply together exponentially for dramatic power increases.

‚ùå Poor Positioning

Problem: Getting too close with 100 HP and no armor results in rapid death.
Solution: Maintain medium to long range. Leverage Firestaff's range to maintain safe positioning.

‚ùå Ignoring Final Swarm Preparation

Problem: Not having survivability items when endless waves spawn after missing the teleporter at 20 minutes.
Solution: Acquire Chonkplate, Holy Book, or Mirror before 20 minutes. Endless red ghost waves are extremely deadly.
