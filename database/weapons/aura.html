🔓 How to Unlock Aura

Survive 2 consecutive minutes without taking any damage. Practical tips:

1
Choose Defensive Starts
Characters with armor/defensive passives are ideal (e.g., Sir Oofie). Luck-oriented starts can also help you find key items sooner.
2
Play Safe & Create Space
Prioritize movement speed and ranged weapons. Avoid corners and stay mobile to kite enemies.
3
Safer Early Weapons
Lightning Staff, Bow, or Revolver keep threats at distance while you focus on dodging.
4
Defensive Priorities
Look for movement speed, armor/damage reduction, and regeneration. If you get hit, reset your focus and play extra safe.

💡 Practical Tip

Keep to open areas, keep moving, and reroll for early Size/Cooldown tomes only if your economy allows. The goal is consistency—don’t greed for damage during the 2-minute window.

⚙️ Aura Weapon Mechanics

🔴 Core Functionality

  • Constant AoE: A persistent damage field centered on the player.
  • Primary Scaling: Upgrades increase damage and size.
  • Attack Frequency: Benefits from attack speed/cooldown effects that increase tick rate.
  • No Innate CC: Aura itself does not stun; stuns come from items (e.g., Lightning Orb) or certain passives.
  • Projectile Count: Quantity/projectile effects do not create multiple Auras.

📊 Growth Overview

What upgrades do

Damage Upgrades
Increase tick damage of the field.
Size Upgrades
Increase the radius/coverage area.
Attack Speed / Cooldown
Increases how often Aura deals damage.

🎯 Best Stat Priorities

  1. Size: Dramatically boosts AoE radius and safety margin.
  2. Damage: Direct multiplier to Aura’s ticks.
  3. Attack Speed / Cooldown: More frequent damage ticks.
  4. Survivability: Armor, shields, evasion—especially early.

🏗️ Aura Build Strategies

🌊 Walking Coverage
HIGH POTENTIAL

Strategy: Maximize AoE coverage via Size and steady tick rate—walk and everything melts.

🎯 Core Items

  • Size Tome (high priority)
  • Damage multipliers
  • Cooldown/attack speed effects
  • Movement speed for safer pathing

✅ Pros

  • Low APM, strong clear
  • Safe mid-range positioning
  • Scales well with tomes

❌ Cons

  • Early game can feel slow
  • Needs Size to feel powerful
⚡ Speed Synergy
A-TIER (GEAR DEPENDENT)

Strategy: Stack movement speed and convert it into offensive tempo where possible.

🎯 Core Items

  • Movement speed items
  • Turbo Socks + Turbo Skates
  • Balanced Size & Damage
  • Attack speed/cooldown support

✅ Pros

  • High mobility, hard to hit
  • Fast map coverage

❌ Cons

  • Item-dependent ceiling
  • Lower raw damage than pure size stacking
🛡️ Tank Aura
A-TIER (SAFE)

Strategy: Lean into survivability and sustain for stress-free runs.

🎯 Core Items

  • Health/armor and damage reduction
  • Lifesteal & regeneration
  • Chonkplate for overheal

✅ Pros

  • Very forgiving
  • Great for challenges

❌ Cons

  • Slower boss kills
  • Less exciting for some players

🎯 Advanced Aura Strategies

🔄 Size & Attack Speed Stacking

Push Size and tick frequency for reliable screen control. Complement with items that add control or extra damage sources.

  • Size Tome first: Coverage = safety and DPS.
  • Cooldown/AS effects: More ticks = smoother clear.
  • Item synergies: Lightning Orb (stuns), spicy/DoT effects, sustain pieces.

🎮 Positioning Mastery

Even with a passive AoE, positioning matters:

  • Center Control: Fight where spawn density is high to maximize uptime.
  • Corner Avoidance: Don’t get pinned; keep kiting lanes open.
  • Boss Movement: Keep moving to maintain safe distance while ticks work.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • Neglecting Size: Without Size scaling, Aura feels underwhelming.
  • Standing Still: You still need to path and kite.
  • Expecting Multi-Aura: Quantity/projectile count doesn’t create multiple Auras.

🏆 Mastery Goal

Cover most of the screen with safe, consistent ticks while staying mobile. When enemies fade before reaching you, you’ve hit the sweet spot.
