Cactus

Kill 100 cactus enemies with Thorns damage

0.56% Unlocked - RAREST
Difficulty:
‚òÖ ‚òÖ ‚òÖ ‚òÖ ‚òÖ
‚ö†Ô∏è THE RAREST ACHIEVEMENT
Only 0.56% of players have this - the absolute hardest in Megabonk. Requires multiple layers of RNG: cactus enemies must spawn (rare), you need Thorns Tome upgrades (Athena Tome Level 9), and you must let cactuses HIT you so thorns kill them. Use "Hyper" challenge on Desert for more spawns.
üéØ Required Setup
  • Athena - +2 Thorns per level (unlock Thorns Tome Lv 9)
  • Aegis weapon - Block attacks for thorns damage
  • Desert stages - Only place cactus spawn
  • Stack Thorns items - Spiky Shield, Quin's Mask
  • Let cactuses HIT you to trigger thorns retaliation
üí° Grinding Strategy
Play Desert Tier 1 "Hyper" challenge for doubled enemy spawns. Requires 20-50+ runs due to RNG. Hug cactuses to let them hit you while thorns kills them. Track progress carefully - kills only count if thorns damage is the killing blow. Extreme patience required.

Chaos Tome

Charge every Charge Shrine on a Tier 3 run across all 3 stages without leaving the charge zone

<2% Unlocked
Difficulty:
‚òÖ ‚òÖ ‚òÖ ‚òÖ ‚òÖ
‚ö†Ô∏è Precision Challenge
Complete Tier 3 (all 3 stages) and charge EVERY charge shrine (15 per stage, 45 total) without leaving the charge circle even for a SPLIT SECOND. Leaving the zone even once voids the entire run. Knockback can ruin attempts. No counter to track progress - must count manually.
üéØ Recommended Setup
  • Tony McZoom or Calcium - High speed for map coverage
  • Weapons: Blood Magic, Aegis, Lightning Staff
  • Tomes: Quantity, Cooldown, Agility, XP
  • Must have enough damage to clear while stationary
  • Check pause menu shrine count (adds +3 per stage)
üí° Critical Tips
Map has 15 charge shrines per stage. Pause menu shows shrine count (includes 3 non-charge shrines + Greed Shrines). Move carefully around map scanning for hidden shrines behind trees. Avoid charging during mini-boss spawns or swarm events. Exit zone knockback = instant fail. Count shrines manually with screenshots.
