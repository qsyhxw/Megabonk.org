üîì How to Unlock Frostwalker

Unlocking Frostwalker is a unique challenge that requires dedication and luck. Follow this comprehensive guide to add this powerful crowd control weapon to your arsenal:

  1. Find the Ice Cube Item
    The Ice Cube is a Legendary item, making it extremely rare. It can appear from:
    • Chest drops (higher tier chests have better chances)
    • Shrine rewards
    • Lucky random spawns during runs
    Focus on opening every chest you find and using shrines frequently to increase your chances.
  2. Understand the Freeze Mechanic
    Once you have Ice Cube equipped, every projectile or attack has a 20% chance to freeze enemies. That's roughly 1 in 5 hits. The freeze temporarily disables enemies and counts toward your unlock progress.
  3. Maximize Your Hit Rate
    To reach 1,000 freezes efficiently:
    • Use weapons with high projectile count (Lightning Staff, Firestaff)
    • Focus on attack speed upgrades
    • Survive to late game where enemy density is highest
    • Target large swarms rather than single enemies
  4. Farm Freezes Across Multiple Runs
    You don't need to get all 1,000 freezes in one run. Progress is cumulative across all runs where you have Ice Cube equipped. Each successful run with 100-200 freezes brings you closer to the goal.
  5. Track Your Progress
    Use the interactive freeze counter below to manually track your progress. The game tracks this internally, but monitoring helps you estimate how many more runs you'll need.
  6. Complete the Unlock
    After reaching 1,000 total freezes with Ice Cube, return to the Main Menu, navigate to Unlocks > Weapons, and purchase Frostwalker for 2 Silver.

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå Prioritizing Projectile Count

Projectile Count does NOTHING for Frostwalker. It's a duration-based weapon, not a projectile weapon. Focus on Duration and Size instead.

‚ùå Standing Still During Boss Fights

Your only defense is the frozen trail. If you stop moving, you lose your primary survival tool. Keep moving constantly, even in small circles.

‚ùå Giving Up on Ice Cube Farming

Yes, it's rare, but finding Ice Cube is worth the effort. Many players give up too early. Keep farming chests - the payoff is huge.

‚ùå Ignoring Movement Speed

Movement speed directly impacts how much area you cover and how effective your kiting is. It's a core stat for Frostwalker builds.

‚ùå Using Frostwalker as Your Only Weapon

While strong for survival, Frostwalker alone lacks burst damage for bosses. Always carry a high-DPS secondary weapon like Sniper Rifle or Lightning Staff.
