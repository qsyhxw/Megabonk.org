‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best character in Megabonk?
Dicehead and CL4NK are currently the strongest S-tier characters in version 1.0.17, with unlimited crit scaling potential and glass cannon builds respectively. Dicehead excels with consistent crit damage while CL4NK offers high-risk, high-reward gameplay.
How often are Megabonk tier lists updated?
Our tier lists are updated immediately after each game patch. The most recent update for version 1.0.17 ensures all rankings reflect the current meta, balance changes, and competitive strategies.
What makes a weapon S-tier in Megabonk?
S-tier weapons demonstrate meta dominance with consistent leaderboard presence, strong synergy potential, and game-changing mechanics. Examples include Aura's 360¬∞ area damage, Dice's RNG multipliers, and weapons that enable powerful build archetypes.
Should I follow tier lists for casual play?
Tier lists are primarily optimized for competitive play and leaderboard climbing. For casual play, all characters and weapons are viable - choose what fits your preferred playstyle and have fun experimenting with different combinations.
What is the Holy Trinity in Megabonk?
The Holy Trinity refers to the three essential S-tier tomes: XP Tome, Curse Tome, and Luck Tome. This combination provides accelerated leveling, enhanced drop quality, and powerful synergies that are fundamental to competitive runs.
