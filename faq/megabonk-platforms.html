Megabonk Platforms Guide

Is Megabonk on Switch, Xbox, or PS5? Here is the 2026 platform breakdown.

Current Platform Availability (2026)

Since its release on September 18, 2025, players have been eager to know what platforms Megabonk is available on. As a highly addictive roguelike survival game, it is perfectly suited for handhelds and consoles, but officially, the game is currently a PC exclusive.

Platform Status Notes
Windows PC (Steam) ✅ Available Primary platform
Linux (Steam) ✅ Available Native support
Nintendo Switch ❌ Not Available Most requested port
Xbox Series X|S / Xbox One ❌ Not Available No current plans
PlayStation 5 / PS4 ❌ Not Available No current plans
💡 Want to play with a controller? The PC version of Megabonk features full controller support right out of the box! You can plug in your Xbox or DualSense controller to play your favorite Megabonk Builds from your couch.

Will Megabonk Come to Nintendo Switch or Xbox?

Because the gameplay loop of Megabonk is reminiscent of titles like Vampire Survivors, many fans consider it a perfect fit for the Nintendo Switch and Xbox ecosystems.

The developer, vedinad, has briefly addressed these requests within the official community Discord. When asked about a console release, vedinad stated:

"Maybe? I think porting it can be a bit time consuming though, I also don't know anything about it."
Developer Context: Vedinad is a solo developer. Porting a game to Nintendo Switch, Xbox, or PS5 requires separate developer kits, platform certifications, and optimization processes that are notoriously difficult for a one-person team to manage while simultaneously updating the core PC game.

While a console port remains a "cool idea" for the future, there is absolutely no official timeline or guarantee that Megabonk will release on Switch, Xbox, or PlayStation.

Playing on Handhelds: Steam Deck & ROG Ally

If you prefer the console experience, the best way to play Megabonk right now is via PC handhelds.

  • Steam Deck: Megabonk runs flawlessly on Steam OS via Proton. It is an ideal way to play the game on the go, effectively serving as a substitute for a Nintendo Switch version.
  • ASUS ROG Ally & Lenovo Legion Go: Since these devices run native Windows, Megabonk will install and play natively through Steam with full integrated controller detection.
