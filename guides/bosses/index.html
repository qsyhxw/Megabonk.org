General Boss Strategy Tips

🛡️ Defensive Priority

Always prioritize survivability over damage output. A dead character deals zero DPS. Build resistances appropriate for each boss type.

🎯 Learn Attack Patterns

Every boss has predictable attack patterns and timing windows. Study these patterns and practice the timing until they become muscle memory.

⚔️ Weapon Selection

Different bosses favor different weapon types. Ranged weapons for mobile bosses, AOE for minion-spawning bosses, sustained DPS for tanks.

🏃 Positioning Mastery

Movement is your primary defense. Learn optimal positioning for each phase and always have an escape route planned before engaging.

💊 Resource Management

Save your best healing items and abilities for later phases when bosses become more dangerous. Use weaker heals early to stay topped off.

🧠 Mental Preparation

Boss fights are marathons, not sprints. Stay calm under pressure and stick to your strategy even when things look desperate.
