Character Overview

Master the art of speed-based combat with Calcium's unique mechanics

⚡ RNG Blessing: Speed Demon

Calcium's speed-based power system works as follows:

  • Speed Buildup: Movement speed increases over time during combat
  • Damage Scaling: Higher speed = higher damage output
  • Damage Penalty: Speed is halved when you take ANY damage
  • Risk/Reward: High damage potential but requires perfect damage avoidance

This makes Calcium a high-skill, high-reward character perfect for experienced players.

⚠️ Critical Warning: Damage = Speed Loss

Taking damage halves your speed (and damage) - avoid it at all costs!

  • One hit can ruin minutes of speed buildup
  • Perfect positioning and timing are essential
  • Consider health items only as emergency backup
  • Focus on mobility and avoidance over tanking

✅ Strengths

  • Infinite Speed Scaling: Speed builds up indefinitely
  • High Damage Potential: Speed converts to damage
  • Bone Weapon: Unique projectile with good range
  • Mobility Master: Outrun any enemy or projectile
  • Exciting Gameplay: Fast-paced, skill-based combat

❌ Weaknesses

  • Damage Vulnerability: One hit ruins speed buildup
  • High Skill Floor: Requires excellent positioning
  • Unlock Requirements: Need to kill 1,000 skeletons first
  • Inconsistent Power: Performance varies based on damage taken
  • Stress Inducing: Constant pressure to avoid damage
