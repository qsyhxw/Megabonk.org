Overview

The Knockback Tome increases the distance enemies are pushed back when hit by your attacks. This effect is weapon-dependent - it only works with weapons that have built-in knockback effects in their upgrade paths.

The exact per-level percentage increase is not publicly documented in official sources. Community analyses suggest that most Tomes (except Damage Tome which uses multiplicative scaling) stack additively rather than multiplicatively.

Critical Note: This Tome is completely ineffective with weapons that lack knockback in their base mechanics or upgrade options. Always verify your weapon has knockback before investing points.

How Knockback Works

  • Weapon Dependency: Only affects weapons that have knockback in their upgrade list or base effects
  • Distance Scaling: Increases how far enemies are pushed back per hit
  • Likely Additive Stacking: Based on community analysis, levels stack additively (not multiplicatively)
  • Crowd Control Tool: Creates space between you and enemies, useful for defensive positioning
  • Boss Limitations: Developers have mentioned plans to limit maximum knockback on boss enemies
Source: Steam community guides (Sept-Oct 2025) and technical analysis suggest additive stacking for most Tomes except Damage Tome which uses multiplicative scaling.
