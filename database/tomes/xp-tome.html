The XP Tome increases the total amount of experience the player earns in Megabonk, leading to faster level-ups and earlier access to stronger choices. By accelerating progression, it enables builds to come online sooner and snowball more reliably.

Overview

  • Stat: XP Gain (Experience Multiplier)
  • Base upgrade per level: +7% XP gain
  • How to unlock: Break 20 pots in a single run
  • Max level: 99
  • Category: Economy / Tempo

XP Tome is one of the most powerful tempo Tomes in Megabonk. By increasing experience gain, you level up faster, which means more upgrade opportunities, earlier power spikes, and stronger overall builds.
