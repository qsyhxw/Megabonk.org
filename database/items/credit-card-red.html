How It Works

Credit Card (Red) grants a flat +2.5% Damage when you open a chest. The bonus stacks per chest opened during the run (community-verified from player testing).

Verification Notes
  • Effect value and wording are consistent across multiple guides and databases.
  • Stacking behavior is based on community consensus and Steam/Reddit testing threads.
  • No official patch note references a change to this item as of v1.0.17 (Oct 19, 2025).

Tips (Community)

  • Picking it early increases total stacks, since you’ll open more chests across the run.
  • Pairs conceptually with any strategy that helps you open more chests (keys, chest routes), but exact item synergies vary by build.
