Overview

Duration Tome increases how long attacks and projectiles persist in the world before disappearing. This creates longer-lasting effects, better battlefield control, and more opportunities for damage. Particularly powerful with lingering projectiles and AoE zones.

Key Benefit: Projectiles and effects that linger in the world will stay active significantly longer, covering more area and hitting more enemies over their lifetime. This tome transforms short-lived attacks into persistent battlefield control tools.

Strategy Guide

  • Best used with weapons or skills that linger, like AoE fields, spinning attacks, or slow projectiles
  • Not as strong on weapons with instant-hit attacks
  • Strong pick for players who want more screen control and safer zoning
  • Excellent for area denial builds that keep enemies at bay
  • Combine with Size for maximum battlefield dominance
  • Particularly powerful in boss fights with adds
  • Test with your weapon first - if it doesn't create lasting effects, skip Duration
