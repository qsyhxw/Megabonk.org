Overview

Gym Sauce is a simple but reliable damage booster for any run. It adds a flat +10% Damage and is available from the start, so it often appears early and helps you stabilize your build. If you are browsing more items, see the complete items index.

Practical Use & Pairing Ideas

Because Gym Sauce provides unconditional +10% Damage, it works with most archetypes. Players often pair it with sustained-DPS weapons or early-game farming builds.

Firestaff — consistent tick damage benefits from early multipliers.

Aura — map control and crowd damage scale nicely with flat buffs.

Bow — steady single-target pressure appreciates universal boosts.

Strategy here is community advice, not additional hidden stats. Always prioritize survivability if playing high-aggro builds.
