Easy Challenges (30)

‚úÖ Perfect Starting Point

These challenges introduce core mechanics and can be completed by any beginner. Focus on these first to unlock rewards that make harder challenges easier!

1

Survive 10 Minutes

Easy
Complete a full 10-minute run without dying on any stage. This is the gateway challenge that teaches you survival basics.
üìã Requirements:
  • Survive for exactly 10:00 minutes
  • Any character, any stage
  • Can take damage (just don't die)
üí° Strategy:
  • Use Sir Oofie for maximum survivability (+1% Armor per level)
  • Focus on defensive upgrades early (HP, Armor, Shield)
  • Keep moving constantly - never stand still
  • Prioritize XP gems to level up faster for more upgrades
  • Take Aegis Shield if offered - it's the best defensive item
üõ°Ô∏è Recommended: Sir Oofie
üéÅ Rewards:

Unlocks: Basic Tome, 500 Gold, Achievement Badge

Easy
Reach a total kill count of 1,000 enemies in a single run. This teaches you the importance of AOE weapons and positioning.
üìã Requirements:
  • 1,000 total kills in one run
  • All enemy types count
  • Time limit: None
üí° Strategy:
  • Aura weapon is perfect for this (360¬∞ passive damage)
  • Take Curse Tome to spawn more enemies
  • Stay near enemy spawn points to maximize kill rate
  • Size Tome + Aura = massive kill zone
  • Typically takes 15-20 minutes at normal pace
üí™ Recommended: Megachad (Aura starting weapon)
üéÅ Rewards:

Easy
Level up to 20 in a single run. This introduces the importance of XP farming and the XP Tome.
üìã Requirements:
  • Reach character level 20
  • Single run requirement
  • XP from all sources counts
üí° Strategy:
  • XP Tome is mandatory - find it within first 5 minutes
  • Chase every blue XP gem aggressively
  • Use Magnet shrines after big fights to vacuum gems
  • Clear dense enemy packs for XP gem showers
  • Should reach level 20 around minute 15-18
ü¶ä Recommended: Fox (Luck helps find XP Tome faster)
üéÅ Rewards:

Unlocks: XP Booster Item, Experience Shrine

Defeat a Boss

Easy
Defeat any boss in the game. The easiest is Skeleton King at the 10-minute mark.
üìã Requirements:
  • Kill any boss enemy
  • Skeleton King spawns at 10:00
  • Must be the killing blow
üí° Strategy:
  • Wait for Skeleton King at 10-minute mark (easiest)
  • Stay at medium range to dodge bone throws
  • Immediately kill the skeleton adds he summons
  • Lightning Staff or Aura work great for damage
  • Have at least 50% HP before engaging
‚öîÔ∏è Recommended: Sir Oofie (tanky enough to survive mistakes)
üéÅ Rewards:

Unlocks: Boss Buster Item, 2,000 Gold

Easy
Survive for 10 minutes without pressing any movement keys. Requires specific AFK build setup.
üìã Requirements:
  • Survive 10 minutes completely AFK
  • Cannot press WASD or movement
  • Can look around with mouse
üí° Strategy:
  • MANDATORY: Megachad with Aura + Aegis + Idle Juice
  • Idle Juice doubles damage while standing still
  • Stack Size Tome to maximize Aura range
  • Get Mirror for damage reflection
  • Position in corner of map before going AFK
  • See our Walking Apocalypse build
üí™ Recommended: Megachad (only viable character for this)
üéÅ Rewards:

Unlocks: Idle Juice (if not owned), AFK Master Badge, 3,000 Gold

Recommended Completion Order

üéØ Optimal Path to 100/100

Follow this order to unlock items and characters that make later challenges easier:

Phase 1: Foundation (Challenges 1-30)

  • Complete all easy challenges first
  • Unlock XP Tome, Luck Tome, basic items
  • Learn game mechanics thoroughly
  • Expected time: 10-15 hours

Phase 2: Building Power (Challenges 31-65)

  • Focus on medium challenges that unlock strong items
  • Unlock Curse Tome (game-changing)
  • Master Holy Trinity strategy
  • Unlock all characters
  • Expected time: 30-40 hours

Phase 3: Mastery (Challenges 66-90)

  • Hard challenges require full mastery of mechanics
  • Focus on character-specific challenges
  • Work on boss perfect kills
  • Begin long survival runs
  • Expected time: 50-80 hours

Phase 4: Elite Goals (Challenges 91-100)

  • Extreme challenges are long-term goals
  • Some may take 50+ attempts
  • Focus on one at a time
  • Join community for support and strategies
  • Expected time: 100+ hours
