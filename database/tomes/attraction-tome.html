🧲 Attraction Tome

B-TIER

Magnetic Pull - Collect Resources from Afar Without Risk

Quality of Life Utility Tome
📊 Stat Type Pickup Range
⬆️ Upgrade +75%
🔓 Unlock Shrine x8
🎯 Max Level 99

The Attraction Tome makes it easier to collect resources in Megabonk. With each level, your pickup range expands, so XP orbs, items, and other drops fly to you without needing to walk over them. This is especially helpful in chaotic runs where moving around is risky.

Overview

  • Stat: Pickup Range (Magnetic Pull)
  • Base upgrade per level: +75% pickup range
  • How to unlock: Use the Shrine 8 times
  • Max level: 99

Attraction is one of the most universally appreciated quality-of-life Tomes. While it doesn't directly increase your damage or survivability, it dramatically improves gameplay feel and efficiency by reducing risky movement and wasted time.

Effects

  • Greatly increases how far you can pick up XP orbs and items
  • Improves tempo by reducing wasted movement
  • Reduces risk since you don't need to step into dangerous areas for pickups
  • Makes chaotic high-density encounters more manageable
  • Ensures you never miss valuable drops due to positioning

How to Unlock

⛩️
The Shrine
Random event during runs. Provides powerful temporary buffs in exchange for a cost. Appears occasionally on the map.
📋 Unlock Requirement
Use the Shrine 8 times
This can take several runs depending on shrine availability.
💡 Tips for Unlocking
• Explore thoroughly during runs to find shrines
• Use the shrine whenever you encounter it, even if the buff isn't ideal
• Progress is cumulative across all runs
• Some stages have higher shrine spawn rates
• Typically unlocks after 5-10 full runs

Quality of Life vs Efficiency

🧲 Utility Analysis

Attraction Tome provides both comfort and competitive advantages:

✨ Quality of Life
Dramatically improves gameplay feel. No more chasing XP orbs or missing drops. Reduces frustration and mental load during chaotic encounters.
⚡ Efficiency Boost
Saves time and movement. Faster leveling, less risky positioning, better resource collection. Measurable performance improvement.
🛡️ Risk Reduction
Don't need to enter danger zones for pickups. Stay in safe positions while resources fly to you. Indirect survivability boost.
🎯 Resource Optimization
Never miss XP orbs, gold, or item drops. Especially valuable in high-density encounters where items scatter widely.

Synergies

XP Tome

Strategy Guide

  • Amazing quality-of-life Tome that also boosts efficiency
  • Very strong when farming XP-heavy runs
  • Less effective if you already have extremely high mobility
  • Particularly valuable in boss fights with adds
  • Essential for high-density survival runs
  • Great for reducing stress in cluttered battlefields

Best Use Cases

  • All players - Universal quality of life improvement
  • XP farming runs - Never miss experience orbs
  • High-density encounters - Chaotic battlefields with many drops
  • Stationary builds - Don't need to move much to collect
  • Currency farming - Ensure all gold/silver is collected
  • Safe positioning strategies - Stay out of danger while collecting

Pro Tips

  • Once unlocked, it becomes one of the most useful utility Tomes
  • At max level (99), provides 7,425% pickup range increase!
  • Pairs exceptionally well with XP-focused strategies
  • Reduces mental load - you can focus on combat instead of pickup management
  • Particularly valuable when using stationary or slow weapons
  • Can be the difference between life and death in high-pressure situations
