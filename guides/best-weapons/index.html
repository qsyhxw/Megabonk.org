🏆 S-Tier Universal Weapons

These weapons excel on every character and in every situation. Prioritize unlocking and mastering these first.

S-TIER

Tank builds, AFK strategies, beginners

S-TIER

🔥 DoT 📈 Scales 🆓 Default
✅ Best For:
Kiting, crowd control, speed builds
🎲 Dice
S-TIER
Critical • Dicehead's Weapon
The most broken weapon in the game. Permanently increases crit chance with each throw. Infinite scaling potential that only gets stronger.
♾️ Infinite Scaling 💥 Crit 👑 OP
✅ Best For:
Dicehead, crit builds, leaderboard runs

S-TIER
Shield • Athena's Weapon

Athena, Sir Oofie, defensive builds

Ninja, Calcium, speed builds

👥 Best Weapons by Character

Maximize synergy with character-specific recommendations

🦊 Fox (Speed/Luck)

🔥 Flamewalker ⚡ Lightning Staff 🔮 Aura 🍌 Bananarang
Fox's high movement speed synergizes perfectly with Flamewalker's fire trail. Luck passive benefits from weapons with on-hit effects.

🎲 Dicehead (Crit Scaling)

🎲 Dice 🗾 Katana ⚡ Lightning Staff 🏹 Bow
Dice is mandatory - it's Dicehead's signature weapon with infinite crit scaling. Stack precision and crit damage for megacrits.

🤖 CL4NK (Crit Chance)

🔫 Revolver 🦴 Bone 🍌 Bananarang 🔮 Aura
Revolver's multi-bullets maximize CL4NK's crit chance passive. Glass cannon build focused on critical strikes.

🛡️ Sir Oofie (Tank)

🗡️ Sword 🔮 Aura 🛡️ Aegis ⚡ Lightning Staff
Sword is Sir Oofie's default. Aegis unlocks by blocking 500 damage with him. Aura provides defensive AoE coverage.

🗡️ Bandit (Attack Speed)

⚡ Dexecutioner 🗾 Katana ⚡ Lightning Staff 🏹 Bow
Bandit's +1% attack speed per level makes Dexecutioner's execute chance trigger constantly. Best Kevin+Mirror abuser.

💪 Megachad (Tank)

🔮 Aura 🛡️ Aegis 🪓 Axe ❄️ Frostwalker
Aura is Megachad's default and core weapon. Perfect for AFK builds with Idle Juice. Size + Damage scaling is key.

🥷 Ninja (Evasion)

🗾 Katana ⚡ Lightning Staff 🏹 Bow 🔮 Aura
Katana is Ninja's signature weapon. Execute passive triggers on evasion. High mobility weapons complement evasion playstyle.

🦴 Calcium (Speed Demon)

🦴 Bone 🔫 Revolver 🔥 Flamewalker 🏹 Bow
Bone is Calcium's default. Flamewalker synergizes with constant movement. Speed converts to damage - never stop moving.

🩸 Vlad (Lifesteal)

🩸 Blood Magic ⚡ Lightning Staff 🦴 Bone 🔮 Aura
Blood Magic is mandatory - Vlad's signature weapon. Stack lifesteal and maintain low HP for maximum damage.

⏰ Best Weapons by Game Stage

Optimize your weapon choices throughout the run

🌱 Early Game (0-10 Minutes)

Priority: Survival & XP Farming
🔥 Flamewalker 🔮 Aura 🗡️ Sword ⚡ Lightning Staff
Best Choices: Flamewalker (default, no unlock), Sword (Sir Oofie default), or any 360° weapon like Aura.
Strategy: Focus on crowd control and safe farming. Avoid damage, stay mobile, collect XP gems.
Avoid: High-skill weapons like Dexecutioner or Sniper Rifle. You need reliable, safe damage.

⚔️ Mid Game (10-25 Minutes)

Priority: Build Foundation & Scaling
🎲 Dice 🗾 Katana ⚡ Dexecutioner 🔫 Revolver
Best Additions: Dice (if unlocked), Katana, Revolver for multi-projectile.
Strategy: Start stacking weapon upgrades. Prioritize Quantity and Damage tomes. Begin scaling your build.
Key Milestone: By 15 minutes, you should have 3-4 weapons fully upgraded with solid tome foundation.

🔥 Late Game (25-45 Minutes)

Priority: Maximum DPS & Boss Killing
🎲 Dice ⚡ Dexecutioner 🗾 Katana 🛡️ Aegis
Optimization: Dice with infinite crit scaling dominates. Dexecutioner melts bosses. Aegis for defense.
Strategy: Complete your 4-weapon loadout. Max out key tomes. Prepare for Final Swarm.
Boss Focus: Dexecutioner + high attack speed = boss destroyer. Stack execute chance and damage.

👑 Endgame (45+ Minutes / Leaderboard)

Priority: Infinite Scaling & Survivability
🎲 Dice 🔮 Aura 🛡️ Aegis ⚡ Dexecutioner
Meta Weapons: Dice (infinite scaling), Aura (consistent AoE), Aegis (defense), Dexecutioner (boss killer).
Final Swarm: Ghost enemies with unique behavior. Executes and Black Hole less effective.
Leaderboard Strategy: Prioritize weapons with unlimited scaling potential. Dice > Aura > everything else.

⚠️ Weapons to Avoid

Low-tier weapons that will waste your upgrade slots

⚠️ Important Note

These weapons aren't necessarily "bad" - they're just outclassed by meta options. In a casual run, any weapon can work. For competitive/leaderboard play, avoid these.

C-Tier: Situational at Best

🌪️ Tornado
C-TIER • Defensive utility

Why Avoid: Low base damage (8), purely defensive. Birdo-specific synergy only. Better defensive options exist (Aegis).

🚀 Slutty Rocket
C-TIER • Homing

Why Avoid: Requires 15,000 CL4NK kills to unlock. Better ranged options available much earlier.

🎭 Wireless Dagger
C-TIER • Remote damage

Why Avoid: Inconsistent targeting. Requires Lightning Staff level 15 unlock. Not worth the investment.

D-Tier: Generally Not Worth Taking

💣 Mines
D-TIER • Trap weapon

Why Avoid: Requires precise positioning. Enemies must walk over mines. Better AoE options exist. Literally ranked D-tier across all community sources.

🔫 Shotgun
C-TIER • Close range

Why Avoid: 5% RNG drop from Desert tumbleweeds. Close range required. Revolver and Bow are better ranged options.

🗡️ Hero Sword
C-TIER • Trophy weapon

Why Avoid: Extreme unlock (no items/shrines entire run). Not powerful enough to justify the challenge. Trophy unlock only.

🚫 Never Take These If You Have Better Options
  • Mines: Worst weapon in the game by community consensus. Only take if literally nothing else available.
  • Tornado: Only viable on Birdo for air combat synergy. Dead weight on all other characters.
  • Hero Sword: Not worth the unlock challenge. Regular Sword is 90% as effective and free.
  • Shotgun: Close range requirement contradicts survival strategy. Revolver is better in every way.

💡 Pro Tips for Weapon Selection

1. Prioritize Default Unlocked Weapons Early

Flamewalker, Firestaff, Sword are available from game start. Master these before grinding for advanced unlocks.

2. Always Have One 360° Weapon

Aura or Flamewalker in every build. They provide consistent damage and safety regardless of positioning.

3. Match Weapons to Character Passives

Dice on Dicehead, Katana on Ninja, Revolver on CL4NK. Character-weapon synergy is more important than tier lists.

4. Upgrade Quantity First

More projectiles = more hits = more on-hit effects = more damage. Quantity Tome is secretly the best tome.

5. Don't Sleep on Attack Speed

Attack speed affects: weapon DPS, lifesteal healing, Kevin+Mirror combo, on-hit effects, execute procs. Universal stat.

6. Unlock Weapons Strategically

Prioritize: Katana (5% Desert drop) > Aura (2 min no damage) > Aegis (500 block) > Dice (Luck Tome 12) > Dexecutioner (12,500 sword kills)
