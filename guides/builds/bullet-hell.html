üéÆ Gameplay Strategy

Phase 1: Early Game (0-10 minutes)

Building Your Arsenal

Priority Goals:

  • Unlock CL4NK (Forest Tier 1 + 7,500 kills + purchase with Silver)
  • Get XP Tome first - you need levels for "Crit Happens" passive
  • Pick up Forbidden Juice as soon as possible for +10% crit
  • Start with Lightning Staff, add Revolver when available
  • Maintain distance from enemies - you're squishy early
üí° Your crit rate is still building - focus on survivability
üí° Kite enemies in circles while shooting
Phase 2: Mid Game (10-20 minutes)

Crit Stacking Phase

Key Actions:

  • You should be level 40-60 now (40-60% base crit rate)
  • Add Firestaff for the third projectile weapon
  • Stack Battery or Turbo Skates for attack speed
  • Multiple Forbidden Juice stacks push you toward 100% crit
  • Start taking Luck Tome and Curse Tome for Holy Trinity
üí° 60% crit + Forbidden Juice = near-constant crits
üí° Keep maximum distance - let projectiles do the work
Phase 3: Late Game (20+ minutes)

True Bullet Hell

Optimization:

  • You should be level 100+ (100%+ crit chance from passive alone)
  • Every shot is now a guaranteed critical hit
  • Screen is completely filled with projectiles and explosions
  • Enemies die before they can reach you
  • Bosses melt in seconds under sustained crit DPS
  • Push to 200+ levels for maximum power scaling
üí° You're now at peak power - play aggressively
üí° Position to hit multiple enemies with bounces and chains
