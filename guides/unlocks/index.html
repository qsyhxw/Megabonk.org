Megabonk 100% Unlock Guide

Tired of searching for a "Megabonk Unlock All" mod? Save the cheating for other games. Here are the exact requirements to earn every secret character and hidden legendary tome legitimately.

Because Megabonk restricts its most overpowered characters and items behind hardcore achievements, discovering the unlock conditions is half the challenge. We've compiled the solutions for the most frequently asked secrets in the game below.

👤 Hidden Characters

Kevin

Secret Character

Unlock Requirement

Collect exactly 3 Leeching Crystals in a single run.

Strategy: The Leeching Crystal is an item that only appears in the RNG pool. Play on Forest Tier 1 where survivability is easy, stack as much Luck (Luck Tome) as possible, and banish other items to force the crystals into your loot pool.

🦍

Monke

Hidden Objective

Unlock Requirement

Locate the hidden Banana in the Forest map, then find and destroy Monke's cage.

Strategy: Wander off the beaten path in Forest Tier 1 until you spot the cage. Then wander until you locate a giant yellow banana. Pick it up and carry it back to the cage to release him.

💀

Calcium (Skeleton)

Grind

Unlock Requirement

Kill a total of 1,000 Skeleton enemies globally across all runs.

Strategy: This will occur naturally if you play the Undead/Graveyard Stage frequently. Once unlocked, be sure to check out our Best Skeleton Build guide.

📓 Legendary Items & Tomes

Chaos Tome

Insane Challenge

Unlock Requirement

In a Tier 3 run, fully charge all Shrines across all 3 stages without ever leaving the Shrine aura or getting knocked out of it.

Strategy: The hardest challenge in Megabonk. If a boss pushes you out of the blue circle by a single pixel, you must restart the entire mode. Use heavily defensive builds with knockback resistance.

🗡️

Hero Sword

Purist Run

Unlock Requirement

Defeat any Stage Boss without picking up dropped items, treasure chests, or Shrine buffs.

Strategy: You are allowed to pick up XP gems and choose level-up options. However, you cannot interact with shrines or pick up items on the floor (like the Wrench or Turbo Socks). It is a test of pure macro scaling.

Holy Book

Legendary Defense

Unlock Requirement

Defeat a Stage 1 boss without taking a single tick of damage.

Strategy: Go to Forest Tier 1. Activate the Fragile difficulty modifier in the menu before you start (which instantly kills you if damaged). Build pure long-range damage and move cautiously.

Unlocks FAQ

How do you unlock Kevin in Megabonk?

To unlock Kevin, you must collect exactly three 'Leeching Crystals' within a single run. The best way to achieve this is on Forest Tier 1 by banishing unrelated items to force the RNG to drop the blue-rarity crystals.

What are the unlock requirements for Chaos Tome?

The Chaos Tome requires you to fully charge all Shrines across all three stages of a Tier 3 run. You must not step out of the Shrine's aura at any point while it charges, and you cannot be knocked out of the zone by enemy attacks.

How to unlock the Hero Sword?

You must defeat a Stage Boss in any run without collecting a single dropped item, power-up chest, or Shrine buff. You are only allowed to accept the standard upgrade choices gained from leveling up via XP pickups.

How do I get the Holy Book?

You must defeat a first-stage boss without taking any damage during the entire run. We highly recommend activating the 'Fragile' modifier when attempting this to quickly restart if you get hit.

Is there a Megabonk Unlock All cheat or mod?

There is no official 'Unlock All' button. Megabonk is structured around completing specific mastery challenges. You will need to fulfill the hidden objectives to earn each character and item legitimately.
