Community Debate: Bandit's tier ranking is highly controversial. Some players rank him S-tier when optimized with perfect items (especially Kevin+Mirror combo), while others rank him C-tier due to his mediocre performance without specific itemization.

üí° Pro Tips & Advanced Strategies

‚úì Kevin+Mirror Mastery

Bandit is THE best Kevin+Mirror character. His passive attack speed makes overlapping invulnerability frames easier than any other character. Master this combo for S-tier performance.

‚úì Stack Attack Speed First

Prioritize Battery, Cooldown Tome, and Time Bracelet over damage items. Attack speed multiplies the effectiveness of EVERYTHING in your build.

‚úì Overpowered Lamp Synergy

+1 proc chance doubles on-hit effects. With Bandit's attack speed, this creates exponential scaling for poison, lightning, and execute builds.

‚ö†Ô∏è Item Dependency

Bandit requires specific items to reach S-tier. Without Kevin, Mirror, Battery, and Joe's Dagger, he's average. Know when to restart if RNG is bad.

‚ö†Ô∏è Dexecutioner Reliability

Primary weapon is gimmicky without Luck. Execute chance is low base. Stack Luck Tome or focus on secondary weapons (Katana, Lightning Staff).

‚úó Don't Forget Defense

Kevin+Mirror isn't true immortality post-nerf. Gaps between invulnerability exist. Layer additional defense (Lifesteal, Shield Tome, Aegis) to fill gaps.

üîì How to Unlock Bandit

Bandit requires finding and defeating the hidden Bandit boss in the Desert map. This is a challenging unlock requiring specific knowledge of boss location.

1

Reach the Desert Map

Progress through the game until you unlock the Desert area. This typically requires completing Forest stages first.

2

Find the Hidden Bandit Boss

Explore the Desert map thoroughly looking for the Bandit boss. He appears as a hidden enemy that must be fought. Location varies by run.

3

Defeat the Bandit Boss

The Bandit boss has high damage and mobility. Use ranged weapons and kiting strategies. Bring defensive items for survivability.

4

Purchase the Dexecutioner Weapon

After defeating Bandit, you must purchase the Dexecutioner weapon using Silver currency before you can play as Bandit.

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚úó Taking Kevin Without Mirror

Kevin without synergy is run-ending. Constant self-damage with no benefit kills you. Always toggle Kevin off if you don't have Mirror + attack speed.

‚úó Ignoring Battery

Battery (+8% attack speed) is essential for Kevin+Mirror combo. Without it, Kevin proc rate is too slow for effective invulnerability overlap.

‚úó Over-Relying on Dexecutioner

Primary weapon execute chance is unreliable without heavy Luck investment. Use secondary weapons (Katana, Lightning Staff) for consistent damage.

‚úó Skipping Defensive Layers

Kevin+Mirror isn't infinite immunity anymore. Gaps between i-frames exist. Stack Lifesteal, Shield Tome, and Aegis to survive gaps.

‚úó Not Stacking Cooldown Tome

Cooldown Tome reduces Kevin trigger delay. Critical for maximizing invulnerability frequency. Priority tome for Kevin+Mirror builds.

‚úó Forcing Bad Builds

If you don't get Kevin, Mirror, or Battery by minute 15, don't force the build. Pivot to execute/on-hit build or restart.

üîó Best Item Synergies

Kevin + Mirror + Battery

The holy trinity of defense. Kevin self-damages, Mirror grants i-frames, Battery increases Kevin proc rate. Bandit's passive makes this better than any other character.

Joe's Dagger + High Attack Speed

1% execute chance per hit becomes constant executes with 200-300% attack speed. Damage scaling from executes compounds throughout run.

Overpowered Lamp + On-Hit Effects

Doubles all proc chances. With Bandit's attack speed, poison/lightning/explosion procs become overwhelming. Essential for on-hit builds.

Demonic Blade + Attack Speed

+25 lifesteal on critical hits. With fast weapons, crits heal constantly. Pairs well with Forbidden Juice for sustain.

Kevin + Golden Shield

Alternative Kevin synergy. Self-damage generates infinite gold with high attack speed. Use for economic advantage or Robinette comparison.

Blood Mark + Dexecutioner

Execute threshold on enemies plus Dexecutioner's execute chance creates double execute synergy. Thins crowds rapidly.

Balance Through Versions

Version 1.0.7 Kevin+Mirror Nerf:

The infinite invulnerability exploit was removed. Kevin no longer triggers invulnerability frames constantly. Bandit went from S-tier broken to A-tier balanced.

Current Status (Version 1.0.12):

Bandit remains the best Kevin+Mirror character despite nerfs. Attack speed passive ensures highest invulnerability uptime. Still dominates with proper itemization.

Community Perspective:
