üèÜ Megachad Overview & Build Summary

Megachad is the ultimate defensive powerhouse in Megabonk, designed for players who want to tank through endless enemy waves with minimal effort. With his signature Muscle Flex ability and Aura weapon, Megachad creates safe zones on the battlefield and is famous for enabling true AFK (away-from-keyboard) builds. He's consistently ranked S-tier by the community and considered perfect for beginners, experts, and everything in between.

‚öîÔ∏è Weapon & Abilities

üîÜ Aura

Type: Melee/Area-of-Effect

Range: Small circular zone around Megachad

Damage: Continuous damage to all enemies within range

Aura deals constant damage in a protective field around Megachad, automatically hitting all enemies that enter the zone. Range increases with weapon level upgrades.

üí™ Muscle Flex (Passive Ability)

Base Effect: Stops all incoming damage and stuns nearby enemies

Base Cooldown: 15 seconds

Cooldown Reduction: -0.2 seconds per ability level

Maximum Level: 50

Minimum Cooldown: 5 seconds (at level 50)

This ability automatically triggers when cooldown expires, creating a brief window of invulnerability and crowd control. At maximum level, Muscle Flex activates every 5 seconds, making Megachad nearly unkillable.

How Muscle Flex Works

When Muscle Flex activates, Megachad becomes completely immune to damage for a short duration while simultaneously stunning all enemies within a radius around him. This creates safe windows to reposition, collect XP gems, or simply survive overwhelming enemy density. The ability is automatic and cannot be manually triggered.

üéØ Unlock Strategy Guide

Phase 1: Understanding the Damage Tome

The Damage Tome is one of 23 tomes in Megabonk that provide multiplicative damage boosts. It starts at 1x damage and adds +0.08x per common upgrade level.

  • The tome must be unlocked for purchase first (if not already available)
  • Each level requires selecting the Damage Tome when it appears during level-up choices
  • Tome upgrades come in four rarities: Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary
  • You'll see 3 random options each level-up: weapons, tomes, or equipment

Phase 2: Efficient Tome Leveling Strategy

Best Characters for Fast Tome Progression

  • Sir Oofie: Default armored knight with high survivability - perfect for long runs
  • Fox: Starting character with balanced stats and good damage potential
  • Calcium: Speed-scaling skeleton for fast XP collection (if unlocked)

Leveling Tactics

  • Prioritize Damage Tome selections whenever available during level-ups
  • Aim for 20+ minute survival runs to maximize level-up opportunities
  • Focus on XP collection - stay mobile and hoover up diamond-shaped XP gems
  • Balance offense and defense: you need to survive to level up frequently
  • Don't neglect weapon upgrades - you still need damage to kill enemies efficiently

Phase 3: Earning Silver Coins

Once your Damage Tome reaches Level 7, you need 2,000 Silver Coins to purchase Megachad.

  • Silver Coins are earned by completing runs and achieving milestones
  • Longer survival times = more coins earned
  • Completing tier objectives and defeating bosses grants bonus coins
  • Each successful run contributes to your coin total

‚ùì Megachad FAQ

How do you unlock Megachad in Megabonk?

To unlock Megachad, you must first level the Damage Tome to Level 7. This requires playing multiple successful runs and selecting the Damage Tome when it appears during level-up screens. Once the Damage Tome reaches Level 7, go to the Unlocks menu and purchase Megachad for 2,000 Silver Coins. This typically takes 10-20 hours of gameplay depending on your run success rate.

Is Megachad good for beginners?

Absolutely! Megachad is one of the best characters for beginners in Megabonk. His Aura weapon provides automatic damage in a protective zone around him, requiring minimal aiming or positioning skills. More importantly, his Muscle Flex passive ability grants regular invulnerability windows and stuns nearby enemies automatically. This combination creates an extremely forgiving playstyle while still being powerful enough to earn S-tier rankings. Many players consider Megachad the perfect character for learning game mechanics safely.

What is the best Megachad AFK build?

The optimal AFK build focuses on maximum survivability with minimal player input. Prioritize these elements:

  • Max Muscle Flex ability - Reduces cooldown to 5 seconds for near-constant invulnerability
  • Upgrade Aura weapon - Increases damage zone for automatic enemy clearing
  • Stack Health & Armor tomes - Increases tankiness between Muscle Flex activations
  • Add Damage Tome - Boosts offensive output for faster clears
  • Get Pickup Range tomes - Collects XP gems without movement

This setup allows Megachad to literally stand still while surviving and clearing waves, making it perfect for true AFK gameplay.

What weapons work best with Megachad?

Megachad's default Aura weapon is his primary and most important tool - always prioritize upgrading it. For hybrid builds that aren't purely AFK-focused, you can pair Aura with:

  • Area-of-effect weapons - Complement Aura's close-range damage
  • Ranged options - Add damage to distant enemies while Aura handles close threats
  • Crowd control weapons - Synergize with Muscle Flex's stun effect

Important: Focus on upgrading only 2-3 weapons maximum. Spreading upgrades across too many weapons weakens your overall build. For pure AFK builds, avoid high-mobility weapons and stick with Aura as your sole damage source.

How long does it take to unlock Megachad?

Unlocking Megachad typically takes 10-20 hours of gameplay, though this varies based on your skill level and run success. The process involves:

  • Leveling Damage Tome to 7 - Requires multiple successful runs of 15-30 minutes each
  • Earning 2,000 Silver Coins - Accumulated through completing runs and achieving objectives

To speed up the process, focus on 20+ minute survival runs using durable characters like Sir Oofie or Fox. Prioritize selecting the Damage Tome whenever it appears during level-ups, and aim to reach higher wave counts for better coin rewards.

Why is Megachad considered S-tier?

Megachad earns S-tier rankings from the community for several reasons:

  • True AFK capability - Can literally survive runs with zero player input
  • Beginner-friendly - Low skill floor makes him accessible to new players
  • Infinite scaling - Muscle Flex remains powerful throughout entire runs
  • Boss dominance - Invulnerability windows trivialize difficult encounters
  • High skill ceiling - Advanced players can optimize timing for even better results

His combination of safety, consistency, and power makes him one of the most reliable characters for completing high-difficulty content and achieving long survival times.
