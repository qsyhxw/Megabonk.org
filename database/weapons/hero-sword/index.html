🗡️ Weapon Overview

The Hero Sword is an enhanced sword that slashes through multiple enemies and fires a slashing projectile. It blends melee reliability with ranged coverage, rewarding clean positioning and projectile count scaling.

📜 Unlock Rules – Allowed vs Forbidden

One accidental pickup fails the attempt. Know the rules before you start:

✓ ALLOWED

💰Gold: You may collect gold (but do not spend it on vendors/chests).
✨Experience (XP): Collect XP freely to level up.
⬆️Level-up Upgrades: Weapon/Tome choices from level-ups are permitted and essential.
🫙Pots/Jars: Breaking is allowed; be vigilant to avoid any spawned powerup orbs.

✗ FORBIDDEN

⛩️Shrines: All shrine types are banned — Succ, Moai, Greed, Challenge, Boss Curse — and do not charge Upgrade Shrines.
⚡Powerup Orbs: Speed, shield, damage boosts, etc. — any temporary powerup fails the run.
🎁Chests: Never open chests (including boss drops).
🛒Vendors: Don’t buy from the Shady Guy or any map vendor.
📦Item Pickups: Any item from enemies or environment is disallowed.
⚠️ Critical: If you touch any forbidden pickup, the attempt ends. Restart immediately.

📋 Step-by-Step Unlock Strategy

A concise, community-tested flow you can follow:

  1. Pick a Safe Character

    Choose steady scalers or tanky options:

    • CL4NK — +1% Crit Chance per level; pairs well with ranged weapons.
    • Sir Oofie — +1% Armor per level; very forgiving for mistakes.
    • Others — Speedy/ranged picks also work; avoid very “squishy” starts if you struggle.

  2. Prefer Forest Tier 1

    Gentler enemies and terrain. Learn spawns and boss patterns here first.

  3. Farm XP Aggressively

    Prioritize Projectile Count → Damage → Attack Speed. Pick up XP and Gold; ignore tempting drops.

  4. Avoid Forbidden Pickups

    Powerup orbs can spawn in XP piles — if in doubt, leave the XP. Mark shrine locations mentally and route around them.

  5. Summon Boss When Ready

    Many players trigger around the ~7 minute mark if damage feels sufficient. Don’t over-farm — more time = more risk.

  6. Boss Fight

    • Play at max safe range; let projectile slashes work.
    • Dodge telegraphed attacks; use terrain for brief resets.
    • After kill: do not open the chest. End the run cleanly.

  7. Purchase & Celebrate

    Hero Sword appears in Unlocks → Weapons: buy it for 30 Silver.

🪵 Optional helper: Enable the “Sticks & Stones” challenge to disable chests. You still must avoid all shrines and powerup orbs.

⏱️Time-box your farm: Commit to a boss window; lingering invites mistakes.
🎯Upgrade order: Projectile Count → Damage → Attack Speed → Size.
👀Practice runs: Do a few scouting runs just to mark shrine spawn habits and chest clusters.

⚠️ Common Mistakes That Ruin Attempts

❌ Autopilot chest opening

Muscle memory is the #1 run-ender. Treat chests as lava.

❌ Confusing orbs with XP

Powerup orbs are larger/brighter. If unsure, leave the pile.

❌ Shrine curiosity

Every shrine interaction is disallowed — including “charging” upgrade shrines.

❌ Over-farming

The longer you stay, the higher the chance of a misclick or wrong pickup.
