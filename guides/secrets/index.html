Monke

Hidden Character

The most famous hidden character in Megabonk. Monke is trapped in a cage deep in the Forest biome and requires exploration, combat, and a special key to unlock.

üóùÔ∏è Complete Unlock Guide

  1. Navigate to the Forest biome
  2. Find the Bananarang weapon (required for unlock)
  3. Locate the Gold Key hidden in the forest
  4. Find Monke's cage in the northeast section
  5. Use Gold Key to unlock the cage

üìç Exact Location

The cage is located in the northeast corner of the Forest map, past the Ancient Tree Shrine. Look for a golden cage with banana decorations.

üí° Pro Tips

  • Gold Key spawns randomly in Forest - check all corners
  • Bananarang must be in your inventory when unlocking
  • Monke has +2 Max HP per level and wall climbing ability
  • After unlock, buy Bananarang weapon for 500 silver

Bush

Secret Boss Character

Bush is both a hidden boss and unlockable character. This secretive sniper hides in the Forest and must be found and defeated to unlock.

‚öîÔ∏è How to Find & Defeat

  1. Explore the Forest biome thoroughly
  2. Look for "Suspicious Bush" enemy spawn
  3. Approach the suspicious bush to trigger boss fight
  4. Defeat Bush (High HP, long-range attacks)
  5. Character unlocks + Sniper Rifle available for purchase

üìç Spawn Location

Bush spawns near the center-west area of Forest, disguised as a normal bush. The suspicious bush is slightly larger and has a faint glow.

üí° Combat Strategy

  • Bush has high HP and long-range sniper attacks
  • Use cover from trees to avoid his precision shots
  • Bring sustain items (lifesteal/regen) for long fight
  • After unlock, Bush excels with precision builds

Bandit

Desert Secret Boss

The Desert's hidden boss character. Bandit roams the desert waiting to ambush unsuspecting players with his Dexecutioner weapon.

‚öîÔ∏è Unlock Process

  1. Play in the Desert biome
  2. Search for Bandit's spawn location (random)
  3. Engage in boss fight when found
  4. Defeat Bandit to unlock character
  5. Purchase Dexecutioner weapon (500 silver)

üìç Desert Location

Bandit spawns randomly in the Desert biome. Check near oases, rock formations, and the desert edges. He appears more frequently in higher tiers.

üí° Character Benefits

  • +1% Attack Speed per level (best in game)
  • Flowstate passive makes him Kevin+Mirror king
  • Dexecutioner weapon has execute mechanics
  • Scales exponentially with on-hit items

Dicehead

Ultimate Unlock

The ultimate secret character requiring 100 quest completions. Dicehead's Gamba passive creates unlimited crit scaling, making him the strongest character in the game.

üèÜ Massive Quest Grind

  1. Complete 100 total quests (track in menu)
  2. Focus on easy repeatable quests first
  3. Estimated time: 20-30 hours of gameplay
  4. Character unlocks automatically at 100
  5. Purchase Dice weapon (500 silver)

üìä Quest Optimization

Fastest quest types: Kill X enemies, Survive Y minutes, Collect Z items. Avoid boss-specific quests early. Use strong characters (Sir Oofie, Megachad) for quest grinding.

üí° Why He's Worth It

  • Currently dominates 99% of leaderboard top 100
  • Gamba passive: roll 6 = permanent crit increase
  • Unlimited scaling with proper RNG management
  • Meta king for endless wave and kill count runs

Spaceman

Challenge Master

Unlock Spaceman by completing 6 challenges. This cosmic character wields the Black Hole weapon with gravitational control mechanics.

üéØ Challenge Strategy

  1. Complete 6 total challenges (any type)
  2. Focus on easy challenges first (see challenge guide)
  3. Estimated completion time: 10-15 hours
  4. Character unlocks after 6th challenge
  5. Purchase Black Hole weapon (750 silver)

üéÆ Recommended Challenges

Easiest 6: Survive 10 min, Kill 1K enemies, Reach Level 20, Defeat any boss, Collect 25 items, Visit 10 shops. Avoid AFK and no-damage challenges initially.

üí° Spaceman Abilities

  • Gravitational Mastery passive enhances Black Hole
  • Black Hole weapon creates vortex pulling enemies
  • S-tier for area denial and crowd control
  • Requires Cooldown + Duration tome optimization
