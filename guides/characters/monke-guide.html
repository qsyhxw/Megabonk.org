Step-by-Step Unlock Process

  1. Enter the Forest Area
    Navigate to the Forest biome and begin thorough exploration. Look for hidden paths and secret areas that aren't immediately visible.
  2. Locate the Bananarang Weapon
    Search the Forest environment for the distinctive Bananarang weapon. This boomerang-style weapon is often hidden in tree hollows or behind foliage.
  3. Find the Gold Key
    The Gold Key is typically located in a separate area from the Bananarang. Check around ancient forest structures, under fallen logs, or in clearings.
  4. Discover Monke's Cage
    Look for a distinctive cage structure where Monke is imprisoned. This is often in the deeper parts of the Forest area.
  5. Use Gold Key to Free Monke
    Approach the cage with the Gold Key in your inventory and interact to unlock Monke permanently.
