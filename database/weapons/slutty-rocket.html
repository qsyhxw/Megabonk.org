🎯 How to Unlock

Slutty Rocket unlocks when you kill 15,000 enemies as CL4NK.

Related Unlock: After obtaining Slutty Rocket, killing 7,500 enemies with it unlocks the Mines weapon.

Fast Unlock Tips

  • Play CL4NK Crit Builds: His passive grants +1% Crit Chance per level; crit scaling accelerates kills.
  • Choose dense maps: T2 Desert/Forest waves spawn fast for kill farming.
  • Level fast: Quantity/XP tomes and pickup range help you snowball.
Note: Only kills achieved while playing CL4NK count toward the 15,000 requirement.

⚙️ Core Mechanics

  • Homing Missiles: Rockets curve toward targets and explode on hit.
  • Explosive AoE: The blast damages enemies in a radius, ideal for swarms.
  • Screen Coverage: Scales especially well with extra projectiles.

🎯 Practical Tips

Positioning & Use

  • Lead shots into the densest part of the horde so homing rockets overlap their blasts.
  • Pair with passive damage (Aura) to cover misses and chip elites.
  • Stack projectile count early for the largest power spike.

Common Mistakes

  • Overvaluing Cooldown on Bandit (his passive provides attack speed per level).
  • Ignoring Crit Chance on CL4NK—this build lives on crit scaling.
  • Treating Size as a weapon upgrade—increase AoE via the Size Tome instead.
