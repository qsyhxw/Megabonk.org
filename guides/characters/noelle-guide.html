‚ùÑÔ∏è Passive Ability: Enduring

Noelle's passive ability provides straightforward but powerful scaling:

  • +1% Duration per level: Increases the duration of all abilities, effects, and status effects
  • Synergy with Freeze: Longer freeze duration from Frostwalker and Ice Crystal
  • Scales to maximum level: Can reach 100%+ Duration bonus through leveling
  • Universal benefit: Affects all timed effects including DOTs, buffs, and debuffs
üí° Core Identity: Noelle excels at controlling enemy movement through freeze mechanics. Her Enduring passive doubles down on this by extending freeze duration, making her the premier crowd control character. She requires close positioning with Frostwalker but rewards skilled play with near-permanent enemy lockdown at high Duration values.

üîì How to Unlock Noelle

Unlock Requirement: Use Microwave to Duplicate Ice Crystal 3 Times in a Row

This is one of the most complex unlock sequences in MegaBonk. You must obtain multiple prerequisite items, then execute a precise duplication sequence.

Prerequisite Step: Unlock Frostwalker

Before you can unlock Noelle, you must first unlock Frostwalker weapon:

  • Find Ice Cube (Legendary item)
  • Use Ice Cube to freeze 1,000 enemies
  • Frostwalker unlocks and costs 2 Silver to purchase
  • This step can take 1-3 runs depending on luck

Prerequisite Step: Unlock Ice Crystal

After unlocking Frostwalker, unlock Ice Crystal item:

  • Use Frostwalker weapon in a run
  • Get 50 kills with Frostwalker
  • Ice Crystal becomes available in item pools
  • Ice Crystal is Common rarity - easier to find than Ice Cube

Step 1: Find Microwave

Locate a Microwave on the map:

  • Appears randomly in Forest or Desert biomes
  • Marked with green diamond on minimap
  • Comes in 4 rarities: Common (3 uses), Rare (2 uses), Epic (2 uses), Legendary (1 use)
  • Requires gold to activate
  • Don't use it yet - save it for Ice Crystal

Step 2: Find Ice Crystal

Locate Ice Crystal in the same run:

  • Appears in shops, level-up rewards, or drops
  • Common rarity - relatively easy to find
  • Once you have both Microwave and Ice Crystal, prepare for duplication
  • Make sure you have enough gold for 3 Microwave uses

Step 3: Execute Triple Duplication

This is the precise sequence - must be done correctly:

  • Duplication 1: Use Microwave on Ice Crystal ‚Üí Now have 2x Ice Crystal
  • Duplication 2: Use Microwave on Ice Crystal again ‚Üí Now have 3x Ice Crystal
  • Duplication 3: Use Microwave on Ice Crystal final time ‚Üí Now have 4x Ice Crystal
  • CRITICAL: Don't use Microwave on ANY other item between these duplications
  • If you duplicate something else, the counter resets - start over
‚ö†Ô∏è Sequence Must Be Consecutive: The "3 times in a row" means three consecutive Microwave uses on Ice Crystal with NO other duplications in between. Track carefully: "1st duplication... 2nd duplication... 3rd duplication!"

Step 4: Purchase Noelle

After successful duplication sequence:

  • Notification appears: Noelle unlocked
  • Noelle becomes purchasable in Unlocks menu
  • Costs Silver currency (check menu for exact amount)
  • Permanently available for all future runs
üí° Unlock Strategy Tips:
  • Prioritize unlocking Frostwalker and Ice Crystal first - may take multiple runs
  • When attempting Noelle unlock, play conservatively to ensure long run
  • Mark Microwave location mentally - don't approach until you have Ice Crystal
  • Use Common Microwave (3 uses) if possible - gives buffer for mistakes
  • Track duplication count: say "1... 2... 3!" aloud to avoid confusion
  • This unlock typically requires 5-10 total runs including prerequisites

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is Noelle good for beginners?

No. Noelle has an extremely difficult unlock sequence requiring multiple prerequisite steps across several runs. Her playstyle demands understanding Duration scaling, close-range positioning, and freeze mechanics. She's considered B-tier with weak early game. Start with Fox or other easier characters instead.

How long does it take to unlock Noelle?

Variable, typically 5-10 total runs. You need: (1) Find Ice Cube and freeze 1,000 enemies to unlock Frostwalker (1-3 runs), (2) Get 50 Frostwalker kills to unlock Ice Crystal (1 run), (3) Find both Microwave and Ice Crystal in the same run and execute duplication sequence (1-5 runs). Total time: 3-8 hours depending on RNG.

What if I accidentally duplicate the wrong item?

The counter resets completely. You need 3 consecutive Ice Crystal duplications with no other items duplicated between. If you mess up, you must start the sequence over: find Ice Crystal again and do 3 new consecutive duplications. The game does not track partial progress.

How much Duration do I need?

Minimum 50% for meaningful freezes, optimal is 75-100%. At 100% Duration, freezes last twice as long - game-changing. Below 50%, Noelle struggles. Her Enduring passive provides +1% per level (20% at max level), so you need 30-80% from items to hit optimal values.

Does freeze work on bosses?

Partially. Bosses have freeze resistance - they're slowed rather than fully frozen. However, you can freeze boss minions and adds, creating safer boss encounters. Use freeze defensively during boss fights rather than offensively. Focus freeze on dangerous adds, not the boss itself.

Best items for Noelle besides Frostwalker?

Ice Crystal is essential (freeze + execution). Prioritize Duration items, Size increases (expand Frostwalker range), Movement Speed (better trail coverage), and damage multipliers to kill frozen enemies faster. Avoid items that don't synergize with close-range freeze playstyle.

Can I unlock Noelle across multiple runs?

Partially. The prerequisites (Frostwalker unlock and Ice Crystal unlock) persist across runs. But the triple duplication sequence must happen in a single run - progress doesn't save between runs. Once you have prerequisites unlocked, you need one good run with Microwave + Ice Crystal to complete unlock.

Why is Noelle only B-tier?

Despite strong crowd control, she has significant weaknesses: requires close-range positioning with Frostwalker (risky), weak early game before Duration stacks, ineffective against bosses due to freeze resistance, and completely build-dependent. Top-tier characters have fewer restrictions and stronger early games. Noelle is powerful when built correctly but situational.

Late game strategy with Noelle?

At 100%+ Duration with Size scaling, Noelle dominates. Create massive Frostwalker trails covering large areas. Enemies freeze on contact and remain frozen long enough to kill. Circle around enemy groups continuously, weaving freeze trails. Ice Crystal executes low-HP frozen enemies instantly. You control battlefield pacing completely - enemies move at your discretion.

What's the Enduring passive maximum value?

The Enduring passive provides +1% Duration per character level. Maximum character level varies by run, but typically reaches 15-25 levels, providing 15-25% Duration from passive alone. Combined with Duration items (10-20% each), you can realistically reach 75-125% total Duration in optimized builds.
