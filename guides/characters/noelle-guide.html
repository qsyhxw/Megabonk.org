❄️ Character Overview

🏆 Unlock Method

Use Microwave to duplicate Ice Crystal 3 times in a row

🎯 Playstyle

Freeze / Size Scaling

⚔️ Starting Weapon

Frostwalker

📈 Current Passive

+0.75% Size per level

❄️ Passive Ability: Enduring (Current)

Noelle's current passive was reworked in V1.0.17 and adjusted again in the Spooky Update:

  • +0.75% Size per character level: increases Frostwalker coverage and other size-scaling effects.
  • +1.5% damage per frozen enemy: each enemy currently frozen adds to Noelle's damage multiplier.
  • Example: 20 frozen enemies give a 1.30x multiplier; 50 frozen enemies give 1.75x.
  • Former mechanic: Noelle no longer gains Duration per level. Older guides describing +1% Duration are outdated.
💡 Core Identity: Build Size to freeze more enemies at once, then convert the number of frozen targets into damage. Duration remains useful for control, but it is a build stat—not Noelle's level-scaling passive.

✅ Strengths

  • Size grows naturally with every level
  • Damage scales with the number of frozen enemies
  • Strong control against dense waves
  • Frostwalker benefits directly from the passive

❌ Weaknesses

  • Complex multi-step unlock
  • Needs nearby enemies frozen to maximize damage
  • Boss phases provide fewer passive stacks
  • Close-range Frostwalker positioning can be risky

🎯 Recommended Build Direction

1. ❄️ Size + Freeze Core

Goal: Expand Frostwalker, freeze large packs and maintain the passive damage multiplier.

  1. Frostwalker — starting weapon and primary freeze field
  2. Size Tome — stacks with +0.75% Size per level
  3. Cooldown Tome — improves field uptime and control consistency
  4. Ice Cube / Ice Crystal — additional freeze application
  5. Damage and Quantity — convert control into faster clears

2. 🌨️ Defensive Control

Use Size and Cooldown first, then add movement and defensive tools. This is safer when enemy density or Curse makes close positioning dangerous.

Build-page handoff: See the Best Noelle Build for complete weapons, Tomes, items and upgrade order.

⏱️ Strategy Timeline

0–5 Minutes: Establish Coverage

Level safely, prioritize Size and keep moving so Frostwalker touches as many enemies as possible.

5–15 Minutes: Freeze Packs

Add Cooldown and extra freeze sources. The passive becomes stronger when you hold many enemies frozen simultaneously.

15–30 Minutes: Convert Control into Damage

Once coverage is stable, add Damage and Quantity. Do not overinvest in Duration at the expense of Size or kill speed.

30+ Minutes: Manage CC Limits

Late Final Swarm enemies gradually become harder to freeze, so retain direct damage and survivability instead of relying on permanent crowd control.

💡 Pro Tips & Common Mistakes

✅ Current-version priorities

  • Size first: More coverage means more simultaneous frozen enemies and a larger multiplier.
  • Keep enemies grouped: Black Hole and controlled pathing help maintain frozen-enemy stacks.
  • Add Duration selectively: It helps freeze uptime but does not grow from Enduring anymore.
  • Bring boss damage: The passive is weaker when few targets can be frozen.

❌ Avoid outdated advice

  • Do not calculate +1% Duration per level; that was replaced in V1.0.17.
  • Do not use the old 2% damage-per-frozen-enemy value; the current value is 1.5%.
  • Do not assume crowd control remains unlimited deep into Final Swarm.

🔓 How to Unlock Noelle

Unlock Requirement: Use Microwave to Duplicate Ice Crystal 3 Times in a Row

This is one of the most complex unlock sequences in MegaBonk. You must obtain multiple prerequisite items, then execute a precise duplication sequence.

Prerequisite Step: Unlock Frostwalker

Before you can unlock Noelle, you must first unlock Frostwalker weapon:

  • Find Ice Cube (Legendary item)
  • Use Ice Cube to freeze 1,000 enemies
  • Frostwalker unlocks and costs 2 Silver to purchase
  • This step can take 1-3 runs depending on luck

Prerequisite Step: Unlock Ice Crystal

After unlocking Frostwalker, unlock Ice Crystal item:

  • Use Frostwalker weapon in a run
  • Get 50 kills with Frostwalker
  • Ice Crystal becomes available in item pools
  • Ice Crystal is Common rarity - easier to find than Ice Cube

Step 1: Find Microwave

Locate a Microwave on the map:

  • Appears randomly in Forest or Desert biomes
  • Marked with green diamond on minimap
  • Comes in 4 rarities: Common (3 uses), Rare (2 uses), Epic (2 uses), Legendary (1 use)
  • Requires gold to activate
  • Don't use it yet - save it for Ice Crystal

Step 2: Find Ice Crystal

Locate Ice Crystal in the same run:

  • Appears in shops, level-up rewards, or drops
  • Common rarity - relatively easy to find
  • Once you have both Microwave and Ice Crystal, prepare for duplication
  • Make sure you have enough gold for 3 Microwave uses

Step 3: Execute Triple Duplication

This is the precise sequence - must be done correctly:

  • Duplication 1: Use Microwave on Ice Crystal → Now have 2x Ice Crystal
  • Duplication 2: Use Microwave on Ice Crystal again → Now have 3x Ice Crystal
  • Duplication 3: Use Microwave on Ice Crystal final time → Now have 4x Ice Crystal
  • CRITICAL: Don't use Microwave on ANY other item between these duplications
  • If you duplicate something else, the counter resets - start over
⚠️ Sequence Must Be Consecutive: The "3 times in a row" means three consecutive Microwave uses on Ice Crystal with NO other duplications in between. Track carefully: "1st duplication... 2nd duplication... 3rd duplication!"

Step 4: Purchase Noelle

After successful duplication sequence:

  • Notification appears: Noelle unlocked
  • Noelle becomes purchasable in Unlocks menu
  • Costs Silver currency (check menu for exact amount)
  • Permanently available for all future runs
💡 Unlock Strategy Tips:
  • Prioritize unlocking Frostwalker and Ice Crystal first - may take multiple runs
  • When attempting Noelle unlock, play conservatively to ensure long run
  • Mark Microwave location mentally - don't approach until you have Ice Crystal
  • Use Common Microwave (3 uses) if possible - gives buffer for mistakes
  • Track duplication count: say "1... 2... 3!" aloud to avoid confusion
  • This unlock typically requires 5-10 total runs including prerequisites

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is Noelle's current passive?

Enduring grants +0.75% Size per level and a +1.5% damage multiplier contribution for each frozen enemy. The old +1% Duration-per-level description is obsolete.

Does Noelle still need Duration?

Duration can help keep enemies frozen, but it is no longer her passive stat. Prioritize Size and reliable freeze application first, then add enough Duration or Cooldown to stabilize control.

How long does it take to unlock Noelle?

The unlock requires Frostwalker and Ice Crystal first, followed by three consecutive Ice Crystal duplications with Microwave. The time varies with item and Microwave RNG.

Can I unlock Noelle across multiple runs?

The prerequisite unlocks persist, but the three consecutive Ice Crystal duplications must be completed in one run.

What happens in boss fights?

With fewer freezable targets, Noelle gains fewer frozen-enemy damage stacks. Bring direct boss damage and use freeze mainly to control adds.
