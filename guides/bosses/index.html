🎯 Universal Boss Strategies

Core principles that work against all bosses

📖

Study Patterns

Every boss has predictable attack patterns. Learn the timing windows and practice until they become muscle memory.

⚔️

Weapon Choice

Different bosses favor different weapons. Ranged for mobile bosses, AOE for minion spawners, sustained DPS for tanks.

🏃

Movement First

Movement is your primary defense. Learn optimal positioning and always have an escape route planned.

💊

Resource Management

Save your best healing items for later phases when bosses become more dangerous. Use weaker heals early.

🧘

Stay Calm

Boss fights are marathons, not sprints. Stay calm under pressure and stick to your strategy.

🔄

Adapt & Learn

Each defeat teaches you something. Analyze what went wrong and adjust your build accordingly.
