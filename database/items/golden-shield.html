How Golden Shield Works

Golden Shield converts each instance of you being hit into gold. This makes it an economy tool that rewards controlled chip damage, rather than pure avoidance. Because the exact gold value per hit is not disclosed by official notes or media, treat it as a qualitative bonus and build accordingly.

No official number: As of October 2025, no official patch note or press guide publishes a fixed gold-per-hit value. If a future update exposes a number, we’ll revise this page.
