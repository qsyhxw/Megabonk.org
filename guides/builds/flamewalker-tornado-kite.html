🎯 Build Overview

Character‑agnostic mechanics build. Keep moving to “kite the wind.” Tornado forms a safe buffer that pushes mobs back; Flamewalker paints burning paths they must cross; Lightning Staff harvests clumped packs; choose Revolver or Slutty Rocket to delete elites and bosses. Tome order: Size ▶ Damage ▶ Cooldown ▶ Quantity, with optional Knockback/Projectile Speed for feel.

Playstyle

High‑mobility kiting • spacing control • burn overtime

Difficulty

Medium — positioning & pathing matter

Power Spike

Early–Mid when Size/Damage ranks land

Best Maps

Open arenas with room to loop paths

⚔️ Core & Supporting Weapons

Keep to 3–4 total weapons to avoid upgrade dilution

Tornado

CORE

Primary crowd control. Gains immensely from Size and Knockback to keep enemies at arm’s length.

Flamewalker

CORE

Constant burn while pathing. Bigger Size = wider carpets and more ticks on chased mobs.

Lightning Staff

CLEAR

Auto‑finishes clumped packs. Loves Cooldown to chain more often.

Revolver (or Slutty Rocket)

BOSS

Pick Revolver for consistent single‑target; choose Slutty Rocket if you want burst and don’t mind setup.

📚 Tome Priority

Order: Size ▶ Damage ▶ Cooldown ▶ Quantity · optional Knockback/Projectile Speed for feel and spacing

Size

#1

⚠️ Common Mistakes

Skipping Size early

Tornado and Flamewalker scale hardest with area — grab it first.

Too many weapons

3–4 total keeps upgrades focused and power online.

No pickup radius

Without Sucky Magnet you backtrack through danger and stall levels.

🧠 Why This Works

Tornado is widely recommended as a premier control core (commonly highlighted for Birdo) and benefits from stacking Knockback/Size/Damage. Flamewalker turns pathing into DoT pressure. Paired with Lightning Staff cleanup and a dedicated single‑target finisher, you get a reliable, repeatable kiting loop that’s strong across characters.
