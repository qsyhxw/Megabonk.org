⚙️ Blood Magic Mechanics
Understanding vampire sustain
🩸 Life Steal
Every enemy killed with Blood Magic restores health. Higher damage = more healing. Creates a sustain loop for endless survival.
Heal Rate: ~5-15% of damage dealt
Works On: All enemy types
Scaling: Increases with damage tomes
🔴 Blood Orbs
Killed enemies drop blood orbs that provide instant health recovery. Orbs automatically move toward player when nearby.
Drop Rate: ~25% chance per kill
Heal Amount: Moderate instant healing
Range: Auto-collect within 3 tiles
🌊 Blood Wave
Creates expanding waves of blood magic that damage all enemies in radius. Wave size increases with weapon level.
Area: Large circular waves
Damage: Moderate AoE damage
Frequency: Every 3-4 seconds
🧛 Vampire Synergy
Perfect synergy with Vlad character. Vlad's abilities enhance Blood Magic effectiveness and provide additional vampire powers.
Character Unlock: Requires Desert Tier 2
Synergy Rating: Perfect (100%)
Playstyle: Sustain-focused combat

🧠 Advanced Blood Magic Strategies
Master the vampire arts
🩸 Sustain Management
Don't rely solely on life steal. Combine with blood orbs and health items for reliable sustain. Health should never drop below 30%.
🌊 Wave Positioning
Blood waves originate from your position. Stay centered in enemy groups for maximum coverage. Don't chase stragglers.
⚡ Damage Scaling
Higher damage = better healing. Prioritize damage tomes early for sustain benefits. Don't neglect offense for pure defense.
🔴 Orb Collection
Blood orbs have limited duration. Move to collect them quickly. Use magnet items to improve collection efficiency.
🧛 Vampire Timing
Best used in prolonged fights rather than hit-and-run. Stay engaged with enemies to maintain healing momentum.
🎯 Target Priority
Focus on groups of weaker enemies for consistent orb generation. Save waves for elite encounters and boss fights.
