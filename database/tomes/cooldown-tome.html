⚡ Cooldown Tome

S-TIER

Unleash Lightning-Fast Attacks and Dominate the Battlefield

Attack Speed Tome
📊 Stat Type Attack Speed
⬆️ Upgrade +7.5%
🔓 Unlock Default
🎯 Max Level 99

The Cooldown Tome speeds up your attacks in Megabonk. It lowers the delay between actions, making projectiles fire faster and melee swings feel smoother. It's one of the strongest offensive Tomes, especially when paired with damage scaling, and is considered mandatory in most meta builds.

Overview

  • Stat: Attack Speed
  • Base upgrade per level: +7.5%
  • How to unlock: Default (available at start)
  • Max level: 99

Attack speed is the foundation of DPS builds. Cooldown Tome reduces the time between attacks across all weapons, making gameplay feel more fluid and aggressive. It's one of the best Tomes to rush early for snowball potential.

Effects

  • Increases attack speed across all weapons
  • Reduces downtime between shots or strikes
  • Makes gameplay feel more fluid and aggressive
  • Scales exponentially with other offensive Tomes
  • Increases on-hit effect procs and lifesteal healing

How to Unlock

✅ Unlocked by Default

This Tome is available from the start of the game. No quest or condition is required.

Synergies

Damage Tome
Faster attacks mean more damage per second. This is the core synergy for any DPS build.
Quantity Tome
More projectiles combined with faster firing overwhelms enemies. Creates screen-filling bullet storms.
Precision Tome
More attack rolls give more chances for critical hits. Essential for crit-focused builds.
Knockback Tome
Faster hits push enemies back more often, controlling space and keeping threats at bay.
Agility Tome
Faster attack speed combined with mobility helps maintain safe DPS with kiting tactics.
Lifesteal / Regen Tome
More attacks mean more healing. Critical for sustain in long runs without defense investment.

Strategy Guide

  • Rush early for snowball potential - One of the best first Tome investments
  • Works with almost every weapon and playstyle - Universal offensive boost
  • Stacks multiplicatively with weapon base attack speed
  • Essential for builds that rely on on-hit effects like Kevin+Mirror
  • Increases lifesteal healing rate dramatically when combined with lifesteal items
  • Core part of the "Speed Demon" and "Bullet Hell" meta builds

Important Considerations

  • Without sustain or defense, you might outpace your survivability
  • High attack speed can drain ammunition-based weapons faster
  • Some weapons have attack speed caps - check weapon-specific pages
  • Balance with Agility for kiting capability

Pro Tips

  • A core DPS Tome, often considered mandatory in offensive builds
  • Greatly increases synergy potential with other scaling Tomes
  • Pairs exceptionally well with characters that have attack speed bonuses
  • At max level (99), provides over 700% attack speed - transforming any weapon
  • One of the three "must-have" Tomes in most leaderboard builds
