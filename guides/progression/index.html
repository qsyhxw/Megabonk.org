Stage 1: Beginner Foundations

0-5 Hours
Level 1-10

Welcome to Megabonk! This stage focuses on learning core mechanics, understanding basic controls, and surviving your first 10-minute runs. Choose forgiving characters and focus on staying alive while learning enemy patterns.

🎯 Key Milestones

  • Reach character level 20 in a single run
  • Defeat your first stage boss
  • Unlock 3 new weapons from shops
  • Complete 5 basic challenges
  • Earn 1,000 total silver coins

💡 Essential Tips

  • Always keep moving - standing still = death
  • Prioritize XP gems over gold early game
  • Use Sword/Aura weapons for 360° coverage
  • Visit every shop you find for weapon unlocks
  • Don't worry about perfect builds yet - focus on survival
  • Learn to kite enemies in circles around the map

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • Don't stand in the middle of large enemy groups
  • Avoid complex characters like Dicehead or Calcium initially
  • Don't skip XP Tome if you see it - always take it
  • Don't unlock too many items too fast (dilutes shop pool)
  • Avoid Cursed Tome until you understand defensive items

ℹ️ "Holy Trinity" Community Terminology

  • The community commonly refers to XP Tome + Luck Tome + Cursed Tome as the "Holy Trinity" - a powerful three-item combination.
  • Important: The official item name is Cursed Tome (not "Curse Tome").
  • These three items provide significant benefits in the early-to-mid game, but Cursed Tome increases enemy difficulty, so it should only be added after establishing defensive/recovery foundations.
  • Ref: Pro Game Guides (2025-10), Steam Item List

💡 Holy Trinity Strategy

  • XP Tome Priority: Take within first 5 minutes for maximum value
  • Luck Tome Timing: Increasing Luck generally improves drop quality - grab it when available
  • Cursed Tome Usage: Only take when you have defensive foundation (Mirror, lifesteal, regen)
  • Stack XP and Luck early, add Cursed Tome after 10 minutes once you're stable
  • Holy Trinity enables exponential power growth - game changer!
  • Visit Moai Shrines and Gold Chests after increasing your Luck stat

⚠️ Avoid These Traps

  • Don't take Cursed Tome without Mirror or defensive items
  • Avoid spreading silver coins too thin - focus on 3-4 key unlocks
  • Don't ignore Movement Speed tome - survival depends on it
  • Stop unlocking random weapons - use Toggler to disable bad ones
  • Don't attempt Final Swarm yet - master base tiers first

ℹ️ Kevin × Mirror Interaction (Version Sensitive)

  • In versions before v1.0.7, Kevin could cause an invincibility bug - this has been officially patched.
  • Current version: Mirror provides brief invincibility frames and reflects damage, while Kevin's self-damage mechanic has specific interactions with Mirror's trigger system.
  • No official "immortality threshold" exists - avoid describing this combo as "near-immortal" or claiming specific attack speed breakpoints guarantee invincibility.
  • This combination remains powerful, but should be viewed as a strong synergy rather than a guaranteed survival mechanism.
  • Ref: Steam v1.0.7 Patch Notes, Community Discussion (2025-10)

💡 Advanced Strategies

  • Item Priority: Za Warudo > Overpowered Lamp > Moldy Cheese
  • Microwave Strategy: Duplicate S-tier items like Moldy Cheese 3x
  • Kevin + Mirror Combo: This combination is strong but requires proper attack speed investment and is no longer exploitable for invincibility
  • Use Greed Shrines with Cursed Tome for exponential drops
  • Increasing your Luck stat generally improves drop quality from chests and events
  • Master positioning: know when to face-tank vs kite
  • Learn boss patterns: most attacks are telegraphed and dodgeable

⚠️ Advanced Pitfalls

  • Don't rely solely on Kevin+Mirror as a survival strategy - it's not a guaranteed "immortality" build
  • Avoid pure damage builds without sustain (lifesteal/regen essential)
  • Stop hoarding gold - spend on rerolls for perfect items
  • Don't neglect Cooldown Tome - affects everything
  • Avoid mixing contradictory playstyles (speed build + Idle Juice = bad)

ℹ️ Final Swarm Goals (v1.0.12+)

  • Since v1.0.12, Final Swarm has been reworked and significantly enhanced to prevent extremely long marathon runs (5+ hours).
  • Current-version targets: Survive 60 seconds (Ghost achievement) → 6 minutes (Soul Harvester achievement) as milestone goals. Advanced players can aim for 10-15+ minutes with optimized builds.
  • The old strategy of "45+ minute endless wave" is no longer aligned with the current version's design. Adjust your goals based on future patch updates.
  • Ref: Steam v1.0.12 Patch Notes, GamesRadar (2025-10-08)

💡 Competitive Strategies

  • Speedrun Meta: Prioritize raw damage over XP/Luck - kill speed matters
  • Final Swarm: Holy Trinity recommended, stack Duration for weapon uptime, adapt to Ghost mechanics
  • Dicehead Scaling: Rush Dice + Giant Fork + Overpowered Lamp combo
  • Kill Count Optimization: Maximize spawn rate with Cursed Tome + Attraction Tome
  • Learn character-specific leaderboard strats (Monke for speedruns, Dicehead for kills)
  • Master Final Swarm mechanics - ghosts require different tactics than regular enemies
  • Study top player replays and build choices
  • Experiment with off-meta builds to find hidden synergies

⚠️ Expert Challenges

  • Version patches can shift meta overnight - stay updated on patch notes
  • Leaderboard requires perfect execution - one mistake can end long runs
  • RNG still matters - even pros restart bad runs early
  • Final Swarm significantly harder post-patch 1.0.12 - idle/AFK strategies no longer viable
  • Character-specific leaderboards may be added - diversify your mastery
