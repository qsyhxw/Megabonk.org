üå≥ Character Overview

Monke is one of the most unique secret characters in Megabonk, offering unprecedented mobility with wall-climbing abilities through the "Monke Grip" passive and a distinctive boomerang-style weapon. Hidden deep within the Forest map, Monke requires exploration and the discovery of a Gold Key to unlock, making them a rewarding find for dedicated players exploring Vedinad's viral roguelike hit.

üîç Secret Character Alert

Exploration Required: Monke is not unlocked through normal progression or completing runs. You must actively explore the Forest map, find the hidden Gold Key, and free Monke from their cage. After unlocking, you must also purchase Monke with in-game Silver currency to make them playable.

üóùÔ∏è Complete Unlock Guide

Step-by-Step Unlock Process

  1. Start a Run on the Forest Map
    Begin a new run in the Forest biome. This is the starter map where most players begin their Megabonk journey.
  2. Explore Thoroughly for the Gold Key
    Search the Forest environment for the hidden Gold Key. It can spawn in various locations around the map - check behind trees, near rocks, in clearings, and off the beaten path.
  3. Locate Monke's Cage
    Find the distinctive cage where Monke is imprisoned. The cage is typically found in the deeper sections of the Forest map and will be clearly marked once you approach it.
  4. Use the Gold Key to Free Monke
    Approach the cage with the Gold Key in your inventory and interact with it to unlock Monke. This permanently unlocks the character.
  5. Purchase Monke with Silver
    After unlocking, return to the character selection screen and purchase Monke using in-game Silver currency to make them playable in future runs.

üó∫Ô∏è Gold Key Finding Tips

  • The Gold Key spawns randomly each run in the Forest map
  • It appears as a glowing golden key that stands out from the environment
  • Check all corners and edges of the map systematically
  • Don't confuse it with regular keys - it's specifically labeled "Gold Key"
  • Once found, it remains in your inventory until you use it or the run ends

üçå Weapon & Passive Ability

üçå Bananarang

Monke's starting weapon is the Bananarang, a unique boomerang-style weapon that returns after each throw. It provides consistent ranged damage with curved trajectory patterns, allowing you to hit enemies from angles and maintain pressure from a distance.

üßó Passive Ability: Monke Grip

Wall Climbing: Climb up walls and vertical surfaces to access elevated areas and escape ground-based threats

Health Scaling: +2 Max HP per level gained

Maximum Benefit: +100 Max HP at level 50 (the level cap)

Unique Mobility: Only character in Megabonk with true vertical movement capabilities, allowing access to areas and strategies unavailable to other characters

üí° Monke Grip Strategic Advantages

  • Survivability: The +2 HP per level makes Monke one of the tankiest characters at higher levels
  • Positioning: Wall-climbing lets you kite enemies, create distance, and control engagement timing
  • Exploration: Access hidden areas and vantage points other characters cannot reach
  • Boss Fights: Vertical mobility can trivialize certain boss patterns by letting you avoid ground-based attacks

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Can't find the Gold Key? A: The spawn is random. Explore every corner of the Forest map methodically. It's always there!
  • Q: Found the cage but no key? A: Keep searching the map. You can mark the cage location mentally and continue exploring for the key.
  • Q: Does the unlock persist between runs? A: Yes! Once unlocked and purchased, Monke is permanently available.
  • Q: How much Silver to purchase? A: Silver costs vary, but you'll earn plenty from successful runs to afford Monke after unlocking.
  • Q: Is Monke worth using? A: Absolutely! The wall-climbing gives unique advantages, and the +100 HP scaling makes Monke excellent for both beginners and advanced players.

Note: Tier ratings vary by source and playstyle. Some players rank Monke even higher (S-tier) while others place it lower (B/C-tier) depending on individual skill with the wall-climbing mechanic.
