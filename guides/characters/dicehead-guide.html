üìä Stage-by-Stage Strategy

0-5

Early Game: Survival Mode (Minutes 0-5)

Priority: Don't die. Dicehead is extremely weak early.

  • Focus on XP farming and staying alive
  • Avoid enemies when possible, kite constantly
  • Take any defensive items (Oats, Medkit, Mirror)
  • Get XP Tome as first priority
  • Pray for lucky dice rolls - restart if terrible RNG
5-15

Mid Game: Build Foundation (Minutes 5-15)

Priority: Secure Holy Trinity and essential items

  • Get Luck Tome and Curse Tome (Holy Trinity complete)
  • Hunt for Giant Fork - absolutely critical item
  • Add Forbidden Juice, Gym Sauce, Demonic Blade
  • Start seeing crit procs from dice rolls accumulate
  • Weapon loadout should be mostly complete
15-30

Late Game: Power Spike (Minutes 15-30)

Priority: Maximize crit scaling and damage output

  • Crit chance should be 30-40%+ from dice rolls
  • Stack Damage Tome before Crit Damage Tome
  • Megacrits from Giant Fork deal exponential damage
  • Add Joe's Dagger and Overpowered Lamp for more procs
  • Become aggressive - you're now powerful enough
30+

Endgame: Unstoppable Force (Minutes 30+)

Priority: Dominate and climb leaderboards

  • Crit chance 50%+ and climbing every level
  • One-shotting bosses with Megacrit procs
  • Kill count acceleration for leaderboard position
  • Focus on aggressive farming over survival
  • This is where Dicehead separates from all other characters

üí° Pro Tips & Advanced Strategies

‚úì Stack Damage First

Always max Damage Tome before Crit Damage Tome. Higher base damage makes Megacrits exponentially more powerful. A 5000 base damage Megacrit at x20 multiplier = 100K damage.

‚úì Giant Fork is Mandatory

Without Giant Fork, Dicehead loses 80% of his power. Aggressively reroll shops and shrines to find it. Consider restarting if you don't have it by minute 10.

‚úì Embrace the Holy Trinity

XP + Luck + Curse tomes create exponential growth. More levels = more dice rolls = more permanent crit. This compounds throughout the entire run.

‚ö†Ô∏è Patience is Key

Dicehead has the longest power curve in the game. Don't get frustrated with weak early game. If you survive to 20 minutes, you'll dominate.

‚ö†Ô∏è Watch for Restart Signals

If you roll 10 dice without a single 6 in first 5 minutes, consider restarting. Terrible early RNG can cripple scaling potential.

‚úó Don't Skip Defense

Dead Dicehead = no scaling. Even with infinite damage potential, you must survive. Take Mirror, get Lifesteal, don't be greedy. One death ends the run.

üîì How to Unlock Dicehead

Dicehead requires completing 100 quests, making him one of the most difficult characters to unlock. This represents approximately 15-20 hours of gameplay.

1

Focus on Quest Completion

Prioritize completing quests over survival time or kill count. Check your quest log frequently and build specifically to complete current objectives.

2

Efficient Quest Strategy

Some quests are easier than others. Focus on kill-based quests (kill X enemies with Y weapon) and survival quests (survive X minutes) first.

3

Purchase the Dice Weapon

Once unlocked, you must also purchase the Dice weapon using Silver currency. Make sure you have enough Silver saved before attempting your first Dicehead run.

4

Practice with Similar Characters

Before unlocking Dicehead, practice crit builds with CL4NK (also has crit scaling) and Bandit (high attack speed). This prepares you for Dicehead's playstyle.

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚úó Going Full Aggro Early

Dicehead is extremely weak before minute 15. Playing aggressively will end your run. Kite, farm XP safely, and be patient.

‚úó Ignoring Giant Fork

Without Giant Fork, Dicehead is B-tier at best. The item transforms regular crits into Megacrits (x20 damage). It's literally build-defining.

‚úó Skipping Precision Tome

Precision Tome increases crit damage multiplier. With permanent crit stacking from dice rolls, this multiplies your scaling exponentially.

‚úó Not Leveraging Luck

High Luck increases your chance of rolling 6 on the dice. Max Luck Tome early for more frequent permanent crit gains.

‚úó Giving Up Too Early

Many players quit Dicehead after weak first 10 minutes. If you survive to 20 minutes, you win. The power curve is extreme.

‚úó Wrong Weapon Priority

Multi-hit weapons are critical for crit builds. Revolver, Katana, Bow all provide more crit opportunities. Avoid slow single-hit weapons.

üîó Best Item Synergies

Giant Fork + Forbidden Juice

The core combo. +10% crit chance from Forbidden Juice with Giant Fork's Megacrits creates consistent explosive damage.

Dice Weapon + Permanent Crit

Dice weapon naturally has crit synergy. Combined with 50%+ permanent crit from Gamba, every dice roll becomes a potential Megacrit.

Joe's Dagger + High Attack Speed

1% execute chance per hit. With fast weapons like Katana and high attack speed, constant executes thin crowds rapidly.

Demonic Blade + Crit Scaling

+25 lifesteal on critical hits. With 50%+ crit rate, every other hit heals, creating sustain without sacrificing damage.

Overpowered Lamp + On-Hit Effects

Doubles all on-hit effect procs. With multiple procs (poison, lightning, execute), damage multiplies exponentially.

Big Bonk + Crit Build

2% chance for x20 damage multiplier. Combined with Megacrits, can create absurd one-shot moments (x20 √ó x20 = x400 damage).
