🚀 SPACEMAN 🌌

The Black Hole Master - Gravity Control Specialist

🌠 Character Overview

🏆 Unlock Method

Complete 6 Challenges

🎯 Playstyle

Area Denial / Gravity Control

⚔️ Default Weapon

Black Hole (must purchase)

📈 Difficulty

Hard - Advanced mechanics

🌌 Passive Ability: Gravitational Mastery

Spaceman's cosmic passive provides unique advantages when using gravity-based mechanics:

  • Enhanced Black Hole: Black Hole weapon has increased pull radius and duration
  • Gravity Synergy: All gravity-based effects are amplified
  • Area Control: Enemies caught in gravitational fields take bonus damage
  • Cosmic Power: Perfect for grouping enemies and burst damage combos
💡 Core Identity: Spaceman excels at controlling the battlefield through gravity manipulation. Black Hole becomes a devastating tool for grouping enemies, enabling massive AOE damage combos. This character rewards strategic positioning and understanding of enemy wave patterns.

✅ Strengths

  • Incredible area control and crowd manipulation
  • Massive burst damage potential with grouping
  • Excellent against dense enemy waves
  • Unique gravity-based playstyle
  • Strong synergy with AOE weapons
  • Safe positioning through enemy control

❌ Weaknesses

  • Requires Black Hole purchase to shine
  • Difficult unlock (6 challenges needed)
  • High skill ceiling - positioning critical
  • Weaker against bosses (less grouping value)
  • Passive less useful without gravity items
  • Learning curve for optimal play

🎯 Recommended Builds

1. 🌌 Black Hole Singularity

Goal: Maximize Black Hole effectiveness for ultimate crowd control

Core Items (Priority Order):

  1. Black Hole - Your signature weapon, essential
  2. Cooldown Reduction - More frequent Black Holes
  3. Duration - Longer gravitational pull
  4. Area of Effect - Larger pull radius
  5. Damage % Scaling - Amplify burst potential

Supporting Stats:

  • Cooldown Reduction - Spam Black Holes constantly
  • Duration - Keep enemies grouped longer
  • Area Size - Pull from further away
  • Damage - Maximize burst when grouped

Gameplan:

Cast Black Hole at the center of enemy waves. As enemies are pulled together, follow up with AOE damage abilities. With maxed Cooldown and Duration, you can maintain near-permanent crowd control, making you virtually untouchable while dealing massive damage.

2. ⚡ Gravity + Burst Combo

Goal: Combine Black Hole with explosive burst damage

Core Items (Priority Order):

  1. Black Hole - Group enemies
  2. Explosive Weapons - Grenade, Rocket Launcher, Bomb
  3. Crit Chance/Damage - Maximize burst
  4. Cooldown Reduction - Frequent combos
  5. Attack Speed - More explosions

Supporting Stats:

  • Crit Chance/Damage - Massive grouped burst
  • Cooldown Reduction - More Black Holes
  • Damage - Amplify explosions

Gameplan:

Use Black Hole to pull enemies into a tight cluster, then unleash explosive weapons for devastating AOE damage. A single crit explosion can wipe entire waves when enemies are grouped. This build has the highest damage ceiling in the game.

3. 🔮 Gravity Control Tank

Goal: Use gravity for defense and sustained damage

Core Items (Priority Order):

  1. Black Hole - Core control tool
  2. Lifesteal / Regen - Sustain through fights
  3. Max HP - Survivability
  4. Duration + Cooldown - Constant control
  5. Damage Over Time - Poison, Burn, etc.

Supporting Stats:

  • Max HP - Survive mistakes
  • Lifesteal/Regen - Healing sustain
  • Duration - Keep enemies locked down

Gameplan:

Focus on survival while using Black Hole to keep enemies at bay. Apply DOT effects to grouped enemies and let them die slowly while you heal. This is the safest Spaceman build, perfect for learning the character or tackling difficult challenges.

⏱️ Strategy Timeline

0-5 Minutes: Survival Phase

Goal: Survive until you can afford Black Hole

  • Spaceman is weak without Black Hole - play defensively
  • Take any weapon that helps you clear safely
  • Focus on collecting gold for first shop
  • Prioritize survival over aggressive play
  • Learn enemy spawn patterns and positions
⚠️ Critical: Your passive barely helps without Black Hole. Don't expect to dominate early game - just survive and reach the shop.

5-15 Minutes: Black Hole Acquisition

Goal: Purchase Black Hole and start mastering gravity control

  • First shop priority: BUY BLACK HOLE
  • Practice pulling enemies into tight groups
  • Position Black Hole at enemy wave centers
  • Learn the pull radius and timing
  • Start stacking Cooldown Reduction
💡 Black Hole Mastery: Cast Black Hole slightly ahead of enemy movement, not on their current position. This catches more enemies as they walk into the gravitational field.

15-30 Minutes: Power Scaling

Goal: Build synergies and dominate through crowd control

  • Stack Cooldown Reduction to 40-60%
  • Add Duration for longer Black Hole uptime
  • Pair with AOE damage weapons (explosives, beams, etc.)
  • Position strategically to group maximum enemies
  • Use Black Hole proactively, not reactively
🌟 Combo Timing: Cast Black Hole → Wait 1 second for grouping → Unleash burst damage. Don't waste burst before enemies are grouped!

30+ Minutes: Gravitational Dominance

Goal: Perfect crowd control and massive burst combos

  • Near-permanent Black Hole uptime with CDR
  • One-shot entire waves with grouped burst damage
  • Use gravity to position enemies for safe farming
  • Experiment with multiple Black Holes overlapping
  • Master advanced positioning for maximum efficiency
🏆 Late Game Dominance: With maxed Cooldown and Duration, you can have multiple Black Holes active simultaneously. Enemies literally cannot reach you - you control the entire battlefield.

💡 Pro Tips & Common Mistakes

✅ Advanced Techniques

  • Predictive Placement: Cast Black Hole where enemies will be, not where they are now
  • Corner Grouping: Use Black Hole near walls/corners to prevent enemy escape
  • Chain Casting: With high CDR, cast second Black Hole before first expires for continuous control
  • Burst Window Timing: Wait 1-2 seconds after Black Hole cast for maximum grouping before bursting
  • Boss Strategy: Use Black Hole to keep boss minions grouped away from you
  • Movement Baiting: Move to bait enemies into grouping, then Black Hole them
  • Overlap Strategy: Multiple overlapping Black Holes create a massive death zone
  • AOE Weapon Priority: Always prioritize weapons that benefit from grouped enemies
  • Duration Breakpoints: At 100% Duration, Black Hole lasts twice as long - game-changing
  • Safe Farming: Use Black Hole to position enemies away from you while you farm safely

❌ Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Playing Without Black Hole: Your passive is weak without it - buy it first shop, no exceptions
  • Poor Black Hole Placement: Casting randomly instead of at wave centers wastes potential
  • Bursting Too Early: Attacking before enemies group together wastes your combo
  • Ignoring Cooldown: Without CDR, Black Hole has too much downtime
  • Neglecting Duration: Short Black Holes don't group enemies properly
  • Wrong Weapon Choice: Single-target weapons don't benefit from grouped enemies
  • Reactive Casting: Casting Black Hole in panic instead of strategically
  • Over-Positioning: Standing too close to grouped enemies - they can still hurt you
  • Forgetting Bosses: Black Hole doesn't pull bosses much - different strategy needed
  • Stat Imbalance: Stacking only damage without CDR/Duration makes build incomplete
⚠️ Critical Mistake: The biggest error is trying to play Spaceman like a normal character. You MUST build around Black Hole with Cooldown + Duration + AOE damage. Without this trinity, Spaceman is significantly weaker than other characters.

🔓 How to Unlock Spaceman

Unlock Requirement: Complete 6 Challenges

Challenges are special tasks found in the main menu. You must complete 6 different challenges to unlock Spaceman. Here are recommended challenges for beginners:

✅ Easy Challenges (Recommended to Start)

  1. Kill 100 Enemies: Simple survival - complete any run
  2. Reach Wave 10: Basic progression challenge
  3. Collect 50 Items: Pick up items naturally during runs
  4. Deal 10,000 Damage: Happens passively in most runs

⚡ Medium Challenges (Good Progress)

  1. Win a Run on Tier 1: Complete any biome on Tier 1
  2. Survive 20 Minutes: Requires decent build
  3. Use 5 Different Weapons: Pick up various weapons
  4. Heal 1000 HP: Stack lifesteal/regen items

🔥 Harder Challenges (If Needed)

  1. No Damage Taken for 5 Minutes: Requires good dodging
  2. Kill a Boss: Complete Tier 1 of any biome
  3. Reach Wave 20: Strong build required
  4. Complete Run with Specific Character: Use recommended characters

Step 1: Check Challenge Menu

Open the Challenges menu from the main screen. Review all available challenges and identify which ones you can complete easily with your current skill level.

Step 2: Strategic Challenge Selection

Focus on challenges that can be completed simultaneously. For example, "Kill 100 enemies" and "Reach Wave 10" can both be done in one run.

Step 3: Use Strong Characters

Complete challenges using powerful characters like Fox, MegaChad, or Dicehead. These characters make challenges much easier.

Step 4: Complete 6 Total

Once you've completed any 6 challenges, Spaceman will be permanently unlocked! You'll receive a notification when unlocked.

💡 Unlock Strategy: Focus on easy challenges first. Many challenges can be completed passively during normal runs. Don't attempt hard challenges until you're comfortable with game mechanics. The easiest 6 challenges should take 3-5 hours of gameplay total.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Spaceman good for beginners?

No. Spaceman has one of the hardest learning curves in the game. The character is weak without Black Hole, requires strategic positioning, and demands understanding of enemy patterns. Recommended for players who have completed 10+ runs first.

Can I play Spaceman without Black Hole?

Technically yes, but it's extremely suboptimal. Spaceman's passive barely functions without Black Hole. You'd be playing a significantly weaker version of other characters. ALWAYS buy Black Hole first shop, no exceptions.

What's the best weapon combo with Black Hole?

Explosive weapons (Grenade, Rocket, Bomb) are best. They deal massive AOE damage to grouped enemies. Second best are piercing weapons (Laser, Spear) that can hit multiple grouped targets. Avoid single-target weapons.

How much Cooldown Reduction do I need?

Minimum 40%, optimal is 60-80%. At 60% CDR, Black Hole has nearly 100% uptime with Duration scaling. This lets you maintain constant crowd control.

Does Black Hole work on bosses?

Bosses have high knockback resistance, so Black Hole barely pulls them. However, it's excellent for controlling boss minions and creating safe space. Use it defensively against bosses.

What if I can't afford Black Hole first shop?

Unfortunate but happens. Focus on gold generation (kill enemies efficiently, pick up gold) and skip buying other items. Save everything for Black Hole. Consider using Robinette if you need guaranteed shop purchases.

Best biome for Spaceman?

Desert and Factory are excellent - dense enemy waves that benefit from grouping. Forest is also good. Avoid biomes with spread-out enemies where Black Hole value is lower.

How do I position Black Hole correctly?

Cast it at the center of enemy waves, slightly ahead of their movement direction. Avoid casting on edges where enemies can escape. Use walls/corners to trap enemies in the gravitational field.

Is Spaceman viable for late game?

Absolutely. With maxed Cooldown and Duration, Spaceman becomes one of the safest characters. You can maintain permanent crowd control, preventing enemies from ever reaching you while you deal massive AOE burst damage.

Can I stack multiple Black Holes?

Yes! With high Cooldown Reduction, you can cast a second Black Hole before the first expires. Overlapping Black Holes create a massive gravitational death zone that's nearly impossible for enemies to escape.
