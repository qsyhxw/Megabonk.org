How Thunder Mitts Works

Thunder Mitts adds a passive lightning strike whenever your attacks hit an enemy. The effect has a global 10s cooldown and is available from the start of the game once you begin a run.

Does Attack Speed help?
Attack Speed doesn’t shorten the 10s cooldown, but frequent hits ensure the strike procs as soon as the cooldown ends (community observation; not officially documented).

Best Synergies & Play Tips

Lightning Staff

Pairs naturally with on-hit lightning. Consider the Lightning Staff stun-chain build or check the weapon page for stats and upgrades: Lightning Staff.

Electric Plug

Another damage aura that triggers when you’re hit. While it procs on a different trigger, combining area chip damage with periodic strikes helps clear clustered enemies.

FAQ

What does Thunder Mitts do?
On hit, it generates a lightning strike. The effect has a 10-second cooldown.
How do I unlock Thunder Mitts?
It is unlocked by default and can appear from the start of your play.
What’s the item’s rarity?
Unconfirmed. Community wikis often list it as Uncommon, but we did not find two independent sources that agree. We omit a hard label until more official documentation appears.
