Achievement Hunting Tips

üí° Plan Your Runs

Focus on 2-3 achievements per run. Many achievements can be combined - for example, weapon kill counts while farming silver for character unlocks.

üéØ Prioritize Easy Wins

Knock out high-percentage achievements first (80%+). These provide confidence and unlock resources needed for harder challenges.

üîÑ Use Meta Builds

The Holy Trinity (XP + Curse + Luck Tome) with Aura or Dice makes most achievements significantly easier. Master this build first.

üìä Track Progress

Use Steam's achievement tracker and our detailed guides for each category. Check rarity to identify which achievements need special strategies.

Some achievements are much easier with specific characters. For example, use Athena for thorns achievements or CL4NK for crit-based challenges.
