🎯 Build Overview

A ranged lane‑clear shell that capitalizes on Bow’s piercing. You’ll “draw lines” through packs by kiting laterally and firing down lanes. Pair with Revolver (tight angles, single‑target) or Firestaff (cone burn) to cover blind spots, keep Aura for contact safety, and add Dice or Bananarang as flexible lane fillers. Tome order: Quantity ▶ Cooldown ▶ Precision ▶ Damage, with optional Projectile Speed for feel.

Playstyle

Mid‑range kiting • piercing lanes • sidearm cleanup

Difficulty

Medium — spacing and pathing matter

Power Spike

Early–Mid as Quantity + CDR land

Best For

Open maps, speed‑clears, ranged mains

⚔️ Core & Supporting Weapons

Keep to 3–4 total weapons to avoid upgrade dilution

Bow

CORE

Piercing arrows excel at lane clearing. Scales hardest with Quantity and Cooldown; later appreciates Precision/Damage to finish packs.

Revolver (or Firestaff)

COVERAGE

Pick Revolver for angles and elite focus, or Firestaff for cone burn cleanup.

Aura

BODYGUARD

Hands‑free chip damage vs. melee chasers; stabilizes greedy ranged play.

Dice (or Bananarang)

FLEX

Flexible lane filler: Dice scales passively into late game; Bananarang offers orbiting coverage that loves Size.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

Skipping Quantity early

Throughput beats raw damage in this archetype.

Too many weapons

3–4 total keeps upgrade density high.

Standing still

This build expects lateral kiting so arrows pierce entire waves.

Ignoring QoL

No Projectile Speed/Battery can make the build feel clunky.

🧠 Why This Works

Bow’s pierce lets you “draw lines” through dense packs, and meta write‑ups frequently highlight Revolver + Bow as a fast clear combo. Quantity and Cooldown multiply arrow throughput, while Precision/Damage secure kills. Aura ensures safety for a greedy ranged posture, and Dice/Bananarang flexibly fill lanes depending on preference.
