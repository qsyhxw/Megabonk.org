Demonic Blood

⭐ RARITY: UNCONFIRMED
Effect (verified): Gain Max HP on enemy kills, stacking up to a cap (community-verified model: +0.5 Max HP per kill, up to 200 stacks).
Unlock: Raise your Max HP with Blood Magic a total of 400 times (account-wide).

Note on conflicting descriptions: Some aggregate guides list Demonic Blood as “+9 Max HP with Armor/Thorns.” Those summaries are inconsistent with stacking behavior observed in community testing/footage and recent patch discussions. This page follows the stacking Max HP on kills model until official notes state otherwise.

Overview

Demonic Blood is a scaling health item that rewards aggressive play. Each kill adds to your Max HP until you hit the item’s cap, making it a cornerstone for high-HP “tank” archetypes and any build that converts health into damage. If you’re new to items, browse the full Items database index first.

🔓 How to Unlock

  1. Equip or find Blood Magic.
  2. Play runs where you frequently increase Max HP (kills, lifesteal spikes, events).
  3. When your account-wide total reaches 400 Max-HP gains from Blood Magic, Demonic Blood enters your item pool permanently.
Tip: Characters who snowball health (e.g., Vlad) and maps with dense spawns speed up the grind.

🔗 Strategy & Synergies

Core Ideas

  • Kill-driven Max HP: Demonic Blood scales during combat, making longer waves and high DPS especially rewarding.
  • Tank-to-DPS conversion: Pair with items that convert Max HP into damage for multiplicative payoff.

Beefy Ring

Damage scales with Max HP. Demonic Blood’s scaling feeds this directly for “HP → DPS” builds.

Medkit / Regen

More Max HP means more to refill; passive regen stabilizes low-risk farming.

Leeching Crystal

Trade regen for big Max HP. Use with caution; it supercharges HP totals for HP-scaling builds.

Recommended Users

High-HP or sustain-oriented characters—like Vlad—benefit the most, especially when combined with Beefy Ring or steady healing from Medkit.

❓ FAQ

Q: What exactly does Demonic Blood do?
A: It stacks Max HP off kills up to its cap (community-verified model: +0.5 Max HP per kill, 200 stacks).

Q: How do I unlock it?
A: Increase your Max HP using Blood Magic a total of 400 times across runs.

Q: Why do some guides say “+9 HP, Armor, Thorns”?
A: That summary appears in several aggregate lists but conflicts with stacking behavior and recent discussions. Unless an official patch note states otherwise, treat it as outdated.
