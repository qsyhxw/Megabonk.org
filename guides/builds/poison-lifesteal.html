Amog - The Toxic Survivor

Unlock: Poison 50,000 enemies with Moldy Cheese
Default Weapon: Poison Flask

☠️ Unique Passive: Toxic Resilience

Poison damage heals you for a percentage of damage dealt. The more poison you spread, the more you heal!

This makes Amog incredibly tanky despite not having direct defensive stats. Your offense IS your defense - killing enemies keeps you alive.

☠️ How Poison Works in Megabonk

💀
DoT Damage
Poison applies damage over time. Enemies take continuous damage even after you've moved away.
📊
Stacking
Multiple poison applications stack! Each hit adds another layer of DoT, ramping up total damage.
⏰
Duration
Poison lasts 5-8 seconds depending on items. Refresh duration with additional hits.
💚
Lifesteal
Amog's passive converts poison damage to healing. More stacks = more healing per second.

🎮 Gameplay Strategy

Phase 1: Early Game (0-10 minutes)

Poison Foundation

Priority Goals:

  • Unlock Amog (poison 50,000 enemies first)
  • Get XP Tome for faster progression
  • Pick up Vampiric Ring ASAP for lifesteal
  • Your sustain is weak early - avoid risky plays
  • Focus on applying poison to multiple enemies
💡 Hit enemies and move away - let poison do the work
💡 Kite enemies in circles to stack poison on groups
Phase 2: Mid Game (10-20 minutes)

Sustain Activation

Key Actions:

  • You should have: Poison Flask + Vampiric Ring
  • Add Duration Amulet for longer poison
  • Get Damage Amplifier to boost both damage and heals
  • Your healing is starting to feel significant now
  • Can take Curse Tome safely at this point
💡 Notice your health bar constantly regenerating from kills
💡 Stack poison on tough enemies before focusing them
Phase 3: Late Game (20+ minutes)

Immortal Plague Doctor

Optimization:

  • Full build: Poison Flask + Vampiric Ring + Duration + Damage Amp
  • Add Health Pendant for safety
  • You're healing faster than enemies can damage you
  • Poison clouds cover the entire screen
  • Can stand in boss attacks and outheal them
💡 You're nearly unkillable - play aggressively
💡 Focus on maximizing poison coverage for optimal healing

💡 Advanced Tips

🎯
Poison Management

Always maintain poison on as many enemies as possible. More poisoned enemies = more passive healing. Refresh stacks before they expire.

💚
Healing Optimization

Your healing scales with both poison damage AND the number of poisoned targets. Prioritize multi-target poison application over single-target focus.

⚡
Boss Strategy

Stack poison on boss immediately, then focus on nearby adds. The boss takes continuous damage while you heal from killing adds.

✅ Pros & Cons

✓ Strengths

✓ Infinite Sustain: Heal from every poisoned enemy constantly
✓ DoT Damage: Keep damaging enemies even while running away
✓ Safe Playstyle: Apply poison and kite while it kills
✓ Scales Well: Gets stronger the more enemies you fight

✓ Forgiving: Healing compensates for mistakes

✗ One-Shot Vulnerable: Can't outheal instant death attacks early
