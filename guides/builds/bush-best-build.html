⏱️ Suggested Progression

A pragmatic, drop-driven route—feel free to adapt per run

0–3 min

🌱 Establish Economy

Pick up Sniper Rifle early if offered. Many players favor XP / Luck / Cursed as an opener for momentum. Stay at range and level safely.

3–8 min

🎯 Build into Crit

Add Precision and Quantity. Slot Bow second for lane control. If you see Tactical Glasses or Battery, they plug in cleanly.

8–14 min

⚡ Stabilize DPS

Pick Revolver as third weapon, then weave in Cooldown / Projectile Speed. Add Boss Buster for elite/boss focus and Mirror for safety.

14+ min

🔥 Multiply Damage

Layer Damage tomes once core scaling is online. Optional utility like Clover (Luck) or on-hit AoE (Power Gloves / Thunder Mitts) depends on drops.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌ Misreading Item Effects

Tactical Glasses is +20% damage vs >90% HP (not crit chance). Power Gloves / Thunder Mitts are on-hit effects, and Time Bracelet is +8% XP.

❌ Calling “Headshots” a Separate Stat

The build scales with crit/overcrit. Use Precision, then Quantity/Cooldown to increase attempts.

❌ Standing Too Close

Play to Bush’s strength—manual-aim, long-range, piercing shots. Keep space and kite.

❌ Over-diluting Weapons

Three-weapon core keeps tomes concentrated. Add a defensive weapon only if you truly need it.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best weapon for Bush?

Sniper Rifle for single-target focus (manual aim, piercing). Add Bow for lane pierce and Revolver for cadence.

Which tomes should I prioritize for Bush?

Typical route: Precision → Quantity → Cooldown, with Projectile Speed as needed. The early XP/Luck/Cursed trio is a common—but optional—start.

What items work best with Bush?

Tactical Glasses (+20% vs >90% HP), Boss Buster (+15% vs elites/bosses), Battery (+8% attack speed), and Mirror (reflect + i-frames). Luck aids like Clover are helpful.

Is Aegis an item or a weapon?

Aegis is a weapon (blocks and releases shockwaves). See the Aegis weapon page.

💡 Pro Tip: Align enemies in lanes for Bow and take deliberate shots with Sniper from max range. Once Precision/Quantity are rolling, Boss Buster meaningfully accelerates elite/boss phases.
