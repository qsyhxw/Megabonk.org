🏜️ DESERT UNLOCK REQUIRED
Break tumbleweeds on Desert Stage 1 for a 5% chance to drop the Katana. Requires completing Forest stages first.
1. Complete Forest Tier 1 to unlock Desert access
2. Enter Desert Stage 1 and locate tumbleweeds
3. Break tumbleweeds until Katana drops (5% chance each)
4. Return to main menu and purchase with Silver

💡 Pro Tip: Tumbleweeds respawn throughout the stage. Break every one you see for maximum chances!

🧠 Advanced Katana Mastery
Expert techniques for blade masters
⚡ Dash Strike Pattern
Master the dash-in, strike, dash-out pattern. Move in fast, deliver maximum damage in minimum time, then retreat to safety before enemies can react.
🎯 Target Prioritization
Focus high-value targets first. Use Katana's high damage to quickly eliminate ranged enemies and elites before dealing with swarms.
🌊 Attack Speed Stacking
Unlike most weapons, Katana benefits enormously from attack speed. Stack Cooldown tome with attack speed items for machine-gun slashing.
🏃 Positioning Mastery
Stay at the edge of melee range. Too close and you get swarmed, too far and you miss attacks. Find the sweet spot for maximum effectiveness.
💀 Execution Timing
With Ninja, time your dodges for maximum executions. Each successful evasion becomes a guaranteed kill - practice the timing until it's instinct.
⚠️ Common Mistakes
Don't face-tank enemies! Katana requires active positioning. Avoid damage tomes early - prioritize speed and attack rate first.

🗾 Ninja Combat Techniques

🌪️ Whirlwind Slash

Circle strafe around enemy groups while attacking. The katana's wide arc hits multiple enemies while you maintain maximum mobility and safety.

Best Used: Dense enemy groups, crowd control situations

⚡ Lightning Counter

With Ninja, deliberately trigger evasions to activate executions. Let enemies get close, dodge at the last second, then counter-attack for guaranteed kills.

Best Used: Elite enemies, boss phases, clutch situations

🏃 Hit and Fade

Use maximum movement speed to dart in and out of combat. Strike once or twice, then immediately retreat before enemies can respond.

Best Used: Boss fights, overwhelming enemy numbers
