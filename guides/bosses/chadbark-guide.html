📖 Boss Overview

Welcome to Tier 2 - where Megabonk stops being friendly. Chadbark is Lil Bark's bigger, badder brother, and he doesn't mess around. This is where the game shifts from "lighthearted bonking to a true survival challenge." The boss health pool jumps from a manageable ~100K in Tier 1 to a staggering ~500K in Tier 2 - that's a 25x increase!

Chadbark serves as the real DPS check in Megabonk. If your build isn't optimized, you'll struggle to kill him before the Final Swarm overwhelms you. This fight teaches you the importance of focused weapon investment, smart tome choices (hello, Holy Trinity!), and proper timing. Master Chadbark, and you'll unlock the ability to teleport to Stage 2, opening up new progression paths and eventually access to the Desert biome.

⚖️ Tier 1 vs Tier 2: What Changed?

🌱 Tier 1 (Lil Bark)
  • Boss HP: ~20K-100K
  • Forgiving damage output
  • Simple projectile patterns
  • No teleportation available
  • Beginner-friendly pacing
  • Can win with basic builds
  • Mini Boss: 7:00, 2:00
🔥 Tier 2 (Chadbark)
  • Boss HP: ~500K (25x increase!)
  • Punishing, faster attacks
  • Enhanced projectile density
  • Unlocks 1 teleport ability
  • Intense Final Swarm
  • Requires optimized builds
  • Mini Boss: 7:00, 2:00 (same)
⚠️ The Difficulty Spike

Many players report that Tier 2 feels like hitting a brick wall. The jump from 20K to 500K HP isn't just about numbers - it's a fundamental shift in how you need to approach the game. You can't rely on luck anymore. Focused builds, proper tome selection, and smart stat priorities become mandatory.

Players frequently struggle here because they spread upgrades across too many weapons. For Chadbark, you need to go "deep not wide" - max out 1-2 weapons rather than having 3-4 mediocre ones.

🗺️ How to Unlock Tier 2

1

Clear Both Tier 1 Stages

To unlock Tier 2, you must clear both levels of Tier 1 in the Forest (or Desert). This means defeating Lil Bark, using the teleporter to reach Stage 2, and completing that stage as well. Both stages must be cleared in a single run.

2

Select Tier 2 Before Starting

Once you've unlocked Tier 2, you can select it from the difficulty menu before starting your next run. Tier 2 becomes permanently available for that biome (Forest or Desert) once unlocked.

3

Build for DPS, Not Just Survival

Before attempting Chadbark, make sure your build has strong DPS potential. Prioritize: Attack Speed Tome, Damage Tome, or the Holy Trinity (Curse + XP + Luck). Don't enter Tier 2 with a pure tank build - you need to kill fast.

4

Timing is Everything

Summon Chadbark with about 1 minute left on the clock. This gives you enough time to kill him before the Final Swarm becomes unmanageable. Black Ghosts spawn at -1:00, so you want the boss dead before then!

🚀 Teleport System Unlocked!
Once you defeat Chadbark in Tier 2, you unlock the Teleport System! This allows you to teleport to the next area after beating Stage 1's boss. In Tier 2, you get 1 teleport (Stage 1 → Stage 2). In Tier 3, you get 2 teleports. This dramatically speeds up progression and lets you access more challenging content and better loot drops.

⚔️ Enhanced Attack Patterns

Chadbark uses similar attacks to Lil Bark, but everything is faster, stronger, and more numerous. Here's what to expect:

🔴

Enhanced Red Spheres

High Threat

The familiar red projectiles return, but now they fire 2-3x faster and with greater tracking. The projectile density is significantly higher, creating a true bullet-hell environment. Each sphere also deals more damage than in Tier 1.

Counter Strategy:

Maintain aggressive circular movement - wider orbits than Tier 1. Movement speed upgrades are now mandatory, not optional. Consider taking the Speed Boots item if offered. Don't try to face-tank anything.

🌀

Projectile Storm

Extreme Threat

At 50% and 25% HP thresholds, Chadbark triggers a devastating projectile storm - filling the entire screen with red spheres. This phase lasts 5-10 seconds and is designed to punish players with poor mobility or insufficient survivability stats.

Counter Strategy:

When you see HP threshold warnings, prepare for the storm. Use invincibility shrines if available. If you have lifesteal or shields, this is when they matter most. Don't panic - keep moving, don't stop attacking.

💥

Area Denial Zones

High Threat

Chadbark occasionally creates hazard zones on the ground that deal continuous damage. These zones last longer in Tier 2 and can overlap, restricting your movement options significantly.

Counter Strategy:

Stay aware of the arena. Don't let hazard zones box you into corners. If you get surrounded, use your best movement ability to escape rather than trying to tank through the damage.

⏰

DPS Check Mechanic

Extreme Threat

The biggest challenge isn't Chadbark's attacks - it's killing him fast enough. With 500K HP and the Final Swarm at 0:00, you're racing against time. If you don't have sufficient DPS, you'll die to the swarm, not the boss.

Counter Strategy:

This is why build optimization matters. Focus 100% of your upgrades into 1-2 weapons max. Take damage-boosting tomes. Use the Magnet shrine before engaging to quickly level up. Every second of uptime matters - stay close and keep attacking!

🎯 Winning Battle Strategy

1

Pre-Fight Optimization (0:00-5:00)

Use the first 5 minutes to maximize your power:

  • • Take Greed Shrines early (synergizes with Curse Tome)
  • • Activate Challenge Shrines for better loot
  • • Focus ALL upgrades into 1-2 weapons
  • • Prioritize Attack Speed and Damage over everything
  • • Use Magnet shrine at ~6:00 mark for quick level ups
2

Boss Summon Timing (1:00 remaining)

The sweet spot for summoning Chadbark is around 1:00 remaining on the clock. This gives you enough time to kill him (usually takes 30-45 seconds with a good build) while leaving a small buffer before the Final Swarm at 0:00. If you summon earlier, you might get overwhelmed by regular enemies. If you wait too long, you'll die to Black Ghosts.

3

Phase 1: Establish Rhythm (100%-50% HP)

Start with your circular kiting pattern - just like Tier 1, but wider orbits and faster movement. Chadbark's projectiles are faster, so you need to stay ahead of them. Keep constant DPS pressure. Don't get greedy trying to maximize damage at the cost of taking hits. Every hit matters in Tier 2.

4

Phase 2: Projectile Storm (50% HP)

When Chadbark hits 50% HP, expect the projectile storm. The screen fills with red spheres. This is the panic check. Stay calm, widen your orbit even more, and DON'T STOP MOVING. If you have any defensive cooldowns or invincibility pickups, use them here. Continue attacking - you need to push through this phase quickly.

5

Phase 3: Final Push (25%-0% HP)

Another projectile storm triggers at 25%. This is the victory stretch. Chadbark is almost dead, but so are you if you make mistakes. Keep your movement rhythm, stay patient, and trust your build. If you've made it this far, you have the damage to win. The Final Swarm should be approaching, so finish strong!

⏰ Time Management is Critical
Rule of thumb for Tier 2:
  • • 0:00-5:00: Power farming and optimization
  • • 3:00: Mini Boss spawn - kill quickly
  • • 2:00: Second Mini Boss - prioritize over regular enemies
  • • 1:00: Summon Chadbark
  • • 0:30-0:00: Chadbark should be dead or nearly dead
  • • 0:00: Final Swarm starts - finish boss ASAP!
  • • -1:00: Black Ghosts spawn - you MUST be done by now

If Chadbark isn't dead by -1:00, the Black Ghosts will push you around and kill you. Time management is as important as your DPS!

💡 Advanced Tips & Common Mistakes

⚔️

Single Weapon Focus

THE #1 mistake in Tier 2: spreading upgrades across 3-4 weapons. You don't have the DPS. Pick ONE primary weapon (Revolver, Katana, Aura) and max it to level 7+ before even considering a second weapon.

🎲

Embrace The Holy Trinity

Curse Tome + XP Tome + Luck Tome is not optional in Tier 2. This combo is the difference between struggling and dominating. The synergy creates exponential scaling that lets you outpace the difficulty curve.

💰

Take Greed Shrines

Greed Shrines increase difficulty by 5% but give gold. If you're running Curse Tome, this "downside" is actually an upside - more enemies means more XP. Always take them early game.

🏃

Movement Speed Priority

Movement speed is no longer optional. Take the Speed Boots item if offered. You can't kill what you can't catch, and you can't dodge what you can't outrun. Speed is life in Tier 2.

🧲

Magnet Shrine Timing

Save the Magnet (Shrine of Succ) for right before the boss fight. Activate it around 1:30-2:00 remaining to collect all the XP you've left behind, giving you 2-3 quick level ups before engaging.

💀

Mini Boss Priority

Don't ignore the Mini Bosses at 7:00 and 2:00. They drop guaranteed items and significant XP. Kill them quickly, then return to farming. They're speed bumps, not walls.

🛡️

Don't Neglect Survivability

While DPS is priority #1, you need SOME survivability. Invest in at least one of: HP, Lifesteal, Armor, or Shields. A dead character deals zero DPS. Find the balance.

⚡

Attack Speed > Raw Damage

Counterintuitively, Attack Speed is often better than raw Damage in Tier 2. More attacks means more lifesteal procs, more consistent DPS, and better overall damage output. Prioritize it.
