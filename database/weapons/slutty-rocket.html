üéØ How to Unlock Slutty Rocket

The Slutty Rocket requires a significant time investment to unlock, making it one of the more challenging weapons to acquire in Megabonk.

Unlock Requirement: Kill 15,000 enemies using the CL4NK character, then purchase the weapon for Silver from the Unlocks menu.

Efficient Unlock Strategy

  1. Use CL4NK's Default Weapon (Revolver): Take advantage of CL4NK's +1% Crit Chance per level passive to maximize damage output.
  2. Focus on High-Density Maps: Desert and Forest Tier 2-3 provide the best enemy spawn rates for farming kills.
  3. Stack XP and Curse Tomes: Level faster and spawn more enemies simultaneously.
  4. Prioritize AoE Weapons: Aura, Lightning Staff, and Flamewalker help clear massive enemy waves efficiently.
  5. Multiple Runs Required: Expect 3-6 hours of total gameplay across multiple runs to reach 15,000 kills.
  6. Track Your Progress: Check the Statistics menu to monitor your kill count with CL4NK.

‚öôÔ∏è Core Mechanics

The Slutty Rocket functions as a multi-projectile explosive weapon with unique firing patterns and delayed detonation mechanics.

Primary Mechanics

  • Multi-Rocket Volley: Fires multiple rockets in a spread pattern with each attack cycle
  • Tracking Projectiles: Rockets have some degree of homing or arc trajectory toward enemies
  • Delayed Explosion: Rockets detonate after a short delay or upon impact
  • Area Damage: Explosions deal AoE damage to all enemies within blast radius
  • Barrage Potential: High projectile count creates screen-filling explosive coverage

Upgrade Paths

Upgrade Type Effect Priority

üéØ Advanced Strategies & Pro Tips

Positioning and Timing

  • Predictive Firing: Shoot rockets where enemies will be, not where they are
  • Funnel Control: Use terrain to force enemies into tight groups before firing
  • Stagger Volleys: Don't waste multiple volleys on the same group
  • Retreat and Reload: Create distance between volleys to avoid being swarmed

Build Optimization

  • Prioritize Projectile Count Early: Get to 4-6 rockets per volley ASAP
  • Delay Crit Investment: Focus on volume first, crit scaling later
  • Pair with AoE Support: Aura or Aegis covers weak points
  • Avoid Single-Target Weapons: Doubling down on AoE is more effective

Enemy Type Strategies

  • Swarms: Fire into the center of crowds for maximum AoE overlap
  • Fast Enemies: Lead shots and aim for choke points
  • Bosses: Slutty Rocket struggles‚Äîrely on secondary weapons
  • Tier 3 Content: Requires near-perfect build to remain viable
