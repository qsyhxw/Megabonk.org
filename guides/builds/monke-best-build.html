‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå Misreading Power Gloves

Power Gloves are not attack speed. They add chance-based on-hit explosions. Use Battery for real attack speed.

‚ùå Confusing Crit vs Luck

Forbidden Juice adds crit chance. Clover adds Luck (better rolls), not crit.

‚ùå Wrong Slot for Aegis

Aegis is a weapon, not an item. Compare it with Aura in the weapon slot‚Äînot with defensive items.

‚ùå Over-collecting Weapons

Focus on 3-weapon cores (Bananarang + one Finisher + one Defensive). Spreading too thin slows tome scaling.

‚ùå Skipping Projectile Speed

Projectile speed (Projectile Tome) dramatically improves Bananarang coverage. Don‚Äôt ignore it.
