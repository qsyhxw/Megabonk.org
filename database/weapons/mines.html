Mines places stationary explosives that detonate when enemies enter their trigger radius. It is best thought of as a support or area-control weapon: you kite through chokepoints, leave traps behind, and let waves pop as they follow. Community tier lists commonly place Mines in C-tier due to delayed damage and limited control over placement, though it can shine in slower, route-based play.)

How Mines Deal Damage

  • Mines are dropped periodically near your position.
  • They detonate when enemies step into their proximity, dealing AoE damage.
  • Because placement is passive and path-dependent, Mines work best along planned kite routes and chokepoints.
