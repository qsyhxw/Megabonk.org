2

3

🎯 Advanced Knight Strategies

🛡️ Defensive Mastery

  • Health Management: Never let your HP drop below 30% unless you have healing items nearby
  • Positioning Priority: Stay in areas with multiple escape routes, avoid corners
  • Damage Timing: Learn enemy attack animations to minimize damage taken
  • Shield Usage: Time shield activation right before taking damage for maximum efficiency

⚔️ Offensive Techniques

  • Spin Timing: Start your spin animation just before enemies enter range
  • Group Management: Pull large groups together for maximum spin effectiveness
  • Combo Attacks: Combine sword spin with lightning for devastating AoE damage
  • Target Priority: Focus on high-threat enemies first, then clean up weak ones

🎮 Pro Tips

  • Animation Canceling: Cancel spin recovery frames by moving for faster attacks
  • Wall Hugging: Use walls to limit enemy approach angles and control engagement
  • Upgrade Order: Prioritize Health → Armor → Damage → Speed for optimal scaling
  • Emergency Tactics: Always keep an escape route planned when health is low

❌ Common Beginner Mistakes

🚫 Positioning Errors

  • × Getting Cornered: New players often back themselves into corners with no escape route
  • × Overextending: Chasing low-health enemies into dangerous areas
  • × Poor Spin Timing: Spinning too early or too late, missing enemy groups

🚫 Build Mistakes

  • × Ignoring Defense: Focusing only on damage upgrades early game
  • × Wrong Weapon Synergies: Picking weapons that don't work well together
  • × Upgrade Order: Not prioritizing the right stats for your playstyle
