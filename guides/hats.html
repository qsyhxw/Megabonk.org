üìñ About Hats in Megabonk

Hats are purely cosmetic items that let you customize your character's appearance. They were introduced in the v1.0.64 "H A T S" update on January 25, 2026, which added 21 new hats. The Wizard's Hat was added earlier in the 2025 Christmas patch.

Each hat is unlocked by completing a specific achievement or challenge. Some are easy to obtain, while others require significant time investment or mastery of specific characters and weapons.

üí° Quick Tip

Hats are purely cosmetic and provide no gameplay benefits. Focus on unlocking them at your own pace while enjoying the game!

üí° General Tips for Unlocking Hats

Recommended Unlock Order

  1. Start with the Frog hats - they're easy exploration challenges
  2. Work on Shady Guy hats passively as you play
  3. Focus on character-specific hats while leveling those characters
  4. Save Crown and Kevin Hat for last - they take the longest

Tracking Progress

Check your Steam Achievements to track progress on hat unlocks. Many hat challenges are tied directly to achievements.

üíæ Don't Lose Progress!

If you ever lose your save file, use the new Save Recovery Feature to restore progress based on your Steam achievements.
