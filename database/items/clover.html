üçÄ How Clover Works

Clover is one of the most universally valuable items in Megabonk. As a default starting item, it requires no unlock and can appear in your very first run. Its simple yet powerful effect‚Äî+7.5% Luck‚Äîquietly shapes the quality of everything you acquire throughout your run.

What Does Luck Actually Do?

Luck is an invisible stat that directly influences the rarity and quality of rewards you receive. Higher Luck means better loot across multiple game systems:

  • Chest Rewards: Increased chance of Uncommon, Rare, Epic, and Legendary items from all chests
  • Level-Up Upgrades: Better upgrade options when you level up, with more frequent high-tier choices
  • Shrine Outcomes: Improved rewards from Charge Shrines scattered throughout maps
  • Merchant/Moai Offerings: Higher rarity items in shop inventories
üî¨ What Luck Does NOT Affect

Luck in Megabonk exclusively impacts item drop rarity and upgrade quality. It does not influence critical hit chance, item proc rates, or on-hit effect trigger frequency. These are separate mechanics entirely.

The Snowball Effect

Investing in Luck early creates a powerful compound effect. Better items lead to stronger combat performance, which allows you to survive longer and reach even more valuable loot opportunities. A single early Clover can significantly improve the trajectory of your entire run.

Luck Scaling Breakpoints

Understanding Luck thresholds helps you plan your build:

  • 0-50% Luck: Minimal noticeable effect
  • 50-100% Luck: Improved drop quality becomes apparent
  • 100-150% Luck: Dramatic increase in Rare/Epic/Legendary rolls
  • 150%+ Luck: Premium RNG levels‚Äîconsistently excellent rewards
üí° Pro Tip: Delayed Chest Opening

Don't open chests immediately. Stack Luck first (Clover + Luck Tome levels), then open all saved chests for dramatically better rewards. This strategy is especially effective with CL4NK and Fox.

üìã Practical Tips & Strategy

Early Run Priority

  1. Break 20 Vases in your first stage to unlock Luck Tome for future runs
  2. Take Clover as soon as it appears (first 5 minutes ideal)
  3. Secure XP Tome + Curse Tome to complete Holy Trinity
  4. Level Luck Tome to at least 5-8 by mid-game
  5. Save chests until you have 50-100% Luck stacked

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Skipping Luck entirely: Runs feel much harder without quality upgrades
  • Opening chests too early: Low Luck means wasted chest potential
  • Over-investing in Luck: After 150%, focus on direct combat stats
  • Ignoring XP Tome: Luck is useless if you're not leveling efficiently

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What does Clover do in Megabonk?
Clover is a default starting item that increases your Luck stat by 7.5%. Higher Luck improves the quality of items from chests, better upgrade options when leveling, and enhanced Shrine rewards.
Q: Is Clover available from the start?
Yes. Clover is a default item that requires no unlock. It can appear in your item pool from the very first run.
Q: Does Luck affect critical hit chance?
No. Luck in Megabonk exclusively affects item drop rarity and upgrade quality. It does not influence critical hit chance, proc rates, or on-hit effect frequencies. Those are separate mechanics.
Q: Which character benefits most from Clover?
Fox benefits most due to the RNG Blessing passive that grants +1% Luck per level. Combined with Clover and Luck Tome, Fox can reach 150%+ Luck for premium drop quality. However, Clover is valuable for all characters.
Q: Can you stack multiple Clovers?
Yes. You can find multiple Clovers in a single run, and each one stacks additively (+7.5% per Clover). Two Clovers = +15% Luck, three = +22.5%, etc.
Q: When should I skip Clover?
Skip Clover only if: (1) you're in late-game and need immediate combat power, (2) you already have 150%+ Luck from other sources, or (3) critical survival items like Medkit or defensive gear are available and you're struggling.
Q: Does Clover affect boss drops?
While boss drops are generally fixed rewards, higher Luck can improve the quality of chest rewards from boss kills. The main benefit is better general loot throughout your run leading up to bosses.
Q: What's the best Luck-focused build?
Play Fox with the Holy Trinity strategy: Luck Tome + XP Tome + Cursed Tome. Add Clover and Credit Card (Green) for maximum Luck scaling. This is the proven meta for RNG-optimized runs.
