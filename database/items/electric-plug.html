How Electric Plug Works

Electric Plug is a reactive utility item: whenever you take a hit, it emits a brief electric shock that can hit nearby enemies. There are no officially published numbers for its damage or radius, so treat it as a light crowd-control tool that rewards intentional tanking and proximity play.

Reliable points

  • Default unlock: appears in your pool from the start of the game.
  • Trigger: procs when you take damage; does not require you to hit enemies.
  • Range: affects enemies around you (non-official radius; empirical community observation).
