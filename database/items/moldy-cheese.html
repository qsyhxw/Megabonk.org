üß™ How Moldy Cheese Works

Moldy Cheese is one of the most consistent damage-over-time (DOT) items in Megabonk. Its 40% proc rate makes it reliable for applying poison to large groups of enemies, allowing you to focus on survival while your poison does the work.

Poison Mechanics

  • Proc Rate: 40% chance per hit to apply poison
  • Damage Type: Deals damage over time (DOT) with regular ticks
  • Stacking: Multiple poison applications can refresh duration and stack damage
  • Visibility: Poisoned enemies have a green particle effect
  • Scaling: Poison damage scales with your base weapon damage and % damage modifiers
üî¨ Poison Damage Calculation

Poison damage scales with your base weapon damage and any % damage modifiers from tomes and items. The more damage you deal, the stronger your poison ticks become.

Why Moldy Cheese is Essential

Unlike many rare items, Moldy Cheese is available from the start, making it a cornerstone for early-game survival and late-game poison builds. Here's why it's so powerful:

‚úì Advantages

  • Available immediately - no unlock required
  • 40% proc rate = consistent poison application
  • Excellent for crowd control and passive damage
  • Synergizes with multiple build archetypes
  • Poison continues damaging enemies while you kite
  • Can obtain multiple copies via drops/shops/Microwave duplication

‚úó Limitations

  • Requires complementary items to maximize effectiveness
  • Poison damage is delayed - not instant
  • Less effective against bosses with high HP pools alone
  • Needs consistent hits to trigger reliably
  • Not ideal for burst damage builds

üìö Strategy & Tips

Maximizing Moldy Cheese Effectiveness

1. Attack Speed from Cooldown Tome

The more hits you land, the more poison you apply. Prioritize Cooldown Tome for attack speed, not Agility Tome (which affects movement/positioning).

2. AOE Weapons Multiply Value

Weapons that hit multiple enemies (Lightning Staff, Tornado, Bow with Pierce) can apply poison to entire groups simultaneously, maximizing your 40% proc rate.

3. Kiting Strategy

Poison is a fire-and-forget damage source. Apply poison, then kite enemies while the DOT finishes them off. This playstyle is safer than face-tanking.

4. Boss Fight Tips

Against bosses, maintain poison uptime. Stack Damage Tome levels to increase poison tick damage, as bosses have massive HP pools.

5. Multiple Copies Work

Unlike some items, you CAN obtain multiple Moldy Cheeses through drops, shops, or Microwave duplication. Multiple copies stack effects and increase poison frequency.

üéØ Advanced Technique: "Poison Stacking"

With Moldy Gloves (poison clouds) and high hit rate via Cooldown Tome, you can maintain constant poison pressure on groups. Each application refreshes duration and can stack damage.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What does Moldy Cheese do in Megabonk?
Moldy Cheese provides a 40% chance to poison enemies on hit, dealing damage over time (DOT). It's a Default starting item available from the beginning of the game, making it excellent for early poison builds.
Q: Does Moldy Cheese stack with other poison items?
Yes! Moldy Cheese stacks with items like Gas Mask (armor/lifesteal scaling), Moldy Gloves (poison clouds), and Toxic Barrel (poison release on taking damage). Combining multiple poison items creates synergistic builds.
Q: What are the best items to combo with Moldy Cheese?
The best synergies are: Gas Mask (armor/lifesteal per poisoned enemy), Overpowered Lamp (+1 on-hit proc), Moldy Gloves (poison clouds), and Toxic Barrel (defensive poison). These form the core of most poison builds.
Q: Is Moldy Cheese good for boss fights?
Moldy Cheese is decent but not optimal for bosses alone. Poison's strength is in passive, sustained damage, which works better against groups. For bosses, combine Moldy Cheese with damage scaling from Damage Tome and high weapon damage to make poison ticks meaningful.
Q: Can you have multiple Moldy Cheeses?
Yes! You can obtain multiple Moldy Cheese items through drops, shops, or duplication via Microwave (part of Moldy Gloves unlock quest). Multiple copies stack effects and increase poison application frequency.
Q: Does poison damage scale with my weapon damage?
Yes! Poison damage scales with your base weapon damage and any % damage modifiers from Damage Tome and other damage-boosting items. The stronger your base damage, the harder your poison ticks hit.
Q: What's the best character for Moldy Cheese builds?
Fox (+Luck per level) and Vlad (+Lifesteal per level) are excellent choices. Robinette works well for ranged poison builds with pierce weapons.
Q: Is Moldy Cheese viable in late game?
Absolutely. Moldy Cheese scales into late game when paired with the right items. A full poison build (Moldy Cheese + Gas Mask + Overpowered Lamp + Moldy Gloves + Toxic Barrel) can easily carry you through Tier 5+ content.
Q: What does Gas Mask actually do?
Gas Mask grants armor and lifesteal for every poisoned enemy, NOT bonus damage. It's a defensive/survivability item that scales with the number of enemies you've poisoned, making it essential for tanky poison builds.
