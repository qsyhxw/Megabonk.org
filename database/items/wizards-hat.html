Wizard's Hat is the Tome equivalent of Pot (Stainless Steel). While Pot now only affects weapon level caps, Wizard's Hat specifically targets Tome (passive book) level caps.

Effect Details

  • +5 Tome Level Cap per Wizard's Hat collected
  • Allows all Tomes to level 5 more times
  • Stacks with multiple hats (no diminishing returns like Pot)
  • Does NOT affect weapon level caps

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What does Wizard's Hat do?
Wizard's Hat increases all Tome (passive book) level caps by +5, allowing your tomes to level higher and gain more stats.
When was Wizard's Hat added?
It was added in v1.0.49 (Christmas Patch, December 25, 2025) as a companion item to the reworked Pot (Stainless Steel).
Does Wizard's Hat affect weapons?
No. Wizard's Hat only affects Tomes. Use Pot (Stainless Steel) for weapon level caps.
Does Wizard's Hat have diminishing returns?
No! Unlike Pot (Stainless Steel), Wizard's Hat stacks linearly. Each hat gives +5 tome levels with no reduction.
How do I unlock Wizard's Hat?
Wizard's Hat can be found as a random drop or in chests during runs. It's an Epic rarity item.
