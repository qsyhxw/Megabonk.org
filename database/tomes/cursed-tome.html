The Cursed Tome is basically the "hard mode" button in Megabonk. Instead of buffing you, it buffs the enemies. They come faster, hit harder and there are a lot more of them. The upside is that all this chaos gives you way more XP and loot if you can survive.

Overview

  • Stat: Difficulty (Enemy Scaling)
  • Base upgrade per level: +3.5% difficulty per level
  • How to unlock: Beat a Stage Boss in under 5 minutes
  • Max level: 99
  • Category: Challenge Tome

Cursed Tome is for players who want to push their limits. It transforms every run into a brutal survival challenge, but compensates with significantly increased rewards. This is the ultimate "risk equals reward" Tome in Megabonk.

Strategy Guide

  • Don't pick this Tome unless you're confident in your build
  • Stack some defense and healing so you don't just melt

  • Boss fights get scarier, so make sure you're killing them quickly
  • Start with low levels (5-10) and gradually increase as you get stronger
  • Combine with XP Tome for maximum power-leveling efficiency
  • Always have an escape plan - mobility saves lives
