📊 Understanding Our Tier System

S-TIER

Meta Dominant

Best-in-class performance. Consistently dominates competitive play and leaderboards. Must-pick for serious runs.

A-TIER

Highly Competitive

Excellent choices with strong viability. Capable of top-tier performance with proper builds and execution.

B-TIER

Solid & Viable

Good choices for most situations. Reliable performance but may lack the power spike of higher tiers.

C-TIER

Situational

Workable in specific builds or playstyles. Generally outperformed by higher tiers but can succeed with dedication.

📰 Recent Meta Changes

🗓️ Version 1.0.12 | October 8, 2025
Major Balance Overhaul: Final Swarm introduced, Joe's Dagger capped at 2,000% damage, XP Tome nerfed from 9% to 7% per level. Duration bug fixed enabling Dexecutioner/Axe/Sword builds.
🗓️ Version 1.0.7 | September 29, 2025
Kevin + Mirror Nerf: Immortality exploit removed, leaderboards reset. Athena buffed +150% attack speed when unshielded. Character diversity dramatically increased.
🗓️ Current Meta Focus
Skill-Based Era: Meta has shifted from exploit-based to skill-focused gameplay. Holy Trinity (XP + Curse + Luck) remains essential for competitive runs.

🎯 Our Ranking Methodology

🏆 Competitive Performance (40%): Leaderboard presence, top player usage, tournament results

🔄 Versatility (20%): Build options, synergy potential, map adaptability
📊 Meta Impact (15%): Influence on competitive scene, forcing bans/restrictions, defining strategies
