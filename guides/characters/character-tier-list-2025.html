📊 Ranking Methodology

Characters are ranked based on:

  • Accessibility (40%) - Unlock requirements and ease of obtaining
  • Power Level (30%) - RNG blessing strength and weapon effectiveness
  • Consistency (20%) - Reliability across different situations
  • Uniqueness (10%) - Special mechanics and gameplay variety

Rankings prioritize characters available to most players. Many F-tier characters may be very powerful but are ranked low due to extreme unlock requirements.
