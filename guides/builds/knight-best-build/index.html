The Knight (Sir Oofie) Beginner Build

Are you dying too fast on the first few maps? Meet Sir Oofie, the starting character with the game's best defensive passive. Here is the exact meta weapon setup to carry you to your first hard-mode wins.

🛡️ Why Sir Oofie is the Beginner's Best Friend

In Megabonk, survivability rules the early game as you learn boss patterns. Sir Oofie comes with the unique passive ability called Reinforced: He gains a free 1% maximum Armor for every level he achieves.

This means without even picking up an Armor Tome, his natural damage reduction scales automatically into the late-game, granting incredible leniency against mistakes.

⚠️ His Biggest Weakness

He is painfully slow, and his starting Sword swings sluggishly. The entire goal of this build is to patch up his attack speed and supplement his melee attacks with "fire-and-forget" AoE weapons.

⚔️ The Weapon Setup

To fix his mobility issues, we must buy items that allow him to farm enemies efficiently while walking right through them.

1. Primary: The Sword

Stick with his default weapon. Scale it entirely with Attack Speed and Raw Size so it chunks groups around him.

2. Sub-Weapon: Aura

Since he's tanky, enemies will bump into him. Aura creates a continuous damage circle shielding him while he runs.

3. Sub-Weapon: Lightning Staff

Crucial for handling elites or ranged mobs at an extended distance, something the Sword completely fails at doing.

📚 Mandatory Tomes & Items

Do NOT waste money rerolling for fancy Crit mechanic builds. Sir Oofie flourishes beautifully with simple, brute-force flat stats.

📈 Attack Speed Tome + Agility Tome (Top Priority)

You must find the Attack Speed tome to drastically lower his Sword swing animations. Pair this with the Agility Tome so he can outrun later map zones.

💍 The Beefy Ring (Core Engine)

Because Oofie naturally becomes a tank, if you also grab the Health Tome, the Beefy Ring will convert a percentage of your massive HP pool directly into a permanent flat damage multiplier.

🧴 Gym Sauce

Instead of crit, grab Gym Sauce for pure flat damage that immediately shores up early-game wave-clear issues with his slow Sword swings.

💡 Advanced "AFK" Route

If you really want to lean into his defensive nature, once you get comfortable with the basics, you can pivot him into a Thorns Build where his high incoming armor actively hurts the enemies that punch him.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Sir Oofie good for beginners?

Yes, Sir Oofie is widely considered the best starter character. His 'Reinforced' passive grants him free scaling armor every time he levels up, making him incredibly forgiving against chip damage when you don't fully know the boss patterns.

What is the best weapon for the Knight build?

You should stick with his default Sword as your primary melee weapon, but immediately supplement it by picking up the Aura or Lightning Staff/Bone to cover his severe lack of ranged AoE potential.

Why shouldn't I build Crit on Sir Oofie?

Because the Sword attacks very slowly early on. Stacking flat damage sources like the Gym Sauce and scaling Max HP using the Beefy Ring yields far more consistent and reliable early-game DPS than praying for a random critical strike.
