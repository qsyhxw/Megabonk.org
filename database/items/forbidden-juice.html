How Forbidden Juice Works

Forbidden Juice passively raises your Critical Chance by 10%. This simple modifier scales any build that converts crits into extra damage or effects. It is especially valuable on high-crit weapons and characters that can stack crit bonuses reliably.

Why it’s strong

Many top builds lean on frequent crits. Increasing your base crit rate pushes more hits over the threshold, amplifying burst windows and enabling on-crit effects from other items.

How to Unlock Forbidden Juice

Requirement: Land 100 Critical Hits in a single run.

Tips to unlock fast
  • Pick a weapon with naturally high crit potential (e.g., Revolver or Sniper Rifle).
  • Consider crit-leaning characters/builds (e.g., CL4NK, Bandit).
  • Play safely to extend run time; longer runs = more crit opportunities.

Synergies & Build Ideas

Forbidden Juice shines in crit-centric setups and pairs naturally with items/weapons that scale off crits:

Giant Fork

Converts some critical hits into Megacrits, massively boosting damage. Higher crit rate → more chances to trigger.

Demonic Blade

Critical hits have a chance to heal you. More crits from Forbidden Juice improve sustain loops.

Precision-leaning builds

Crit-stacking tomes/builds translate the +10% into consistent damage spikes.

High-crit weapons
