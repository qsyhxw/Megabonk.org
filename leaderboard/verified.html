Verification Rules, Anti-Cheat & Appeal Process

Active Anti-Cheat Protection
Yes, the Megabonk leaderboard is legitimate. All rankings use strict verification measures including automated anti-cheat detection, manual review by moderators, and video proof requirements for top scores. Every verified entry has passed multiple validation checks to ensure fairness.

üîç How Verification Works

The Megabonk leaderboard uses a three-layer verification system to ensure all scores are legitimate and fair.

1

Automated Anti-Cheat Detection

Every submitted score is automatically scanned by anti-cheat algorithms that detect impossible stat combinations, modified game files, and suspicious timing patterns.
Checks include: Damage per second thresholds, kill count vs. run time ratios, weapon combination validation, build possibility analysis, and game version verification.
2

Manual Moderator Review

Scores that pass automated checks are reviewed by experienced moderators who look for suspicious patterns, verify build viability, and check for consistency with known game mechanics.
Review criteria: Build synergy validation, damage calculation verification, boss kill timing analysis, item/tome combination checks, and comparison with established records.
3

Video Proof Requirement

All scores in the top 100 rankings must provide video proof of the complete run. This allows the community to verify legitimacy and helps other players learn winning strategies.
Video requirements: Full unedited run from start to finish, clearly visible UI showing stats, build details visible at end screen, and audio/gameplay sync verification.
‚úÖ Verification Badge
Scores that pass all three verification steps receive a green checkmark badge (‚úÖ) next to their ranking. Only verified scores appear in the official global leaderboard rankings.

üìù How to Submit an Appeal

Step 1: Gather Evidence
Collect video proof (full run, unedited), build screenshots, game logs, and version number. Ensure video clearly shows all UI elements and final score screen.
Step 2: Submit Appeal
Contact support via Official Discord or email support@megabonk.fun with your evidence and detailed explanation.
Step 3: Wait for Review
Moderators review appeals in order received (typically 3-7 days). You'll receive email notification with decision and explanation.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my score marked "Under Review"? ‚ñº
Scores are automatically marked "Under Review" if they trigger any anti-cheat flags or fall within the top 100 rankings. This is normal‚Äîmost legitimate scores pass review within 24-48 hours. Check your email for updates or provide video proof to speed up verification.
Do I need video proof for every submission? ‚ñº
Video proof is only required for top 100 leaderboard positions. However, providing video proof for any score significantly speeds up verification and helps the community learn from your strategies. Lower-ranked scores (101+) are verified through automated checks and manual spot-checking.
Can I get banned for using specific builds or strategies? ‚ñº
No, all in-game builds, item combinations, and strategies are allowed as long as they don't involve modified game files or external tools. Even if a build seems "overpowered," it's legitimate if it's achievable within normal game mechanics. Report bugs through proper channels instead.
How can I report a suspicious leaderboard score? ‚ñº
Use the "Report Score" button on the leaderboard page or contact moderators via Discord. Provide the player name, rank, and specific reason for suspicion. False reports or spam reporting may result in your account being flagged, so only report genuinely suspicious entries.
What happens if I accidentally submit a bugged run? ‚ñº
If you unknowingly benefited from a game bug, your score will be flagged but you won't be banned‚Äîbugs are the developers' responsibility, not yours. Simply explain the situation in your appeal. However, intentionally exploiting known bugs repeatedly may be considered cheating.
