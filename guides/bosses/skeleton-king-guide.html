Attack Patterns

Bone Throw
MEDIUM THREAT

Pattern: Throws 3-5 bone projectiles in straight lines toward player position

Damage: 150-200 per bone

Frequency: Every 3-4 seconds

Counter: Side-step or move perpendicular to avoid projectiles. Bones travel slowly and are easy to dodge.

üíÄ
Summon Skeletons
HIGH THREAT

Pattern: Spawns 6-8 skeleton minions around the battlefield

Minion Health: 200 HP each

Frequency: Every 12-15 seconds

Counter: Clear minions immediately with AOE weapons. Don't let them accumulate!

Always kill skeleton minions first! They will overwhelm you if ignored. The Skeleton King is much easier to handle alone.

Counter: Maintain medium range. Never fight melee unless you have high armor.

Phase Guide

1
Opening Phase (100-75% HP)

Boss focuses on bone throw attacks. Stay mobile and learn the attack timing. Minion spawns are less frequent. Good time to establish positioning and rhythm.

2
Minion Chaos (75-40% HP)

Frequent minion spawns with overlapping summon cycles. This is the most dangerous phase. Focus entirely on minion control. Boss becomes secondary target.

3
Desperate Measures (40-0% HP)

Faster attack speed and more aggressive positioning. Ground slam range increases slightly. Maintain distance and stay patient - victory is close!
