Step-by-Step Build Guide

1

Early Game Foundation (Levels 1-5)

Start with Spinning Sword levels 1-3. Take any Health or Armor items you see. Focus on staying alive while you learn enemy patterns.

2

Damage Addition (Levels 6-10)

Add Battle Axe levels 1-3 for single-target damage. Look for Attack Speed items - they boost both weapons significantly.

3

Survival Focus (Levels 11-15)

Add Whirlwind levels 1-4 for evolution setup. Prioritize Lifesteal and Health Regen items - you'll be taking more damage as enemies get stronger.

4

Evolution Time (Levels 16-20)

Max Spinning Sword to level 6 to trigger Blade Tornado evolution. Add Shield Bash for crowd control and emergency escapes.

5

Optimization Phase (Level 20+)

Focus on items that enhance your evolved weapons. Look for Movement Speed to help with positioning and rare items that boost melee damage.
