🎯 Build Overview

A character‑agnostic execute melee shell that shines on melee‑leaning kits (e.g., Ninja). Open with Size and Cooldown to feel safe and consistent; add Quantity once your core is online; finish with Precision/Damage to push kill thresholds. Bananarang creates a size‑scaling safety ring; pick Black Hole for grouping or Aegis if you prefer block‑and‑retaliate control.

Playstyle

Close‑range kiting • execute bursts • lane control

Difficulty

Medium — early discipline; big payoff mid‑late

Power Spike

Mid‑game as Dex + Size/CDR align

Best For

Elites/bossing, dense waves, melee mains

⚠️ Common Mistakes

Rushing Quantity

Add after Size/CDR so you don’t slow breakpoints.

Too many weapons

3–4 total keeps upgrades focused for faster execute scaling.

Ignoring Precision/Damage

End‑game thresholds suffer without these finishers.

Skipping safety early

Without Bananarang/Golden Shield, early dives can snowball into deaths.

🧠 Why This Works

Katana + Dexecutioner is widely recognized as a meta pairing: Katana’s multi‑hit/pierce primes packs while Dex converts them with execute bursts. Dex scales especially well with Size, Cooldown, and later Quantity, so your kill checks accelerate over time. Bananarang forms a size‑scaling safety orbit, and Black Hole/Aegis give you the control lever you prefer.
