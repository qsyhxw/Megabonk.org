🎮 Gameplay Strategy

Phase 1: Early Game (0-10 minutes)

Building Your Arsenal

Priority Goals:

  • Unlock CL4NK (Forest Tier 1 + buy Revolver)
  • Get XP Tome first - you need levels for crit chance
  • Pick up Lucky Charm as soon as possible
  • Add Lightning Staff for your second weapon
  • Maintain distance from enemies - you're squishy
💡 Your crit rate is still low - focus on survivability
💡 Kite enemies in circles while shooting
Phase 2: Mid Game (10-20 minutes)

Crit Stacking Phase

Key Actions:

  • You should be level 25-50 now (25-50% crit rate)
  • Add Fireball for the third projectile weapon
  • Get Crit Damage Gem to amplify your crits
  • Add Attack Speed Gear for more bullets
  • Start taking Luck Tome and Curse Tome
💡 50% crit rate = every other shot crits = good damage
💡 Keep maximum distance - let your projectiles do the work
Phase 3: Late Game (20+ minutes)

True Bullet Hell

Optimization:

  • You should be approaching or at 100% crit chance
  • Every shot is now a critical hit
  • Screen is filled with projectiles and explosions
  • Enemies die before they can reach you
  • Bosses melt in seconds under your sustained DPS
💡 You're now at peak power - play aggressively
💡 Position to hit multiple enemies with pierce/chain effects
