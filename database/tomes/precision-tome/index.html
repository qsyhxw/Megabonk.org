Overview

Precision Tome increases your Critical Hit Chance, enabling critical strikes that deal significantly increased damage. When your total Crit Chance exceeds 100%, you unlock the Overcrit mechanic, allowing multiple critical hits on a single attack for exponentially higher burst damage.

How It Works: Each level of Precision Tome adds +7% Crit Chance (based on community testing and guides). This value is additive with other sources of Crit Chance from items and upgrades. When combined with Crit Damage modifiers and base damage boosts, Precision greatly amplifies overall DPS output.

Overcrit Mechanism

When your total Crit Chance exceeds 100%, the "overflow" translates into a probability to land multiple crits on a single hit, each multiplying the damage further:

Popular Build Combinations

Precision Tome is frequently paired with other offensive Tomes in high-damage builds. Common combinations referenced in community discussions include:

  • Precision + Damage + Cooldown: A popular "offensive trio" for maximizing DPS
  • Precision + Luck + XP/Cursed: Another common setup focusing on item quality and progression
  • Precision + Quantity: More projectiles = more chances to land crits

Note: There is no single "Holy Trinity" confirmed by developers. Build preferences vary based on character, weapon, and playstyle.

Example Weapon Synergies

Precision Tome benefits all weapons universally since Crit Chance applies to every hit. However, weapons with high hit frequency or multi-projectile attacks maximize the value:

  • Multi-hit weapons (e.g., rapid-fire weapons): More hits per second = more crit rolls
  • Projectile-based weapons (e.g., Shotgun, multi-projectile setups): Each projectile can crit independently
  • AoE weapons: Critical hits apply to all enemies within the area
  • High base damage weapons: Crits multiply already-large damage numbers

Examples are illustrative; Precision is effective across all weapon types.

  • Overcrits are community-observed but not fully documented in official sources; they add significant burst potential at very high levels
  • Essential for boss-killing builds that need burst damage
  • Scaling accelerates as you invest more levels, especially after crossing 100% Crit Chance
  • Target 100%+ Crit Chance to unlock Overcrit mechanics for maximum power spikes
