Overview

Projectile Speed Tome (also known as Projectile Tome in some community references) increases the travel speed of projectiles fired by your weapons. Faster projectiles reach enemies more quickly, improving hit consistency against mobile targets and extending effective range for certain projectile types.

How It Works: Each level adds approximately +15% Projectile Speed (based on community testing and tier list documentation). This stat affects only the velocity of projectiles in flight—it does NOT increase your attack rate or firing speed. The effect is most noticeable with slow-moving projectiles like thrown flasks or magical orbs.

Important:
  • Projectile Speed is separate from Attack Speed/Cooldown—this Tome only affects how fast projectiles travel, not how often you fire
  • For some time-based projectiles (e.g., flasks that despawn after X seconds), higher speed can effectively extend the distance covered before despawning
  • For distance-capped or instant attacks, the range benefit may be minimal

Naming & Classification

This Tome is officially listed as "Projectile Tome" in community wikis and game databases, with the effect described as "+Projectile Speed". Some guides and tier lists refer to it as "Projectile Speed Tome" for clarity. Both names refer to the same Tome. In this guide, we use "Projectile Speed Tome" for descriptive clarity.

Incompatible Weapons

Projectile Speed has NO effect on:

  • Melee weapons (Sword, Hammer, etc.) - No projectiles to accelerate
  • Instant-hit/hitscan weapons - Hit detection is immediate upon attack
  • Aura-based weapons (Aura, Disco Ball) - Effect areas, not projectiles
  • Beam weapons - Continuous beams rather than discrete projectiles
  • Certain special weapons - Always check weapon upgrade screens for "Projectile Speed" option

Note: Some weapons may have derived projectile effects from upgrades—test individual weapon interactions in-game.

Strategy Guide

  • Excellent choice for slow-moving projectile weapons like Bow, Firestaff, or thrown weapons
  • Especially helpful against fast-moving enemies that easily dodge slower shots
  • Improves quality of life significantly in chaotic late-game encounters
  • Essential for kiting builds that need consistent hits at range
  • Particularly valuable in boss fights with mobile targets
  • Indirect DPS boost—doesn't increase damage directly, but improves hit consistency and effective DPS

When to Invest

Consider prioritizing Projectile Speed if you are:

  • Using slow projectile weapons - Bow, Firestaff, thrown weapons, etc.
  • Playing long-range builds - Kiting strategies and characters focused on range
  • Facing fast enemies consistently - When enemies frequently dodge your shots
  • Running multi-projectile builds - Benefits multiply with each additional projectile from Quantity
  • Playing high difficulty runs - Where consistency and reliability matter more than peak damage

When to Skip or Deprioritize

Projectile Speed may be less valuable if:

  • ⚠️ Using melee or non-projectile weapons - Zero benefit from this stat
  • ⚠️ Already using fast projectiles - Diminishing returns on already-quick travel speed
  • Prioritizing core DPS stats - Damage, Cooldown, Precision typically offer higher raw DPS gains
  • Short-range playstyle - Travel time is negligible at close quarters
  • Limited Tome slots available - May need to focus on more universally impactful stats first

Final Thoughts

Projectile Speed Tome is a weapon-dependent utility Tome that shines with slow-moving projectile weapons. While it doesn't directly boost damage numbers, it significantly improves hit consistency and effective DPS by ensuring your projectiles reach targets faster and more reliably. The difference between level 0 and level 20+ is dramatic—your shots become much more reliable, especially against mobile targets. Consider it essential for slow projectile builds, and optional but valuable for standard ranged setups. Skip entirely if using melee or non-projectile weapons.
