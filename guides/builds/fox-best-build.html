🦊 Fox (Kitsune) — Ranged Luck Build

Passive: RNG Blessing → +2% Luck per level (v1.0.17)

🎯 Build Overview

Fox excels at safe, ranged combat that snowballs through Luck. With v1.0.17, Fox’s passive grants +2% Luck per level, improving offer rarity and drop quality. Pair fast-firing ranged weapons with projectile count and cooldown to scale quickly, then add defensive tools to cover close-range threats.

🎮 Playstyle

Ranged kiting • Aim for high uptime and screen coverage

💪 Difficulty

Medium • Safer than melee; rewards positioning

⚡ Power Spike

Mid game • When Quantity/Damage/Cooldown Tomes stack

🏆 Best For

Consistent clears • Bossing with Sniper/Bow support

📈 Recommended Progression

Use this as a practical checklist—not a script. Adapt to your shop/offers.

0–3 min

Establish Safe Ranged DPS

Pick up Revolver (or Bow if it appears first). Add Quantity or Cooldown when offered. Keep moving; don’t get cornered.

3–6 min

Enable Luck Engine

Grab Luck Tome and Clover (+7.5% Luck). If available, add Battery (+8% AS). Slot Lightning Staff for wave control.

6–10 min

Scale Damage

Stack Quantity + Damage. Add Forbidden Juice (+10% Crit Chance) and Tactical Glasses for boss openers.

10–15 min

Add Defense

Choose Aegis or Aura. Consider Mirror / Phantom Shroud for safety in high-curse runs.

15+ min

Polish & Adapt

Continue upgrading Cooldown/Damage/Quantity. Add situational items like Eagle Claw (+66% vs airborne) when relevant.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌ Confusing Luck with Crit

Clover and Luck Tome raise Luck (offer/drop quality)—they do not directly raise crit chance.

❌ Over-greeding on Weapons

3–4 weapons is a good cap to avoid diluting upgrades. Revolver + Bow/Sniper + Lightning Staff + one defense is a solid template.

❌ Neglecting Defense

High-curse or dense waves require a safety layer (Aegis/Aura) or defensive items (Mirror/Phantom Shroud).

❓ FAQ

What is the best weapon for Fox?

There isn’t a single mandatory pick. Revolver is a flexible core with Quantity/Cooldown. Bow offers piercing coverage; Sniper Rifle shines for bosses. Use Lightning Staff for waves and add Aegis/Aura for defense.

Which tomes should I prioritize?

Quantity, Damage, Cooldown are the backbone. Luck Tome is excellent with Fox’s passive. XP Tome is +7% since v1.0.12—still good early. Consider Shield or Curse based on comfort.

What items work best with Fox?

Clover (+7.5% Luck), Battery (+8% AS), Forbidden Juice (+10% Crit Chance). Great complements: Tactical Glasses (+20% vs >90% HP), Eagle Claw (+66% vs airborne; 8% launch), Mirror (reflect + i-frames), Phantom Shroud (evade → 2× hit + brief AS/MS boost).

Patch note: Fox passive buffed to +2% Luck per level in v1.0.17. XP Tome was reduced to +7% in v1.0.12. Item effects above reflect current community-verified values.
