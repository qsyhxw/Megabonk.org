Battery

85.4% Unlocked
üéØ Strategy
  • Cooldown Tome is one of the most powerful tomes in the game
  • Prioritize picking it whenever it appears in level-up choices

  • Level 5 requires consistent XP farming and smart level-up choices
üí° Tips
Works well with any character. Focus on staying alive and gaining XP. The Cooldown Tome synergizes with almost every weapon, making this an easy early achievement.

~70% Unlocked
üéØ Strategy
  • Agility Tome increases movement speed - essential for kiting and survival
  • Particularly useful for speed-based builds like Calcium
  • Helps with dodging attacks and repositioning during boss fights
  • Combine with speed boots power-up for maximum effect
üí° Tips
Best achieved with speed-focused characters. The increased mobility makes farming easier and safer. Essential tome for Final Swarm survival.

  • Requires multiple successful runs to achieve

Ghost

Survive the Final Swarm for 60 seconds

98.6% Unlocked
üéØ Strategy
  • Final Swarm triggers when the stage timer reaches zero
  • Build for speed and mobility - constant movement is key
  • Run in large circles around the map to avoid getting cornered
  • Use speed power-ups if you find them before Final Swarm
üí° Tips
This is likely your first Final Swarm achievement. Don't try to fight - just survive! Practice your movement and dodging. Any character can achieve this with good positioning.
üíÄ

Bob (Dead)

Survive the Final Swarm for 2 minutes

35.6% Unlocked
üéØ Strategy
  • Requires a strong defensive build with high survivability
  • Best characters: Sir Oofie (armor), Megachad (Aura), Athena (Aegis)
  • Stack evasion, armor, and lifesteal items
  • Recommended weapons: Aegis for blocking, Aura for 360¬∞ defense
üí° Recommended Build
Tank Build: Aegis + Health Pendant + Armor items + Evasion boots. Circle the outer edge of the map. Let enemies come to you while Aegis blocks damage. Use lifesteal to stay alive.
üëπ

Soul Harvester

Survive the Final Swarm for 5 minutes

~8% Unlocked
üéØ Strategy
  • One of the hardest survival achievements in the game
  • Requires perfect build optimization and execution
  • Use Walking Apocalypse build: Aura + Mirror + Kevin combo
  • Stack max HP, armor, evasion, and regen
üí° Expert Build
Immortality Build: Kevin + Mirror + Aura + Health Pendant + Lucky Charm + Speed Boots. The Kevin+Mirror combo makes you nearly invincible. Circle the map slowly and let enemies die to your Aura. Patience is key.

Boss Buster

Defeat a Stage Boss in under 30 seconds

70.2% Unlocked
üéØ Strategy
  • Build for maximum DPS - ignore defensive stats for this run
  • Best weapons: Dice (RNG burst), Katana (high attack speed), Aura (if large)
  • Stack Damage Tome, Attack Speed Tome, and Size Tome
  • Target the Forest Tier 1 boss - easiest boss for speed kills
üí° Tips
Rush to find the boss early before Final Swarm. Start damaging immediately. Use any damage power-ups you find beforehand. CL4NK with high crit build works well.
üëì

Tactical Glasses

Defeat a Miniboss in 25 seconds

96.2% Unlocked
üéØ Strategy
  • Minibosses are the smaller elite enemies that spawn during stages
  • Much easier than stage bosses - achievable with any decent build
  • Focus fire on one miniboss at a time
  • Works well with multi-hit weapons like Revolver or rapid Sword attacks
üßÉ

Forbidden Juice

Crit 100 times in a single run

96.9% Unlocked
üéØ Strategy
  • Use CL4NK - his passive gives +1% crit chance per level
  • Equip Revolver (his default weapon) for rapid fire
  • Stack crit chance items and Lucky Charm
  • By level 20-30, you'll naturally hit 100 crits
üí° Tips
Very easy achievement with the right character. CL4NK makes this almost impossible to miss. Just play normally and you'll unlock it.

Anvil

19.6% Unlocked
üéØ Strategy
  • Challenges are special game modes with modified rules
  • Start with easier challenges that match your playstyle
  • Speed challenges are generally easier than restriction challenges
  • Can be completed over multiple attempts
üí° Recommended Challenges
Start with: Speed Run Challenge (reach boss in 10 min), No Damage Challenge (with defensive build), and XP Rush Challenge (level up 30 times). Save harder challenges for later.

~40% Unlocked
üéØ Strategy
  • Requires extended runs with high XP gain

üí° Pro Tips for Basic Achievements

üéØ Efficient Progression
Focus on multiple achievements per run. For example, work on tome upgrades while also aiming for survival and boss time achievements. This saves time and maximizes efficiency.
üìö Master the Holy Trinity
XP Tome + Curse Tome + Luck Tome is the meta build. These three tomes make ALL achievements easier. Learn this build early and use it as your foundation.
‚ö° Character Selection
Fox and Sir Oofie are best for basic achievements. Fox for general progression, Sir Oofie for survival challenges. Both are available from the start and very beginner-friendly.
üîÑ Practice Makes Perfect
Don't worry about failing. Each death teaches you enemy patterns and map layouts. The "Cursed Doll" achievement exists specifically to normalize failure as part of learning.
