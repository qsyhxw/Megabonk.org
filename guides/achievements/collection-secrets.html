üí° Collection Mastery Tips

üéØ Banish + Toggler Priority
These two meta upgrades (40 quests + 40 shop purchases) are ESSENTIAL. They let you control your item pool and build consistency. Rush these before anything else.
‚ö†Ô∏è Avoid Dilution Trap
Don't blindly complete all quests! Each one unlocks items that dilute your pool. After getting Banish/Toggler, be selective about which quests you complete.
üó∫Ô∏è Systematic Exploration
For secret achievements, dedicate entire runs to exploration. Don't rush to boss. Check every corner, interact with suspicious objects, try different characters.
üí∞ Silver for Everything
Most collection achievements require buying items/chests. Use silver farming builds (Luck + Aura) to accumulate currency faster. Prioritize purchases over rerolls.
üìö Tome Synergy
Max XP Tome first - it speeds up leveling ALL other tomes. Then focus on Luck and Curse for better drops and faster quest completion.
‚è∞ Long-Term Mindset
100% collection takes 50-100 hours. Don't burn out trying to rush it. Enjoy the journey, try different builds, and let achievements come naturally.
