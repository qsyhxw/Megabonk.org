DirectX 12 Support

The game now supports running in DX12 mode, though DX11 remains the recommended option. If you're experiencing crashes, DX12 might help stabilize performance. A Steam popup will appear when launching the game, allowing you to choose your rendering mode. If the popup doesn't appear, try restarting Steam.

Note: When running in DX12, certain graphical features will be disabled or broken, including Ambient Occlusion and Color Grading.

Visual Opacity Controls

A new particle and effects opacity slider has been added to Visual settings. Auto opacity is enabled by default and should work well for most players. This feature addresses the infamous "late-game light show" that previously required wearing sunglasses.

While transparent projectiles are slightly more demanding on your GPU, performance impact is negligible until extreme late-game scenarios. Once your screen is completely covered with massive projectiles, you can experiment with full transparency for better FPS, or set to 0 transparency for absolute best performance.

üéÆ New Game Features

Fog of War Mapping

The most significant addition in this patch is a full map with fog of war functionality. You can now track exactly where you've explored during each run, making navigation and area discovery much more intuitive. Check out our tips and tricks guide for strategies on efficient map exploration.

Quality of Life Improvements

  • Silver Tome: Can now be toggled on/off from the Unlocks menu using the Toggler. Learn more in our Silver Tome database entry.
  • Charge Shrines: Now display a different color on the minimap compared to other shrines for easier identification
  • Hero Sword & Corrupted Sword: Fixed scaling issue where projectile speed didn't increase with size multiplier
  • Money Particles: Move faster during final swarm, allowing you to collect them while running
  • Multi-Level Stats: When gaining multiple levels instantly, stat changes now appear in real-time on the right side as you pick upgrades

üìà Character Buffs

  • Fox ‚Äî Passive luck increased from 1% to 2% per level. This doubles Fox's scaling potential with luck-based builds and legendary item acquisition. Check out our Fox best build guide for updated strategies.
  • Birdo ‚Äî Passive now grants 1% Airborne Damage per level. This new scaling mechanic significantly improves Birdo's damage output throughout the run. View the Birdo best build for aerial combat strategies.
  • Bush ‚Äî Passive crit damage increased from 0.5% to 1% per level. Additionally, Bullseyes now appear more frequently on enemies, and explosion size scales with size multiplier. These changes make Bush's crit-focused playstyle much more powerful. Try the Bush best build with Sniper Rifle.
  • Megachad ‚Äî Passive now grants 2.5% damage every time you flex. This stacking damage bonus rewards aggressive flexing throughout the run. Discover Megachad's optimal build.
  • Noelle ‚Äî Major passive rework: Now gains Size instead of Duration per level. Additionally, gains a damage multiplier for each frozen enemy (0.02x per frozen enemy, meaning 50 frozen enemies = 2x damage multiplier). This change dramatically improves Noelle's scaling and leaderboard viability. Her Frostwalker weapon synergizes perfectly with this new passive. See the updated Noelle freeze build.
  • Dicehead ‚Äî Passive rewards now fall off much slower. Previously rewards were halved by level 25; now they don't halve until level 50. This extends Dicehead's power curve significantly. Check the Dicehead build guide.
  • Calcium ‚Äî Passive simplified and buffed. Now gains 0.75% damage per level and an additional 0.5% damage per 1% Speed Multiplier. This damage is applied gradually as Calcium approaches top speed, making the speed-demon playstyle more rewarding. Perfect for speed demon builds.
  • Ogre ‚Äî Passive damage buffed from 1% to 1.5% per level, providing a straightforward 50% increase to scaling. View the Ogre best build.
  • Spaceman ‚Äî No longer takes fall damage, a quality-of-life improvement that removes a minor but annoying risk factor. His Space Noodle weapon remains unchanged.

üìâ Character Nerfs

  • Robinette ‚Äî Passive damage gain is now reduced after collecting 200,000 gold to balance super late-game leaderboard runs. Early and mid-game performance has been buffed, but this change primarily affects extreme endgame scenarios. This nerf was implemented to increase character diversity on official leaderboards, as Robinette was dominating the rankings. Her Bow weapon still excels in pierce builds.

"It would be nice to buff all other characters to become as strong as Robinette, but that is not something I can do overnight, so a short-term solution was to just nerf her passive late-game for now. I'd like to eventually get in per-character leaderboards too, which also kinda solves this problem."

  • Scarf ‚Äî Damage increased from 33% to 50%, making it a much more valuable damage multiplier item. Essential for high-damage builds.
  • Brass Knuckles ‚Äî Scaling changed from Additive to Flat, which provides significantly stronger scaling. Additionally, now increases size per amount stacked, adding a new dimension to stacking this item. Check our item tier list for updated rankings.
  • Cursed Doll ‚Äî Can now curse 5 enemies per tick (increased from 1) every 1 second. This 5x effectiveness increase makes Cursed Doll far more impactful for curse-based builds. Pairs well with Cursed Tome.
