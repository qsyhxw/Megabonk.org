🔓 How to Unlock Dice (Verified)
This corrects common misconceptions
✅ Summary
• Dice unlocks by getting the Luck Tome to Level 12 in a single run.
• Luck Tome is not unlocked by breaking pots — it unlocks from a 0.01% enemy drop (once found, it’s permanent).
• XP Tome is the one unlocked by breaking 20 pots in one run and simply helps you level faster.
1) Farm until Luck Tome drops once (0.01% enemy drop) to add it to your tome pool.
2) Start a run focused on finding & upgrading Luck Tome; take rerolls if needed.
3) Use XP Tome and magnets/shrines to accelerate leveling (optional but recommended).
4) Reach Luck Tome Lv.12 in the same run → Dice unlocks permanently.

💡 Tip: Fewer tome slots = higher odds your level-up offers include Luck Tome. XP Tome helps, but it’s not part of the Dice unlock condition.

⚙️ Dice Mechanics (Real Data)
What the weapon actually does in-game
🎲 Throw & Roll
Dice throws a die that deals 1–6 damage. When a 6 is rolled, Dice’s own critical hit chance increases permanently for the rest of the run.
Base Behavior: 1–6 damage per throw
On 6: Permanent crit chance gained (stacks)
Scope: Affects Dice weapon (post-patch), not global crit
⚡ Attack Speed Synergy
Faster attacks = more throws = more chances to roll 6. Items/tomes that increase attack speed or reduce cooldown meaningfully accelerate scaling.
Great Picks: Battery (+~8% Atk Speed), Cooldown Tome, Quantity Tome
Movement → Speed: Turbo Skates can convert move speed to attack speed
🔥 Crit Damage Multipliers
As Dice’s crit chance climbs, Crit Damage becomes increasingly valuable. The Giant Fork can upgrade crits into Mega-Crits for huge bursts.
Key Items: Giant Fork (Mega-Crits), Big Bonk (2% for 20× hit), Forbidden Juice (+10% Crit Chance)
Note: Big Bonk is strong but unreliable due to low proc chance
🧙 Character Notes
Dicehead: passive grants a random stat on each level (not “double level”); long runs feed Dice’s scaling. CL4NK: gains +1% crit chance per level and thrives in crit builds (great overall, though Dice’s stacks are weapon-local).
Dicehead: Random stat per level (great scaling runs)
CL4NK: +1% Crit Chance per level (crit archetype staple)

🧠 Advanced Tips
To make Dice truly pop
⚡ Prioritize Tempo
Cooldown/attack speed first so you’re rolling the dice more often. Battery and Turbo Skates are standouts.
🎯 Scale Crit Damage
Once Dice’s crit chance is healthy, stack crit damage and chase Giant Fork for Mega-Crits.
🍀 Understand Luck
Luck improves offer/chest quality; it does not change the 1-in-6 roll that grants Dice’s crit stacks.
📈 Leveling Flow
Use XP Tome, magnets, and dense waves to spike levels, especially mid-game when build pieces start to appear.
🛡️ Survive Early
Dice is weak early. Take survivability picks (shields, regen, movement) until your crit engine comes online.
📋 Unlock Strategy (Step-by-Step)
From zero to Dice unlocked

Phase 1: Get Luck Tome

Goal: Trigger its first drop

  • • Farm enemies until it drops once (0.01% chance)
  • • After the first drop, Luck Tome becomes permanent in your pool
  • • Optional: Run characters with strong clear for more kill volume

Phase 2: Leveling Setup

Goal: See more upgrade rolls

  • • Use XP Tome (unlocked by breaking 20 pots in one run)
  • • Consider reducing tome slots to raise odds of seeing Luck offers
  • • Farm magnets/shrines to chain level-ups

Phase 3: Push Luck Tome to 12

Goal: Unlock Dice

  • • Prioritize Luck Tome every time it appears
  • • Maintain survival & clear to keep offers flowing
  • • Once at Lv.12, Dice unlocks for future runs

⚠️ Common Mistakes

Wrong Unlock Info: Luck Tome ≠ pots; that’s XP Tome.
Too Many Tome Slots: Lowers chance to see Luck Tome offers.
Ignoring Tempo: No attack speed/cooldown → slower Dice stacks.
Over-greed Early: Dying before stacks build.
