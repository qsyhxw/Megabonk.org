How Coward’s Cloak Works

Coward’s Cloak adds a small but always-on mobility bump and grants a short burst of speed whenever you take damage. Use it as an escape valve in dense waves or as insurance while repositioning.

Pairs with Electric Plug

Both trigger when you’re hit—move out faster while Plug zaps nearby enemies.

Stacks with Turbo Socks

Flat +15% Move Speed amplifies both your baseline and post-hit mobility.

Speed → Damage (Turbo Skates)

Convert Move Speed into Attack Speed for aggressive “hit-and-run” builds.
