💡 Pro Tips for Achievement Hunting

🪙
Coin Farming Strategy
Use Knight with coin multiplier items. Focus on 10-15 minute runs for optimal coins/hour ratio. Always grab coin chests.
🎯
Stack Achievements
Plan runs to complete multiple achievements simultaneously. Example: Genocide + Survivor + Boss achievements in one run.
💾
Save Scumming
For difficult achievements like Pacifist or No Damage, consider backing up saves before attempts (if playing offline).
📺
Video Guides
Watch speedrunners and achievement hunters on YouTube/Twitch for advanced strategies and routing optimization.
🔄
Reset Efficiency
For RNG-dependent achievements, reset immediately if conditions aren't met early. Don't waste time on doomed runs.
📊
Track Progress
Use Steam's achievement tracker or external tools to monitor your progress and plan your next sessions efficiently.
