Roberto Best Build Guide

Maximize Hoarder free chests, dominate with Scythe, and snowball every run with optimized weapon, tome, and item loadouts.

v1.0.64 | March 2026

👻 Why Play Roberto?

Roberto is a high-ceiling DPS character introduced in the Spooky Update. His Hoarder passive spawns a free chest every 60 seconds, giving him a unique economic advantage no other character can match. Combined with the Float movement ability and the 360° Scythe weapon, Roberto rewards players who understand item synergies and can survive his vulnerable early game.

💡 Key Synergy: Roberto's Hoarder passive + Luck Tome + Clover = consistently pulling Legendary items from free chests by mid-game. This is the foundation of every successful Roberto build.

🎯 Game Phase Strategy

🌅 Early Game (Levels 1–30) — Survive & Stack Luck

  • Float aggressively to avoid damage — Roberto's HP is very low at start
  • Grab Aura immediately if offered for passive defense
  • Rush Luck Tome as your first or second tome upgrade
  • Use Toggler to disable useless items before starting the run
  • Collect your free chests near safe areas — don't die reaching them

☀️ Mid Game (Levels 31–70) — Economy Snowball

  • Stack Luck Tome upgrades to pull Legendary items from every chest
  • Invest in Size Tome to expand Scythe's effective range
  • Prioritize Clover and Credit Card items from chest drops
  • Note: Chest interval increases with level (+2.5s/level) — maximize early chests
  • Focus on 2–3 weapons maximum to ensure upgrades concentrate

🌙 Late Game (Levels 71+) — Maximum Power

  • Chonkplate overheal effectively doubles your HP pool
  • Elite-charged Scythe attacks clear entire screens of enemies
  • Float + movement speed items make you nearly untouchable
  • Free chests still provide value even at 120s+ intervals
  • Look for Legendary synergy items: Pot, Overpowered Lamp, Giant Fork
⚠️ Common Mistake: Don't ignore survivability items early. Many Roberto players focus purely on Luck/damage and die before mid-game. Secure Chonkplate or HP Regen before scaling damage.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best build for Roberto in Megabonk?
The Luck Hoarder build is the strongest: Scythe + Aura weapons, Luck Tome and Size Tome priority, Clover + Green Credit Card + Chonkplate items. This maximizes value from Roberto's free chests.
What weapons should Roberto use?
Scythe is always your core weapon. Pair with Aura for passive AoE defense, Bananarang for ranged coverage, or Lightning Staff / Dexecutioner for advanced setups. Keep to 2–3 weapons maximum.
How does Roberto's Hoarder passive work?
Hoarder spawns a free chest every 60 seconds. The interval increases by approximately 2.5 seconds per level. Pair with Luck Tome and Clover to ensure these chests drop high-quality items.
Is Roberto good for beginners?
Roberto is challenging for beginners due to his low starting HP and complex unlock requirements (defeat Big Bob + Spooky Steve, 81 Silver total). New players should try Megachad or Sir Oofie first.
