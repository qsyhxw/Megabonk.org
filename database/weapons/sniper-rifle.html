HIGH-TIER WEAPON

Boss-focused, high-damage piercing shots with manual aim

🎯 Weapon Overview

The Sniper Rifle is a long-range, manually aimed weapon that fires piercing bullets. It excels at single-target damage and deleting elites/bosses from a safe distance. Use a secondary AoE weapon to handle large swarms.

🔓 How to Unlock Sniper Rifle

Pick up a Precision Tome during a run and level it to Lv.10. After meeting the requirement, the Sniper Rifle becomes available to buy in the Shop for Silver (amount may vary by progression/version).

  1. Find the Precision Tome
    Take it as soon as it appears.
  2. Prioritize Tome upgrades
    Reroll/pick upgrades to push Precision to Lv.10 within the same run.
  3. Finish the run & check Shop
    Once unlocked, purchase it to add Sniper Rifle to your weapon pool.
💡 Tip: Damage/Cooldown tomes make leveling safer while you chase Precision Lv.10.

Weapon Upgrades (on Sniper Rifle)

  • Damage — High base (22) with strong per-upgrade scaling.
  • Projectile Count — More bullets per shot dramatically increases DPS.
  • Size — Increases hitbox width for easier pierce lines.

Tomes & Items That Synergize

  • Precision Tome — Crit Chance (core for sniper play).
  • Damage Tome — Global damage multiplier.
  • Cooldown/Attack Speed sources — Mitigate slow firing to lift sustained DPS.

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Rushing attack speed first

Without damage/crit foundations, early attack speed feels underwhelming. Balance it.

❌ Fighting up close

Maintain distance to leverage range and manual aim; reposition constantly.

❌ Skipping projectile count

Extra bullets per shot are among the best DPS multipliers for Sniper.

❌ No AoE partner

Pair with an AoE weapon (e.g., Lightning Staff/Aura) to handle crowds efficiently.
