Weapon Slots

61.21% Unlocked
🎯 Important Unlock
Increases your maximum weapon slots by 1, allowing you to carry more weapons simultaneously. Essential for stronger builds. Takes approximately 8-12 runs to reach.
🚫

Banish

Complete 40 quests

50.95% Unlocked
🔥 CRITICAL UNLOCK
Banish is one of the most important meta upgrades! Lets you remove one item from the item pool for a single run, dramatically improving build consistency. Rush to 40 quests ASAP.
💡 Efficient Questing
  • Focus on combat quests (kills, damage, crits)
  • Avoid exploration quests until you know maps well
  • Stack similar quests (e.g., multiple weapon kill quests)
  • Takes approximately 15-20 runs to reach 40 quests
📚

Tome Slots 2

Complete 55 quests

24.56% Unlocked
⚠️ Beware the Dilution Trap
Completing too many quests can actually hurt your runs! Each quest completion unlocks new items, diluting your item pool. After unlocking key items and meta upgrades (Banish at 40 quests, Toggler at 40 unlocks), consider slowing down quest completion. Focus on quality over quantity.
🎲

Dicehead

Complete 100 quests - Unlocks Dicehead Character

3.82% Unlocked
🎯 Major Milestone Achievement
Unlocks the powerful Dicehead character upon completing 100 quests. This is a long-term goal requiring 30-50 hours of gameplay. Dicehead has unique dice-rolling mechanics that provide random buffs each level.
💡 Smart Progression
  • Unlock Banish (40 quests) and Toggler (40 unlocks) first
  • These allow you to control your item pool
  • Then complete quests strategically for specific unlocks
  • Use Toggler to disable unwanted items

💡 Collection Mastery Tips (Updated)

🎯 Banish + Toggler Priority
These two meta upgrades (40 quests for Banish + 40 unlocks for Toggler) are ESSENTIAL. They let you control your item pool and build consistency. Rush these before anything else.
⚠️ Avoid Dilution Trap
Don't blindly complete all quests! Each one unlocks items that dilute your pool. After getting Banish/Toggler, be selective about which quests you complete.
🗺️ Systematic Exploration
For secret achievements, dedicate entire runs to exploration. Don't rush to boss. Check every corner, interact with suspicious objects, try different characters.
💰 Silver for Everything
Most unlocks require Silver currency. Use silver farming builds (Luck + Silver Tome) to accumulate currency faster. Prioritize character/weapon unlocks for Toggler.
📚 Tome Synergy
Max XP Tome first - it speeds up leveling ALL other tomes. Then focus on Luck and Cursed for better drops and faster quest completion. All 23 tomes go to level 99.
⏰ Long-Term Mindset
100% collection takes 50-100 hours. Full completion (102 achievements) averages 32 hours but can take longer for all collectibles. Enjoy the journey!
