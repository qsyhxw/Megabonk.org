Overview

The Damage Tome increases your character's Base Damage, a universal stat that acts as a damage multiplier affecting all weapon types. Each level adds +0.08x (approximately 8%) to your damage multiplier, which then gets applied to your weapon's base damage in the final damage calculation.

According to community technical documentation from v1.0.7-v1.0.12 (September-October 2025), upgrades within the same Tome stack additively with each other. The final damage multiplier from all your Damage Tome levels is then multiplied with your weapon's base damage to calculate your actual damage output.

Version Note: Data based on community guides and player documentation from v1.0.7-v1.0.12 (September-October 2025). Official patch notes may supersede this information.

How Damage Scaling Works

The Damage Tome modifies a character-level damage multiplier that applies universally to all weapons. Understanding how it scales is important for build optimization:

  • Additive Within Itself: Each Damage Tome level adds +0.08x to your total damage multiplier (e.g., 10 levels = +0.8x total)
  • Multiplicative with Weapon Base: Your total damage multiplier then multiplies with your weapon's base damage
  • Universal Application: Affects all weapon types equally - melee, ranged, and AoE
  • Character Stat: Modifies a character-level stat, so it works with every weapon you use
Example Calculation: If you have 10 levels in Common Damage Tome (+0.8x multiplier) and a weapon with 50 base damage, your actual damage would be: 50 √ó (1 + 0.8) = 90 damage per hit.
