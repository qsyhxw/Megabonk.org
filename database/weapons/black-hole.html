Black Hole Complete Guide

The Ultimate Crowd Control Weapon

B-Tier
Situational A-Tier
Version 1.0.12 Post-Nerf

🎯 Weapon Overview

4 Base Damage (Lowest)
7s Cooldown
2s Base Duration
B Tier Rating
⚠️ CRITICAL NOTE: Black Hole is NOT a damage weapon - it's a crowd control enabler! Success requires understanding its unique setup-and-execute gameplay loop. Low base damage (4) means secondary weapons must carry damage output.

🔓 How to Unlock

  1. Level Knockback Tome to Level 10 in any run (15-20 minutes)
  2. Use any character - Fox, Megachad, or CL4NK recommended for survivability
  3. Focus on XP gain and survival rather than damage
  4. Purchase Black Hole for 2 Silver from Unlocks menu
✅ Beginner-Friendly Unlock: Knockback Tome is available by default with no prerequisites. This is one of the simplest weapon unlocks in Megabonk!

⚙️ Core Mechanics

Gravitational Pull System

Black Hole fires projectiles that create gravitational fields, pulling enemies toward a central point and clustering them for devastating AoE follow-up attacks. Enemies are grouped but NOT stunned - they continue attacking if you position poorly!

💡 Strategic Depth: Predictive placement matters more than reactive firing. Shoot where enemies WILL BE, not where they are. Think defensively - Black Holes create safe zones where enemies can't reach you.

Four Upgrade Paths

📈 Damage

Increases direct damage from 4 base. Low priority since crowd control is the main value.

⏱️ Duration

CRITICAL! Base 2s requires tome investment. Keeps enemies grouped longer for combo execution.

🔢 Projectile Count

Multiple Black Hole zones = screen-wide control. Essential for late-game swarms.

📐 Size

MANDATORY! "Size Tome is what makes this build function" - Martyr_Dom. Difference between 3 enemies vs 30.

📜 Version 1.0.12 Balance Changes (October 8, 2025)

✅ Buffs

  • +20% Base Size: Compensatory buff addressing community complaints about ineffective pull radius
  • Duration Bug Fixes: Projectiles no longer stop prematurely. Duration Tome now scales consistently
  • Performance Optimization: "Significantly less FPS drop when combined with Dexecutioner" - no more slideshow gameplay

❌ Nerfs

  • Proc Coefficient: 0.9 → 0.7 (30% reduction in item synergy effectiveness)
  • Hitbox Bug Fix: Eliminated map-wide coverage. Previously affected enemies across ENTIRE map regardless of projectile location
⚠️ Developer Quote: "Fixed a bug where the Black Hole hitboxes were like 10x the size of the actual projectile, basically covering the entire map lol." This "bug fix" was actually the largest effective nerf - Black Hole lost its unintentional god-tier status.

Net Result

Black Hole dropped from A-tier/low S-tier to solid B-tier with A-tier potential in specific builds. No longer a universal power pick but remains a respected specialist tool for crowd control enabler strategies.

⚔️ Best Weapon Combinations

🎯 Black Hole + Dexecutioner

Most Recommended Pairing! PCGamesN: "pairs perfectly." Group enemies with Black Hole, then Dexecutioner's execute chance hits multiple targets simultaneously. Post-optimization, no performance drops!

🔥 Black Hole + Aura

PCGamesN: "works exceptionally well." Aura's 360° damage synergizes perfectly with clustered enemies. Creates death zones.

⚡ Black Hole + Flamewalker

AoE vortex builds. Flame trails on grouped enemies = screen-wide burn. Excellent for swarms.

⚔️ Black Hole + Katana

World record Bandit build component. Fast attacks + Size scaling synergy. Great early-game carry.

💣 Black Hole + Mines

Place mines in Black Hole zones. Enemies walk into trap clusters. Excellent crowd control combo.

🪓 Black Hole + Axe

Overlapping AoE circles shred grouped enemies. Size Tome makes axes screen-fillers.

☠️ Black Hole + Poison Flask

DOT on clustered groups. Especially powerful with Moldy Cheese stacking for lifesteal healing.

👥 Character Synergies

✅ Best Characters

🗡️ Bandit - S-Tier

+1% Attack Speed per level multiplies Black Hole firing frequency AND secondary weapon effectiveness. World records consistently use Bandit + Black Hole. High levels = near-constant battlefield coverage!

💪 Megachad - B-Tier

Defensive bonuses allow safer positioning for optimal Black Hole placement. Survival-focused playstyle capitalizes on zone control.

🦊 Fox / 🤖 CL4NK - Neutral

General purpose characters work adequately with Black Hole as utility weapon, though lack specialized synergies.

⚠️ Spaceman - The Controversy

Default Black Hole Character, But...

Despite having Black Hole as starter weapon, community overwhelmingly considers Spaceman the WEAKEST and most challenging character. Player quote: "Completing 6 challenges which took me at least 10 hours... to get such a pitifully weak character... was so disappointing it makes me not want to play anymore."

Why? Black Hole's inherently weak early game + Spaceman having NO defensive or damage-boosting passives = brutal difficulty spike. His +1.5% XP per level helps reach tome scaling faster, but doesn't compensate for survival struggles.

❌ Anti-Synergy Characters

  • Calcium (Speed Demon): High mobility conflicts with Black Hole's stationary zone control
  • Crit-Focused Builds: Black Hole has low proc coefficient and minimal base damage for crit scaling
  • Single-Target Specialists: Gain minimal benefit since Black Hole's strength is grouping multiple enemies

🎓 Advanced Strategies

🎯 Positioning Tactics

  • Think Defensively: Black Holes create safe zones where enemies can't reach you. Use them as shields!
  • Predictive Placement: Shoot where enemies WILL BE, not where they are. Lead your shots!
  • Corner/Wall Positioning: Limits enemy approach angles and concentrates Black Holes
  • Circular Movement: While kiting, place Black Holes in enemy pursuit paths
  • Choke Point Control: Excels at narrow passages where Black Hole forces clustering

⏱️ Timing Strategies

  • Early Game (0-10min): Focus mobility between 7s cooldown shots. Black Hole alone won't carry damage
  • Mid Game (10-30min): Coordinate Black Hole shots with secondary weapon high-damage windows
  • Late Game (30min+): Maintain constant battlefield coverage through high Projectile Count

👑 Boss Fight Strategies

⚠️ CRITICAL BOSS WARNING: Final Boss deletes all weapons except your HIGHEST-LEVELED weapon. If Black Hole is highest level, you face final boss with only 4 base damage!

Solution: Keep a high-damage weapon (Dexecutioner, Katana, Revolver) as highest level throughout run!
  • Single Target Weakness: Low base damage (4) means secondary weapons must carry boss DPS
  • Add Control: Black Hole excels at controlling boss-spawned minions. Group adds for AoE clear
  • Positioning Over Damage: Use Black Hole to create safe zones during boss special attacks

🌊 Swarm Strategies

Where Black Hole Truly Dominates:

  • Dense Mob Swarms: Pull 10-30+ enemies into single points for massive AoE damage
  • Multiple Approach Angles: Create defensive zones at chokepoints
  • Ghost Overtime (60min+): Black Hole required for survival in endless swarms
  • Cursed Tome Runs: Massive hordes that Black Hole uniquely controls

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

🔴 Newbie Errors

  • Treating Black Hole as Primary Damage Source: Base damage is only 4! You MUST pair with damage follow-up weapons
  • Ignoring Size Tome: Prima Games: "Size Tome is what makes this build function." Without it, pull radius is too small
  • Poor Positioning: Destructoid: "you'll have to position the hole carefully." Random placement wastes utility
  • Expecting Instant Results: Early game is genuinely weak. Don't abandon before mid-game scaling transforms it!
  • Playing Spaceman: Despite having Black Hole as starter, he's widely considered the worst character

🟡 Experienced Player Mistakes

  • Over-Investing in Proc Synergies Post-Nerf: Coefficient reduction to 0.7 reduced on-hit effectiveness by 30%
  • Neglecting Duration Tome: Enemies won't stay grouped long enough for combo execution without duration scaling
  • Over-Relying on Final Swarm: Developers specifically nerfed Black Hole's late-game ghost effectiveness
  • Wrong Weapon Priority for Final Boss: If Black Hole is highest level, you're stuck with 4 damage against the boss!
