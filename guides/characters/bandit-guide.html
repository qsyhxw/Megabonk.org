üí° Pro Tips & Advanced Strategies

‚úì Kevin+Mirror Mastery

Bandit is THE best Kevin+Mirror character. His RNG Blessing attack speed creates the most consistent invulnerability frame overlap. Despite the 1.0.7 nerf, he still excels with this combo.

‚úì Stack Attack Speed First

Prioritize Battery, Cooldown Tome, and Time Bracelet over damage items. Attack speed multiplies the effectiveness of EVERYTHING in your build.

‚úì Overpowered Lamp Synergy

"+1 to proc on-hit effects" effectively doubles proc chances. With Bandit's attack speed, this creates exponential scaling for Lightning Orb, Power Gloves, and Bloodmark builds.

‚ö†Ô∏è Heavy Item Dependency

Bandit requires specific items to reach S-tier. Without Kevin, Mirror, Battery, and Joe's Dagger, performance drops significantly. Know when to restart if RNG is bad.

‚ö†Ô∏è Joe's Dagger Cap (1.0.12)

Patch 1.0.12 added a max damage increase cap of 200 per minute to Joe's Dagger. Still extremely powerful, but no longer infinite scaling.

‚úó Kevin Nerf Awareness

Version 1.0.7 removed Kevin's infinite invincibility exploit. While still strong, you're not truly immortal. Layer additional defense (Lifesteal, Shield, Aegis).

üîì How to Unlock Bandit

Bandit requires finding and defeating the Bandit miniboss in the Desert map. This is a moderately challenging unlock requiring map exploration.

1

Reach the Desert Map

Progress through the game until you unlock the Desert area. This typically requires completing Forest stages first.

2

Find Bandit's Statue

Explore the Desert map looking for Bandit's statue. Interact with it to spawn the Bandit miniboss fight.

3

Defeat the Bandit Miniboss

The Bandit miniboss has high damage and mobility. Use ranged weapons and kiting strategies. Bring defensive items for survivability.

4

Character Unlocked

After defeating Bandit, the character is immediately unlocked and available to play with the Dexecutioner starting weapon.

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚úó Taking Kevin Without Mirror

Kevin without synergy is dangerous. Self-damage with no benefit can be lethal. Always have Mirror + attack speed before using Kevin.

‚úó Ignoring Battery

Battery (+8% attack speed) is essential for Kevin+Mirror combo. Without it, Kevin proc rate is too slow for effective invulnerability overlap.

‚úó Over-Relying on Dexecutioner

Primary weapon execute chance is unreliable without heavy Luck investment. Use secondary weapons (Katana, Lightning Staff) for consistent damage.

‚úó Expecting Infinite Invincibility

Post-1.0.7, Kevin+Mirror provides great defense, not immortality. Gaps between i-frames exist. Stack Lifesteal, Shield Tome, and Aegis.

‚úó Not Stacking Cooldown Tome

Cooldown Tome reduces Kevin trigger delay. Critical for maximizing invulnerability frequency. Priority tome for Kevin+Mirror builds.

‚úó Forcing Bad Builds

If you don't get Kevin, Mirror, or Battery by minute 15, don't force the build. Pivot to execute/on-hit build or restart.

üîó Best Item Synergies

Kevin + Mirror + Battery

The core defensive trinity. Kevin self-damages, Mirror grants i-frames, Battery increases Kevin proc rate. Bandit's passive makes this better than any other character.

Joe's Dagger + High Attack Speed

Execute chance becomes frequent executes with 200-300% attack speed. Damage scaling compounds throughout run (now capped at 200/min in 1.0.12).

Overpowered Lamp + On-Hit Effects

"+1 to proc on-hit effects" effectively doubles proc chances. With Bandit's attack speed, Lightning Orb/Power Gloves/Bloodmark procs become overwhelming.

Demonic Blade + Attack Speed

+350 lifesteal with 25% crit heal chance. With fast weapons, crits heal constantly. Essential sustain for aggressive play.

Kevin + Golden Shield

Alternative Kevin synergy. Self-damage generates gold when damaged with high attack speed. Use for economic advantage runs.

Bloodmark + Dexecutioner

Unstable Transfusion's Bloodmark (27% chance) creates execute threshold. Combined with Dexecutioner's execute, creates double execute synergy.
