The Regen Tome improves your natural health regeneration in Megabonk. Each level increases the amount of HP you recover per minute, making it easier to survive long runs and recover from mistakes. Unlike burst healing, regeneration provides consistent, reliable sustain throughout your entire run.

Overview

  • Stat: HP Regen
  • Base upgrade per level: +40 HP per minute
  • How to unlock: Default (available at start)
  • Max level: 99

Regeneration is the foundation of sustain-focused builds. While it won't save you from burst damage, it allows you to recover between encounters and maintain your health pool during prolonged fights. Essential for characters who lack innate healing.

Effects

Strategy Guide

  • Works best if you can avoid constant damage, letting the regen kick in
  • Strong defensive pick for learning players who struggle with mistakes
  • Not as explosive as lifesteal but more reliable over time

Best Use Cases

  • Long endurance runs - Continuous healing over extended periods
  • Learning the game - Forgiveness for mistakes and chip damage
  • Tank builds - Foundation of all sustain-heavy strategies
  • Kiting playstyles - Heal while maintaining distance from enemies
  • Characters without innate healing - Fill the sustain gap
  • Runs with frequent weapon swaps - Passive healing during transitions

Pro Tips

  • Consistent healing source that helps in all builds
  • Scales nicely in long stages where downtime allows for full recovery
  • More effective when combined with damage mitigation (armor, shields, evasion)
  • At max level (99), provides 3,960 HP per minute (~66 HP/second)
  • Works even at 1 HP - can save you from death if you survive long enough
  • Stack with percentage-based healing items for exponential recovery
