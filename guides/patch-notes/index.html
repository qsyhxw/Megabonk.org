About Megabonk Updates

Megabonk receives regular updates from solo developer vedinad, addressing community feedback, fixing bugs, and introducing new features. Since its launch on September 18, 2025, the game has seen consistent improvements to gameplay balance, performance optimization, and quality of life features.

Update Schedule

Patches are typically released weekly or bi-weekly, with hotfixes deployed as needed for critical issues. Major updates are announced on the official Steam page and the developer's social media channels.
