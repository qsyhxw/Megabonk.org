Status Effects & Enemy Debuffs

Mastering status effects is crucial for pushing high loops in Megabonk. Learn how the 5 core debuffs work, how they scale with your stats, and the hidden mechanics of Crowd Control (CC) Immunity.

⚡ GAME MECHANICS • DEBUFFS

🔥 Damage Over Time (DoT)

These status effects actively drain enemy health. They benefit greatly from Damage Brackets and the Duration stat.

☠️
Poison
Stacking DoT
Base Duration 5 Seconds
Damage Rate Every 0.5s (2 Ticks/sec)
Poison is a stacking DoT that can spread among groups. Total damage scales with your overall damage multiplier. Increasing Duration adds more damage ticks.
Applied By: (40% Chance)
🔥
Burn
DoT
Base Duration ~3 Seconds
Damage Rate Every 1.0s (1 Tick/sec)
Burn deals damage once per second. The number of ticks scales purely with your Duration multiplier. Highly effective against single targets with high HP.
Applied By: (15% Chance)

❄️ Crowd Control (CC) Effects

These effects restrict enemy movement. They are subject to the hidden CC Immunity mechanic to prevent infinite stun-locking.

🧊
Freeze
Slow / Immobilize
Base Duration ~3 Seconds
Effect -70% Movement Speed
Enemies turn teal/blue colored.
Applied By: (20% Chance)
⚡
Stun
Stop Movement
Base Duration ~3 Seconds
Effect 0 Movement Speed
Enemies gain a golden/yellow hue.
Applied By: (25% Chance)
⚠️ Boss Stun Mechanics

While bosses CAN be stunned (turning yellow and stopping movement), Stun does not silence them! Bosses can still shoot projectiles and use attacks while stunned. It only roots them in place.

🛡️ The Hidden "CC Immunity" Mechanic

Enemies build resistance to crowd control. After roughly 30 procs, an enemy becomes immune to further Stuns and Freezes. Furthermore, after 20 minutes into a run, this threshold is heavily decreased, making it incredibly difficult to CC late-game enemies. Use Duration to get the most out of every single stun proc before immunity hits!

🩸 Special Debuff: Bloodmark

Bloodmark (also known as Bleed) is a unique execution mechanic separate from standard DoTs or CC.

✖️
Bloodmark
Delayed Execute

Marks a portion of an enemy's health bar dark red. If the enemy's total health drops to this marked threshold, they are instantly executed — this works on Bosses!

Shows as a dark-red section on the HP bar.

If they don't reach the threshold, the mark explodes for damage after 5 seconds (or 4 seconds on some triggers).

Applied By: (Triggered via Lifesteal hitting enemies)

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Does Proc Coefficient affect Status Effects?

Yes! If you trigger a status-inducing item (like Moldy Cheese) via a weapon with a low Proc Coefficient, the chance to apply the status effect is reduced accordingly.

Why do enemies stop getting stunned late game?

Because of the CC Immunity mechanic. After a 20-minute run timer, the threshold of procs required for an enemy to become immune to Stun/Freeze drops significantly.

Does Duration increase Poison damage?

Yes. Poison triggers a damage tick every 0.5 seconds. By increasing the duration of the Poison stack, you increase the total number of ticks the enemy suffers before it wears off.

Can multiple status effects be on an enemy at once?

Absolutely. You can Poison, Burn, and Stun an enemy simultaneously. Stacking multiple diverse debuffs is highly recommended.
