üìù Developer's Note

Yo, thanks for the great launch so far. I'm a bit tired but I've tried to fix and implement some of the most common feedback I've seen so far.

This patch focuses on making the Final Boss more accessible after community feedback about its difficulty spike, while also addressing character balance, bug fixes, and quality of life improvements requested by players.

‚öîÔ∏è Final Boss Fight Changes

The Final Boss (Bark Vader) fight has been significantly rebalanced to create a more engaging and less punishing experience for all players:

üéØ Weapon Return System: For every phase, the boss gives back one of your weapons. You only need to do the first phase with your starter weapon now.

  • BUFF Weapon Return: Boss returns one weapon per phase, drastically reducing difficulty
  • BUFF Pylon Zones: Pylon zones are bigger and heal the boss less
  • BUFF Orb Damage: Orbs deal less damage overall
  • BUFF Blue Orbs: Freeze duration reduced from 3 seconds to 1 second
  • BUFF Orb Cooldown: Orbs have slightly longer cooldown, reducing projectile spam
  • BUFF Weapon Range: Increased weapon range during the fight

These changes address the most common complaint about the final encounter being too punishing, especially the weapon-stripping mechanic that invalidated most builds. For detailed strategies on defeating the boss with these new changes, visit our complete Bark Vader guide.

üë• Character Balancing Updates

Universal Changes

All 20 playable characters received significant quality-of-life improvements:

  • BUFF Movement Speed: All characters are about 10% faster with improved acceleration (see stats guide)
  • BUFF Slower Characters: Previously slow characters (like Ogre, Sir Oofie) received much larger speed buffs
  • BUFF Slot Unlocks: Weapon and Tome slots unlock earlier:
    • 3rd slots: Level 25 (Weapon) and Level 35 (Tome) (was higher)
    • 4th slots: Level 45 (Weapon) and Level 55 (Tome) (was higher)

üêõ Bug Fixes & Quality of Life

This update addresses 15+ critical bugs and quality-of-life issues reported by the community since launch on September 18, 2025.

Critical Bug Fixes

  • FIX Pink Textures: Fixed graphical issue causing pink textures for some players
    • Affected players with certain GPU configurations - should now render correctly
  • FIX Invincibility Exploit: Fixed bug where dying while teleporting made you invincible
    • Major exploit that was being used in leaderboard runs - now patched
  • FIX Out of Bounds: Fixed issue where falling out of bounds didn't teleport you back to map
    • Players could get stuck in void areas - now properly respawns on map

Audio Improvements

  • FIX Music Spam: Theme songs now only play 25% of the time (can be selected with boombox)
    • Addresses feedback about repetitive music - manual selection still available
  • FIX Music Persistence: Music doesn't randomly stop anymore
    • Improves audio experience consistency throughout runs

Gameplay Fixes

  • FIX Enemy Spawns: Enemies don't spawn on top of trees or castles anymore
    • Prevents enemies from becoming unreachable on terrain
  • FIX Camera Clipping: Camera shouldn't clip into walls anymore
    • Developer note: "hopefully idk i tried to fix it" - may still have edge cases
  • FIX Tornado Physics: Tornadoes shouldn't abduct you anymore
    • Players were being launched off-map by Tornado weapon effects
  • FIX Boss Knockback: You can't knock bosses out of the map anymore
    • Prevents cheese strategies that allowed instant boss kills

UI/UX Improvements

  • FIX UI Bugs: Fixed various UI issues and display glitches
  • FIX Crit Damage Display: Crit damage multiplier now displays properly in stats screen
  • BUFF Quest Claiming: Claiming quests is now faster
    • Reduces downtime between runs - see achievements guide
  • BUFF Shop Access: Shop in Menu now unlocks earlier in progression

Bug Reports: Found a new bug? Report it on the Steam Community Hub or Twitter. The developer actively monitors community feedback.

‚ú® New Features & Settings

Gameplay Settings

  • NEW Projectile Targeting: Game setting to choose how many projectiles use random vs. targeting mode (first projectile always uses targeting)
    • Helpful for players who prefer manual aim control - adjust in game settings
  • NEW Zoom Out Setting: Video Settings option to zoom out more by default
    • Improves visibility in late-game swarms - especially useful on larger displays
  • NEW Upgrade Screen Input: Option to disable numbered keys selection in upgrade screen
    • Prevents accidental selections during intense gameplay

Navigation & UI

  • NEW Chest Prices HUD: Chest prices now visible in HUD (next to level at top right)
    • Helps with silver management decisions - no need to hover over chests
  • NEW Minimap Lock: Setting to lock the minimap instead of having it rotate
    • Preferred by players who find rotating minimaps disorienting

Environment

  • BUFF Sandstorms: Shorter duration and less obstructive
    • Visibility improvements address community feedback about environmental obstacles

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

When was V1.0.4 released?

Version 1.0.4 was released on September 25, 2025 by VedinadGames. Check the complete version history.

What's the biggest change in this patch?

The Final Boss (Bark Vader) rebalance is the most significant change. The boss now returns weapons each phase, making the fight significantly more accessible while still challenging.

Did all characters get buffed?

All characters received a 10% movement speed buff and earlier slot unlocks. Additionally, Athena, Ogre, Calcium, and Bush received specific buffs, while Dicehead was nerfed for late-game balance.

What is Quin's Mask?

Quin's Mask is a new Rare item added in V1.0.4. Visit the item database for complete details on effects and unlock conditions.

Are the leaderboards working?

Leaderboards are temporarily impacted by cheaters. The developer is working on a proper fix and plans to add all-time and weekly leaderboards in future updates. Check current standings at the leaderboard page.

Should I change my build after this patch?

Most builds remain viable. If you relied on the Dice crit bug or Cactus spam, you'll need adjustments. The easier Final Boss means more experimental builds can succeed. Check our build guides for post-patch strategies.
