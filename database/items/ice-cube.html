Ice Cube

⭐ LEGENDARY ITEM
Effect: 20% chance to inflict Ice on hit, which can freeze enemies.
Rarity
Legendary

20%

Progression
Frostwalker unlock uses Ice Cube (1,000 freezes)

Ice Cube is a late-game defining CC tool: freezing clusters buys time, protects you from rushdowns, and sets up safe bursts. If you’re browsing items, see the full Items Database for stats and unlocks.

🧊 How Ice Cube Works

Mechanics

  • On every hit, there is a 20% chance to apply Ice.
  • Ice can freeze enemies, briefly stopping movement and actions.
  • Item is Legendary and available by default in the item pool.
Tip

Freeze uptime scales with how many times you hit — multi-hit or rapid-hit weapons can keep packs locked down.

✓ Advantages

  • Reliable crowd control against fast mobs
  • Pairs well with stun/slow chains for near-perma control
  • Enables safe chest opening and objective play in late waves

✗ Disadvantages

  • RNG-based; streaks without freezes can happen
  • Legendary rarity: chance to appear is lower

🔗 Progression & Synergies
