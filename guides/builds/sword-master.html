Sir Oofie - The Noble Knight

Unlock: Default Character (Available from start)
Starting Weapon: Sword
Base Stats: 50 HP | 0.25 Armor | 0.988 Move Speed

üõ°Ô∏è Unique Passive: Iron Will

Gain +1% Armor per character level. This stacks infinitely as you level up during runs, making you progressively tankier throughout the game.

Important: Armor in Megabonk uses diminishing returns. The formula is: Armor / (0.75 + Armor). This means 100% armor gives ~57% damage reduction, 200% armor gives ~73% reduction, and 400% armor gives ~84% reduction. You can reach 80-90% effective damage reduction with proper itemization!

üéÆ Gameplay Strategy

Phase 1: Early Game (Levels 1-30)

Building Your Foundation

Priority Goals:

  • Get XP Tome ASAP - level faster to gain more armor
  • Your starting 0.25 armor is low - avoid unnecessary damage
  • Focus on Leeching Crystal or Oats for HP boost
  • Let your Sword auto-attack while you focus on positioning
  • Stay near enemies to maximize sword hits
üí° Even 10-30% armor provides noticeable damage reduction early on
üí° Break pots to unlock XP Tome (need 20 pots broken)
Phase 2: Mid Game (Levels 30-100)

Tank Transformation

Key Actions:

  • You should have 30-100% armor (24-57% damage reduction)
  • Add Aegis Shield as second weapon for blocks and counterattacks
  • Stack Health Tome + HP items for larger HP pool
  • Add Cursed Tome to speed up leveling
  • You can now face-tank most enemy groups comfortably
üí° 50-100% armor = 40-57% reduction - significantly tankier
üí° Stand in the middle of hordes - let your weapons auto-attack
Phase 3: Late Game (Levels 100+)

Immortal Knight

Optimization:

  • 100-200+ armor from passive (57-73%+ damage reduction)
  • Add Armor Tome to push toward 80-90% reduction cap
  • Add Spiky Shield + Thorns Tome for reflect damage
  • Mirror provides additional safety and damage reflection
  • You're now nearly unkillable - walk through everything
üí° 200-400% armor = 73-84% reduction - true tank mode
üí° Focus on upgrading Sword size and projectiles for better clear
üí° You can idle in boss fights - let thorns and counterattacks win

üí° Advanced Tips

üéØ
Positioning

As a melee tank with auto-attacking weapons, position yourself in the center of enemy groups. Your armor lets you survive while your Sword and Aegis clear everything automatically.

üìä
Armor Math

Remember: Armor / (0.75 + Armor) = actual reduction. You need ~300-400% armor to hit 80-84% reduction. Stack Armor Tome, Spiky Shield, and Sir Oofie's passive to reach this.

‚ö°
Upgrade Priority

Early: XP Tome > HP items > Defensive stats. Mid: Cursed Tome > Armor stacking. Late: Max out Sword size/projectiles, then optimize thorns build.
