üì≤ How to Safely Play Megabonk on Your Phone

Steam Link is an official, free app from Valve that streams PC games from your computer to your mobile device over your home network. It's completely legitimate and supported by Valve.

For local streaming (recommended), ensure both your PC running Megabonk and your mobile device are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. For best performance, use 5GHz Wi-Fi if available.

4

Open Steam Link on your phone. The app will automatically scan for computers running Steam on your network. Follow the on-screen instructions to pair your devices using the provided code.

Steam Link works great with Bluetooth controllers (Xbox, PlayStation, or other compatible gamepads), and on mobile it also offers an on-screen touch controller overlay. For a fast action game like Megabonk, a physical controller still feels much more precise than virtual buttons.

6

Launch Megabonk

Once connected, navigate to your Steam library through the Steam Link app and launch Megabonk. The game will stream from your PC to your phone!

7

If you experience lag or stuttering, open Steam Link settings and lower the streaming quality/resolution. You can also adjust the bandwidth limit to match your network capabilities.
