🎯 Build Overview

A poison sustain shell that snowballs off chain DoTs and detonations. Poison Flask spreads stacks; Black Hole groups packs so more enemies share the same poison cloud; Aura protects you while you weave; finish with Lightning Staff (clear) or Revolver (single‑target). Tome order: Duration ▶ Damage ▶ Quantity ▶ Cooldown, with optional Lifesteal/Regeneration for comfort. Works especially well on poison‑leaning kits (e.g., Amog) but remains character‑agnostic.

Playstyle

Kiting sustain • grouped DoT chains • on‑contact detonations

Difficulty

Medium — early survival matters; late game snowballs

Power Spike

Mid‑game once Duration/Damage + Black Hole are online

Best For

Endless waves, dense maps, sustain learners

⚠️ Common Mistakes

Over‑grouping too early

Black Hole shines after Damage/Duration — grouping healthy mobs is risky.

Skipping sustain

Without Lifesteal/Regeneration or Leeching Crystal, mid‑game can feel brittle.

Too many weapons

3–4 total to keep upgrade density high.

Ignoring pickup radius

No Sucky Magnet = stalled levels, fewer tomes, slower snowball.

🧠 Why This Works

Poison Flask scales superbly with Duration and Damage, and grouping via Black Hole multiplies poison spread and explosion overlap. Aura adds hands‑free safety so you can kite, while a finisher (Lightning Staff or Revolver) tightens the loop. Popular meta guides similarly recommend Flask + grouping + sustain tomes/items, and community discussion continues to track its tuning across patches.
