B-TIER WEAPON

Burst-fire sidearm with strong crit & bounce scaling — exceptional with CL4NK.

🎯 Weapon Overview (Real Data)

Revolver fires a burst of bullets and scales excellently with Crit Chance/Damage, Projectile Count, and Projectile Bounces. It is especially potent on CL4NK, whose passive “Crit Happens” grants +1% Crit Chance per level and who starts with Revolver by default.

📌 Verified Base Stats (Level 1)

  • • Damage: 5 (upgrade +2.5)
  • • Crit Chance: 5% (upgrade +10%)
  • • Crit Damage: 0% (upgrade +20%)
  • • Projectile Count: 6 (upgrade +1)
  • • Projectile Bounces: 0 (upgrade +1)
  • • Projectile Speed: 2.0 (upgrade +0.4)

Note: Revolver is not the only weapon with crit upgrades (e.g., Bow/Katana also roll crit stats).

🎮 How It Plays

Early: Modest single-target DPS until you add Quantity/Crit upgrades. Play safe and build survivability if needed.

Mid: With Crit Chance from Precision Tome and a few Crit Damage rolls, Revolver starts melting elites.

Late: High projectile count + bounces + crit scaling = excellent boss damage and strong lane-clear.

📚 Tomes to Prioritize (Factual)

Precision → Damage → Quantity → Cooldown
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
  • Precision Tome: Raises Crit Chance and unlocks Overcrit beyond 100% Crit.
  • Damage Tome: Multiplies weapon damage; pairs perfectly with Crit Damage rolls.
  • Quantity Tome: More projectiles per attack — major DPS & farming boost.
  • Cooldown Tome: Reduces cooldowns → higher effective attack speed.

Agility, HP/Evasion can be added for comfort depending on tier/map.

CL4NK “Crit Happens” Core
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Leverage CL4NK’s +1% Crit Chance/level to focus upgrades on Crit Damage, Quantity, and Bounces.

  • Rush Precision ranks early (hit consistent crits ASAP).
  • Then stack Crit Damage on weapon level-ups.
  • Add Quantity and Bounces to spread and multi-hit.

Easy to pilot once online; strongest overall pairing for Revolver.

“Bullet Hose” Variant
⭐⭐⭐⭐

Leans on projectile volume and fire-rate. Less crit-dependent; great for waves.

  • Quantity + Cooldown as primaries.
  • Mix in Damage and a bit of Projectile Speed for comfort.
  • Pair with a crowd tool (e.g., Lightning Staff) while upgrading Revolver.

✅ Strengths & Weaknesses

✓ Strengths

  • High projectile baseline (6) → early Quantity upgrades are huge.
  • Access to Crit Chance & Crit Damage on weapon level-ups.
  • Bounces provide multi-hit & wave control.
  • Best-in-class synergy with CL4NK (free Crit Chance per level).
  • Good boss damage once Crit Damage is stacked.

✗ Weaknesses

  • Early game can feel slow before crits/quantity come online.
  • Line-aiming and positioning matter; less forgiving than true AoE.
  • Relies on upgrade RNG for crit/bounce rolls to hit peak performance.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

  • ❌ Ignoring Quantity—it’s one of your best DPS scalers early.
  • ❌ Overinvesting in raw Damage while neglecting Crit Damage rolls on the weapon.
  • ❌ Forgetting Cooldown (effective attack speed) — especially important for burst weapons.

📊 Comparisons

Revolver vs. Sniper Rifle

Sniper Rifle: Highest base damage (22), excels at single-target burst.

Revolver: Lower base damage (5) but more bullets & bounce scaling → better sustained DPS once built.

Revolver vs. Bow

Bow: Pierces and also rolls crit stats; easier laneing.

Revolver: Gains power through bounces + quantity; higher burst potential with CL4NK.

🎓 Final Verdict

Should you use the Revolver?

Yes — if you enjoy precision projectile builds and want a weapon that scales hard with Crit + Quantity, especially on CL4NK.

While not as universally dominant as Aura/Dice, its ceiling is high with the right tomes and items.
