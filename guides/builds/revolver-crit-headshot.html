🎯 Build Overview

Revolver is your high‑crit, high‑tempo core. Stack Precision first, then Cooldown (rate of fire) and Quantity to flood the screen with lethal rounds. Add Bow (straight‑line lanes) or Bone (ricochet coverage), keep Lightning Staff to stun/clean edges, and run Aura or Aegis for safety. Optional Projectile Speed improves feel and reach.

Playstyle

Crit strings • rapid fire • lane carving with control support

Difficulty

Low–Medium — positioning and lane discipline matter

Power Spike

Early–Mid once Precision + CDR land

Best For

Crit‑synergy kits (e.g., CL4NK), speed‑clears, elite deletion

⚠️ Common Mistakes

Delaying Precision

Crit scaling is the build’s engine — take it first.

Too many weapons

3–4 total keeps upgrade density high.

Skipping control

No Lightning Staff often means missed lines and chip deaths.

Standing still

You want straight lanes; lateral kiting dramatically ups hit count.

🧠 Why This Works

Revolver has clean synergy with crit‑centric passives (e.g., CL4NK). Its damage scales multiplicatively with Precision and additively with Cooldown and Quantity for more crit rolls per second. Bow/Bone solve angle coverage, Lightning Staff maintains crowd control so shots land, and Aura/Aegis provide safety to keep firing.
