🎯 Weapon Overview

The Revolver is Megabonk's premiere precision weapon and CL4NK's signature firearm. Unlike most weapons in the game, the Revolver has a unique ability to naturally scale with both Crit Chance and Crit Damage through level-up upgrades, making it one of the few weapons that can achieve absurd crit-focused builds.

This weapon fires multiple bullets in quick succession with high base damage, but requires proper positioning and understanding of its mechanics to reach its full potential. While challenging for beginners due to its precision requirements, experienced players can turn the Revolver into an absolute monster that deletes bosses and clears waves with critical hit explosions.

🌟 What Makes Revolver Special?

Unique Crit Scaling: The Revolver is one of the only weapons in Megabonk that can roll Crit Chance and Crit Damage as natural upgrade options. Most weapons cannot access these stats through level-ups, making the Revolver exceptionally powerful in crit-focused builds.

CL4NK Synergy: Perfectly designed for CL4NK's "Crit Happens" passive (+1% Crit Chance per level), creating exponential damage scaling that few other character-weapon combinations can match.

🎮 How It Actually Plays

Early Game (Levels 1-10): Feels slow and underwhelming. Low fire rate and few projectiles make it challenging. Focus on survival and getting Attack Speed upgrades.

Mid Game (Levels 10-20): Starts to shine as you stack Crit Damage and Attack Speed. Crits begin to feel satisfying, clearing groups with critical bursts.

Late Game (Level 20+): ABSOLUTE MONSTER. With maxed Crit Chance (especially as CL4NK), high Crit Damage, and rapid fire rate, the Revolver becomes a screen-clearing, boss-melting machine. Each burst is a cascade of critical hits.

📊 Advanced Strategies

🎯 The "CL4NK Crit Formula"

Understanding why CL4NK + Revolver is so powerful:

Damage Formula:

Crit DPS = (Base Damage × Crit Multiplier × Crit Chance × Attack Speed × Projectile Count)

Why This Scales Exponentially:

  • CL4NK Passive: Guarantees 50%+ Crit Chance by level 50 (no items needed)
  • Revolver Upgrades: Can roll Crit Damage naturally (unlike most weapons)
  • Multiplicative Scaling: Crit Chance × Crit Damage = exponential growth
  • Attack Speed: More shots = more crits per second

Example: At level 50 with 200% Crit Damage and 6 projectiles per burst, CL4NK is effectively dealing 600-800% normal damage constantly. This is why he dominates leaderboards.

🎮 Playstyle Tips by Stage

Early Game (Levels 1-10) - Survival Focus:

  • Play extremely defensively - CL4NK is fragile
  • Prioritize Attack Speed from shrines to smooth out fire rate
  • Take ANY Crit Damage upgrade immediately
  • Use kiting and positioning - stay at range
  • Consider taking a defensive item (Shield, HP) for safety

Mid Game (Levels 10-20) - Scaling Phase:

  • Start taking Damage % from shrines aggressively
  • Continue stacking Crit Damage every chance you get
  • Add Projectile Count for more bullets per burst
  • Look for Overpowered Lamp item (game-changing)
  • Begin playing more aggressively as crits become reliable

Late Game (Level 20+) - Domination:

  • You're now a boss-melting machine - play confidently
  • Circle bosses while maintaining distance, let crits do the work
  • Final swarm: Position carefully, critical bursts will clear paths
  • With 50%+ crit chance, every other burst is full crits
  • Focus fire on priority targets - your single-target DPS is unmatched

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the Revolver only good with CL4NK?

A: No, but he's BY FAR the best character for it. His passive (+1% Crit Chance per level) is specifically designed to synergize with the Revolver's crit scaling. Other crit-focused characters like Bandit can also use it well, but CL4NK is the definitive choice.

Q: Why is Revolver only B-tier if it's so powerful?

A: The B-tier rating reflects its challenging early game and character dependency. While CL4NK + Revolver builds are S-tier, the weapon alone is B-tier because it requires specific builds and isn't universally strong across all characters like Aura or Dice.

Q: Should I take Crit Chance upgrades as CL4NK?

A: NO! CL4NK's passive already provides Crit Chance. Focus exclusively on Crit DAMAGE, Attack Speed, and base Damage. Taking Crit Chance upgrades is wasted - his passive will naturally max it out.

Q: How many projectiles should I aim for?

A: 6-10 projectiles is the sweet spot. More bullets = more chances to crit = more consistent DPS. Beyond 10, you hit diminishing returns and should focus on other stats.

Q: What's the best secondary weapon for Revolver builds?

A: Depends on your goal. Dice for permanent crit stacking (meta), Sniper Rifle for more boss damage, Bow for additional crit scaling, or Lightning Staff for mob clear while you focus bosses.

Q: Is Revolver good for beginners?

A: Not really. The slow early game, CL4NK's fragility, and need for specific builds make it challenging. New players should start with easier weapons like Sword, Firestaff, or Aura. Come back to Revolver once you understand game mechanics better.
