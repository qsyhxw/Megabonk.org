👥 Best Weapons for Each Character

While you can equip any weapon to any class, matching a character's passive trait with complimentary weapon mechanics is required to conquer Tier 3 mappings. For entire setups check out our Synergies & Combos Guide.

🛡️ Sir Oofie (The Knight)
  • Aegis (S): Scales with his massive starting Armor.
  • Aura (S): Covers his low movement speed weakness.
  • Sword (B): His default; reliable front-cleave.

Avoid Dice. Focus purely on survival.

🎲 Dicehead
  • Dice (S): Mandatory. Fuels his infinite RNG kit.
  • Revolver (S): Rapid hits proc his critical chance.
  • Sniper Rifle (A): Converts high Crit % to massive damage.
🥷 Bandit
  • Dexecutioner (S): Bandit scales Attack Speed, which scales execution procs.
  • Katana (S): Best high-attack speed melee.
  • Revolver (S): Insane fire rate on Bandit.
🦊 Zorro / Fox
  • Flamewalker (S): The best kiting tool for high move speed.
  • Lightning Staff (A): Fox scales luck natively for better crits.
  • Lightning Bolt / Aura: Covers the trail blindspots.
