Overview

Megabonk Items Database entry for Turbo Socks — a straightforward, always-on movement boost. The +15% speed helps with shrine routing, chest runs, and survivability during dense waves, especially on slower champions.

Patch-current facts

All numbers and the unlock requirement below are verified from multiple 3rd-party sources as of Oct 30, 2025.

How to Unlock

  1. Upgrade Agility Tome to Level 5 using Agility Fragments at the Anvil.
  2. After reaching Lv.5, Turbo Socks permanently enter your item pool and can appear in future runs.

Simple prerequisite, big quality-of-life improvement for any mobility-leaning build.

Strategy & Synergies

Who benefits most?

  • Slower or tanky champions that need help dodging and repositioning.
  • Builds that route shrines/pots aggressively or kite large swarms.
  • Speed-conversion builds (e.g., pairing with effects that scale off movement speed).

Cowards Cloak

Higher mobility makes hit-and-run repositioning smoother, reducing risk during low-HP moments.

Thunder Mitts

Speed helps maintain safe spacing while proccing on-hit tools from nearby enemy contact.

Spicy Meatball

Mobility improves chase range and grouping, indirectly increasing on-hit uptime.
