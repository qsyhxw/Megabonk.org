üîì How to Unlock Noelle

Unlock Requirement: Use Microwave to Duplicate Ice Crystal 3 Times in a Row

This is one of the most technical unlock requirements in the game. You must find both Microwave and Ice Crystal, then successfully duplicate Ice Crystal three consecutive times.

Step 1: Find Microwave

Microwave is a special item that allows you to duplicate other items. It's found randomly in shops or as a drop. Priority tips:

  • Check every shop you encounter
  • Microwave is rare - may take multiple runs to find
  • Use characters with shop advantages (Robinette)
  • Reroll shops if you have spare gold
  • Some biomes have higher shop spawn rates

Step 2: Find Ice Crystal

Ice Crystal is an ice-themed item that needs to be duplicated. Finding it:

  • Ice Crystal appears in item pools randomly
  • More common in certain biomes (cold-themed areas)
  • Can appear in shops or as drops
  • Once you have Microwave, actively search for Ice Crystal
  • Don't duplicate other items yet - save for Ice Crystal

Step 3: Execute the Duplication

This is the technical part. You must duplicate Ice Crystal 3 times IN A ROW without duplicating anything else:

  • Duplication 1: Use Microwave on Ice Crystal (now have 2x Ice Crystal)
  • Duplication 2: Use Microwave on Ice Crystal again (now have 3x Ice Crystal)
  • Duplication 3: Use Microwave on Ice Crystal one more time (now have 4x Ice Crystal)
  • Important: Don't use Microwave on ANY other item between these duplications
  • If you accidentally duplicate something else, the counter resets
‚ö†Ô∏è Critical: The "3 times in a row" means three consecutive Microwave uses on Ice Crystal with NO other duplications in between. Plan carefully!

Step 4: Confirmation

After successfully duplicating Ice Crystal three consecutive times, you'll receive a notification that Noelle is unlocked. She'll be permanently available for all future runs!

üí° Unlock Strategy:
  • Use Robinette for better shop access to find Microwave
  • Once you find Microwave, DON'T use it until you find Ice Crystal
  • When you have both, focus entirely on the duplication sequence
  • Mark your progress mentally: "1st done... 2nd done... 3rd done!"
  • This unlock may take 3-5 attempts as items are RNG-dependent

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is Noelle good for beginners?

No. Noelle has an extremely difficult unlock requirement (finding two specific items and executing a precise sequence). Additionally, her playstyle requires understanding freeze mechanics, positioning, and timing. Recommended for experienced players only. Start with Fox or MegaChad instead.

How long does it take to unlock Noelle?

Variable, but typically 3-5 hours of gameplay. The challenge is finding both Microwave AND Ice Crystal in the same run, then executing the duplication sequence. RNG can make this faster or slower. Be patient!

What if I accidentally duplicate the wrong item?

The counter resets. You need 3 consecutive Ice Crystal duplications with no other items duplicated in between. If you mess up, you'll need to start the sequence over: find Ice Crystal again and do 3 consecutive duplications.

Can I use other items between duplications?

Yes, you can pick up and use other items normally. The restriction is only on Microwave usage - you can't use Microwave on any other item during the sequence. Regular item pickup and usage is fine.

Best way to find Microwave?

Play characters with shop advantages (Robinette), check every shop, reroll when possible, and play longer runs to encounter more shops. Microwave is rare but not extremely rare - patience is key.

How much Duration do I need?

Minimum 50%, optimal is 100%+. At 100% Duration, freeze effects last twice as long, which is game-changing. Below 50%, freezes are too short to be meaningful. Prioritize Duration heavily.

Does freeze work on bosses?

Partially. Bosses have freeze resistance, so they're slowed rather than fully frozen. However, you can still freeze boss minions, creating safer boss fights. Use freeze defensively against bosses rather than offensively.

Best weapon besides Frostwalker?

Frostwalker is strongly recommended because it synergizes with Noelle's passive. However, if you must use alternatives, choose weapons that benefit from enemies being stationary: Rocket Launcher, Grenade, Laser Beam. Avoid weapons that require enemy movement (chasing projectiles).

Can I unlock Noelle across multiple runs?

No. The 3 consecutive duplications must happen in a single run. The game doesn't track progress across runs. You need Microwave + Ice Crystal in the same run, then do 3 duplications before that run ends.

Late game strategy?

With maxed Duration and Cooldown, you achieve near-permanent freeze/slow on all enemies. Position strategically, freeze waves before they reach you, burst down frozen enemies with shatter bonus, repeat. You control the battlefield completely, making even difficult content trivial.
