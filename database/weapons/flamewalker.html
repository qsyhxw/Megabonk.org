üß† Advanced Strategies & Tips
Master-level techniques for Flamewalker mastery
üå™Ô∏è Circular Kiting
Move in large circles around enemy groups. This creates overlapping fire trails that deal massive damage while keeping you safe. The larger the circle, the more coverage you create.
‚ö° Attack Speed Priority
Unlike most weapons, Flamewalker massively benefits from attack speed. Each "attack" creates a new trail segment, so Cooldown and Quantity tomes are incredibly valuable.
üéØ Trail Management
Don't just run randomly - plan your movement. Lead enemies through existing trails, create chokepoints, and use terrain to maximize trail effectiveness.
üöÄ Speed Stacking
Stack as many speed bonuses as possible. Turbo Socks + Agility Tome + speed items can make you nearly untouchable while creating devastating trail coverage.
üîÑ Trail Overlap
Multiple Flamewalker trails can overlap for bonus damage. With Quantity tome, you can create multiple trails simultaneously for incredible area coverage.
‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes
Don't stand still! Even a moment of stillness means zero damage. Avoid damage-focused tomes early - prioritize speed and quantity first, damage second.

üéñÔ∏è Expert Techniques

üåÄ The Figure-8 Pattern

üèÉ Trail Herding
