Official Steam Rankings vs Community VOD Leaderboard

Updated for v1.0.12+
Megabonk has two separate leaderboard systems: the official Steam in-game leaderboard maintained by developers, and megabonk.fun ‚Äî a community-run VOD verified leaderboard. Each operates independently with different rules and requirements.

üìä Two Leaderboard Systems Explained

Understanding the key differences between official and community rankings.

üéÆ

Official Steam Leaderboard

Developer Maintained
The official leaderboard is built into the Steam version of Megabonk and maintained directly by the game developers (vedinad). Scores are automatically submitted from within the game.
  • Integrated into Steam game client
  • Automatic score submission
  • No video proof required
  • Global ban list for confirmed cheaters
  • Fixed with v1.0.12 patch (Oct 8, 2025)
üõ°Ô∏è Anti-Cheat Protection
As of v1.0.12, developers established a global ban list. Confirmed cheaters are permanently removed from leaderboards and will never appear again.
üìπ

Community VOD Leaderboard

Community Operated
Megabonk.fun is a community-run leaderboard that requires full video proof for all submissions. This system is not affiliated with the developers and operates independently.
  • All submissions require full unedited video
  • Must be performed on current patch
  • Typically reviewed within ~1 hour (EST working hours)
  • Displays detailed build information
  • Submission statuses: pending/approved/denied
‚ö†Ô∏è Independent Operation
Megabonk.fun is community-operated and not affiliated with vedinad (the game developer). It follows separate rules from the official Steam leaderboard.

üéÆ Official Steam Leaderboard

How the official in-game leaderboard works and what protection measures are in place.

‚úÖ v1.0.12 Update (October 8, 2025)
Leaderboards Fixed: The developers repaired the previously broken leaderboard system and established a global ban list. Known cheaters are permanently banned and will never appear on leaderboards again.

How It Works

  • Automatic submission: Scores are automatically uploaded to Steam when you complete a run
  • No video required: Players don't need to provide video proof for any ranking
  • Access location: View rankings directly in the Steam game client
  • Ban system: Developers maintain a global list of confirmed cheaters who are permanently excluded
  • Version tracking: Leaderboards may be reset with major patches (e.g., v1.0.7 reset on Sept 28, 2025)
üö´ Reporting Cheaters
If you suspect cheating on the Steam leaderboard, report it through the official Megabonk Discord: discord.com/invite/BGxud7HPEe

üìπ Community VOD Leaderboard (megabonk.fun)

Requirements and process for the community-operated video-verified leaderboard.

üìù Submission Requirements

  • Full unedited video: Complete run from start to finish, no cuts or edits
  • Current patch only: Run must be performed on the most recent game version
  • Visible UI: All game stats, build details, and final score screen must be clearly visible
  • Build information: Complete details of weapons, items, and tomes used
  • Video platform: Upload to YouTube, Twitch, or similar and submit link

‚è±Ô∏è Review Process

Submissions are typically reviewed within approximately 1 hour during EST working hours. Real-time updates are provided, and you can check your submission status on the website. Status options include:

  • Pending: Awaiting review
  • Approved: Verified and added to leaderboard
  • Denied: Does not meet requirements (reason provided)
‚ÑπÔ∏è Not Affiliated with Developer
Megabonk.fun clearly states at the bottom of their FAQ: "Not affiliated with vedinad." This is a community initiative operated independently from the game developers.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Which leaderboard should I focus on? ‚ñº
Both have value! The Steam official leaderboard is the "official" ranking maintained by developers and automatically tracks all players. Megabonk.fun is great if you want detailed build visibility and proof of legitimacy through video, as it requires full VOD verification and shows complete build details for every submission.
Can my score appear on both leaderboards? ‚ñº
Yes! Your score will automatically appear on the Steam leaderboard when you complete a run. If you also want it on megabonk.fun, you need to separately submit it through their website with video proof. They are completely independent systems.
Do I need video proof to be on the official Steam leaderboard? ‚ñº
No. The official Steam leaderboard does not require video proof for any ranking. Scores are automatically submitted from the game. However, developers maintain a global ban list for confirmed cheaters who are permanently excluded from all leaderboards.
How do I report a suspicious score? ‚ñº
For the Steam official leaderboard, report through the official Megabonk Discord at discord.com/invite/BGxud7HPEe. For megabonk.fun, use their website contact form. Each system handles reports independently.
Will leaderboards be reset with new patches? ‚ñº
Both leaderboards may reset with major version updates. The Steam leaderboard was reset with v1.0.7 on September 28, 2025. Megabonk.fun requires runs to be on the current patch and manages submissions by version. Check official announcements for reset information.
Can I appeal if my megabonk.fun submission is denied? ‚ñº
Yes. If your submission is denied, you'll receive a reason. You can resubmit with corrections or contact megabonk.fun through their website contact form to discuss the denial. Make sure your video meets all requirements: full unedited run, current patch, visible UI, and complete build details.
Are powerful builds or strategies considered cheating? ‚ñº
No. Any build, item combination, or strategy that's achievable within normal game mechanics is legitimate, even if it seems overpowered. Cheating only refers to modified game files, external tools, or exploiting known bugs intentionally. If you discover a bug, report it through proper channels.
