üß† What Shattered Knowledge Does

When enabled, the XP shards you generate become short-range projectiles that cut through enemies as they travel. This turns normal XP collection into a stream of incidental damage, especially noticeable during heavy waves.

Unlock Requirement

  1. During a run, level the Attraction Tome to Level 8.
  2. After meeting the condition, the item is added to your item pool.
