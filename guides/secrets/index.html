Monke

Medium Difficulty

Hidden character locked in a cage in the Forest map. Requires finding the rare Golden Key to release.

Unlock Steps:
  1. Play on Forest map
  2. Find the Golden Key (random ground spawn)
  3. Locate Monke's cage (no map marker, search thoroughly)
  4. Use Golden Key to open the cage

Passive Ability: Wall climbing + extra HP growth per level

Related Weapon: Bananarang (can be purchased with silver after unlocking Monke)

Sources: Destructoid, GameRant, Deltia's Gaming

Birdo

Extremely Challenging

One of the most difficult characters to unlock, requiring precise execution during tornado events.

Unlock Requirements:
  1. Play on Desert map
  2. Equip the Tornado weapon
  3. Get carried by a tornado
  4. Kill 100 enemies WHILE being carried by the tornado
Difficulty Warning: This is extremely challenging. Community estimates 5-10 hours of attempts. Requires both skill and favorable RNG for tornado spawns.
Sources: Destructoid, TheGamer
