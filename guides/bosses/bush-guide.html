üîç How to Find the Suspicious Bush

Bush is a unique hidden boss that doesn't appear naturally. You must find and interact with a special object to trigger the encounter.

1

Explore the Forest

Bush can appear in any Forest tier (1, 2, or 3). Thoroughly explore dense forest areas and hidden clearings while progressing through your run.

Search Tips: Check areas with thick tree coverage, corners of the map, and spots near boss arenas. The Sus Bush blends in with regular bushes but has a special indicator.
2

Look for "Sus Bush" Prompt

When you get close to the hidden boss location, you'll see an interaction prompt that says "Sus Bush" instead of the normal bush indicator.

Warning: Don't interact unless you're prepared! Once you shake the Sus Bush, the boss fight begins immediately with no way to back out.
3

Prepare Your Build

Before interacting, ensure you have:

  • Full or near-full health bar
  • Strong weapons upgraded to high levels
  • Good defensive items (HP, Armor, Lifesteal)
  • Movement speed for dodging
4

Shake the Bush!

Press the interact key (E) to shake the Sus Bush. Bush will emerge and the boss fight begins in the surrounding area. Get ready for explosive combat!

Bush's Attack Patterns

Sniper Shots HIGH THREAT

Pattern: Long-range precision shots that deal heavy damage. Bush positions himself at distance and fires accurately.

Counter: Constant lateral movement. Don't move in straight lines toward or away from Bush. Zigzag pattern works best.

Explosive Shots (Bullseye Passive) HIGH THREAT

Pattern: Bush's Bullseye passive marks random enemies with crosshairs. When these marked enemies are shot, they explode and can damage you and nearby enemies.

Counter: Keep medium distance - close enough to deal damage, far enough to avoid explosion radius. Watch for crosshair marks on enemies before explosions trigger.

Repositioning MEDIUM THREAT

Pattern: Bush moves to maintain optimal sniping distance and find better positioning throughout the fight.

Counter: Use this window to close distance or heal. Bush is vulnerable during repositioning.

Winning Strategy

üí°

Maintain Medium Range

Too close = explosive damage from Bullseye marks. Too far = hard to hit Bush. Find the sweet spot at medium range where you can attack while dodging sniper shots.

üí°

Prioritize Survival

With 12,500 HP, Bush takes time to defeat. Focus on not dying rather than rushing damage. Lifesteal and HP regeneration are very valuable.

üí°

Use Ranged Weapons

Ranged weapons (Firestaff, Katana, Revolver) are safer than melee. You can maintain the optimal distance while dealing consistent damage.

üí°

Watch for Crosshair Marks

Bush's Bullseye passive marks enemies with crosshairs. When you shoot these marked enemies, they explode. Be careful not to stand too close to marked enemies!
