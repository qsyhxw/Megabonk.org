⚙️ Dexectioner Mechanics
What’s confirmed in-game
🗡️ Piercing Line
Attacks travel in a straight line and can hit multiple enemies along the path.
Pierce: Multi-target line (no official cap disclosed)
Scaling: Benefits from Size/Quantity/Cooldown upgrades (per community builds)
💀 Execute Proc
Has a small chance to instantly execute an enemy regardless of remaining HP. Exact percent isn’t disclosed by official sources.
Notes: Execute frequency can feel higher with more hits/projectiles. Boss interactions vary by effect—plan normal DPS for bosses.
⚡ Damage Profile
High per-hit power and strong scaling with core tomes/items; excels when you can line up large packs.
Plays best with: Size, Quantity, (situational) Cooldown tomes; crit/luck synergies via items
🏴‍☠️ Bandit Synergy
Bandit is built around Dexectioner, but you still必须完成沙漠雕像战才能解锁该角色。
To play Bandit: Defeat Bandit statue (Desert) + own/purchase Dexectioner + buy Bandit with Silver
