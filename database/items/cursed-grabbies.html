Overview

Browse the full items database or read on for a verified, spoiler-lite breakdown of Cursed Grabbies. This item increases overall difficulty and slightly reduces your Max HP, but grants a small on-hit chance to apply a curse that damages nearby enemies. It’s unlocked by raising the Cursed Tome to Level 10.

When to Consider It

  • When your build benefits from frequent curse procs (crowd chip-damage while moving through packs).
  • If you can compensate the 0.8× Max HP hit via HP stacking or mitigation.

How to Unlock

Requirement: Get the Cursed Tome to Level 10 in your collection. This permanently adds Cursed Grabbies to your item pool.

Build Notes & Light Synergies

Cursed Doll

Cursed Grabbies adds extra curse uptime; Doll provides the stronger DoT ticks. Pair for reliable pack clear. (community meta)

Corrupted Sword

Curse/Corruption themed builds often stack incidental AoE; Grabbies adds passive curse procs while you swing. (community meta)

Obvious pairing—unlock requirement and damage identity align. Consider once you’re comfortable with the higher difficulty.
