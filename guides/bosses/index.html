‚ùì Megabonk Boss FAQ

Quick answers to the most common boss questions

How many bosses are in Megabonk?

+

There are currently 6 bosses in Megabonk: 3 Forest bosses (Lil Bark, Chadbark, Bark Vader), 1 Desert boss (Anubis), and 2 hidden bosses (Bush and Bandit). The hidden bosses unlock unique playable characters when defeated.

Are there hidden bosses in Megabonk?

+

Yes! Bush (Suspicious Bush) spawns in the Forest region and requires the Sniper weapon to unlock as a playable character. Bandit spawns in the Desert region and requires the Dexecutioner weapon to unlock. Both are extremely difficult encounters that should only be attempted with late-game builds.

What is the Boss Curse system in Megabonk?

+

Boss Curse shrines allow you to spawn additional bosses during the final fight. Each curse adds one more boss and slightly increases their HP pool, but rewards you with an extra loot chest. This system is required for certain achievements like "Speedboi" and offers high-risk, high-reward gameplay.

How do I unlock Bush and Bandit bosses?

+

Bush: Find the "Suspicious Bush" in the Forest region and attack it. You need the Sniper weapon unlocked to play as Bush after defeating it. Bandit: Triggered in the Desert region (requires CL4NK Forest Tier 2 completion first). You need the Dexecutioner weapon to unlock Bandit as a playable character.

What is the hardest boss in Megabonk?

+

Bark Vader is considered the hardest standard boss with 18 million HP and the unique mechanic of deleting all weapons except your starting weapon. Bush is the hardest hidden boss due to extremely high damage output requiring a fully optimized late-game build.

How do I unlock the Desert region?

+

Complete Forest Tier 2 (Chadbark) using the CL4NK character. This unlocks the Desert region and allows you to fight Anubis and discover the hidden Bandit boss.

What's the best strategy for Bark Vader?

+

Focus ALL upgrades on your starting weapon since Bark Vader deletes all other weapons. Prioritize movement speed to quickly destroy shield pylons during healing phases. Avoid investing in secondary weapons as they'll be removed when the boss spawns.

Can I fight multiple bosses at once?

+

Yes, through the Boss Curse system. Activating curse shrines spawns additional bosses during the final fight. Each curse adds one more boss, increases their collective HP, and grants an extra chest reward. This is essential for certain achievements and speedrun strategies.
