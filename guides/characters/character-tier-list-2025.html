🎯 Quick Selection Guide

Not sure which character to pick? Here's a quick guide based on your playstyle preferences:

🔰 New Players

🦊 Kitsune (Fox) - Fast, simple, effective. Perfect for learning the game mechanics without being overwhelmed.

⚔️ Knight - Tanky and forgiving. Great for players who want to focus on positioning rather than survival.

⚡ Speed Demons

🏃 Swift Runner - Ultimate mobility with dash abilities. For players who never want to get caught.

🦊 Kitsune - Fast movement with good damage. The perfect balance of speed and power.

💥 Damage Dealers

🥷 Shadow Ninja - Highest burst potential with stealth and crits. For players who want big numbers.

🔥 Flame Mage - Massive AOE explosions. Perfect for clearing screens of enemies.

🛡️ Safe Players

🏹 Elven Archer - Long range keeps you safe while dealing consistent damage.

🛡️ Divine Paladin - Self-healing and high health. Nearly impossible to kill.

🧠 Strategic Players

⛏️ Dwarven Engineer - Turrets and traps for tactical gameplay.

⚡ Storm Wizard - Chain lightning requires positioning and timing mastery.

🎲 Chaos Lovers

🎪 Court Jester - Random abilities every run. Never know what you'll get!

🧪 Alchemist - Complex crafting system with unlimited potential combinations.
