🧪 How Spicy Meatball Works

Every time you land a hit, there’s a 25% chance to trigger a small explosion that deals 65% damage in an area. It’s a straightforward crowd-control effect that scales with how many times you hit enemies per second.

Advantages

  • Excellent at clearing grouped enemies
  • No setup or targeting required
  • Works in most builds that hit frequently

Considerations

  • Single-target DPS gain can be modest
  • Relies on consistent hit frequency
Patch Insight

In v1.0.12, the developer clarified proc coefficient (how often weapon hits can trigger on-hit items like Spicy Meatball). Changes to weapon proc coefficients (e.g., Black Hole) indirectly affect your explosion uptime.
