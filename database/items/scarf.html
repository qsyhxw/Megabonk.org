🧪 How Scarf Works

Scarf grants a flat damage multiplier only while your character is airborne. This was buffed from +33% to +50% on Oct 19, 2025 (v1.0.17), making aerial windows a sizable DPS spike.

Patch context

The +50% value is current as of v1.0.17. Older guides that list +33% are pre-patch.

🔗 Strategies & Synergies

Scarf favors jump-heavy play and weapons that keep pressure while you reposition mid-air.

Feathers (mobility)

Higher, longer jumps = more airborne uptime to capitalize on Scarf’s multiplier.

Turbo Socks (movement)

Extra movement speed helps you enter/exit aerial windows safely.

Bananarang

High uptime while kiting; easy to keep damage rolling during jumps.

Aura

Passive AoE keeps ticking as you hop around to maintain airborne damage windows.

❓ FAQ

Is Scarf available from the start?
Yes. Multiple guides list Scarf as unlocked by default.
How much damage does Scarf add now?
+50% while airborne (since v1.0.17 on Oct 19, 2025).
What is Scarf’s rarity?
Community sources list it as Rare, but we do not yet have two independent confirmations.
