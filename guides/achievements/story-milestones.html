🎯 Early Game Strategy (0-10 minutes)

  • Choose Fox (Kitsune) for beginners - best survivability
  • Prioritize movement speed upgrades first
  • Focus on Lightning-based weapons for crowd control
  • Stay mobile and avoid getting cornered
  • Pick up every XP gem to accelerate progression
  • Learn basic enemy patterns and timing

⚔️ Mid Game Strategy (10-20 minutes)

  • Evolve your primary weapon to maximum level
  • Balance offensive and defensive upgrades
  • Master the Skeleton King boss mechanics
  • Start building weapon synergies
  • Maintain optimal positioning against Flame Demon
  • Utilize environmental advantages

🏆 Late Game Strategy (20+ minutes)

  • Perfect your build synergy combinations
  • Master all boss attack patterns
  • Optimize movement for maximum efficiency
  • Plan your upgrade path strategically
  • Maintain focus during intense sequences
  • Practice perfect execution for flawless runs

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Greedy XP Collection

Don't risk your life for XP gems during intense moments. Survival is more important than faster progression.

❌ Ignoring Boss Patterns

Each boss has telegraphed attacks. Learn the patterns instead of relying on luck or panic movement.

❌ Poor Upgrade Planning

Don't randomly select upgrades. Plan your build synergy from the beginning for optimal performance.

❌ Static Positioning

Stay mobile at all times. Static positioning leads to being overwhelmed by enemy swarms.

❌ Panic During Bosses

Maintain calm and execute learned strategies. Panic leads to poor positioning and unnecessary damage.

❌ Neglecting Defense

Balance offense with defense. Pure damage builds often fail at higher difficulty spikes.
