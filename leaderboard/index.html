How to Climb the Leaderboard

üéØ Master the Holy Trinity

XP + Luck + Cursed Tomes are the foundation of every top-tier run. This combination creates exponential growth that outscales difficulty.

  • XP Tome: Faster leveling = more upgrade choices
  • Luck Tome: Ensures legendary/epic upgrades every level
  • Cursed Tome: Harder game = dramatically better drops

‚ö° Prioritize Attack Speed

Attack speed is the most important scaling stat. It affects Kevin+Mirror combo, lifesteal healing, and all on-hit effects.

  • Enables Kevin+Mirror immortality
  • Multiplies lifesteal effectiveness
  • Triggers more on-hit item effects

üíé Optimize Item Farming

Don't open chests immediately. Wait until you've stacked Luck to 100+ for better quality drops from vendors and chests.

  • Visit shrines often with high Luck
  • Spend currency on rerolls liberally
  • Save chests for mid-game power spike

üìä Follow the Meta Timeline

Each phase of your run requires different priorities for optimal progression and maximum kill counts.

  • 0-5 min: Focus on XP and leveling speed
  • 5-15 min: Build your weapon loadout
  • 15+ min: Stack damage and survive Final Swarm

üõ°Ô∏è Don't Skip Defense

Take at least one shield upgrade. It dramatically increases effective HP and prevents one-shot deaths in late game.

  • Shields absorb burst damage
  • Allows riskier positioning
  • Critical for 100K+ kill runs

üéÆ Study Top Players

Watch streams and study leaderboard builds. Learn positioning, timing, and decision-making from the best.

  • Analyze successful Final Swarm strategies
  • Learn optimal shrine/vendor timing
  • Understand when to portal vs farm
