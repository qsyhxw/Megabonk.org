🔮 Quantity Tome

More Projectiles = More Damage Output!

Offensive Tome / DPS Multiplier
📊 Stat Type Projectile Count
⬆️ Per Level +1*
🔓 Unlock 5,000 Projectiles
🎯 Max Level 99*
⚠️ Data Notice:
Values shown are based on community testing (Common rarity). Max level 99 is community consensus. Always verify with current patch.

Quantity Tome increases your Projectile Count (投射物数量) for weapons that use projectile-type attacks. It does not affect non-projectile area weapons like Aura, Frostwalker, or Dragon's Breath.

Sources: Destructoid (Sep 29, 2025), GameRant (Oct 1, 2025), Steam Community Guides (Sep 28, 2025)

Overview

  • Stat: Projectile Count (Attack Instances)
  • Base upgrade per level: +1 projectile (Common rarity)
  • How to unlock: Fire 5,000 projectiles (community consensus: progress is cumulative across runs)
  • Max level: 99 (community/media consensus; not officially documented)

Quantity Tome is highly valued for projectile-based weapons in Megabonk. By multiplying the number of projectiles or attacks, it can dramatically increase DPS and screen coverage. However, weapon compatibility is critical—some weapons benefit massively while others gain nothing at all.

Effects

  • Adds extra projectiles or attack instances for compatible weapons
  • Does NOT affect Aura, Frostwalker, or Dragon's Breath
  • Linear scaling - each level adds +1 projectile (Common base)
  • Part of DPS formulas that may interact multiplicatively with other stats (exact formulas are community-derived)
  • Improves both single-target DPS and crowd control
  • More projectiles = better screen coverage

Sources: Steam discussions (Sep 29, 2025), Reddit r/MegabonkOfficial (Sep 2025)

Popular Synergies (Build Suggestions)

Many build guides suggest pairing Quantity Tome with the following for enhanced DPS and screen coverage:

Damage Tome
More damage per projectile combined with more projectiles creates powerful DPS scaling.
Precision Tome
Critical hits with multiple projectiles can dramatically improve kill speed, especially for clearing enemy swarms.
Cooldown Tome
Faster attacks combined with more projectiles floods the screen with damage.
Size Tome
Larger projectiles with higher quantity improves coverage and hit consistency.
Duration Tome
Longer-lasting projectiles with higher quantity creates screen saturation and overlapping damage.
Luck Tome
Higher Luck increases the chance of Legendary upgrades, which give +2 projectiles per level instead of +1.

Note: These are build strategy suggestions from community guides. Exact stat interactions (additive vs. multiplicative) vary. Consult community formulas for details.

Sources: TheGamer (Oct 12, 2025), ProGameGuides (Oct 2025), Steam formulas (Sep 28, 2025)

Strategy Guide

  • Some weapons don't benefit, so prioritize only if your weapon is compatible

  • Effective against both crowds and bosses

When to Use Quantity Tome

  • ✅ Best for: Projectile-based weapons (Bow, Revolver, Lightning Staff, etc.)
  • ✅ Best for: Building for pure DPS optimization
  • ✅ Best for: Combining with Damage and Cooldown Tomes
  • ✅ Best for: Improving crowd control and screen coverage
  • ❌ Skip for: Aura, Frostwalker, and Dragon's Breath (typically no effect)
  • ❌ Skip for: Builds that don't fire projectiles
  • ⚠️ Test first: Melee weapons or weapons with unclear projectile mechanics

Pro Tips & Important Notes

  • Unlock condition is straightforward and comes naturally with ranged play
  • At max level (99), can provide ~100 total projectiles (assuming Common +1/level and base of 1)

  • Can dramatically impact runs when paired with compatible weapons
  • Community-suggested "DPS trinity": Damage + Quantity + Cooldown Tomes
  • Higher rarity upgrades (Legendary = +2 projectiles) make it even stronger
  • High projectile counts may have visual display limitations but still apply full damage
