🧪 How Golden Sneakers Works

Golden Sneakers turns movement into income. As you traverse the stage, coins are generated passively. This makes it ideal for route-based farming and for builds that naturally traverse large parts of the map.

Tip: Because the effect rewards distance covered, it pairs naturally with movement speed items and map-clearing routes rather than stationary boss camping.

When to Pick

  • Gold-farming runs or leaderboard pushes that benefit from fast shop/chest cycling.
  • Builds that already stack movement speed and cover the whole map naturally.

🔓 How to Unlock

  1. Load into any map and target destructible pots along safe routes.
  2. Use speed items (e.g., Turbo Socks) to sweep the stage quickly.
  3. Once you’ve broken 150 pots, Golden Sneakers is permanently added to your item pool.

Need more economy pieces? Try Golden Glove for kill-based gold bonuses.
