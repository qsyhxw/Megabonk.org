üîì How to Unlock Pot (Stainless Steel)

Unlocking Pot (Stainless Steel) requires completing a challenging speed-collection task in the Crypt. You must collect every single pot and open every chest before the escape timer expires.

üè∫ Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Enter the Crypt: Access the Crypt area from the Graveyard map using 4 Crypt Keys.
  2. Check the Timer: If the escape timer shows less than ~1:20, restart for better odds.
  3. Collect Everything: Smash every pot and open every chest in the Crypt before time expires.
  4. Use Movement Boosts: Disable other items except movement speed enhancers (Turbo Socks, Agility Tome) before starting.
  5. Complete Before Timer: You don't need to escape‚Äîjust collect everything within the time limit.

‚öôÔ∏è Item Effect Breakdown

Weapon Level Cap (Diminishing Returns)

Pot (Stainless Steel) only affects weapons (not Tomes). The stacking mechanic works as follows:

  • 1st Pot: +10 weapon level cap
  • 2nd Pot: +9 weapon level cap
  • 3rd Pot: +8 weapon level cap
  • ... (continues decreasing by 1)
  • 9th+ Pot: +2 weapon level cap (minimum)

Need Tome Level Caps?

Use Wizard's Hat instead! This new item (added in v1.0.49) provides +5 Tome level cap, functioning like the old Pot effect for Tomes.

When is This Item Best?

  • Extended runs: The longer you play, the more value from extra weapon levels.
  • Weapon-focused builds: Pairs excellently with items that scale per weapon level.
  • Combine with Wizard's Hat: Get both weapon and tome level caps for maximum scaling.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do you unlock Pot (Stainless Steel)?
Collect every pot and open every chest in the Crypt before the timer expires. Use movement speed boosts and practice bunny hopping.
Do I need Bandit to unlock this item?
No, the Bandit requirement was removed in a recent update. Any character with good movement can complete the challenge.
Do I need to escape the Crypt?
No! You just need to collect everything before the timer runs out. Reaching the exit is not required.
Is Pot (Stainless Steel) worth the effort?
Yes, but with caveats. The first pot gives +10 weapon levels which is very powerful. However, stacking has diminishing returns. Consider combining with Wizard's Hat for tome levels too.
Does Pot (Stainless Steel) still affect Tomes?
No! Since v1.0.49 (Christmas Patch), it only affects weapons. Use Wizard's Hat for Tome level caps.
