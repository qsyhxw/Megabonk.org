Brass Knuckles

UNCOMMON ITEM
Effect: Deal +20% damage to nearby enemies.

Looking for more items? Browse the full Items database.

🔓 How to Unlock

Defeat enemies with the Sword until you reach 5,000 kills in total. Once achieved, Brass Knuckles enters your item pool for future runs.

Tip: If you’re actively farming the unlock, run a melee-leaning build (Sword/Katana) on easier maps to keep enemies close and clear waves quickly.

🧪 What Brass Knuckles Actually Does

  • Flat bonus near targets: Attacks against enemies within a short radius gain +20% damage.
  • Best for melee/close-range: Ranged/kite builds benefit less because they avoid proximity.
  • Patch v1.0.17: Changed “additive → flat” and the effective area scales per stack, improving consistency for close-range builds (see Notes).

🧭 Strategy & Uses

When to pick Brass Knuckles

  • You're on a melee or mid-range plan and can consistently stay close to enemies.
  • You need a simple, reliable global damage bump for horde clearing.

Synergy ideas (safe & general)

  • Sword or Katana: continuous close contact.
  • Aegis / Corrupted Sword: brawling playstyles that sit in pack range.

Not ideal if your plan is to full-kite or rely on long-range projectiles. Consider other multipliers instead.

📋 Patch & Balance History

  • v1.0.17 (Oct 19, 2025): Brass Knuckles changed from additive to flat scaling; area increases per stack. Improves scaling for melee builds. See “Notes & Sources”.
