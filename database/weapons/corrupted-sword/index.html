⚙️ Corrupted Sword Mechanics
What the weapon actually does
🔁 Twin Slash Pattern
Slashes forward and backward each attack, providing reliable coverage around the character.
Range: Short (melee)
Improves with: Size, Quantity (Projectile Count)
❤️ Low-HP Damage Boost
Deals increased damage when your HP is low. This enables a risky “berserker” playstyle with big burst windows.
Playstyle: Dip low safely, then capitalize
Keep alive with: Sustain items & careful positioning
📚 Scaling & Tomes
Benefits from Damage, Size, Cooldown, and Quantity tomes. Size/Quantity add more coverage and hits; Cooldown increases swing frequency.
Typical picks: Damage • Size • Cooldown • Quantity
Works with: Anvil upgrades for faster power spikes
👑 Sir Chadwell Link
Sir Chadwell starts with Corrupted Sword. He scales into late game but starts slow; consider adding a ranged weapon early.
Role: Starter weapon for Chadwell
Note: Unlocking Chadwell requires Forest Tier 3 (separate from the sword’s challenge)

🧠 Advanced Tips
Practical notes from community-proven runs
🎛️ Limit Slots
Refund to ~2 Weapon / 2–3 Tome slots so Cursed Tome appears more often during level-ups.
⛏️ Save a Magnet
Clear safely, then grab a Magnet shrine late to jump several tome levels at once.
🗺️ Hyper Desert Route
Hyper Desert Tier 1 is popular for XP density; if damage falls off, downshift to safer tiers.
🪓 Ranged Partner
Early ranged weapons (Dexecutioner, Katana, Aura) stabilize clears while the sword scales.
⚖️ Play the Edge
Use the low-HP bonus, but keep sustain handy to avoid sudden deaths during swarms.
🔁 Banish & Reroll
Banish non-critical tomes; reroll aggressively for Cursed/XP picks during the challenge.
