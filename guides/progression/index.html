  • Luck Tome Timing: Grab before opening chests for better drops
  • Curse Tome Usage: Only take when you have defensive foundation
  • Stack XP and Luck early, add Curse after 10 minutes
  • Holy Trinity enables exponential power growth - game changer!
  • Visit Moai Shrines and Gold Chests for free items after Luck

  • Don't attempt Final Swarm yet - master base tiers first

üí° Advanced Strategies

  • Item Priority: Za Warudo > Overpowered Lamp > Moldy Cheese
  • Microwave Strategy: Duplicate S-tier items like Moldy Cheese 3x
  • Kevin + Mirror Combo: Stack 200%+ attack speed for near-immortality
  • Use Greed Shrines with Curse Tome for exponential drops
  • Delay chest openings until Luck is high (40%+) for legendaries
  • Master positioning: know when to face-tank vs kite
  • Learn boss patterns: most attacks are telegraphed and dodgeable

‚ö†Ô∏è Advanced Pitfalls

  • Don't neglect Cooldown Tome - affects everything
  • Avoid mixing contradictory playstyles (speed build + Idle Juice = bad)
