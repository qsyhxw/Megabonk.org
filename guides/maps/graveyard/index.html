🔓 How to Unlock the Graveyard

The Graveyard map doesn't simply unlock by playing. It involves one of the longest specific unlocking quest-chains in the game, requiring you to play hide-and-seek with ghosts.

The Unlock Sequence

  1. Acknowledge Prerequisites: Make sure you have unlocked Calcium (the skeleton) by defeating 1,000 skeletons, and ensuring the Desert Map and Desert Tier 2 difficulty are unlocked.
  2. Start a Specific Run: You MUST select Desert Tier 2 and you MUST play as Calcium.
  3. Hunt the Graves: While surviving, look at your minimap. Search for four specific coffin-like grave objects marked with boss icons.
  4. Slay the Ghosts: Interacting with the graves spawns four color-coded ghosts (White, Black, Purple, Red) consecutively. Kill them all.
  5. Defeat The Father: Immediately after the ghosts die, hunt down the giant Golden Statue Boss (Calcium's Dad / The Undead Boss). Defeating him instantly pops the achievement and unlocks the Graveyard forever!

NOTE: You do not need to finish or win the Desert Tier 2 run. The exact millisecond the Undead Boss dies, the Graveyard is permanently added to your map selection screen. Need a build to beat him? View our Undead Boss Strategy Guide.

⚰️ How the Graveyard Crypt Works

Quick answer: A Graveyard run starts inside a timed Crypt. Find the exit before the timer ends, collect four keys from guarded coffins outside, return to the marked Crypt Door, clear the second timed Crypt, and go through its final door to fight Big Bob.

Phase 1

Escape the starting Crypt

Follow the available platforms and doors until you reach the outdoor Graveyard. Treat treasure as optional when the exit timer is running low.

Phase 2

Collect four keys

Search the outdoor map for four red-beam coffins. Open each coffin, defeat its guardian, and pick up the Crypt Key.

Phase 3

Return to the Crypt Door

Look for the red gust of wind that marks the door. Insert all four keys to enter the second Crypt section.

Phase 4

Reach Big Bob

Cross the second timed interior and pass through the last door before the timer expires to begin the boss fight.

When a Crypt timer reaches zero: A short grace window remains before Spooky Steve appears. He is a high-health optional boss rather than an enemy you should expect an early build to defeat. Beating him unlocks Roberto.

🔑 How to Find All Four Crypt Keys

The key coffins are scattered around the outdoor Graveyard rather than kept together inside one building. They are not fixed to one reusable coordinate route.

💀 Big Bob: Door, Fight, and Scythe Unlock

After inserting all four Crypt Keys, enter the Crypt Door and follow the second timed route to its final door. Big Bob waits beyond that exit. Defeating him unlocks the Scythe weapon.

Unlocks tied to the Crypt route

  • Pot (Stainless Steel): In the first Crypt, open every chest and pot. Any character works, and the displayed timer may reach zero during the brief grace window before Spooky Steve arrives.
  • Scythe: Defeat Big Bob after completing the four-key route.
  • Bob's Light: Land the finishing blow on Big Bob with damage from charging a lamp. Its current effect is a 33% chance to one-shot Red Ghosts, increased from 15% in v1.0.49.
  • Roberto: Defeat Spooky Steve after he appears.

Before opening the four-key door

  • Finish the four-key pickup check; do not assume a defeated guardian automatically added the key.
  • Make sure ordinary Graveyard enemies die quickly enough that the boss arena will not become crowded.
  • Bring reliable boss damage plus enough movement to reposition without losing all damage uptime.
  • Once inside, prioritize the timed route. Optional treasure is not worth missing the final door.

👻 Graveyard Boss Outcomes

Big Bob — Scythe Unlock

Big Bob is the destination of the four-key Crypt route. Defeat him after clearing the second timed Crypt to add Scythe to the weapon pool.

Spooky Steve — Roberto Unlock

Spooky Steve appears shortly after a Crypt timer reaches zero. His unusually large health pool makes him a deliberate damage test, not a normal route target. Defeat him to unlock Roberto. (Use the Spooky Steve boss strategy).

🐍 Crypt Secret Room: How to Find Snek

The Crypt also contains a hidden route to Snek. This is separate from the four-key Big Bob objective, so decide whether the current attempt is a boss clear or a Secret Room search.

  1. Inside the Crypt, look for the room with two jump pads and a central pillar. The layout can appear mirrored, so use these landmarks rather than a fixed left/right instruction.
  2. Drop to the lower platform and step on the pressure plate. An activation sound indicates that the hidden entrance opened.
  3. Check the lava wall and enter the newly opened tunnel.
  4. Follow the tunnel to the hidden chest or object and collect Snek.

Common mistake: jumping into the lava before activating the pressure plate. Find the plate first; otherwise the hidden tunnel is not open.

🔮 Other Map Secrets and Special Items

Exploration within the Graveyard rewards observant players with unique artifacts that don't spawn natively in the Forest or Desert.

  • Tomestones: Look for purple dots on your minimap. Activating a Tomestone provides an immediate +10% EXP buff at the cost of a static +5% difficulty scalar to enemies.
  • Shrines of Balance: These tricky shrines offer a massive +200% multiplier to one of your stats, but indiscriminately slap a -50% debuff onto another random stat. They are run-defining gambles.
  • The Old Mask: Usually generated at the absolute summit of the castle-like tower structures towards the edge of the map layout.
  • The Hidden Pot Item: Found exclusively on the Graveyard map, picking this up allows you to limit-break your current weapons and upgrade them beyond their intended level caps.

✅ Crypt Route Checklist

Before committing to Big Bob, confirm:

  • The starting Crypt exit is complete.
  • All four guardian ghosts are defeated.
  • All four keys were actually picked up.
  • The red-wind Crypt Door is located.
  • The build is ready for a timed interior and boss.

❓ Graveyard Crypt FAQ

Where is the Crypt in Graveyard?

A Graveyard run begins inside a timed Crypt. Escape it to reach the outdoor map; later, four Crypt Keys open the marked Crypt Door leading to Big Bob.

How many Crypt Keys are required?

Four. Each key comes from a red-beam coffin encounter guarded by Jeff, Mike, Ted, or Gary.

How do I find a Crypt Key coffin again?

Move close enough to discover it. The coffin is then added to the map, allowing you to level up and return when ready.

What does defeating Big Bob unlock?

Defeating Big Bob unlocks the Scythe weapon. To unlock Bob's Light, make damage from charging a lamp deal the finishing blow. Bob's Light currently has a 33% chance to one-shot Red Ghosts (up from 15% in v1.0.49).

Where is the Secret Room?

In the Crypt, find the room with two jump pads and a central pillar. Activate the pressure plate on its lower platform, then enter the tunnel that opens in the lava wall to collect Snek.

How do I unlock Pot (Stainless Steel)?

Open every chest and pot in the first Crypt. Any character works; zero on the displayed timer is not an instant failure, but Spooky Steve arrives after a short grace window.

What happens if the Crypt timer reaches zero?

A short grace window begins, then Spooky Steve appears. Defeating this high-health optional boss unlocks Roberto.
