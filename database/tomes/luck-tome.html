Overview

Luck Tome boosts your Luck stat. According to media testing and community guides, Luck improves the probability of obtaining higher rarity rewards during level-ups, Shrine interactions, and Chest/enemy drops. While it doesn't directly enhance combat stats, it significantly improves overall reward quality and growth potential throughout your run. It's commonly paired with XP Tome and Cursed Tome for snowball-style builds (based on current version).

üí° Key Point: Luck Tome is universally considered one of the best Tomes in the game once unlocked, but its extreme rarity makes it a late-game achievement for most players. Its compound effect means the earlier you invest in it during a run, the more impactful it becomes.

Sources: Destructoid (2025-09-22), TheGamer (2025-09-26), FandomWire (2025-10-15)

How to Unlock Luck Tome

Based on multiple recent sources, Luck Tome is obtained as an extremely rare drop, commonly described as having a ~0.01% chance to drop when killing any enemy.

There are also references in some older guides to unlocking it by "breaking 20 pots in a single run," though this may reflect outdated information or version differences. The rare enemy drop method appears to be the current standard across most media and wiki sources.

Recommendation: Treat this as a rare drop and expect to play many runs before obtaining it. When it does drop, prioritize it immediately.

Sources: GameRant (2025-10-01), Fandom Wiki (regularly updated), Destructoid, Deltia's Gaming

You acquire Luck Tome and invest a few levels into it during the first 5-10 character levels

Best Synergies & Pairings

While Luck Tome is powerful on its own, combining it with certain Tomes and Items creates game-breaking strategies:

XP Tome
The ultimate synergy. XP Tome accelerates leveling, multiplying the number of high-quality rewards you receive from Luck. This combo enables the fastest possible power spikes.
Cursed Tome
Cursed Tome dramatically increases enemy density, leading to more kills, more XP, more drops, and more opportunities for Luck to shine. Risk vs. reward at its finest.
Golden Tome
Better rewards from Luck pair perfectly with more currency to buy them. Alternatively, Kevin + Golden Shield can generate infinite money if you can outheal the self-damage.
Chaos Tome
Chaos Tome provides random stat bonuses. Higher Luck increases the frequency of Legendary-tier Chaos rolls, which can lead to absurd stat combinations.
