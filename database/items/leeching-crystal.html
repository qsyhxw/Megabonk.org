Leeching Crystal

⭐ UNCOMMON ITEM
Effect: Increase Max HP by 50% but reduce HP Regen by 50%.
Unlocks in your item pool after Regen Tome reaches Level 8.

As of v1.0.17 (Oct 19, 2025), shield recharge is no longer interrupted by Leeching Crystal (or Kevin). Build advice below reflects this change.

🧪 What Leeching Crystal Does

Leeching Crystal is a high-impact survivability modifier: it massively increases your health pool while throttling your passive regeneration. If you’re browsing the full item database, think of it as a “big tank, low idle healing” switch.

Mechanics

  • +50% Max HP (multiplicative to your current maximum).
  • -50% HP Regen (applies to your total regen value).
  • Stacks: multiple copies further reduce regen; over-stacking can nullify sustain—pair with lifesteal or burst healing.
⚖️ Balance & Conflicts in Guides

Some older/community guides list “+50 Max HP (flat)”. Recent, widely-cited guides agree on “+50%”. This page follows the newer consensus and current patch meta.

🔓 How to Unlock

  1. Upgrade Regen Tome to Level 8.
  2. Start runs and open chests—Leeching Crystal will now appear in your item pool.
🧩 Related Unlock (Kevin)

Obtaining 3 Leeching Crystals in a single run unlocks Kevin.

🔗 Best Uses, Synergies & Cautions

Core Picks to Pair

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ EXCELLENT

Lifesteal Enablers

Use on-hit lifesteal (e.g., Demonic Blade) to offset reduced regen.

⭐⭐⭐⭐ GOOD

Medkit & Regen Sources

Medkit and similar effects restore steadiness after the −50% hit.

⭐⭐⭐⭐ GOOD

Max-HP Scaling

Items that scale with Max HP (e.g., Beefy Ring) benefit from a larger health pool.

⭐⭐⭐⭐ GOOD

Kevin (after unlock)

Crystal stacks help meet requirements for Kevin; play with strong sustain.

⚠️ Use with Care

❗ CAUTION

Pure Regen Plans

Over-stacking crystals undermines regen-centric setups (even with Campfire); mix in lifesteal/burst healing.

🛡️ Shields After v1.0.17

Shield recharge is no longer interrupted by Leeching Crystal. This improves hybrid tank-shield lines compared to pre-patch behavior.
