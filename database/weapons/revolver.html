üîì How to Unlock Revolver

The Revolver is CL4NK's default weapon and can be unlocked for all characters through a straightforward progression path.

1 Complete Forest Tier 1
Reach and complete the first tier of the Forest biome. This typically requires surviving for 10-15 minutes and defeating the area boss.
2 Purchase the Weapon
After completing Forest Tier 1, the Revolver becomes available for purchase in the weapon shop. Cost may vary based on game progression.
3 Unlock CL4NK (Optional)
If you want to use the Revolver as a default weapon, unlock CL4NK by completing Forest Tier 1. CL4NK starts with the Revolver and gains +1% Crit Chance per level.
üí° Pro Tip: Focus on completing Forest Tier 1 as quickly as possible. Use Fox or Sir Oofie (default characters) for your first run, as they have balanced stats suitable for new players.

Understanding the Revolver's mechanics is crucial for maximizing its effectiveness.

üéØ Precision Shooting

The Revolver fires single, high-damage projectiles at targeted enemies. Unlike area weapons, it excels at eliminating high-priority targets quickly.

üí• Critical Hit System

Base 10% critical hit chance that scales with character progression. CL4NK gains +1% per level, making him the ultimate Revolver user.

üìè Range Control

Optimal effectiveness at medium to long range. Projectile speed allows for leading targets and maintaining safe distance from threats.

üîÑ Fire Rate

Moderate base fire rate that can be enhanced through cooldown reduction items and speed upgrades. Balance between burst damage and sustained DPS.

üéì Advanced Techniques

Perfect Positioning: Maintain medium range (50-70% of maximum range) for optimal damage and safety. Too close and you risk getting overwhelmed; too far and you waste time repositioning.
Target Priority System: Always eliminate threats in this order: Boss > Elite Enemies > Ranged Attackers > Melee Mobs. The Revolver's single-target damage makes it perfect for this strategy.
Ammunition Economy: While the Revolver has no actual ammunition system, it has cooldowns. Maximize efficiency by timing shots between enemy movements and avoiding wasted shots on low-HP targets.
Combo with AoE: Pair the Revolver with strong AoE weapons like Aura, Firestaff, or Tornado. Use AoE for crowd control while reserving Revolver shots for high-value targets.
Critical Mass Strategy: Once you reach 50%+ crit chance, focus entirely on crit damage multipliers. This creates exponential damage scaling that can one-shot even tanky enemies.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is Revolver good for beginners?

Yes! It's straightforward to use and rewards good positioning. Start with CL4NK for the easiest experience.

What's the maximum crit chance?

You can reach 100% crit chance with optimal items and CL4NK at high levels. Realistically, aim for 60-70% for consistent performance.

Does Revolver work solo?

Not recommended. Always pair with at least one AoE weapon for crowd control. Revolver alone will get overwhelmed by mobs.

Best secondary weapons?

Aura, Firestaff, Tornado, or Axe for AoE coverage. Katana or Sword for melee backup if surrounded.

How to farm Forest Tier 1 fast?

Use Fox or Sir Oofie, focus on movement speed items, and clear enemies efficiently. Average completion time: 12-15 minutes.

Revolver vs Sniper Rifle?

Revolver has faster fire rate, Sniper has higher per-shot damage. Revolver is more versatile; Sniper is more specialized.
