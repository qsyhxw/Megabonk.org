Combat Achievement Strategy

Most combat achievements require dedicated farming runs. Don't try to complete them all at once. Many can be combined (e.g., weapon kill counts while farming silver). The rarest achievement in this category, Cactus (0.56%), requires specific builds and patience.

‚öîÔ∏è Combat Achievement Mastery Tips

üéØ Combine Objectives
Many combat achievements can be worked on simultaneously. For example, farming goblin kills for Ogre unlock while also working on Sword kills for Brass Knuckles.
üìä Track Your Progress
Use the kill counters on this page to manually track your progress between runs. Steam doesn't show partial progress for kill count achievements.
üîÑ Specialize Runs
Don't try to work on all combat achievements at once. Dedicate entire runs to specific goals. One run for Sword kills, another for thorns, etc.
‚è∞ Patience for Rarest
The Cactus achievement (0.56%) requires extreme dedication. Save it for last. You need specific character unlock, weapon unlock, and Desert stage access first.
üí™ Master One Build
The Walking Apocalypse build (Aura + Mirror + Kevin) works for most kill count achievements. Master this build first before attempting specialized weapon achievements.
üìà Long-Term Mindset
Combat achievements take time. The 100,000 total kill achievement alone requires 20-30 runs. Don't burn out - work on them gradually while enjoying the game.
