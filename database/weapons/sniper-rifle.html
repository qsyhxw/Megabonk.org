🔓 How to Unlock Sniper Rifle

The Sniper Rifle requires dedication to the Precision Tome, making it a mid-game unlock for players who've mastered the basics. Follow this step-by-step guide to add this powerful weapon to your arsenal:

  1. Locate the Precision Tome
    The Precision Tome can spawn randomly during runs. Prioritize picking it up when you see it, as you'll need to level it up significantly.
  2. Level Precision Tome to Level 10
    This is the main challenge. Focus your run on maximizing XP gain and choosing Precision Tome upgrades whenever possible. This typically takes 2-3 focused runs.
  3. Complete the Run Successfully
    You don't need to beat a boss - just survive and reach Tome level 10 during any run. The unlock will trigger at the end of the run.
  4. Return to Main Menu
    After completing your run, the Sniper Rifle will be available for purchase in the Weapons tab of the Unlocks menu.
  5. Purchase for 2 Silver
    Spend 2 Silver to permanently unlock the Sniper Rifle. It will then start appearing in future runs and can be upgraded normally.
💡 Pro Tip: Use Fox or Sir Oofie for the unlock run - their survivability makes it easier to focus on leveling the Precision Tome without dying prematurely.

🎓 Advanced Strategies

🎯 Position for Piercing: Always try to line up enemies in a straight line. The Sniper Rifle's piercing shots can hit 5-10+ enemies if they're properly aligned.
👑 Boss Priority Targeting: In boss fights, ignore adds and focus exclusively on the boss. Your high single-target DPS will eliminate the boss quickly, ending the encounter.
🔄 Weapon Combo Strategy: Pair Sniper Rifle with an AoE secondary weapon like Lightning Staff or Aura to cover your weakness against swarms while maintaining boss killing power.
⚡ Attack Speed Breakpoints: At ~150% attack speed, the Sniper Rifle becomes a completely different weapon - from deliberate shots to rapid-fire death machine.
💡 Upgrade Path Optimization: Early game: Focus on damage and survivability. Mid game: Add crit chance and attack speed. Late game: Maximize projectile count and pierce.
🏔️ Terrain Usage: Use walls and obstacles to create natural choke points. The Sniper Rifle excels when enemies are forced into narrow corridors.
🎲 RNG Management: With high Luck stat (Fox + items), prioritize chest opening and shrine usage. Better items mean faster scaling for your Sniper build.
🛡️ Defensive Positioning: Always maintain maximum distance from enemies. The Sniper Rifle is most effective when you're at the edge of your screen range.

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Over-focusing on Attack Speed Early

While attack speed is important, prioritizing it too early wastes the Sniper Rifle's natural high damage per shot. Build damage and crit first, then add attack speed in mid-game.

❌ Fighting in Melee Range

The Sniper Rifle's slow fire rate makes you vulnerable up close. Always maintain maximum distance and use terrain to create space.

❌ Ignoring Projectile Count Upgrades

More bullets per shot dramatically increases DPS. Don't sleep on projectile count - it's one of your best scaling options.

❌ Not Using Secondary Weapons

Relying solely on Sniper Rifle leaves you vulnerable to swarms. Always carry an AoE weapon like Lightning Staff or Aura as backup.

❌ Poor Positioning in Boss Fights

Standing still or getting cornered negates all your advantages. Always be moving and maintaining optimal range during boss encounters.
