🪙 Best Robinette Build - Ultimate Economy Guide

⭐ S+ Tier ⚡ OVERPOWERED
🎯 Core Strategy: Stack Golden Tomes to scale damage exponentially with collected gold
💪 Power Level: Highest damage ceiling in game - regularly hits 5000+ damage per projectile
🎮 Difficulty: Medium - requires active gold collection but extremely powerful when mastered
⚔️ Best For: Players who want to absolutely demolish everything with exponential scaling

💰 Why Robinette is Broken

Robinette isn't just strong - she's completely overpowered and arguably needs nerfs according to both the community and game media. Her gold-based scaling creates an exponential feedback loop that no other character can match. The more damage you deal, the faster you clear enemies, which generates more gold, which increases your damage even further. With 3-4 stacked Golden Tomes, the damage numbers become absolutely absurd.

Unlike most characters who hit a power plateau, Robinette's power curve is essentially unlimited. Most builds top out around 1000-2000 total damage. Robinette regularly reaches 5000+ damage PER PROJECTILE by the 8-minute mark. This build trivializes endgame content - bosses die in 2-3 seconds, and you'll be one-shotting elite enemies throughout your run.

🎯 Core Build Components

⚔️ Weapons (Priority Order)

1. Bananarang ESSENTIAL
Perfect synergy with Quantity Tomes. Each projectile can collect gold and scales with your economy. More projectiles = more gold collection = more damage. This is your bread and butter.
2. Revolver ESSENTIAL
High burst damage, excellent base stats, benefits from crit builds. Complements Bananarang's coverage with focused damage. Works great with Quantity Tomes.
3. Aegis RECOMMENDED
Provides survivability while you're collecting gold aggressively. The shield generation keeps you safe during dangerous phases. Excellent defensive option.
4. Aura or Dice OPTIONAL
Aura for passive defense and area damage. Dice for fun RNG scaling and additional coverage. Both are solid 4th weapon choices depending on your playstyle.

📚 Tomes (Stack Priority)

Golden Tome STACK 3-4
THE BUILD-DEFINING TOME. Each Golden Tome makes previous ones exponentially stronger. 3+ Golden Tomes = unstoppable. Never skip this. Ever.
Quantity Tome STACK 6-7
More projectiles = more enemies killed = more gold drops = more damage. This is your economy engine. Stack heavily for maximum gold generation.
Damage Tome STACK 3-5
Multiplicative with Golden Tomes. Each stack significantly boosts your already-insane damage numbers. Essential for late game.
Cooldown Tome STACK 2-3
Faster attacks = more gold generation = faster clear speed. Get 2-3 stacks for optimal attack frequency without over-investing.
Shield Tome STACK 2-3
Survivability for boss fights and intense phases. Stack 2-3 for comfortable safety. Synergizes with Golden Shield item.

🎒 Essential Items

Golden Shield MUST HAVE
Converts your gold into shields AND damage. This single item can double your effective power. Synergizes perfectly with Golden Tomes. Grab this immediately when it appears.
Battery MUST HAVE
Massive cooldown reduction = constant attacks = insane gold generation. This accelerates your entire economy loop. Essential for scaling speed.
Gym Sauce HIGHLY RECOMMENDED
Huge damage multiplier that stacks with everything else. Often provides 50%+ damage increase in one item slot. Take this every time you see it.
Beefy Ring HIGHLY RECOMMENDED
Health and survivability for aggressive gold collection. Keeps you alive while you're running into enemy waves to grab coins.
Credit Cards BOTH COLORS
Direct gold generation multipliers. Both Green and Red Credit Cards stack multiplicatively with Golden Tomes. Take both if you see them.
Golden Glove NICE TO HAVE
Solid all-around stats that benefit any build. Good filler item when core pieces are taken. Provides decent value but not essential.

⚠️ Common Mistakes & How to Avoid Them

🚫 Beginner Traps

  • Ignoring Golden Tomes: This is THE build-defining tome. Never skip Golden Tome. Even if you have 4, take a 5th. The scaling is exponential.
  • Not Collecting Gold: Sounds obvious, but you need to actively move around and collect every coin. Your damage literally depends on it.
  • Taking Non-Synergy Items: Avoid items that don't scale with gold or projectile count. Every item slot matters.
  • Too Many Weapons: Stick to 3-4 weapons max. More weapons dilute your upgrade pool and slow down Golden Tome acquisition.
  • Skipping Defense: Even though damage is insane, you still need Aegis and Shield Tomes. Don't get one-shot.

✅ Pro Tips for Maximum Abuse

  • Golden Tome Synergy: Each Golden Tome makes previous ones stronger. 3 Golden Tomes isn't 3x damage - it's exponentially more.
  • Perfect Route: Golden Tome → Quantity Tome → Golden Tome → Damage Tome → Golden Tome. This sequence is broken.
  • Golden Shield Timing: Get this around minute 4-5 when you have 2-3 Golden Tomes. It becomes absurdly strong.
  • Quantity First: More projectiles = more enemies killed = more gold drops. Quantity Tome directly feeds your economy.
  • Credit Card Stack: If you see both green and red Credit Cards, take both. They stack multiplicatively with Golden Tomes.
  • Battery is Key: With high cooldown reduction, you generate gold so fast the game can barely keep up.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Robinette considered overpowered in Megabonk?
Robinette's gold-to-damage scaling creates an exponential feedback loop that has no practical ceiling. Unlike other characters who scale linearly with item and tome collection, Robinette scales exponentially: more damage → clear faster → more gold → even more damage. With 3+ Golden Tomes, the damage numbers become absurd (5000+ per projectile). Both developers and the community acknowledge she's currently the strongest character, with multiple media outlets discussing potential nerfs. The build is so powerful it trivializes most content including end-game bosses.
What is the best weapon combination for Robinette?
The optimal setup is Bananarang + Revolver + Aegis, with optional Aura or Dice as a 4th weapon. Bananarang is perfect for Robinette because it scales excellently with Quantity Tomes (more projectiles = more gold drops = more damage). Revolver adds high burst damage with crit potential. Aegis provides essential survivability since you'll be collecting gold aggressively. This combination maintains high DPS while keeping you safe during your gold collection routes. Aura can replace Aegis if you prefer passive defense, and Dice adds fun RNG scaling if you want even more variance.
Which items are essential for Robinette's economy build?
The absolute must-haves are: Golden Shield (converts your gold economy into shields and damage - this item alone can double your effective power), Battery (cooldown reduction = more attacks = more gold generation), Gym Sauce (massive damage multiplier), and Beefy Ring (survivability while collecting gold). Beyond these core items, Credit Cards (both colors) significantly boost gold generation, and Golden Glove adds nice stats. The key is prioritizing items that either generate more gold or scale with your gold total - avoid items that don't synergize with this strategy.
How do I maximize gold collection with Robinette?
Golden Tome stacking is everything. Aim for 3-4 Golden Tomes minimum - each one you stack makes previous ones exponentially more valuable. Actively move around the map to collect all gold coins; don't just kill enemies and move on. Take both Credit Cards if you see them (they stack). Prioritize Quantity Tomes early because more projectiles = more enemies killed = more gold drops, creating a virtuous cycle. Sucky Magnet can help automate collection. Most importantly, understand that gold collection isn't passive - you need to actively path around the map grabbing coins while clearing enemies. The more gold you collect early, the stronger you become, which lets you collect even more gold.
