🎮 Gameplay Strategy

Phase 1: Early Game (0-10 minutes)

Building Your Defense

Priority Goals:

  • Sir Oofie is available from start - instant access!
  • Get XP Tome first to level up faster
  • Your armor is still low - avoid taking unnecessary damage
  • Focus on Health Pendant for survivability
  • Work toward unlocking Aegis Shield (block 500 damage)
💡 Even 10-20% armor helps - you're tankier than most characters already
💡 Get close to enemies - your sword excels at melee range
Phase 2: Mid Game (10-20 minutes)

Tank Transformation

Key Actions:

  • You should be level 25-50 (25-50% armor)
  • Get Aegis Shield for blocking and counterattacks
  • Add Mirror to create your armored clone
  • Health Tome + Health Pendant for massive HP pool
  • You can now face-tank most attacks comfortably
💡 50% armor = taking half damage - you're very tanky now
💡 Stand in the middle of enemy groups and cleave them down
Phase 3: Late Game (20+ minutes)

Immortal Knight

Optimization:

  • 75-100% armor achieved - you're nearly unkillable
  • Add Thorns Armor for reflect damage
  • Attack Speed Gear for faster sword swings
  • Curse Tome is safe to take now
  • Walk into boss fights without fear
💡 100% armor + Mirror = two immortal knights wrecking everything
💡 You can literally AFK in the middle of enemy hordes

💡 Advanced Tips

🎯
Positioning

As a melee tank, position yourself in the center of enemy groups. Your armor lets you survive while your sword cleaves through them all. Don't run away!

⚡
Aggro Management

Draw enemy aggro to protect squishier builds in co-op. You're the tank - let them hit you while your team deals damage safely.

🛡️
Aegis Timing

Your shield automatically blocks and counterattacks. Face dangerous attacks head-on - the shockwave damage from blocks is significant.

✅ Pros & Cons

✓ Extreme Tankiness: Near-invincibility with max armor

✓ Default Character: Available from the start, no unlock needed
