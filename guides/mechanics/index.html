Mechanics FAQ

What should I learn first in Megabonk?

Start with the Timer guide, then learn Shrines and Powerups. Move to combat math when a build depends on scaling or proc behavior.

What is the difference between an Item, Powerup and Shrine reward?

They are separate reward systems. Powerups are temporary in-run pickups; Items are inventory effects; Shrine rewards come from Shrines. The Powerups guide explains the boundary.

Where can I learn Megabonk damage scaling?

Use Damage Brackets for multiplier order, Scaling Types for additive and linear behavior, and Proc Coefficient for Item-trigger interactions.
