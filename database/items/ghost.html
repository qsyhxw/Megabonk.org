How Ghost Works

Ghost creates temporary allies each time you interact with stage objects. Typical triggers include pots and certain shrines. This lets you convert map interaction into extra damage and crowd control even when you’re not attacking directly.

Heads-up: Effect consistency depends on how often a stage offers interactables. Routes with dense pots/shrines naturally produce more ghosts.

Why players pick Ghost

  • Synergy with pacifist runs: reliable damage without a weapon.
  • Safe chip damage: helps clear crowds while you kite or objective-play.
  • Low rarity: being Common means it appears relatively often.

How to Unlock Ghost

Requirement: Survive the Final Swarm for 60 seconds.

  1. Bring a durable setup (Armor/Thorns/Lifesteal/Movement) and keep space to maneuver.
  2. Prioritize survival over greed; you only need the timer, not kills.
  3. Use map knowledge to funnel enemies and avoid being surrounded.
