⚔️ Weapon Overview

The Sword is Sir Oofie's default weapon and represents the most basic melee option in Megabonk. It delivers straightforward melee slashes that can hit multiple enemies in front of you. While the Sword's simplicity makes it easy to understand for new players, it suffers from fundamental limitations: slow attack speed, melee-only range, forward-facing attacks only, and weak scaling. The Sword's primary value lies in its role as a weapon unlock farming tool – kill enemies with it to unlock Axe, Brass Knuckles, and the powerful Dexecutioner.

Fundamental Weaknesses

🐌 Slow Attack Speed

Base swing speed is noticeably slow. You'll need significant Attack Speed upgrades to make Sword feel responsive, which competes with other stat priorities.

👊 Melee Range Danger

Being forced into melee range means taking more damage. Sir Oofie's armor helps, but you're still vulnerable to swarms and explosive enemies.

➡️ Forward-Only Attacks

Sword can't hit behind you. Exploding enemies and fast flankers are nightmares. You need Size upgrades to increase the attack arc width.

📉 Poor Late-Game Scaling

Even with perfect upgrades, Sword can't compete with S-tier weapons in late game. Boss DPS and swarm clear speed are both suboptimal.

🎓 Advanced Sword Farming Tips

📊 Kill Efficiency Tracking: A typical 20-minute run should net 800-1,200 sword kills with basic builds. Aim for 1,500+ with optimized setups. Track your average to estimate total runs needed.
🔄 Secondary Weapon Strategy: Keep Sword in slot 2-3 after unlocking Axe. Passive kills from Sword still count toward Dexecutioner unlock while Axe does the heavy lifting.
🎯 Positioning is Everything: With forward-only attacks, constantly adjust your facing direction. Practice "attack-move" patterns: slash, step forward, slash, repeat.
⚡ Attack Speed Breakpoints: At ~150% attack speed, Sword becomes noticeably more responsive. At 200%+, it's actually playable. Prioritize this stat early if grinding kills.
📏 Size > Damage Early: Counterintuitively, Size upgrades help more than Damage early on. Wider attack arc = hitting more enemies per swing = faster clear = more kills.
🛡️ Armor is Mandatory: You will take damage in melee range. Period. Armor Tome + defensive items aren't optional – they're required for any run past 15 minutes.
⏱️ Optimal Run Length: 20-25 minute runs are the sweet spot. Longer runs have diminishing returns on kill count while increasing risk. Better to do more medium-length runs.
🎮 Mental Preparation: Grinding 12,500 Sword kills for Dexecutioner is tedious. Set realistic goals (e.g., "2K kills this session"), take breaks, listen to podcasts. It's a marathon, not a sprint.

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Trying to Make Sword "Viable"

Stop. Just stop. Dozens of weapons are better. Your time is valuable. Use Sword for unlocks only, then immediately replace it. Don't fall for sunk cost fallacy.

❌ Ignoring Movement Speed

Slow movement + slow attack + melee range = death. You NEED movement speed to kite, reposition, and avoid damage. It's not optional with Sword.

❌ Fighting Explosive Enemies Head-On

Those green exploding bugs? They're your worst nightmare with Sword. Learn to recognize and avoid them, or accept frequent deaths. There's no tank build that survives multiple explosions at once.

❌ Using Sword on Squishy Characters

Fox with Sword? Calcium with Sword? You're making it 10x harder than necessary. If farming Sword kills, use Sir Oofie. Period. Don't be a masochist.

❌ Grinding All 12.5K Kills with Sword Alone

After you unlock Axe at 2K kills, switch to it while keeping Sword in a secondary slot. Axe is massively superior while Sword's passive kills still count. Save yourself hours of frustration.
