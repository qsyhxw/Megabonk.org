🔓 How to Unlock

Requirement: Heal a total of 350 HP using Lifesteal in a single run.

  1. Pick a build that attacks fast (more procs → more Lifesteal events).
  2. Prioritize Lifesteal sources early; add sustain to avoid dying mid-progress.
  3. Keep attacking—boss invulnerability phases won’t count; use waves with dense enemies.
Note: You must meet the 350 HP via Lifesteal within the same run for the unlock to trigger.

🔗 Best Uses & Synergies

Demonic Blade shines in crit-centric setups where frequent critical hits convert into steady healing. Consider mixing with attack-speed increases and weapons that naturally reach high crit rates.

Weapons that pair well

Katana

Fast multi-hit melee elevates crit frequency, turning Blade’s heal into consistent sustain.

Sniper Rifle

High crit-damage playstyles benefit from occasional self-heals to stabilize between shots.

Build tips

  • Stack crit chance first; healing triggers only on critical hits.
  • Add attack speed to raise event frequency (more crit rolls → more heals).
  • Combine with generic sustain (regen/medkits) for safety during low-crit streaks.

❓ FAQ

What exactly does Demonic Blade do?
Each critical hit has a 25% chance to heal you. The heal amount scales with your damage output indirectly via more crits.
How do I unlock it?
Heal 350 HP via Lifesteal in one run; the item will join your pool afterward.
What’s the rarity?
Community sources list it as Uncommon. No explicit official rarity label was found as of Patch 1.0.7.
